Not Guilty: since all law written in stone and ink biblically abolished

The full proof  Revelatory Sermons - Page 20  at Godshew.org

Evangelist, make full proof: provide enough evidence to make it evident:
that no "man" is justified by the law in the sight of God, [it is] "evident".

Three Wishes: Grace Mercy Peace

Grace unto you, and peace,
from God our (merciful) Father and the Lord Jesus  Christ
NOT GUILTY!
No Condemnation! Not even to "them"!
Should be everyone's response
whenever accused by law: "accuser of the brethren".

All law written in stone and ink biblically abolished!
Biblical reason given: so that "none should perish".

Contary to popular unbelief Jesus Christ neither obeyed nor disobeyed law, since such firstly undefiled pure religion came to pay a "visit", not a ransom. His grace also remained unspotted: Jam 1:27. And contrary to popular unbelief there is no law at all in "the God of all grace", the only wise God of such foolish/wise Gods as law/grace, the only true God of such false/true Gods, the only "living" God of such dead/living Gods as Holys Ghost/Spirit... in which things are an "allegory", written aforetime for our "learning"(Rom 15:4).

Biblically two Gods are opposed about "them". The true God, "that God" of this/that Gods, who was in "Christ" (of "Jesus Christ") reconciling the world (not some only) unto himself, did NOT impute their trespasses unto them (2Cor 5:19). So why(?) then do churches, pastors and priests thereof, governments and 'ministers' thereof, impute sin by law to any, even to them?

Albeit there is no "them" in "us" to whom God hath given the victory and hath not appointed unto wrath. Nor is there any them in "us-ward" which the Lord is longsuffering unto. But the only way "them" folk (also called "ye" do err) are gonna want to convert, from them to us (from "no escape" to "escape"), and by taking the grace walk "from faith to faith", and "from glory to glory", and from Sinai to Sion, "through the valley of the shadow of death"(Ps 23), which is as through Jesus=>Christ, and as through the cross (drop off enmity) to risen & seated... only way is make them aware God & Son, eternal life thereof, on Sion (not Sinai nor Sion + Sinai). But never accuse nor condemn them; For they already accuse and condemn themselves, oppose themselves, by adding "law worketh wrath" to "my grace is sufficient for thee" (no law req'd)... not to mention condemnation to any results in condemnation to all since there is "no respect of persons with God" & "a little leaven leaveneth the whole".

Biblically only them need redemption, for only them were under the law. We are not under the law, for it's now AD=> not <=BC; "us-ward" time. The global change from <=BC to AD=> signifies the "fulness of time" for law had come when God sent JC (Gal 4), since law had an expiry date; Whereas grace always "is", eternal, never expires. Last Adam (the Lord from heaven) does not need salvation, rather is salvation ("eternal" sort) to first Adam by being the prototype of what to be (perfect, merciful) by being the "author" of "eternal" salvation (instead of temporal salvation like saved + destroyed in Jude5).

Such is why(?) there is no mention of law at all in the conclusion of all Pauline epistles including Hebrews, a mini bible itself, and Revelation (which Paul wrote) which concludes the (entire) Holy Bible containing Old and New Testaments.

Better yet, give no place and hence no authority to law worketh wrath, accusation, condemnation, vengeance, infirmities, destruction, and a dead end. Then police, who enforce the law, will not even accuse you of any crime(sin), for only law defines anything a crime(sin), anyone a criminal(sinner). Only law imputes sin: Rom 5:13, whereas that God in Christ reconciling the world did not impute sin: 2Cor 5:19, not even unto them who were anti-Christ, which is allegorically anti-"end of the law" since Christ biblically "the end of the law". For clarity such is the Christ of Jesus Christ, not the Christ of Christ Jesus.

Who(?) would be anti-"end of the law" once aware it meant no more crime, nor accusation, nor condemnation, nor fear hath torment, nor infirmities, nor destructive wrath, nor death? Only those with a vested interest in $uch, like under-takers. Yet if it meant life or death to them, they'd convert asap once made aware, unless off course they be the suicide-all type who think they do God a service by adding sacrifice to mercy, fear to love, sorrow to joy, death to life, infirmity to wellness, temporal to eternal, all by adding law to grace.

It would help even more if ministers, both religious and political, would stop using law to limit, accuse, condemn, etc... for love of money. Talk about off-ending. Many such still use law to impute sin, for the hell of it, like a child of hell made proselyte and thereby more the child of hell than former fools, hypocrites, vipers, and blind guides who were under the law in Mt 23:all.

Such is what we all ought to hand every judge of every court system which accuses and/or tries to condemn anyone; For all accusation(law) and condemnation(law) is biblically abolished so that none perish. And all that remains is for them (also the people of God) to convert to us, by come to repentance (change of mind). No judge could speak to such, except to say case dismissed; For courts still use the Holy Bible. The evidence therein is not only ample to prove you all not guilty, but it's irrefutible. The evidence is what makes it evident: that no man is justified by the law in the sight of God (Gal 3).

Selah.

And yet we continue to put trust in the law, as if it were "of God". Not! The Bible says law was given by Moses, but grace came by Jesus Christ. The twain are compared as if lie and truth (Jn 1:17). 

So if you've eroneously believed it's Godly to law, you've been as "deceived" (Mt 24:5) as "many" who "go" (opposite of come) broad way, which leadeth to "destruction" (Mt 7:13), the "no escape" sort in 1Thes 5:3 which comes suddenly upon "them" who say Peace & Safety (Grace + Law), as travail (sorrow) comes on a woman (church) with child (it's an "allegory").

Come not, let us reason together. Let us come boldly to the throne of grace, to obtain mercy, and find grace, to help, in time (since there is neither mystery nor time for such in eternity). So, it's an allegorical mystery, to be solved in time; the "operation" of God being to remove law in time lest it kill the whole body of Christ, just as cancer must be removed in time, lest it kill the whole body.

The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ with you all. Amen.

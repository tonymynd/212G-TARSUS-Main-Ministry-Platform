to do the end run along "with patience" does the end run at a "much more" pace: light speed.
For Sion isn't Zion, and a stumblingstone is laid in Sion (heavenly Jerusalem) to stumble on and fall.
Fallen is from grace to law, as from hand to hands, as from God of the living to the living God: fearful.
So to establish grace we take away law; As if to establish God of the living we take away the living God.

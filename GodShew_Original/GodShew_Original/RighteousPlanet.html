Righteous Planet: Dare We Believe It?
by independent pastor Michael Farney, friend of GodShew
links and (brackets) added, are by Daniel Miles, founder of GodShew.Org

The proclamation that we dwell in a righteous planet is for some a readily accepted concept and for others an assault on the very fabric of who they are and how they sayers perceive their present reality: (plural) Realities that are forged by world view, media, personal experience and religious culture. These are all part of the melting pot of our consciousness that we are constantly processing all information that we encounter everyday that ultimately dictates our (plural) views and conclusions (thank God the conclusion of the Holy Bible containing Old and New Testaments is a singular Pauline token of grace). The combination of all this cerebral interaction in turn determines how we structure our personal lives and how we deal with our inward 'goings ons'. It produces our personal safety net when suspended over the abyss of all our perceptions ... our reality ... Which all too often is a Three Ring Circus of life that we all get to both watch and participate in at the same time.

For a short moment of eternity I (Michael Farney) would gently urge you to put aside your safety net and inward defense mechanisms and to be brave enough to look at something that may just bring you into a liberation that all mankind thirsts for. The liberty song that resounds in the soul of every human being ... A song penned by the grand musician of all things and transcribed into the pages of our heart ... A melody that we are accepted, loved, and adored by the one who birthed us out from His own womb of infinite love. What a heart song, what an orchestration of all the instruments of His all encompassing love. Thereby let us begin to awake to the joy that we are His principle players in this symphony of life.  This 'heart song' is the axis of the thought that we are only a 'Righteous Plant'. That is the name of our Band (not a sounding brass or tinkling cymbal bandwagon).

The term 'righteous', for many, and simply understood through our religious paradigms, is usually thought of as being an adverb of doing good things or adjectivally speaking of ones personal prowess in the arena of life and interaction to others. How good can I be is the proverbial thorn in the paw of the lion that has rendered the noble beast helpless and on its way to starvation. The righteousness that I speak of is the mouse that will pull out the thorn and fill you with hope and unending gratitude (for ridding you of this tormenting thorn in the flesh). It is the righteousness that has come not by choice, nor by personal effort, but as a (free) gift to set us free to live in peace on this planet/ universe. Righteousness is everything that is right about God. It is who He is, His character, His innermost, His every thought motive and intention. It is 'all' He is. And this is who He now proclaims that we, all mankind... Already are (not will be).

We are a 'righteous planet' in the mind and intention of God. Righteous mankind is the ultimate expression of God. We are His masterpiece. This is true liberation. This is the beginning of personal revolution and freedom.

Let the song play.

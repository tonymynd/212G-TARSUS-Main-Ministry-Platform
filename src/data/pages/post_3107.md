## Content

Why is a biblical EIGHTH ABOVE SEVEN aspect portrayed in
Ephesians 4:4-6; Colossians 3:12-14; James 3:17; 2Peter 1:5-8 ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Ephesians 4:4-6 KJV
this 8th above 7 aspect occurs for "Father".
8 - and Father of all who is above all, and through all, and in you all.
7 - one God
6 - one baptism
5 - one faith
4 - one Lord
3 - one hope
2 - one Spirit
1 - one body
Colossians 3:12-14 KJV
then it also occurs for "charity", where 7 new man things are listed to put on, then "charity" [agape 2: more excellent way in 1Cor 13 KJV] can be put on as 8th even above forgiving one another is 7th.
8 - charity, above all these (7) things, as the bond of perfectness.
7 - forgiving one another
6 - forbearing one another
5 - longsuffering
4 - meekness
3 - humbleness of mind
2 - kindness
1- bowels of mercies
James 3:17 KJV
then it occurs in James 3:17 for "wisdom that is from above"
8 - wisdom that is from above is first pure, then (7 things):
7 - peaceable
6 - gentle
5 - and easy to be intreated
4 - full of mercy
3 - and good fruits
2 - without partiality
1 - and without hypocrisy.
2Peter 1:5-8
then again for "charity" as 8th in 2Peter 1:5-8, which lists 7 things to add to your faith, then above 7 add charity as 8th above 7, even above brotherly kindness, as if it still had some blindness.
8 - charity (agape that thinketh no evil, never faileth in 1Cor 13:5,8)
7 - brotherly kindness (philadelphia: love of brethren)
6 - godliness
5 - patience
4 - temperance (9th fruit of Spirit in Gal 5:23)
3 - knowledge (still gnosis, not yet epignosis)*
2 - virtue (arete, as more than dynamis)
1 - your faith.
What are your insights about this 8th above 7 aspect portrayed?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen

## Content

RE "Fruit of the Spirit": Galatians 5:22,23. 
Of 9 fruit, it's interesting (even shocking) to notice, in greek mode, especially in KJV, the 5th fruit in 'midst' of 9, KJV called "gentleness" (also elsewhere rendered as goodness), is still like the conditional "goodness" having "severity" (ouch) attached (as in Romans 11:22's allegoric LawGod to behold), or still like the brotherly kindness having some blindness (as in 2Peter 1:9); 
SO 
THEN 
only beyond the midst of 9 fruit is it then the pure grace "goodness" having no condition nor severity attached & the pure kindness (KJV called "charity", not love) having no more blindess.
Interesting stuff to learn when taking a
Walk in the Spirit THROUGH all the fruit,
From Love... to... Temperance = against such no law;
For then, much less governing needed by commanding.

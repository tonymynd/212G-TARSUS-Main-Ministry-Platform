Can Law be trusted to provide Peace and safety? Perhaps Not. 
An Example: 
As I approached a Green Light, which by Law says Safe to Proceed, I felt an inner Spirit unction to stop & observe instead; So I did. But the car driver beside me proceeded, by Law, and got hit by a Red Light Runner. Both vehicles were destroyed, and had to be  towed away. 
POINT: Seems even the Law Believer & Abider got punished.

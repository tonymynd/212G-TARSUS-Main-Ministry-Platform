## Content

Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ. 
QUESTION: 
Is "Christ" the last "Adam" or Neither of 1st & last Adams ?
If 1Cor 15:22 contrasted "Adam" vs "Christ" as "all die" vs "all made alive",
Could "old man [anthropos]" things to put off include put off BOTH the first man [anthropos] "Adam" & the last "Adam" = second man [anthropos] noted in 1Cor 15:45-47?
Is "Christ" the last "Adam" or perhaps neither of Adam1 nor Adam2?
For 1Cor 15:22 had said as in "Adam" (aka Adam1 &  Adam2) all die;
But even so in "Christ" (not in last "Adam") shall all be made alive.
For Rom 3:4 had said let God be true but EVERY man [anthropos] a LIAR.
Seems EVERY man [anthropos] a liar would include the last Adam. For...
Last "Adam" = second man [anthropos] = still anthropos man, not yet aner man.
Seems 2Cor 5:17 denotes a "new creature" in "Christ", whereby old things are passed away, would then be 'aner' man, not still 'anthropos' man such as last Adam = second man [anthropos].
For
Rom 4:8 noted Blessed the 'aner' man to whom no sin is imputed.
1Cor 13:11 noted put away childish things to become 'aner' man.
Eph 4:13 all come unto a perfect man [aner], as fulness of "Christ".
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

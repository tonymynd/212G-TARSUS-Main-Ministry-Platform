GENTLENESS Part 3 (End)
Strange things in the midst for mediators
To ALL: Grace, Mercy and Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
(attribution, study guide and spiritual aspects map in the comments)
Finally coming to a end of gentleness, thanks God it was gentle and not so longsuffering LoL. Seems Spirit helps us to innerstand that is necessary to decide rather than being LukeWarm, and we better keep walking because this is like the twilight zone in the fruit, just before goodness. Let us move forward.
So what can we learn about this gentleness2, of twain, being as if goodness1, of twain?
Goodness1: as the ‘chrestotes’ of Gentleness2, in midst of 9 fruit:
Romans 2:4’s goodness (‘chrestotes’: goodness1 of gentleness2), of goodness, forbearance, longsuffering of God, notes goodness of God leadeth to repentance. It’s said to ‘anthropos’ man (“liar” in Rom 3:4). It’s about how such a man who judges others (eg homosexuals & lesbians) is also as “inexcusable” as them who are “without excuse”. Judging them is not how to escape the judgment of a law God who has no accuse-others and excuse-me partiality. So repentance is needed.
Romans 11:22’s goodness (‘chrestotes’: goodness1 of gentleness2) is goodness of a God who spared not; So says behold (both) goodness and severity of (this God), who’s goodness toward thee is conditional upon thee continuing in such goodness, lest also be not spared, described as cut off=hewn down. Such goodness of a God who “spared not” is not [yet] your Father is “merciful”. OMG.
So, perhaps it’s why we should ponder about this gentleness (goodness) being the midst of 9. For it seems to be about a Plural God having both goodness←&→severity, even potential severity after goodness; Kinda like Mt 18:23-35’s forgiven←&→unforgiven. It’s like the order is reversed. If both, should be severity first, goodness after. It’s beginning to seem like Law, being both good←&→evil, ends with evil: reverse of live.
Seems we’re looking at gentleness that’s also something other than gentle, thereby goodness that’s something other than good. Perhaps if we now also look at this gentleness2 (chrestotes) = goodness1 (chrestotes) as kindness (chrestotes), we'll learn more.
Kindness (chrestotes) x4: as gentleness2's goodness1 (how weird is this?)
(1) 2Cor 6:6’s kindness is listed among things like ‘gnosis’ knowledge (not epignosis) on one side and Holy Ghost (not Spirit) on other side, midst sandwiched between pureness→kindness←love unfeigned; So it may not be either, yet as if in midst of both; Kinda like CJ a Mr In-Between as a mediator between God←&→men in 1Timothy 2:5.
2Cor 6:6 “By pureness, by knowledge, by longsuffering, by kindness, by the Holy Ghost, by love unfeigned,”
(2) Eph 2:7’s kindness is equally as strange, speaking of such kindness toward us (reverse of them), to be shewn in ages to come through Christ Jesus (reverse of Jesus Christ)… 2:10 then saying we (not them) got created (not made) in Christ Jesus (them), which this God BEFORE ordained that we should walk in them (not yet us). How weird is this? …
For even Plural God in Gen 1 never said let us create(bara) man, but let us make(asah) man; Yet then created man in his image (reverse of likeness)… as if first play out a reverse scenario: “in our image” as if reverse before of before/after, after being “after our likeness”. Perhaps our likeness should be Jesus Christ, not Christ Jesus. Seems why Paul turns CJ around to JC in Hebrews. For JC is NT foundation to build upon: grace & truth. CJ as if reverse: law & lie. So in 1Pet 5:10’s before/after, CJ is before, as if created of created/made, thereby imperfect. After made perfect is by the God of all grace.
(3) Col 3:12’s kindness is low on a list of upgrades, only 2nd of 7 topped off with charity. Previously 3:10 noted putting on the new man, of old/new, is as being renewed in the ‘epignosis’ knowledge, to be changed from the former corrupt state of the old man. Col 3:12 also mentions the elect, and false Christs arise to seduce even the elect if possible. So the upgrades listed are to take the elect above the potential of being seduced on high. So it’s kinda like getting above(epi: higher) the above(ano: high), as if it’s after the after: After spirit of soul/spirit, also try the spirits: compare spirit of error & spirit of truth, and after spiritual of natural/spiritual, also compare spiritual things with spiritual. For law is both natural & spiritual, but when spiritual it can be spiritual wickedness in high places.
So Paul’s writings say:
Be not highminded←mind not high things→be not highminded
Mind not high things would include a Majesty on high, old/new high priests thereof.
CJ is portrayed as new high priest in Heb 3, mediating BetterLaw, is NotYet Grace.
So in Hebrews, Paul turns CJ around to JC to end the epistle with Grace. Interesting.
(4) Titus 3:4’s kindness and love(philanthropia) toward man(philanthropia) is mentioned as an after scenario, yet seems to refer to them, of them/us, and via CJ, of CJ/JC.
This is quite an interesting and somewhat complicated study of gentleness in midst of 9.
There’s yet a 4th rendering of this gentleness=goodness=kindness=good in Rom 3:12, where it says of “they”: “They are all gone out of the way, they are together become unprofitable, there is none that doeth good(chrestotes), no, not one”. However the context in prior verses seems to suggest a “they”: are “together” unprofitable includes both Jews & Gentiles, as if both are “they”; neither we of they/we, nor us of them/us. Yet “them” Jesus spake to, about bread from heaven, say give “us” this bread in John 6:32-34. So them, of them/us, call themselves us. Oh boy. What is this midst thing all about? Seems we need to ponder such, and discuss such, prior to looking at 6th aspect beyond such midst, aka the goodness2 beyond the goodness1 called gentleness. Hence, consider this only a draft, and open to discussion to continue growing in:
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [is] with you all. Amen

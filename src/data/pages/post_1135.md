## Content

GALATIANS. It's interesting to notice Galatians is like a PEP talk "unto the (bewitched) churches" = "brethren", 
- Begins with a Positive: Grace & peace from God & JC, 
- Evaluates in midst: reasoning & suggesting upgrades, 
- Ends with a Positive: "Brethren, the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit. Amen".
Hmmm, if Galatians is the only book written "UNTO THE CHURCHES", and Revelation 2 & 3 says, seven times, he that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith "UNTO THE CHURCHES", Well then...
Seems it may be what the Spirit saith "unto the churches", noted seven times in Revelation 2 & 3. - which also ends well: "The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

INTERESTING Song called: **Mr In Between**

by Harlan Howard, sung by Burl Ives: 1962

talks of these notable things being **between**:

- pickins mighty **lean**

when I try to live so clean (of dirty/clean)

- too old for girls & too young for women

- looked all around & hope seems dimming

- like a fish that’s not allowed any swimming

- like a choo choo that can’t find a station

- like still being a **part** of a lost generation

- like a sailboat that is still kept in a bottle

- like an engineer that can’t find the throttle

- like a dog with the rabies = no recreation

- makes a fellow **mean**, to be Mr In **Between**

Punch Line:

- so **better leave the scene**: **Jn 16:7**—**Jn16:13**

SongWriter Version:

<http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VCS7yDiXlZc>

Burl Ives Version:

<http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t6iNbigm3X4>

**James 3:15-17** allegory mentions what is **between** God and men:

3:17: **GraceToU** = PureWisdom = ONLY from Above = **God**

**3:16: Between**\* = envy+strife = confusion+[[evil work]] = **between**

3:15: **OtherWise** = DevilishWisdom = NOT from above = **men**

\*between=MediatorMan=CJ=anthropos=liar(Rom3:4;1Tim2:5)

Romans 3:4

_Let God be true, but EVERY_ anthropos _man a liar_

1Timothy 2:5 (reveals CJ = Mr In BETWEEN)

_For_ [there is] _one God, and one mediator **between**_

_God(true) and men_(anthropos), _the man Christ Jesus_

Galatians 3:20 (re a mediator is NOT of one)

_Now a **mediator** is not_ [a mediator**]** _of one, but God is one_.

John 16:7 (tells us who better **leave the scene**)

_Nevertheless I tell you the truth;_

_It is expedient for you that I go away:_

_for if I go not away,_

_the Comforter will not come unto you;_

_but if I depart,_

_I will send him unto you_.

John 16:13 (tells us UpGrade from TheTruth to AllTruth)

_Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come,_

_he will guide you into all truth_

1Corinthians 13:10 (when perfect is come, then part = done away)

_But **when** that which is perfect is come,_

**_then_** _that which is in part shall be done away._

So **when** James 3:17 PureWisdom(GraceToU) is come

**Then** being **part** of **lost generation** = to be done away.

For **then** it’s **neither**  of James 3:15 **nor** James 3:16.

So then, James 3:17 is another of those **3rdof3** verses,

Which is both above & beyond 1/3 and 2/3 unto 3/3;

Like TheWay, TheTruth, **AND** The**Zoe**Life=3rdof3.

Like AMoreExcellentWay is above & beyond

Both such BroadMinded & NarrowMinded = Ways;

For although the NarrowWay does LEAD unto Life,

It is still kinda like Mr In Between: NOT YET Life.

So it seems, way back in 1962, we had

a prolific song with profound info in it.

**POINT**:

Song sung blue is NOT YET Song sung true,

Even though it may be catchy and popular.

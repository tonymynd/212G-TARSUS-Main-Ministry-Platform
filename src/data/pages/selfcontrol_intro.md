**SELF CONTROL**

A Book About A Ghostly Hook That Just

Won’t Let Go Till You Have Self Control

**Introduction**

Objective of writing such words on paper is to inform and encourage readers to achieve a spiritual level Self Control above and beyond just being a living soul; And by taking what’s biblically called a **Walk** in the **Spirit**, through all nine biblical **Fruit** of the Spirit,

**from** Love (1st Fruit) … **unto** Temperance (9th Fruit) = **Self Control**: Bible: NT: Galatians 5

Doing such is intended to bring an end to our **flesh**-y **soul**-ish **child**-ish phase: which notably includes Adult-ery\* first listed among a __such like__ list in Galatians written unto the churches still having childish flaws when Adult, thereby still corruptible: thereby still able to be seduced and bewitched, by believe lies being told, even in the churches.

\*spiritual adult-ery: claiming to be espoused to grace, but found still whoring with law.

So the **goal** of Self Control is bring an end to being **such like**: a childish-adult, a mingled  phase of contrary things such as an adult called a **child** (of God)\*, should grow in grace to become a lord (more than a slave), rather than remain a servant, is still under religious and political tutors and governors, as if their hostage, until Self Control is fully achieved.

\*not a compliment, but an admonition, according to be no more children

The process of attaining Self Control is biblically allegorized in many ways, such as

-  as a Walk, of Walk/Run, to afterward Run a they/we Race without stumble

- 

-  as a Walk in the Spirit, of two Ghost/Spirit (Teacher/Guide) Comforters

- 

-  as being Led (follow me) of the Spirit, of two Driven/Led modes

- 

-  as if to Crucify flesh-y f-law lusts, of flesh vs Spirit, to end conflict

- 

-  as LayAside soul-ish self-ish life, of 2 soul/spirit (psuche/zoe) lives

- 

-  as PutAway all your child-ish things, of child/man, to be man-kind

- 

-  as not under the law IF led of the Spirit (grace and truth) to Self Control

The end result of achieve such Temperance (Self Control), by do such Walk in the Spirit through Fruit of the Spirit, is then biblically called “__against such there is **_no law_**__”; AsIf to say a previous f-law phase of your life will now begin to let go, temporarily vanish, so you may continue your learning AdVenture whereby a 9th Fruit of the Spirit: Temperance is then added unto your faith as the 3rd of 7 things to also add to your diligent faith:

**The Walk in the Spirit Talk**

Of Walk/Run introduction noted, our to-pic is **Walk** in the Spirit, to achieve Self Control first. Sure there’s a race in g-race to also run, whereby a grace runner much more abounds than a f-law runner who enters the race so the offence might abound. But it’s necessary to first Walk in the Spirit, from love to self control, before run any human race.

Eg: it’s a preposterous order to teach first, and learn afterward; Or to run first, walk after. Do it in reverse oft err-or falls if run with such like: either good or corrupt = end corrupt.

So let’s get this said, before taking our Walk in the Spirit:

None of these and-but-or things in Matthew 12:30-37 end well:

-  he not with me, is against me = ends against

-  gather not with me, is scatter abroad = ends scattered

-  all forgiven, but some not forgiven = ends not forgiven

-  either make the tree good, or else corrupt = ends corrupt

-  good man things, and evil man things = ends with evil things

-  by thy words justified and by thy words condemned = ends condemned

The Walk: It’s Not a back←and→forth walk done by fishers of men along with Jesus; Nor a walk through dry places by an unclean spirit cast out seeking rest and finding none; Nor a walk to and fro earth by your adversary, the devil, as a roaring lion seeks to devour. Rather it’s a Big **W**alk to have a Big Talk, as if what(?) the Spirit saith unto the churches.

Eg: Galatians: Paul an apostle, and all brethren which are with me, unto the churches. ….Revelation x7. He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches.

Fishers of men(‘anthropos’: still loving a lie) like to hook lovers of lies with fLaw bait. They string a line attached to a leader in midst of line-leader-hook. Like political ministers promise democracy, religious leaders promise to lead snared men to liberty. Seems like a harmless Catch and Release fishing expedition. But more often than not, good intentioned ministers haven’t taken this Walk in the Spirit to get Self Control; So it oft becomes as if blind leading blind, and somewhere other than born again(from above) is for you of ye/you, as man of child/man, to see there’s no such leaven in higher heaven.

[This] __I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh__:

Galatians 5:16 KJV

We’re not just talking sexual lust, nor lust for money, but many uncontrollable and overwhelming desires to do the things rule of law forbids. It’s called __evil concupiscence__.

Eg: Make a diet rule, and concupiscence will make you desire things on a forbidden list. If u lose this battle, which many do, you’ll be hounded with guilt, needing comfort\* food.

\*comforted by law can be both comfort + dis-comfort, but by grace is comfort only

Eg: Make a do not kill rule, and concupiscence may overwhelmingly drive you to desire to become police or soldier with weapons to kill a perceived enemy; Or to use a weapon of mass deception, which can lead to mass destruction. Result: PTSD, possible suicide.

So Walk in the Spirit to learn not to use nor be enticed by weapons of mass deception, which can seduce, bewitch, deceive, and harm unlearned in many unforeseen ways. If you be such like: unlearned smart ass sheeple, you can even deceive and harm yourself.

__For the flesh lusteth_ **_against_** _the Spirit,  and the Spirit_ **_against_** _the flesh: and these__

__are ↓_**_contrary↑_** _the one to the other: so that ye cannot do the things that ye would__.

Galatians 5:17 KJV

It’s ↓flesh↓←vs→↑Spirit↑ conflict, where only flesh lusteth, due to lacking Self Control. The flesh makes Do-teronomy & Don’t-eronomy laws, then also desires things forbidden. Such like happens in ye (do err) mode, of ye/you; And when under the law, of law/grace. The law seems to hold people as hostage until they Walk in the Spirit unto Self Control.

__But if ye be_ **_led_** _of the Spirit, ye are not under the law__.

Galatians 5:18 KJV

Of Driven/Led introduction noted, our to-pic is Led. __that went before__ (Driven: walk before me) __and that followed__ (Led: follow me)

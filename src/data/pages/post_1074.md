## Content

Bible (allegory) Contrasts: Help us understand Who & What "the only true God" & "the only wise God" IS, by have Bible (allegory) contrasts of what God IS NOT. Examples:
- Matthew 22:32b "God IS NOT the God of the dead, but of the living"... also noted in Mk 12:27; Lk 20:38. (Allegory: He is not Law: ministers life+death, but Grace: ministers life only).
-- 1Corinthians 14:33a "God IS NOT [the author] of confusion, but of peace". (Allegory: God is not an oxymoron; Not both grace 
+ law; Nor both eternal life + surely die; Nor both truth + lie; So not both Gen 2:16+17).
--- Which (plural) things are an allegory, contrasts written to help us learn the script-u-are is not plural & contrary "scriptures".

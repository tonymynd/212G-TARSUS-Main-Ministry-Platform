## Content

Seems in Hebrews 10:31 KJV apostle Paul is allegory (sarcastically) portraying this "living God" (of the Hebrews), having plural "hands" called "fearful" & "fall", as if describing living Law (as a Lord who has "vengeance", as a "judge" noted in Heb 10:30; Perhaps as the "unjust judge" Jesus had said will "avenge", also allegory referring to the law):
Hebrews 10:31 KJV:
It is a fearful thing to fall into the hands of the living God.
So..
where would anyone "fall" from to fall into such "hands"?

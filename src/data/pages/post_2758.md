## Content

Connecting Eternal Life with Peace.
Grace, mercy and peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
"being justified by faith, we have PEACE with God through our Lord Jesus Christ": Rom 5:1
reckon ye also yourselves to be... ALIVE unto God through Jesus Christ our Lord: Rom 6:11
wages of sin death, but the gift of God ETERNAL LIFE through Jesus Christ our Lord: Rom 6:23
So, "lay hold on eternal life" x 2 (1 Tim 6:12,19) is to lay hold on peace, this is the reason why Paul exhorts Timothy:
Fight the good fight of faith, lay hold on eternal life, whereunto thou art also called, and hast professed a good profession before many witnesses. 
1Ti 6:12
Is as if Paul is telling Tim, is a good fight, but still in fight mode, not yet peace, till you ALSO lay hold on eternal life aka peace. This is important, when many try to justify the good fight, not being aware that "fight" mode is still "perilo-us times", not yet:
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [is] with you all. Amen.

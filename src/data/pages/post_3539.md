GENTLENESS Part 2
Untangling the Mystery: Dual and reversed order of things are not gentle. "Let all things be done decently and in order"
To ALL: Grace, Mercy and Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
(attribution, study guide and spiritual aspects map in the comments)
I think perfect love is studious, as 2Tim 2:15 asserts: "Study to shew thyself approved unto God." It's as if our study of gentleness reveals how gentle our God truly is—by comparing temporary gentleness(clemency) with the enduring gentleness of God. In the previous study, we dug into the technical aspects of gentleness, but now, as we go deeper, things are getting even more complex.
---
Oh boy, now seems as if it’s getting more complicated, rather than simpler, and more like a mystery having lots of twists, rather than a comfy notion of gentleness just being “gentle”; Yet not like gentle1 (‘epios’ = affable) in 1Thess 2:7 & 2Tim 2:24 (b), but another gentle2 (‘epieikes’ = patiently equitable) in Titus 3:2 (to all men) & in James 3:17 (as 2nd of 7 aspects of wisdom from above). OMG, it’s like we’ve got twain of everything to sort out: twain gentleness x2, twain goodness x2, twain gentle x2, to learn something.
(b) interesting how this 'epios' gentle (affable) are applied unto children and servant of the Lord
Perhaps it’s why we need to be led of the Spirit, unto spiritual aspects of fruit, rather than just use our natural (carnal, flesh) thinking about such things. For even when spiritual, of natural/spiritual=after, there’s still “comparing spiritual things with spiritual”, after the after.
'* (carnal, flesh) thinking = flesh includes brain, includes comparing our thoughts and imaginations to be compares and evaluated before taking them upward; to think twice before speak/action.
When spirit mode of soul/spirit, there’s still “spirit of error” & “spirit of truth” to contrast(c). Order of twain important, for 1John 4:6’s contrast order of truth←&→error ends error; Like Jesus’ and/or Mt 12:30-37 twains all end as bad as saved←or→damned:
-with + against... ends against
-gather + scatter... ends scattered
-forgiven + not forgiven... ends unforgiven
-Son of man + Holy Ghost... ends Ghostly
-tree good + tree corrupt... ends corrupt fruit
-speak + give account... ends with judgment
-justified + condemned... ends condemned
(c) 1 John 4:6, where John, like in Genesis 2:17, is guiding 'little children' to to eat [truth and error], echoing the duality of [good and evil], perhaps why the exhortation of "be no more children".
---
Shocking to know the comparisons, asif I was told only one side of the story when in child mode. Hope you all remain curious as I am curious as ever for the next part of this journey into gentleness. Could there be an unexpected twist before we reach the end?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

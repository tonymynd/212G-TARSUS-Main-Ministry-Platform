(Luke 23:34, concerning them = know nots)

(Luke 23:46;; then 23:47 says he gave up ghost)
· "Woman, behold thy son"! ("he saith": John 19:26) ... 
and "Behold thy mother" ("saith he": John 19:27)  
· I thirst 
(John 19:28) 
· It is finished 
(John 19:30; then 19:31 says he gave up ghost)

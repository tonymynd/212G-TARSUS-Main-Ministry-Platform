## Content

There is NO respect of persons (aka no partiality) with God.
Romans 2:11 ... James 3:17
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
There is NO respect of persons (aka no partiality) with God.
So,
By Law: sinners all, none righteous.
By Grace: sinners none, all righteous (by end of law).
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

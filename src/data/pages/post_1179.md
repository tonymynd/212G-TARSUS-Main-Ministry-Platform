## Content

CONTRAST is how we learn all the Law vs Grace differences.
Only some are obvious. There's also many hidden to discover. 
So let's Dare Compare; Not only natural vs spiritual, but also "comparing spiritual things with spiritual" after we're spiritual. 
1st: 
natural vs spiritual; Then spiritual law vs spiritual grace.
For law is natural & spiritual, but law is also both good + evil, so it's lastly nasty x2: natural disaster & spiritual wickedness. In contrast spiritual grace is neither disaster nor wickedness. 
1st: "soul" vs "spirit"; Then "spirit of error" vs "spirit of truth". 
For the word(logos) of God described in Hebrews 4:12 first divides asunder "soul and spirit", also "joints and marrow". In 1Corinthians 15:45-47 it's soul vs spirit = Adam1 vs Adam2; But second man='anthropos', which in Romans 3:4 = "liar". This is tricky, for second man is called "the Lord from heaven".
1st: Old Testament vs New Testament (don't agree: Lk 5:36);
Then NT "change of the law" vs NT "end of the law" = grace.
For saying New makes Old ready to vanish away: Heb 8:13; But in New still two kinds of JC "knowledge" to yet contrast:
- the knowledge ('gnosis')*of our Lord Jesus Christ 
- the knowledge ('episgnosis')** of our Lord Christ 
* 'gnosis' knowledge shall also vanish away: 1Cor 13:8 KJV;
**God will have all men saved & 'epignosis' truth: 1Tim 2:4.
1st: old covenant vs new covenant (of OT LORD: Jer 31:31);
Then new covenant of LORD vs God of all grace: 1Pet 5:10.
For new covenant of LORD still has 2 houses: Israel vs Juda;
And this OT LORD still puts "laws" in minds & hearts of Israel;
Also there's no mention of "priesthood" concerning Juda; So
new "High Priest" CJ is not our Lord JC "sprang out of Juda".  
1st: "under the law" vs "under grace"; Romans 6:14 
Then "under grace" vs "with grace": Hebrews 13:9.
For although "under grace" is better than "under the law", even "under grace" is still "under" of under/above; So
Colossians 3 says if risen with Christ, focus on "above".

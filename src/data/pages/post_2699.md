What is your insight about this bible verse?
Seems, to me, John 1:17 allegory defines Jesus Christ as grace and truth, which John 17:2,3 notes was sent from the only true God. So it's like Jesus Christ (aka grace and truth) is the same yesterday, and to day, and for ever.

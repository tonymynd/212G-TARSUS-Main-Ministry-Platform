## Content

coming in the clouds?

"Son of man" is never mentioned beyond LukeWarm Acts. Why?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
It's interesting to notice and consider why there is no more mention of "Son of man" beyond Acts, then only "Son of God", 
And 
Then it's called the gospel (good news) of "Christ", is then noted as being "through" our Lord Jesus → Christ is the end of the law. 
It's allegory like then having gone with him 
through 2 Sons Jesus mode (Mt - Acts), was still both old & new,
to 1 Son Christ mode (Romans → forward), is then all things new.
Seems 
mentions of Son of man are both nice and nasty, lastly nasty, allegory like OT LORD [Yehova] both saved and destroyed after. 
For after heal in cities of disciples, he did then also upbraid and threaten Woe at a coming day of judgment [krisis]: Mt 11:20-24. 
Threats of 
"coming of the Son of man" (Mt 24:27).
"Son of man cometh" (Mt 24:44, 25:13; Lk 12:40, 18:8).
"Son of man shall come" (Mt 16:27; Mt 25:31).
"Son of man coming in clouds" (Mt 24:30) = mourn, not rejoice.
day of Son of man revealed = "destroyed them all" (Lk 17:24-30).
(law) voice of Son of man saying judgment [krisis] also (Jn 5:27).
begin to vanish away as we have no more mention of Son of man,
as if then no more twain-Jesus mention of both salvation and destruction afterward, as last state of good & evil law being worse.
It's tricky in Mt - Acts,
For Lk 9:56 says Son of man did not come to destroy, but save.
Yet,
Lk 17:26-30 twice notes the day of Son of man revealed would be as days of Noah & Lot, resulted in destroyed (not saved) them all.
Such info is not posted to speak against Jesus when he played both Son of man and Son of God modes of old and new for contrast sake; But to aware what is said about such.
What are your insights about such a delicate topic?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

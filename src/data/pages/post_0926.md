## Content

Edification?? Seems even edification can have both law & grace meaning(s) we have to learn to discern. For example, 
- in Mt 24:1,2 law temple "buildings" = oikodome = edifications disciples noted to Jesus, who then said all such would come down, till not one stone(law) upon stone(law). So law edifications can be both build up & tear down, since law both good & evil, both constructive & destructive, as if both comfort & discomfort; 
But, in contrast...
- Grace edification is only to build up & only to comfort. Eg: Apostle Paul notes to Corinthians, twice, in 2Cor 10:8; 13:10, his grace & peace ministry is ONLY for edification, and NOT destruction
Another eg, and why KJV uses both Holy "Ghost" & "Spirit", to distinguish two different Comforter(s) in John 14:26 & 15:26:
- Holy Ghost = Comforter1 = both comfort & discomfort of law
- Spirit of truth = Comforter2 = only comfort of grace & truth

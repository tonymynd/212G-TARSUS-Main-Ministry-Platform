## Content

If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God. Which God? 
The God that freely giveth to all liberally & upbraideth not. 
Which God "upbraideth not" (aka doesn't bite nor smite)?
"The God of all comfort" = no dis-comfort all.
"The God of all grace" = no law at all. 
"That God is light" = no darkness at all.

## Content

GOD CLUES: to help solve the biblical allegoric "mystery" of God.
John 17:3 mentions "the only true God" whom "they" may "know": A God that is ONLY TRUE, AsIf to suggest that God has no false.
John 17:3 KJV:
And this is life eternal, that they might know thee the only true God, and Jesus Christ, whom thou hast sent.
SO 
THEN, That God would be above & beyond false/true duality.
Thereby that God, being light void of darkness, cannot lie nor die.
That God, called all light having no darkness, seems allegory for:
- "all grace" having no law, as if thereby "all truth" having no lie. 
John 1:17 seems to allegory contrast Law & Grace as Lie & Truth: Grace&Truth came by JC contrasted to Law&Lie given by Moses.
SO THEN, "the only true God" would be "the God of all grace", which 1Peter 5:10 KJV says makes you perfect after ye suffer,
and AsIf such Before/After is like a difference between CJ/JC. 
Seems CJ only occurs from Acts 19 to 1Pet 5, then Vanishes;
But JC occurs from Matthew 1:1 KJV to Revelation 22:21 KJV.

CLARITY: Many quote John 3:16. Yet John 3:17 further clarifies: God sent not his Son into the world to condemn the world; but that the world "THROUGH him" might be saved. 
So, Not condemned, But saved, is "THROUGH him". WHY?
Jesus said, in Jn 5:25-29, while 2Sons he still had 2voices:
- voice of Son of God = "live" (not condemned), and 
- voice of Son of man = also "judgment" (still condemned).…

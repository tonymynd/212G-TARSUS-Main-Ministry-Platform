## Content

Dear Children, Both "children" of the wicked (still under the law) & "children" of God (not under the law, but under grace): BE NOT CHILDREN... GROW UP in grace, mercy, peace...
- in 1Cor 13:11 Paul tells child-ish Corinthians feuding to put away "childish" things to be a "man" (is no longer a 'child')... 
- And in 1Cor 14:20 Paul  says "be not children" (not even better "children" of God than "children" of the wicked) for all 
"children" still have "malice"...
- And in Gal 4:1-7 Paul notes a child=servant, so still has to be governed & commanded... until "no more a servant"...
- same in Eph 4:13-31 to child-ish Ephesians, Paul says "be no more children"... grow up unto Christ = perfect "man"... also put away six child-ish things, all "malice"...
POINT: Grace does NOT want to command you, NOT even with a new commandment (law). But rather aware you with truth... until you no longer need to be commanded, not even to love one another, which WAS still a commandment, which has both begin: John 13:34 and "end": 1Timothy 1:5 KJV...
So apostle Paul tells bishop Timothy (who's still being child-ish), in 1Timothy 1:5 KJV:
Now the end of the commandment is charity (not love),
- out of a pure heart (established with grace: Heb 13:9), and
- of a good conscience (no longer an evil conscience), and
- of faith unfeigned (aka grace unfeigned, as law is not of faith)

## Content

Some need a change, a diaper change, 
to be rid of their poop + piddle, 
also allegory called jot + tittle, 
also sounding brass + tinkling cymbal.
Perhaps what many need is a change,
from diaper blessed in Romans 4:7,
to the man blessed in Romans 4:8.
Romans 4:7 KJV (from Psalms 32:1)
Blessed [are] they whose iniquities are forgiven, 
and whose sins are covered. 
Romans 4:8 KJV (from Psalms 32:2)
Blessed [is] the man* 
to whom the Lord will not impute sin. 
*the 'aner' man, of anthropos/aner

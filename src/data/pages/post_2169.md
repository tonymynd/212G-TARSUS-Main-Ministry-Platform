15 Guidelines for King James Bible Translators:
When King James commissioned a 7th English Bible to be made from 6 previous, he approved 15 guidelines for translation which were instituted by Richard Bancroft, bishop of London in 1604:
1. The ordinary Bible read in the Church, commonly called the Bishops Bible, to be followed, and as little altered as the Truth of the original will permit.…

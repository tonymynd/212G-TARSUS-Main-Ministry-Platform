## Content

ye/we are not UNDER the law, but UNDER grace: Romans 6:14,15; YET there's even more than such twain duality to consider, a 3rd way:
- a heart e-stablished "WITH grace": Hebrews 13:9b KJV; is 
neither UNDER the law nor still UNDER grace.
(b/c what's UNDER grace can still be BetterLaw, NotYet PureGrace)
- a 
"more excellent way": 1Cor 12:31...13 KJV = "charity" is
neither BroadWay love of Many nor NarrowWay love of Few
(b/c non-partial ALLness of true God is neither Many nor Few) 
- reconciled unto "the God of all grace" cannot lie nor die is
neither in 2SonsJesus mode nor in false/true Christs mode
(b/c the only true God has only 1Son & not false/true Christs)
SO, let us grow in grace & truth above & beyond any duality;
As if above & beyond plural Law/Grace God to Grace ONLY.

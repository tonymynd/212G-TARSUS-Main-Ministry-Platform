## Description

Absolutely never make these 7 mistakes

## Transcript

Never charge hourly, charge flat rate instead.
Never split labor and materials, charge one lump sum instead.
And absolutely never give prices over the phone,
charge them a diagnostic to come out and give them an estimate.
Never hire on skills, that's like the worst thing you can do.
Instead, hire on character.
You can teach skills, but you can't teach character.
Never rely on a word of mouth to fuel your business,
instead spend thousands of dollars on marketing
so you can actually keep your guys busy and pay your bills.
And never, absolutely never use your name as the business name.
Instead name your business something memorable.

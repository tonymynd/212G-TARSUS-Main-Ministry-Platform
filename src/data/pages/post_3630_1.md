## Content

Philippians 4:8 notes THINK on these things: 
true, honest, just, pure, lovely, good report, virtue[arete], praise.
Interesting this virtue [arete] to think on differs from virtue [dynamis] that came of Jesus when touched in Mk 5:30; Lk 6:19; Lk 8:46. It's another virtue [arete] noted in 2Peter 11:3,5. Hmmm

## Content

Knowledge? Of 'gnosis' & 'epignosis' knowledge in Greek, 1Cor 13:8 says 'gnosis' knowledge shall vanish away, and 1Tim 2:4 says God our Saviour will have all men saved also come unto the 'epignosis' knowledge of the truth. Why?
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Apostle Paul notes in 1Cor 8:1 KJV we all have knowledge, but a knowledge[gnosis] can "puffeth up", instead of only "edify" as "charity"…

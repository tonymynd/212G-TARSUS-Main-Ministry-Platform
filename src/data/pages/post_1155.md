## Content

NEW COVENANT? Not totally new, if of Old Testament "LORD" (Jer 31:31), not only differs from "God" in Noah's Ark story (Gen 6-8), but the day of the LORD is "darkness, and not light" (allegory: law, and not grace): Amos 5:18. 
IN CONTRAST, 2Cor 5:17,18 say in Christ (of through Jesus Christ) it's "all things new", for "old things are passed away"; Then "all things" (new) are "of God" (not LORD), who hath reconciled us to himself by "Jesus Christ" (not "Christ Jesus"  only 
mediates a "better covenant" of LORD). Dare Compare.
Although called "new covenant" and "better covenant", it's not [yet] what's best for "all" of good, better, best=rest of the story. For when analyzed it's only better law, not yet pure grace; And mediated to "them" (not us) by CJ (not JC). It's why Hebrews turns CJ (Heb 3) around to JC (Heb 10-13) to end the epistle with "Grace" (not law nor even better law).
New covenant still has TWO houses: Israel & Juda, with laws (not grace) still put in hearts & minds of Israel; And of Juda it's noted there is no mention of "priesthood". So our Lord "sprang" OUT of Juda. Heb 7:26 describes it as if new high priest (also) got made "higher than the heavens" on high. For a new high priest is set "down" on right hand of a "Majesty" in the (plural) "heavens" on high; But of Christ (of thru JC) it's sitteth on the right hand of "God", as if higher than on high.
As for "Christ Jesus": mediator "man" of new covenant, such is like Mr-In-Between God and men. It's shocking to notice, in greek, this "man"='anthropos' in 1Tim 2:5, which in Rom 3:4 is called "liar". Then in 2Tim 3:12 it notes all who will godly (allegory: childish) in CJ shall "suffer". Then in 1Pet 5:10 it notes we get made more perfect by the God of all grace, which comes "after" suffer a while (not for ever) in CJ mode. 
For me, it took directly ask (without a mediator) the God who does not even upbraid, noted in James 1:5, to then get revelations & study hints to confirm the revelations about it.
Yet such is not posted for condemnation nor belittle of any; Rather only for awareness sake of all, of how traditional things we've been taught & believed, may not be all truth.

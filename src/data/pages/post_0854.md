## Content

In-teresting: how Bible phrase "and the life" occurs thrice in John, as if 1thing (good)...then as 2nd of 2 (better)...then as 3rd of 3 (best):
- Jn 1:4,5 "and the life" was the light - yet not fully comprehened.
- Jn 11:25-27 I am = the resurrection "and the life" -- the "Christ".
- Jn 14:6 I am = TheWay, TheTruth, "and TheLife" --- MuchMore.
SO, as if a 1,2,3 progression of good, better, best = rest of story.

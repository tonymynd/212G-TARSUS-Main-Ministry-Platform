## Content

LIKE a dove... MORE than just a love story
(for those who like to Bible study in the deep end)
Prior to John 1:32, Matthew 10:16 told them be wise as serpents AND harmless(akeraios) as doves. Then of two mentions of "simple" in Romans 16:18,19 only the 2nd is 'akeraios', also rendered twice as "harmless": 1st in Mt 10:16 & 2nd in Phil 2:15 says be blameless AND harmless(akeraios). 
So 
the harmless in Heb 7:26, even though us, of them/us, is still 'akakos', like simple1 (harmless1: akakos) in Rom 16:18, isn't yet simple2 (harmless2: akeraios) in Rom 16:19; So Heb 7:26 harmless1 goes on to made higher than the heavens, as if higher than just do no harm, unto also receive no harm. 
SO the Spirit, LIKE a dove, came from higher heaven, and abode on him, to get him thru do no harm + receive harm, unto also receive no harm, by also going up higher than the heavens.  
POINT: He taketh away 1st: Betterlaw to establish 2nd: Grace.
Simple1 = harmless1 = do no harm
Simple2 = harmless2 = also receive no harm = much more

Morgan Housel is a financial writer and author best known for his book The Psychology of Money. His work isn’t about stock tips or complicated strategies; it’s about how human behavior drives financial outcomes.

He often points out that the key to making money isn’t advanced math or technical knowledge, but the ability to communicate and get along with people you may disagree with.

That’s why his work connects so deeply with people around the world. He doesn’t just teach how to make money, but how to think about money in a way that brings freedom, peace of mind, and long-term success.

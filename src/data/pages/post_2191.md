LOTS TO CONTRAST IN BIBLICAL MYSTERY 
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and our Lord Jesus Christ. 
Here's some contrasts I've been investigating:
LORD{Yehova] & God[Elohyim], as if darkness & light. 
Amos 5:18 says day of LORD is darkness and not light.
1John 1:5 says God is light, and in him no darkness at all.
Also God[Elohyim] & God[Theos], as if duality & reality.
2 Comforters: Holy Ghost[pneuma1] & Spirit[pneuma2] of truth: 
as "walk before me" = "driven" & "follow me" = "led"; 
Only Spirit of truth abiding for ever as "guide" into "all truth".
Son of man & Son of God, 
offering OT "everlasting life" & giving NT "eternal life".
Christ Jesus & Jesus Christ, as if mediate & reconcile.
favour[charis1] in Lk-Acts & grace[charis2] in Lk-Rev
child converted[strepho] & man converted[epistrepho]
child-ish knowledge[gnosis] & man-kind knowledge[epignosis]
word[rhema] of God = sword & word[logos] of God = sharper
vain religion (sweet & bitter) & pure religion (sweet only), 
as earthly, sensual, devilish wisdom & 1st pure wisdom from above
1st finished before cross & 2nd finished at cross: 
Jn 17 & 19, as if in a race 1st finished = win, 2nd finished = place.
Lamb[amnos] of God & Lamb[arnion] in Revelation, as
take away sin of the world & take away peace on earth
plural contrary scriptures & better script-u-are of twain, 
yet also script-u-are of twain & Script-U-Are above all twains.
Adam1(living soul) & Adam2(quickening spirit)
Also Adam1/Adam2 & Christ is neither b/c as in Adam all die
sacrifice & mercy, b/c God will have mercy, not sacrifice
liberty[eleutheria] in CJ & liberty[apolyo] in JC, as
free from law1 by law2 & free from all law by grace & truth
2 Sons Jesus (Mt-Acts) & 1 Son Christ (Rom→fwd)
love[agape1] & charity[agape2] as excellent & more excellent
modern bibles (remove Christ from end) & KJV (ends best)
What are your insights on any of these biblical contrasts?
The grace of our Lord Jesus→Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

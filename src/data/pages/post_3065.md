## Content

TWO KINDS OF IMMORTALITY:
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
in 1Cor 15:53-55 KJV, in Greek, we see TWO kinds of immortality to put on, for death to be swallowed up in victory:
15:53 For this corruptible must put on incorruption [aphtharsia: immortality: Rom 2:7; 2Tim 1:10], and this mortal must put on immortality [athanasia].
54 So when this corruptible shall have put on incorruption, and this mortal shall have put on immortality, then shall be brought to pass the saying that is written, Death is swallowed up in victory.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

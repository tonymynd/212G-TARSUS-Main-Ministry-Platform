## Content

Reconciled? Seems there's 2 modes of reconciled[katallaso] in Romans 5:10a&b. Let's investigate for awareness sake.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father & Jesus→Christ. 
5:10a For if, when we were enemies, we were reconciled to God by the death of his Son,
5:10b much more, being reconciled, we shall be saved by his life.
- 
1stly we got (forgiven) reconciled1 by a DEATH (at cross), 
- 2ndly MUCH MORE ongoing reconciled2, unto eternal LIFE.
Seems such is depicted in Rom 5:'s conclusion:
after grace did (already) much more abound than sin abounded,
it's so grace might reign through righteousness unto eternal life. 
So also Rom 6:22,23 contrasts 2 modes of life:
6:22 ends mention of OT everlasting life (was servant mode)
6:23 clarifies the gift of God is eternal life (no more servant).
Seems
It's like 2 modes of 'charis': favour & grace = yet much more,
2 modes of 'pneuma': Holy Ghost & Spirit of truth = much more,
2 modes of 'agape': love (excellent) & charity ("more excellent"),
2 modes of "converted": 'strepho' & 'epistrepho' (still yet more),
2 modes of "knowledge" of JC: 'gnosis' & 'epignosis' (yet more).
So in the end, no mention of law flawed, nor of love commanded:
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus→Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

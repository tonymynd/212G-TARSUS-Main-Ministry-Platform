## Content

If God did not love & forgive worse sinners like me, Heaven would be empty. When God loses his memory how much does he forget... (1 John1:9 , If we confess our sins he 
is faithful & just to forgive us our sins &  cleanse us from ALL unrighteousness .) The only sin God can not forgive is the one I don't confess. 
A loving God beyond human comprehension treats sinners as if they are righteous/perfect, never failed once in life. As if he is the only one special person like no other on planet earth under the sun. If it wasn't for Calvary cruel cross, my life would have been forever lost. Can't thank you enough,  Lord!

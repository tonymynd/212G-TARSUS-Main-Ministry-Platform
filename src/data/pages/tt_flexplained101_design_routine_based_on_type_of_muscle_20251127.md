Ever wonder why some muscles grow fast, while others barely change, here's the real reason.
Every muscle in your body has its own mix of fast twitch and slow twitch fibers.
Fast twitch fibers love heavy weight, high tension, and explosive reps.
They are the ones that grow quickly.
Slow twitch fibers are built for endurance, long sets, lighter loads, constant tension,
great for stamina, not great for size.
And here's the part most people miss.
Different muscles have different fiber makeups,
chest, triceps and quads, mostly fast twitch.
They grow best with heavier training.
Caves, forearms and parts of your glutes, mostly slow twitch.
They need higher reps and longer time under tension to respond.
So if you train every muscle the same way, you're fighting its biology.
Heavy and explosive for fast twitch, high reps and long tension for slow twitch.
Match the style to the fibers and your gains finally click.

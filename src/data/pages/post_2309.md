## Content

Jesus said love [agape 1] of many shall wax cold: Mt 24:12,
so Paul said charity [agape 2] never failed: 1Cor 13:8 KJV.

In KJV Bible the "more excellent way" of 1Cor 12:31 being described & clarified in 1Cor 13 is "charity" (more than "love", and why KJV uses both "love" & "charity" for 'agape' in greek). It's allegory like "grace" is more than "favour", even though KJV uses both & both mean 'charis' in greek. Eg:
1Cor 12:31 KJV: But covet earnestly the best gifts: and yet shew I unto you a more excellent way.... 
13:1 Though I speak with the tongues of men and of angels, and have not charity…

## Content

Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ.
For your information:
LOVE & CHARITY are both used for Greek AGAPE in KJV.
But 
b/c Jesus said LOVE of many shall wax cold: Mt 24:12,
Only CHARITY "never faileth" in 1Cor 13:8 KJV.
Only CHARITY ↑TOPS↑ these allegoric KJV Lists:
1Corinthians 13:13 KJV list:
- Charity = "greatest" of three (as if of great, greater, greatest)
- Hope
- Faith
Colossians 3:12-15 KJV list (as if the 8th above 7):
- Charity = can be put on above forgiving one another
- forgiving one another
- forbearing one another
- longsuffering
- meekness
- humbleness of mind
- kindness
- bowels of mercies of holy & beloved
2Peter 1:5-9 KJV list (as if the 8th above 7):
- Charity = above brotherly kindness (still had some blindness)
- brotherly kindness
- godliness
- patience
- temperance
- knowledge
- virtue
- your faith
Note: not posted to diminish inseparable love of God, 
but to notice, for awareness sake only, how & why 
KJV uses love & charity, but says charity never faileth.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

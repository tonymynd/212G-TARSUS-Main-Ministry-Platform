RUSSIA TO LEAVE W. H. O.
 Russia's withdrawal from the World Health Organization (WHO) is "on the line" after breaking relations with the Council of Europe. This was stated by Deputy Speaker of the State Duma Pyotr Tolstoy (United Russia faction).
The deputy recalled that there are a number of international organizations, "work in which is no longer relevant for Russia today": "WHO supported sanctions against our country, they closed 
the office [for the prevention of non-communicable diseases in Moscow], which was working. They are trying to impose perceptions and rules on Russia that are not in line with our values. 
The combination of these factors makes us think that we have no business being in this organization

ALL GRACE: noted by Paul & Peter (portrays no law at all)
2Corinthians 9:8 KJV: And God is able to make all grace abound toward you; that ye, always having all sufficiency in all things, may abound to every good [agathos] work:
1Peter 5:10 KJV: But the God of all grace, who hath called us unto his eternal glory by Christ Jesus, after that ye have suffered a while, make you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you.
Seems then, glory Paul said all had come short of (Rom 3:23) would be the "eternal glory" of "God of all grace", is "all grace".

## Content

Some JESUS & CHRIST DIFFERENCES to observe & discuss: 
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ. 
Although Jesus also became the Christ, let's observe some initial Jesus & Christ differences, as why to be ambassadors for Christ:
Jesus was (only) called the Saviour of Israel (Acts 13:23).
Christ is called the Saviour of the world (John 4:42).
Jesus did not come to send nor give peace (Mt 10:34; Lk 12:51).
Christ as one new man of previous twain is our peace (Eph 2:15).
Jesus was still 2 Sons: Son of man & Son of God in Mt - Acts.
Christ is only Son of God in Rom→fwd is the gospel of Christ.
Jesus was called "Master, master", as if two masters (Lk 8:24).
Christ is called "one": your "Master" (Mt 23:8,10). 
Jesus revealed as 2 Sons he still had 2 voices (John 5:25-29).
Christ (as Son of God voice) said I will not accuse (John 5:45). 
Jesus mode, in Mt - Acts, was still both old and new.
Christ mode, noted in 2Cor 5:17, is all things new.
Jesus was only change of law (Mt 5:43,44; Jn 13:34; Heb 7:12).
Christ is the end of the law (Rom 10:4; Eph 2:15).
Clarity: not posted to lessen highly exalted name of Jesus; But to see why peace with God, God's gift of eternal life, God given victory are through our Lord Jesus→Christ (Rom 51: 6:23; 1Cor 15:57). 
So let's focus on the gospel of "Christ", and be "ambassadors for  Christ", to beseech: be ye reconciled to God (cannot lie nor die). 
What are your thoughts & insights about such things?
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus→Christ be with you all. Amen.

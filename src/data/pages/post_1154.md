## Content

AMAZING GRACE. What a learning journey it's been for me, THRU some awful suffer; Yet thanks to God's unconditional love, and His grace will have mercy, multiplies peace, I learned things I did not know. Eg: I didn't know in a body of Love ALL still both suffer & rejoice: 1Cor 12:26; But in a higher awareness body of Grace: "a more excellent way": 1Cor 12:31 = "charity" (not love):13:1-13 KJV, it's rejoice only, b/c suffer mode gets ended by "the God of all grace": 1Pet 5:10. 
Therefore Grace is even more than Love. Amazing Grace.

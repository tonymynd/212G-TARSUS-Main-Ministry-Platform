## Content

BEGIN and END??
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Let's re-read & re-think Genesis 1:
God 
[Elohim] both created [bara] and made [asah] things,
but when it came to man [adam]
God did NOT say let us create [bara] man;
BUT God did Gen 1:26 say:
"let us make [asah] man".
Then, also in Gen 1:26,
gave two (before/after) options for "make" man:
- (before): "in our image [selem]",
- (after): "after our likeness [demut]".
Perhaps for allegory compare-i-son.
So, in NT 1Cor 15:45 we find mention of two Adams:
- first man [anthropos] Adam: made a living "soul" [psyche].
- last Adam (2nd man): made a quickening "spirit" [pneuma].
note:
Seems the likeness of "God is Spirit" (Jn 4:24) is "spirit" (Jn 3:6).
YET,
when in "spirit" mode, of soul/spirit, there is still two:
- (before) "spirit of error" (aka law, is spiritual)
- (after) "spirit of truth" (aka grace is spiritual).
So, it takes being "led of the Spirit" of truth:
the "guide" (aka follow me) into "all truth": Jn 16:13,
who gently persuades us with grace and with truth,
that came by Jesus Christ from the only true God,
until we put away all thought of destruction (error).
Consider book of Revelation:
It allegory seems like a mine field of potential HARMageddon,
especially after Rev 6 says peace is taken away from earth.
So,
it takes being led of the Spirit, the Spirit of truth guide,
to thereby safely make it through such OT f-law crap,
to make it alive unto the end: Revelation 22:21 KJV,
which has no mention of law, sin, death, destruction,
but only pure grace, so it ends well for all in the end,
notably only the grace salutation of Paul (not John),
which Paul thrice noted he wrote such with his "own hand".
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

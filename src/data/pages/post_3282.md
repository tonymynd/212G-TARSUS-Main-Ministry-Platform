What are "THINGS ABOVE" in Colossians 3:1,2 KJV?
If ye then be risen with Christ, seek those things which are above, where Christ sitteth on the right hand of God.
Set your affection on THINGS ABOVE, not on things on the earth.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
I am thinking of things like:
- eternal life (Rom 6:23), not Covid-19 mrna death tolls
- grace, mercy, peace of God, not rule of law spoken by earthly men
- wisdom from above that is first pure, not earthly, sensual, devilish wisdom (James 3:15-17).
What are your thoughts?
The grace of our our Lord Jesus Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

**ALMOST is NOT graceful, NOR merciful, NOR peaceful.
So, Let every man be FULLY persuaded in his own mind.**

Eg: Agrippa to Paul in Lukewarm Acts 26:28 KJV
\`\`Almost thou persuadest me to be a Christian\`\`.

Why Agrippa not fully persuaded? BECAUSE
Preach freedom while still in bonds won\`t do.
It\`s why Politicians, Teachers, Preachers fail.
They are $elling u freedom while still in bonds.

Eg: Apostle Paul to Hebrews: Hebrews 9:22 KJV
almost all things are by the law purged with blood,
and without shedding of blood is no remission.

So then, if there\`s no blood shed, then no remission;
But if better blood shed it\`s still only ALMOST purged.
What a f-law dilemma, aka a mystery to STILL solve.

So even a \`\`_better sacrifice_\`\` does NOT totally purge;
BECAUSE no man is justified BY THE LAW: Gal 3:11.
Jesus offered his blood shed BY THE LAW: Heb 10:8.
So, even if it\`s _better_ bloodshed, it\`s only almost saved.

No marvel then, that atheists are not buying into it.

Eg: X-ians say our killing is better than their killing.
Muslims say their killing more in line with the f-law.
Jews say their crucifiction resulted in Christianity.
It\`s like a 3 ring circus, all three being law clones.

**Almost = Not yet Enter, due to a short fall.**

Almost occurs six times in New Testament;

And so does peace occur six times in John.
So does 1000 yrs occur six times in Rev 20.

But temporal peace is taken away in Rev 6:4;
And devil gets loosed after bound a 1000 yrs.

Then what. Then it\`s not just deceive the elect;
Then it\`s deceive _nations_, and a _second death_.

As if the first death wasn\`t already one too many.

For **if one died for all, result = all dead: 2Cor 5:14**.

**So, we need to get above and beyond almost. For
even if everyone got to almost, it\`s still not enter.**

As a 2011 Sci-fi movie called **In Time** aptly portrays:
no one should have immortality if even 1 person dies.

## Content

Seems like Matthew 5:17 may be one of the most mis-understood verses. I like KJV for Mt 5:17, and the allegory aspect thereof: 
Eg: 
Think not that I am (grace and truth) come to "destroy" the law, or the prophets. I am NOT come to "destroy", but to "fulfill" (aka fill everyone with grace and truth, until all thought of destruction is put away).
POINT: 
Grace & truth does not destroy, but full fills all with awareness of God's holy truth, 
until every thought of destroy is removed.

Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father andJesus Christ our Lord.
Seems the term "Abba" is perhaps Aramaic and/or Chaldean.
Jesus only used "Abba" once in Mark 14:36
andhe said, Abba, Father, all things are possible unto thee; take away this cup from me: nevertheless not what I will, but what thou wilt.
Then Paul used it twice:
Romans 8:15
For ye have not received the spirit of bondage again to fear; but ye have received the Spirit of adoption, whereby we cry, Abba, Father.
Galatians 4:6
andbecause ye are sons, God hath sent forth the Spirit of his Son into your hearts, crying, Abba, Father.
What are your insights about the term "Abba" ?

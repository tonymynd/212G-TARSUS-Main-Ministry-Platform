## Content

10 New Testament GOD CLUES in the order they occur:
- "the God of the living": Mt 22:32; Mk 12:27; Lk 20:38
- "God is a Spirit": John 4:24 (aka Spirit of grace & of truth)
- Spirit "guide" into AllTruth (above TheTruth): John 16:7-13
- "the only true God", who sent JC (grace & truth): John 17:3: 
- a 
God who's author of peace, not of confusion: 1Cor 14:33
- Father of JC and of mercies, the God of all comfort 2Cor 1:3
- "that God" in Christ reconciling world to himself: 2Cor 5:19 
- "God is one" (aka not PluralGod): Galatians 3:20 
- the God who "upbraideth not": to ask for wisdom: Jas 1:5
- "the God of all grace" (aka no law at all): 1Peter 5:10 
- "that God" is light having no darkness in him at all: 1Jn1:5
Kinda makes that God different from:
- God of Israel 
- God of the dead
- Holy Ghost, with unpardonable sin
- a LORD whose day is darkness, and not light
- a PluralGod with plural covenants, neither being grace

...James 1:27...   
...**Pure** **religion** and undefiled before God [mother] and the Father is this, To visit the  
fatherless and widows in their affliction, [and] to keep himself unspotted from the world...  

  
When we [compare](ShewBread8.md#Dare) two sorts of religion, as if [comparing](HowbeitAfterward.md) spiritual things (laws) with spiritual (grace),  and when looking closer at [all this and that](TwainShew8.md) portrayed, such as looking closer at ["Christ Jesus" and "Jesus Christ"](GodShew5.md), it then seems that we are mirrorly comparing this vs that, extortion vs exhortation, paying a "ransom" vs paying a "visit"; the [1st sort](CurrentEvents.md) of religion being [all die](SAQs.md) by [ignorance](ShewBread7.md#Bliss), the [2nd sort](GodShew3.md#Second) being [all live](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) by [awareness](GodsheworgExplanation.md); Which is also to say all perish by law added to grace makes life + death, but none perish by pure grace void of law. For pure grace (no law at all) is [merciful: all mercy](Mercy.md) and [no sacrifice at all](Sacrifice.md); which is then [peaceful: all true peace](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Peace.md) and no [divisions](Division.md) at all. For even [God](GodShew2.md) can't be one by division and alienation; But is one by unity and [reconciliation](Reconciliation.md). For any God, kingdom, house [divided against itself](ShewBread6.md#Gods) falls to [ruin](lawphoto.md); Such is the case with [law vs law](TwainShew4.md#Laws) of a [(false) God "on high"](FalseGod.md) in plural center vs right "heavens". The God of all Grace in "heaven" is "[higher than the heavens](RevelatorySermons21.md)".  
  

Which (law/grace) things are an "[allegory](Allegory.md)" and a "mystery" to solve [in time](Hebrews5.md),  
lest all perish by using of [ordinances](Law.md) instead of none perish by grace ([only](Potentate.md)).  

  
When looking closer at [the will of God](ShewBread5.md#willx2) (in the [greater light](TwainShew4.md#Lights) which rules the day), it seems paying a ransom to a kidnapper addicted to love of money, and if such a ransom is the ultimate sacrifice: the death of [one](ShewBread2.md#OneSlice) = the [death of all](OneLiners.md#Shakespeare), it's not such a viable option, nor [the end God declared from the beginning](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) of such a virtue-all [allegoric](Allegory.md) God [shew](shew.md) written "aforetime" for our "[learning](OneLiners.md#BibleTranslators)". Rather what God would have is [all men saved "and" aware](RevelatorySermons6.md) enabling only enables those addicted to continue lying ([law](Law.md)ing); Whereas [all truth](GodShew2.md#Allness) made known unto [us all](Usward.md) by [one of us](Paul.md), even to a [Full Monty](Revelations.md) (Luke 12:2) revelation (uncovering) without any shame ([I am](ShewBread5.md#IAMTHAT) "not ashamed" of the [gospel of Christ](ShewBread9.md#Expired)), makes [you](YeYou.md) all [free](ShewBread4.md#freedom).  
  
Impure religion in the form of law and lie pays a ransom(sacrifice), resulting in [extinction](OneLiners.md#Ghandi). Pure religion, in the form of grace and truth, which is both pre-sanctified and undefiled, pays a "visit" (not a ransom) to allegorical widows [having no husband: Christ] and fatherless [having no father: God] in "[their](TheirPartYourPart.md) [affliction](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Depression.md)" [grace + law] and also remains unspotted. It's an allegory. Grace cannot lie(law) nor die(law). Grace neither tempts(laws) nor can be tempted(lawed). Law Law does both. Selah.  
  
In the [allegory](Allegory.md) of the Holy Bible containing Old and New Testaments, and a Bible seemingly having [twain of everything: Adams - Worlds](TwainShew.md), even having [twain Gods of such twain worlds](ShewBread7.md#Worlds), even twain [testators](TwainShew6.md#Testators) of twain testaments; In such a Bible there are even two [Holys](TwainShew3.md#Holys): Holy Ghost and Holy Spirit. Yet it's not called twain: Holy Holy Bible, no, rather it's called one: the Holy Bible. And it's Holy from the beginning (where "the end", of [ends](TwainShew2.md#Ends), is declared: Is 46:10) unto the end (where the last trump it is played by [his angel](Paul.md)), and a final singular "Amen" to that, of all this/that, makes both it and you all, only one thing: Holy...  
  
In an allegoric Bible it speaks of "pure religion" as paying a "visit" to those without (those not having father nor husband, which are "one" and "the same" if the truth be known); Not of paying any extortioner's ransom. And such a visitor, visiting such in their affliction, is "undefiled" before God (mother) and the (God) Father; having been sanctified prior to visiting. And such pure religion (grace and truth) comes "after" (in such a before/after shew) impure religion, and allegorically like the [forever priesthood of Christ is "after" a not forever priesthood of Melchisedec](TwainShew5.md#Priesthoods) (mentioned six times in Hebrews 5-7); witch firstly came by Moses [Eg: Aaron]...

What shall we say then,  

based on enough biblical & historical [evidence](CurrentEvents.md) to make it plainly "evident": when you get to the end of your rope, LET GO; rather than hanging on to the BONDAGE of Grace + Law: For cursed everyone that hangeth on a tree, a [dead](TwainShew6.md#Testators) tree of good + evil (grace + law), not to mention such love + [fear](ShewBread4.md#freedom) is also as life + death, witch was evidently a [dead end](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) for all [them](ShewBread2.md#inthem) who hung on to "such like"; Ending in a hall of shame rather than a hall of fame (Heb 11: 13,39), for playing the childish [blame game](GodsheworgExplanation.md) witch evidently has a "sting" (1Cor 15:56).  
  
Cute Allegory: a man once fell over a cliff, but caught onto a tree branch; and after hanging perilously there quite some time, was heard to say: is anyone up there? A voice replied: yes. The desperate man said: can you help me? The reply was repeated: yes. Then, after a short silence which seemed like the space of half and hour, came the clarity of such help from above: "let go"!  
  
The moral of the allegoric story is not to get hung up on the cross, but rather go [through](through.md) such a valley of the "shadow" (law is only a phantom menace) of death when taking your [grace walk](Hebrews.md) [from Sinai to Sion](Sevens2.md), as going from [glory to glory](TwainShew3.md#Glorys); through the cross with Jesus => Christ to risen and [seated](Revelations7.md#21) (at rest: peace) at the right of God (in him is no sin) where it's all right and no center (behind) at all... Leaving (principles: plural, not doctrine: singular Heb 6:1) law [nailed](Law.md) to the cross as the enmity between God and men. Think about it, for thinking is now allowed.  
  
For such enmity not only makes one twofold: more the [child](GodShew.md#nomorechildren) of [hell](lawphoto.md) than those who sit in Moses' seat, and by proselyting such one; but also demands both a partial mediator and a sacrificial ransom, the ultimate sacrifice. But being the [only wise God](GodShew3.md#Only), of two [Gods](TwainShew3.md#Gods) evidently [opposed about them](ShewBread6.md#Gods), God our Father [will not](ShewBread5.md#willx2) pay such a [troll toll](RevelatorySermons4.md), not then, not now, not ever; since paying an extortioner would only enable such to keep the addiction: love of money. For we all know by now, enabling the addicted does not help [them](ShewBread2.md#inthem) get free.  
Rather God our Saviour uses exhortation, and "sharpness" for "exhortation" that's "sharper than any [twoedged sword](Revelations5.md#Rev16)" (promised speaking: plainly of the Father) via [his witness unto all men](Paul.md), and by such all those ensnared and oppressed by devil law may "recover themselves" [Eg: Timothy] from opposing themselves with such grace + law, which is really grace vs law if the truth be known...  
  
And the truth shall be known (no option in: ye shall know the truth), even unto a Full Monty (Luke 12:2) revelation (uncovering) of [both this and that](Revelations2.md#Rev4), but "not ashamed" when it's that (gospe): the gospel of Christ, the Christ (end of the law) of Jesus => Christ, the Son "of God" and "with power", which is the gospel (power: Rom 1:16) of God unto salvation, the eternal sort (which calls for all men being saved "and" aware what they're saved from). And when it's known, it makes [you](YeYou.md) free. And since all the KofG is within "you", it makes "all" therein free.

## Content

Hitherto? John 5:17?

Hitherto? John 5:17 KJV is interesting, in that is says Jesus ANSWERED THEM: My Father worketh "hitherto" (the true Sabbath rest), and I work. ... 
Seems then, the true Sabbath rest is neither of their annual sabbath nor their weekly sabbath. 
It's why I keep focusing myself & others on 3rd of 3 things, such as a MoreExcellentWay for ALL is neither of BroadWay of MANY nor NarrowWay of FEW, since ALL is neither MANY nor FEW.

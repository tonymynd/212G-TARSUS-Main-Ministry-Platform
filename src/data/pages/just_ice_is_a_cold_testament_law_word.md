cOLD ←> LukeWarm → Hot (noted in [Revelation 3:15](https://eng.global.bible/bible/de4e12af7f28f599-02/REV.3?passageId=REV.3.15), [16](https://eng.global.bible/bible/de4e12af7f28f599-02/REV.3?passageId=REV.3.16)).

"justice" has these meanings in Hebrew:
H6663 sadaq: verb: to justify
H6664 sedeq: noun: to be just or righteous (in law)
H6666 sedaqa: noun: law righteousness
H4941 mispat: judgment manner of being right in law

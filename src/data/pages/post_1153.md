Rather "that God" sent JC, by whom came "grace and truth", is to thereby give us awareness of, and access to, true God's "eternal life". Yet lay hold on eternal life requires put away surely die = lie.

The answer is also allegory: "Cast out the bondwoman and her son", meaning Cast out the law and result of the law: aka ministrations of "death" & "condemnation" (2Cor 3:7-11).
For the only true God cannot be Both law and grace, nor Both lie and truth (like false/true Y2Ks); But only true. So Romans 3:4 says "let God be true, but every man ('anthropos') a liar".

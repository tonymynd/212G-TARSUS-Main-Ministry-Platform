## Content

Why did apostle Paul change his Holy Kiss Greeting?
- Holy Kiss 1: Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ. (Romans)
- Holy Kiss 2: Grace [be] to you, and peace, from God the Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ. (Galatians)
- Holy Kiss 3: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. (1Timothy)

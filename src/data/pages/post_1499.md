## Content

"goodness" -vs- "goodness"; As law -vs- grace contrast. FYI: 
- in Romans 11:22 Paul said behold "goodness" & "severity" of "God"; Yet as if allegory portray law as "God", this "goodness" is "if" conditional, always has "and severity" (ouch) attached.
- in Galatains 5:22 "goodness" (6th fruit) differs in greek, not as law's "goodness & severity", but then like grace as God. 
SO THEN,
Learning Point is: if you think ok to keep law is good (Rom 7), be aware law "goodness" always becomes "and severity"; For who can even know, let alone obey every law? Nobody. It's why in Rom 11:21 Paul warned take heed, lest law as God spare not you either. It's also why Heb 5:14 notes when full age we learn to discern (avoid) "both good and evil" of law.
For Gal 5:18 said if led of Spirit result is "not under the law", yet perhaps not til 6th fruit, for 5th still same as law goodness.
Not shared to speak against law, rather to aware about law.

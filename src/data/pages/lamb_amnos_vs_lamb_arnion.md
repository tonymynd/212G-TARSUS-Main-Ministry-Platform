LAMB [amnos] vs LAMB [arnion]: 

"Lamb" [arnion] mentioned in Revelation 28x DIFFERS from "Lamb" [amnos] of God taketh away the sin of the world in John 1:29.

Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.

Many bible readers assume "Lamb" [arnion] mentioned 28x in Revelation is the same as the "Lamb [amnos] of God" mentioned in John 1:29,36; But in Greek mode it's not. 

So, there's a Lamb [amnos] vs Lamb [arnion] contrast in Greek mode to investigate. 
In Revelation 6:16 the Lamb [arnion] has wrath. 
In Romans 4:15 law worketh wrath. 
In Ephesians 4:31 wrath is what to put away. 
In 1Thess 5:9 God has not appointed us to wrath, but to obtain salvation (saved from wrath: Rom 5:9) by our Lord Jesus Christ (aka by grace and truth).

Interesting allegoric mystery

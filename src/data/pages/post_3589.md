## Content

Which God? 
Why these God clarifiers, even for Bible readers?
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Christianity's God is nicer than Jew's Yahovah & Muslim's Allah; But so far Catholics, Protestants, Independants, have not yet proposed nicest God for all, of these God clarifiers: 
- the "only true God", as "God", that "cannot lie", would be "all truth" (no lie, nor even still lie/truth duality). Seems why Spirit of truth got sent to our "guide" (follow me) into "all truth"...
- the "God of all comfort" would be no discomfort, nor still both discomfort/comfort duality; Different from "wrath" of a "God" in Rom 1:18, or goodness and "severity" of "God" in Rom 11:22, or "God" who sends "strong delusion" in 2Thess 2:11...
- the "God of the living", JC said is not God of the dead, would be different from the "living God" in Heb 10:31 who's plural (L/R) "hands" to fall into are called "fearful" & "fall", for this "living God" in Heb 12:22,23 is still called "Judge" (Hmmm, seems it may be sarcastic allegory for a living LawGod).
- "God" who "upbraideth not" in Jam 1:5, to ask for wisdom, would be a God who never upbraids; Different from Jesus  "upbraided" both before & after(Mk 16) a cross scenario...
- the "God of all grace" in 1Pet 5:10, makes perfect after suffer awhile, would be no law, nor still both law/grace duality; As if meet your "all grace" Maker, after law/grace Creator...
- "that God" who is light having no darkness in him in 1Jn 1:5, would be different than an OT LORD(Yahovah) in Amos 5:18 who's day(light) is darkness and not light; As why JC said in Lk 11:35 take heed the light in thee be not darkness...
- although God is love('agape': nicer of 7 NT kinds of love), seems 1John 4 alludes love needs perfecting until "perfect" love has no fear. Yet if love a "commandment", God is love a Commander; it's different than "Grace" to you from God our Father isn't a commandment, but free gift of righteousness.
- the "only wise God" our "Saviour" in Jude 1:25 would only save all, not both save and destroy after like in Jude 1:5.
Humbly posed, as notes to myself first (not to correct others).
Seems I/we all need to question which God, and only if nicest all truth, all comfort, all grace, eternal life God; Then humbly propose nicest God to all dualing Jews, Muslims, Christians.
The grace(God) of our Lord Jesus Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

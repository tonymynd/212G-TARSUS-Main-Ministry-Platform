Love & Charity are both used in KJV: Love is WONDERful, yet Charity is even more ONEderful. So...
Let's also ask God why(?) KJV called this "more excellent way" (1Cor 12:31) "charity" (not still "love") in 1Cor 13 KJV.
I asked, and then got revelations about how "charity" (grace 'agape') is yet more than "love" (law 'agape'); Allegory like "charity" is a higher spiritual knowledge we also go on unto. 
Only 
charity "thinketh no evil" (aka no law, aka no reverse of "live"). So "charity" is like a more pure grace that's above lower grace of law/grace duality.
Seems, in greek mode, 1Cor 13:8 KJV notes when it comes to "charity never faileth", then the "knowledge" that shall "vanish away", is the lower 'gnosis' knowledge of 2 kinds of JC knowledge: 
- 'gnosis' knowledge (love "commandment") of our Lord JC
- 'epignosis' knowledge (higher grace & truth) of our Lord JC. 
Seems it's why 1Cor 13:11 KJV notes it takes put away childish things (aka law commandments) to be man (no longer needs commanding like a child=servant does). Of child/man, let's all be man-kind.

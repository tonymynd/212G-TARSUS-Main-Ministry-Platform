WHERE ARE MISSING PEOPLE?
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Things reported (by locals & observers) that made me go HMMM;
But not to accuse anyone, rather to observe for awareness sake:
- Prior to WEIRD fire, meetings were held to propose making Maui a Smart Island & Lahaina a Smart City run by AI. Locals were opposed.
- Seems why electric grid was not upgraded to prevent fires. Electric was not turned off for areas with downed power lines.
- Warning Siren not sounded, even though web site notes siren also sounds for fire. Person who did not sound siren has resigned.
- Water turned off, so people & firefighters could not put out fire.
- Weird sky (chemtrails) reported day before fire. Some say the aluminum powder falling causes fires to burn hotter than normal.
- Boats on fire in water. How?
- Locals reported police BLOCKED escape routes, sending traffic in circles, to front street, became a death trap.
- Police Chief (John Pelletier) same as police chief who investigated mass shooting in Las Vegas. He is also a coroner (some conflict).
- BOOK: Fire & Fury (about Aug 8 Maui fire) published Aug 10.
 - Gov't's potable water brought in for survivors made people ill.
- Truck with local aid materials got turned away by military, delayed by Mayor. Also 150 meals made by locals for survivors & responders got refused because not cooked in commercial kitchen.
- Some called 911/police got told go jump in ocean (later denied).
- Even police chief admitted fire so hot it melted metal & literally dustified (to ashes) people in houses, cars. This is not normal in a forest or house fire.
- part time morgue worker revealed over 500 bodies (not just 115). Also several temporary morgue trucks (5 x 40 each) contain bodies.
- video revealed a large tree burning from inside out. Microwaved?
- video day after revealed one car untouched amongst burned cars. How?
- FEMA in 5 star hotels, but hard to find for survivors in need. POTUS Biden offers only $700 per household & kitchen fire story.
Many more reports, investigations, even on YouTube. Main thing is what/how did such happen, and where are over 1500 missing?

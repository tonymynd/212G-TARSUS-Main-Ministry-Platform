**Lotto Winning isn't freedom or equality**   
  

**Lotto Winning no more begets Freedom or Equality**

**than Taxation and Tithing sacrifces and offferings  
**

**appease ministerial political and religious pipers.**

  
  

Lotto winning no more produces freedom or equality to man kind in general, nor pleases God, than taxation and tithing sacrifices and offerings appease political and religious pipers (ministers). Trying to appease them is like enabling a drug addict. The more you give, the more they will pipe. Thank God the [troll toll](RevelatorySermons4.md) is decreasing as awareness and understanding increase.  
  
Millions of people hoping to escape the drudgery and torment of paying the piper are losers in the regional and national lotto. And even the occassional winner is still a loser of freedom or equality. Lotto winning does not bring freedom from such law law, nor equality to any.  
  
Millions paying tithe and taxation pipers (religious and political ministers) also feel they are losers of freedom or equality; For gov't and church bodies have oft proven they are neither accountable to voters nor fair to all. Many churches still preach oxymoronic stuff like: you too can have partiality from an impartial God ("there is no respect of persons with God").  
  
Lotto corps are even worse than insurance corps; Who spend more time and money on not paying than on paying. And when public opinion sways toward [truth out](Law.md), they simply use more of people's hard earned money to market more propaganda lies in their favor. Let us hold such accountable.  
  
Odds against lotto winning are so against you it is nigh unto being the greater fool than just a fool to even play such a game. Much like $ickness, which has become the world's biggest [[business]] (so much bigger than food production and food consumption that the food industry pales in comparison), and giving place to it only fuels sickness rather than wellness, poverty rather than riches.  
  
With respect to offerings and [sacrifices](TwainShew6.md#Sacrifices), which are notably "of the law", the Bible is clear: such things never pleased God (Ps 40:6; Heb 10) whose will is "I will have mercy, and not sacrifice" (Hos 6:6; Mt 9:13); Which meaneth (since such things are an allegory) I will have grace, and not law; love and not fear; life and not death; joy and not sorrow.  
  
Indeed there's good and better sacrifices talked about in the Bible, but what's also clarified is God (His Grace) will not have sacrifice. [The God of all grace](GodShew2.md#Allness) (no law at all), [the only wise God](GodShew3.md#Only) (of foolish/wise [Gods](TwainShew3.md#Gods)), the only true God (of false/true [Gods](ShewBread6.md#Gods)), that God (of [this/that Gods](ShewBread7.md#Worlds)), never ever requested, nor took any pleasure in, nor will have, any sacrifice at all: Hebrews 10 (Psalms 40:6). "I will have mercy, and not sacrifice" is not then, not now, not ever.  
  
"To wit, [that God](ShewBread5.md#IAMTHAT) was in Christ (of [Jesus => Christ](GodShew5.md): [the end of the law](ShewBread9.md#Expired)) [reconciling the world](Reconciliation.md) unto [himself](GodShew2.md#Allness), [not imputing their trespasses unto them](ShewBread6.md#Gods) (not imputing sin, witch only law imputes, and brings death to all)": 2Cor 5:19.  
  
Taxation and tithing, insurance and sickness, lotto winning and losing... are all "of the law". And even most children and most of their parents are fully aware [the law is not fair](RevelatorySermons23.md). Law is [imperfect](Hebrews11.md), [faulty](Hebrews9.md), an unjust judge, biblically the schoolmaster witch failed every student: Rom 9:31; Gal 3: 24; Heb 7:23; Heb 11: all; Jude 5. Yet many go about promoting law, demanding more law and more law enforcement, not knowing such law is also a [ministration of death](http://pub30.bravenet.com/forum/2526810710/show/414069).  
  
[Law](Law.md) worketh [wrath](lawphoto.md): Romans 4: 15. God worketh [mercy](ShewBread5.md#willx2) upon all, is "[merciful](HGvsHS.md)" (be ye merciful as your Father in heaven is merciful: Lk 6:36), which is perfect (be ye perfect as your Father in heaven is perfect: Mt 5:48). So it reasons the only [wrath of God](RevelatorySermons18.md) is of a Law God: the foolish God of foolish/wise Gods, the false God of false/true Gods; For the God of all grace is the God of no law. The [allegory](Allegory.md) for "God cannot lie" is Grace cannot law. So also the allegory for "God cannot die" is Grace cannot law. [Contrary things cannot coexist in peace](OneLiners.md#JohnLewis), and "he is our [peace](PeaceLinks.md)" who hath abolished the law.  
  
By abolishing one lousy tree law, biblically called "the law of [commandments](TwainShew2.md#Commandments)" in Eph 2, the law in Genesis 2:17 (which made Gen 2: 16 + 17 an oxymoron: every tree + not every tree), the little leaven (law) witch leavened (lawed) the whole lump, making the OT [all vanity and vexation of Spirit](RevelatorySermons24.md)...  
  
By abolishing one law, Christ of J=>C effectively abolished all law written in stone and ink: 2Cor 3. For all other laws were notably "added", and added "because of the transgressions". But "where no law, there no transgression": Romans 4:15. So if the woman was "in the transgession": 1Tim 2:14, there had to be a law "given" prior to other laws "added", and notably given by [Moses](Allegory4.md) (Jn 1:17). On the contrary grace came by Jesus Christ. Along with grace came truth, about the law, that it's a liar, thief, accuser of the brethren.

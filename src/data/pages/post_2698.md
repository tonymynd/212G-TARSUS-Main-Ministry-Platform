What can we learn by connecting some Bible verses?
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Our Father ..... deliver us from EVIL: Luke 11:4.
Now 
we are delivered from the LAW: Romans 7:6
whatsoever is NOT OF FAITH is sin: Romans 14:23b.
And the law is NOT OF FAITH: Galatians 3:12a.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

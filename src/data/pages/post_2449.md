## Content

ALL SAVED AND AWARE
God our Saviour will have ALL men to be saved (by grace), AND to come unto the (higher spiritual) knowledge [epignosis] of the truth: 1Timothy 2:4
It takes both grace and truth to both save and aware all.
So 
both grace and truth came by Jesus Christ: John 1:17,
who noted he was sent by the only true God: John 17:3.
Yet he said he had to go away, re-ascend to the Father, from where he could send the Spirit of truth to guide you into all truth (no lie): John 16.
Seems being Spirit of truth guided into all truth (aka no lie) involves being guided into the "God of all grace" (aka no law): 1Peter 5:10.
For WHERE no law, THERE no transgression; Romans 4:15,
And WHEN no law, THEN sin is not imputed: Romans 5:13.
So the end to endure unto has no mention of law, only grace:
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

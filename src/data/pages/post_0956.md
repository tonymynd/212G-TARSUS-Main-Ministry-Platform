## Content

Much More? Does Romans 5:10 portray there's a 1st reconciled to God by the death of his Son & then also still a further reconciling unto God's eternal life (void of death) that's even "much more"?
Romans 5:10 KJV:
For if, when we were enemies, we were reconciled to God by the death of his Son, MUCH MORE, being reconciled, we shall be saved by his life.
Seems Romans 5:21 concludes that such "Much More" grace reigns THROUGH righteousness 
UNTO eternal life by Jesus Christ our Lord.

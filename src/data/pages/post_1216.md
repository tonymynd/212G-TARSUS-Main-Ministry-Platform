## Content

It's interesting to notice while in his "Jesus" mode, he was still "commanded" by Father, and said, in Luke 13:32, even he was not [yet] fully perfected, perhaps because he was first made "under the law" to redeem them under the law; But later in his "Christ" mode it's called "the end of the law" = grace; As if by going "THROUGH our Lord Jesus Christ", it takes us from "under the law" to "under grace", whereby we become dead to sin = 
dead to the law = alive unto God (Grace).

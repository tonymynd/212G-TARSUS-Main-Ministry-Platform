## Content

What does it REVEAL if Revelation 22:21 (the end of bible) is the grace salutation of Paul, not John ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
It seems to reveal several things:
Paul, not John, is author of Revelation; as if he sign-ified it by previously revealing he always penned his grace salutation with his own hand. 
The book of Revelation was thereby written before AD 67 (not AD 95 as many suppose), which is prior to AD70 temple destruction, also notable by still mention of temple in Revelation 11.
The grace salutation of Paul has no mention of law, sin, death.
Paul's salutation:
Romans 16:24
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.
Philippians 4:23
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.
2Thessalonians 3:17,18
The salutation of Paul with mine own hand, which is the token in every epistle: so I write. The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.
Revelation 22:21
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

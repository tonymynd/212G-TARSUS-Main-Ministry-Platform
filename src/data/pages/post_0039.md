## Content

Two Gods Allegory: 
Law God says: I will utterly forget you, and I will forsake you. 
Grace God says: I will never leave you, nor ever forsake you. 
Mingle both makes God an oxy-moron. God is one, not twain.
So Christ abolished the law and refused mingled drink offered.

## Content

Learning = increase knowledge; Yet 2Timothy 3:7 says avoid those ever learning, and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth. Seemed odd, til I discovered there's 2kinds of knowledge in Greek: 
- 'epignosis' knowledge, as if more precise & correct knowledge
- 'gnosis' knowledge, also English rendered as "science"
Perhaps as if allegory contrasting law & grace knowledge(s):  
Why?
- in 
1Timothy 2:4 KJV God our Saviour will have all men saved AND come unto the knowledge (epignosis) of the truth
- in 1Corinthians 13:8 KJV whether [there be] knowledge (gnosis), it shall vanish away.

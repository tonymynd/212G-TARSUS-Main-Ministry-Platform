## Content

"life eternal" is reverse of "eternal life" 
(perhaps like "evil" is reverse of "live"). Hmmm...
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Interesting to notice "life eternal" occurs only 4 times in 
Matthew 25:46, John 4:36, John 12:25, John 17:3. 
In contrast, 
the God that cannot lie promised "eternal life": Titus 1:2;
the only true God sent Jesus Christ to give "eternal life": John 17. 
So, 
the "gift of God" is "eternal life" (Romans 6:23), which occurs so much more (27 times). 
Grace reigns through righteousness unto "eternal life" by Jesus Christ our Lord: Romans 5:21. 
So, 
Jude 1:21 says (before entering Revelation) look for the mercy of our Lord Jesus Christ unto "eternal life".
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. 
Amen.

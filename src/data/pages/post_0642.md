## Content

Grace says: I will never leave you, nor ever forsake you.
Law says: I will utterly forget you, and I will forsake you.
To mingle both is to be an oxymoron (bi-polar dis-order).
So I flushed law as if dung, to be graceful, merciful, peaceful.

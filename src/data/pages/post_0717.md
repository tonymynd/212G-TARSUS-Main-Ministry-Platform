Pagan Love Feast of Lupercus: Lupercalia.
The Roman Catholic Church, which originally made this pagan feast of Lupercus: Lupercalia into a religious Saint Valentines Day annual, also canceled it in 1969 when they realized it had gone from an intended festival of love to an orgy of consumerism.

## Content

Apostle Paul's "holy kiss" Greeting has an UpGrade. Why? 
- First: "Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ". (Romans unto 2Thessalonians).
- Upgrade:  "Grace, mercy, and peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord" (1Timothy).
Paul adds "mercy" & changes "the" Lord JC to "our" Lord JC. 
Seems why is no mercy in Revelation, where Grace & peace in Rev 1:4 doesn't last, peace taken away in Rev 6:4; 
Until in the end it's the grace of "our" Lord JC [be/is] "with you all".

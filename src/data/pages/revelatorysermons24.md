When looking at Ecclesiastes, Solomon's conclusion of Law, wise guy Solomon concluded the ([faulty](Hebrews9.md)) Old Testament, grace + law (added), became all vanity and vexation of spirit. He didn't say some of it's "vanity" (vain breath) and "vexation" (frustration) of "spirit" (God a Spirit); For adding dis to God's [grace](GraceUntoYou.md) makes a dis-grace, and a little leaven leaveneth the whole lump. So then it's notably "all vanity and vexation of spirit", everything from Genesis 1:1 to Malachi 4:6, which begins and ends with fear hath torment; And because even "a little [leaven](Law.md) leaveneth the whole lump", as much as allegory says even a little cancer us law makes eternal life no more eternal.

To [allegorically](Allegory.md) reveal to us such grace + law is an oxymoron, Solomon deliberately ends his Ecclesiastes with an oxymoron: "fear God", which is to say fear Love, noting it's his conclusion of the whole matter (of grace + law). Such is revealed to us in the New Testament; And such is to allegory say [the God of all grace](GodShew2.md#Allness) is [pure grace](RevelatorySermons7.md), having no law at all.

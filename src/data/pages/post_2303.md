## Content

Contrasting 1Thess 5:2,3 vs 1Thess 5:9 as OT LORD vs NT God

Is there peace & safety in OT←LORD[Yehovah]? Or even in his new covenant? Perhaps not. Let's investigate:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
For awareness sake, let's see what's said about OT←LORD:
- fear (has torment) is "of the LORD[Yehovah]", noted many times. In contrast NT God[Theos] has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind: 2Tim 1:7.
- 
the OT←LORD[Yehovah] doth "hate": Prov 6:16. In contrast God[Theos] has no hate, only inseparable love & grace for all.
- the day of LORD shall come as "destruction": Isaiah 13:6. In contrast the day of God comes only as "salvation" for "all".
- LORD creates "darkness" & "evil"(reverse of live): Isaiah 45:7. In contrast God is "light" having no darkness nor evil.
- LORD will "forget" & "forsake" you: Jeremiah 23:39. In contrast God will never leave nor forsake you: Heb 13:5.
- LORD will bring "worst" & "destruction", no peace: Ezekiel 7:24,25. In contrast God is only (author) of peace: 1Cor 14:33.
- day of the LORD is "darkness, and not light" (allegory for law, not grace), so "woe" to any who desire it: Amos 5:18. In contrast Grace & peace is from God[Theos] our Father.
SO, Jesus Christ (grace & truth) sent from the only true God, came and said, in Luke 11:35, Take heed, therefore, that the light (aka grace) in thee be not darkness (aka be not lawed).  
AND Paul, as apostle of Jesus Christ, reminded Thesslonians, in 1Thess 5:2,3 the day of the LORD (aka of LAW) comes as a "thief" (JC said comes only to steal, kill, destroy: Jn 10:10) in the "night" (aka in darkness: ignorance). For 
WHEN they (law of OT LORD believers) say Peace & safety (in law of OT LORD works wrath, "worst" & "destruction"). 
THEN sudden "destruction" (not salvation) comes upon them, and they shall "not escape" (law of LORD works wrath). 
But
in contrast, God (Grace & peace) has not appointed us to  wrath (aka not to wrathful & destructive law): 1Thess 5:9.
SO, in Heb 8:10, Paul reminds even a new covenant of an OT LORD, still puts "laws" (not grace & truth) in hearts & minds. 
Seems why news & weather report "worst"←BC scenarios may be due to people not putting away laws of LORD←OT.
Interesting "mystery" details to notice for our awareness sake.
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus→Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

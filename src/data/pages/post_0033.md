## Content

Bible Mystery: the two different Law vs Grace God-Fathers contrasted.
- Jesus' God-Father: forgives + un-forgives + torments: Mt 18:23-35.
- Y(our) God-Father, being "merciful", never torments ppl: Luke 6:36. 
So
Matthew 5:48 KJV says "Be ye therefore perfect, 
even as your Father which is in heaven is perfect". 
And
Luke 6:36 KJV says " Be ye therefore merciful, 
as your Father also is merciful". 
For the perfect "God of all grace" has no f-law at all;
As "the God of all comfort" has no dis-comfort at all.
That God-Father is the light having "no darkness at all".
Any God less than that God can still perceive a dark-end;
Such as "the day of the LORD is darkness, and not light",
Or "last days" of plural God which are also peril-us times.
So let us go for-ward, and also grow up-ward to "that God";
Firstly "through Jesus → Christ" to have the mind of Christ,
then "through Christ God-ward" to be reconciled to that God.
To→wit, that God was in Christ (is the end of the law)
reconciling the world unto himself (the God of all grace),
not (law) imputing their trespasses unto them: 2Cor 5:19.

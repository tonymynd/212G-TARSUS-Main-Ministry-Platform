## Content

Many churches preach a watered-down version of the gospel that preaches ‘grace’ which instead of transforming lives, has become a licence for sinful living. In this context, the grace of God will mercifully cover their sins and has nullified the law. They go on sinning while at the same time claiming grace. This is a deceitful teaching. The truth is that with the grace of God we can now overcome sin that we were not able to do in the 
flesh before grace was made available by the shedding of Christ’s blood on Calvary. True grace empowers; not a licence for sin. Therefore, our righteousness must exceed that of the scribes and Pharisees if we want to enter heaven. You have received power to overcome the world.
Author Lazarus Muoka
From The LORD Chosen daily devotional.

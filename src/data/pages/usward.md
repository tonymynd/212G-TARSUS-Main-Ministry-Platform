So the [Bible Emblem changed x2](biblicalemblems.md), both outward and inward; Not only from old to new, but from them-ward to us-ward, which is neither of them vs them having a [sides war](TheirPartYourPart.md). For God neither makes sides nor takes a side, since all sides wars have side effects.

Of them/us, God hath given "us" the victory  

God hath given "us" the victory over law: sin and death; but it's notably "through Jesus>Christ", "in Christ" thereof. To wit, "[that God](ShewBread5.md#IAMTHAT)" was "in [Christ](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md)" reconciling the world unto [himself](GodShew2.md#Allness)... and hath given "us" the word of reconciliation (grace). Such victory-us ultimate and universal [reconciliation unto Grace](Reconciliation.md) is neither of [two "law"s in Matthew 22 & Romans 8](TwainShew4.md#Laws), is notably [through](through.md) Jesus > Christ, in Christ; Which is allegorically (them vs them) > usward; Which is conversion from all perish (by law) > none perish (by grace). So being [born again](BornAgain.md), which happens to [man](TwainShew.md#Blesseds) of child/man, is also as conversion from them (as in Adam all die) to us (in Christ all made alive). Such conversion from them to us is called us-ward, which is accounted as salvation in 2Peter 3:15.

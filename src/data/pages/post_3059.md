Bible Contrast: DRIVEN -vs- LED
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
DRIVEN: "walk before me" (what OT LORD law said: Gen 17:1)
LED: 
"follow me" (what Jesus Christ grace said: Mt 4:19)
Galatians 5:18 KJV
But if ye be LED of the Spirit, ye are not under the law.
Suggests:
DRIVEN = law.
LED = grace.
Makes you wonder about:
purpose DRIVEN people,
of 
purpose DRIVEN churches.
Consider how book of Revelation is like a minefield, and it is therefore wise to be LED of the Spirit instead of DRIVEN through it.
What are your thoughts/insights about biblical DRIVEN/LED?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

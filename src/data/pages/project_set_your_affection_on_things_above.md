**objective: To share a complete bible study about 17 aspects in  fruit of the Spirit and things to add to your faith**

## “Set your affection on things above.” Colossians 3:3 AKJV

Grace, mercy and peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.

This is an invitation to study the distinct aspects of the Spirit of Grace, others refer to these as “principles” which we are also be “leaving the principles” as told in Heb 6:1, while we grow in grace.

The goal is to inform and encourage the reader to reach a Spiritual level that even goes beyond temperance (Self-control) all the way up to “charity” the only one that thinks no evil and the “greatest” surprisingly even above faith and hope 1 Cor 13:13. We’ll be studying one by one of the seven-teen  aspects, nine of the Fruit from Galatians 5 and eight from 2 Peter 1’s AdVenture list to add to your faith to be fruit-full in the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Aspects in ascending order:

1. charity (grace agape)
2. broderly kindness
3. godliness
4. patience (after temperance)
5. temperance: Self Control

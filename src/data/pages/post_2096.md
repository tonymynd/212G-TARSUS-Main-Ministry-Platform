FAVOUR & GRACE are both 'charis' in NT Greek; BUT GRACE is so much more perhaps we shouldn't call "grace" undeserved "favour".
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Favour[charis] & Grace[charis] both occur in Luke - Acts (LukeWarm mode); But favour only 6x, last requested "against" to "kill": Acts 25:3. So favour never occurs again. In contrast grace occurs over 100x from Luke 2:40 to Revelation 22:21,…

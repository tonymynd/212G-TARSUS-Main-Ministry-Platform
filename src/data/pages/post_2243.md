What does the Spirit say "UNTO THE CHURCHES" ? 
(as what to h-ear noted 7x in Revelation 2 & 3)
"UNTO THE CHURCHES" of Galatia (formerly Asia in Acts 20:4): "Grace be to you and peace from God the Father, and (from) our Lord Jesus Christ" (Galatians 1:2,3 AKJV). 
Hmmm, 
"UNTO THE CHURCHES" occurs 7 times in Revelation 2 & 3:
"He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith UNTO THE CHURCHES" 
(Revelation 2:7; 2:11; 2:17; 2:29; 3:6; 3:13; 3:22 AKJV).
Hmmm,
When connect "UNTO THE CHURCHES" phrases, in Galatians 1 & 7x in Revelation 2 & 3, it seems to reveal what the Spirit saith UNTO THE CHURCHES, noted to hear 7x in Revelation 2 & 3, allegory refers to what's previously said in Galatians 1:2,3. 
...UNTO THE CHURCHES of Galatia: 
Grace be to you and peace from God the Father, and from our Lord Jesus Christ.
The BackStory:
Seems Paul, accompanied by 7 listed in Acts 20:4, established 7 churches in Asia (later called Galatia). Then Paul made Timothy first  bishop over all 7 from Ephesus. But then these churches got "bewitched", as noted in Galatians. 
So Paul noted in 2Tim 1:15  all in Asia got turned away from him. 
Interesting mystery details to ponder.

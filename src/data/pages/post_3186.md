## Content

NIV vs KJV
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Interesting to notice Romans 16:24 KJV (which NIV removes) is the grace salutation of Paul, which Paul always writes with his "own hand", as noted in 2Thessalonians 3:17,18; And that is the end: Revelation 22:21 KJV.
note:
Although NIV removes Romans 16:24, it does retain 2Thessalonians 3:17,18 as clarifying Romans 16:24 is the grace salutation of Paul and written by his own hand.
Yet NIV then changes the end: Rev 22:21, by remove the word "Christ".
Example:
2Thessalonians 3:17,18 NIV
I, Paul, write this greeting in my own hand, which is the distinguishing mark in all my letters. This is how I write.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all.
Revelation 22:21 NIV
The grace of the Lord Jesus be with God’s people. Amen.
vs
2Thessalonians 3:17,18 KJV
The salutation of Paul with mine own hand, which is the token in every epistle: so I write.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.
Revelation 22:21 KJV (Salutation of Paul, not of John):
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

THREE BEST WISHES (1Timothy 1:2)
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
NOTE: 1Timothy is the "epistle from Laodicea" mentioned in 
Colossians 4:16
And 
when this epistle is read among you, cause that it be read also in the church of the Laodiceans; and that ye likewise read "the epistle from Laodicea".
HOW do we know 1Timothy is "the epistle from Laodicea"? Because Paul's Epistles have <end notes> in Oxford KJV. The end note after  1Timothy 6:21 says:
<The first to Timothy was written from Laodicea, which is the chiefest city of Phrygia Pacatiana> 
KJV also does not remove the word "Christ" from the end to endure unto, as most modern bibles now do:
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

Here's a biblical mystery to re-examine, about resurrected Jesus: it gets quite interesting to notice & compare these:

- In TWO different resurrected Jesus's 2nd comings to the same "eleven" recorded in Mk 16, Lk 24, John 20; There is only mention of "Peace" in Lk 24 & Jn 20. In Mk 16:14-16 even a resurrected Jesus still "upbraided" & still said "damned". Hmmm...   
- Previously, in Mt 10:34 & Lk 12:51, Jesus had twice said "Nay" to "peace" (as if when in law mode, then no peace), clarifying he'd neither "give" nor "send" peace on earth. Seems even in Lk 13:32 he said he's not yet perfected.

But then,

and as if a different 2nd coming to same "eleven"  
later on said "Peace" to you in Lk 24 & Jn 20.

Why this speaking change, and to the same "eleven"?

Could it be allegory like a change from being "Son of man" Jesus to being "Son of God" Jesus; And by re-ascend to be sat on RIGHT hand of God, as lastly noted in Mk 16:19?

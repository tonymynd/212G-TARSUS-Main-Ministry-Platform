LAW AND ODOR? LAW WORKS WRATH? RULE OF F-LAW?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Seems evidence is making it ever more evident to ever more people that the law, of 'law and order', and of 'rule of law', and of so-called justice systems, is NOT producing any lasting peace nor true safety, but the opposite. 
So 
then, law, rule of law, and enforcement of law, isn't a solution, but a problem that can become deadly.
this is not to speak against law, but to aware about law.
Seems what the Bible reveals about law is being made evident for our awareness sake. 
Law is "both good and evil", lastly reverse of "live" (by grace).
So, apostle Paul notes because when do law good [kalos], then law evil [kakos] is present: Romans 7:21, we ought to discern and avoid both good and evil, even law good, also has law evil: Hebrews 5:14.  
Law's "goodness" [chrestotes] is conditional ("if") of you being law good, and such law "goodness" has "severity" attached. So apostle Paul uses allegorical satire in Romans 11:22 to note: if you perceive law as God, then what to "Behold" is both goodness & severity.
In contrast, 
grace "goodness" = 'agathosyne' (not 'chrestotes'). So then, we can honestly say true & wise God is always and only good [agathos].
So then, law can be a "ministration of condemnation" & become a "ministration of death"; Which should be "done away": 2Cor 3:6-11.
Prayer: Our Father ... deliver us from EVIL: Luke 11:4.
Answered: Now we are delivered from the LAW: Rom 7:6.
Seems "law worketh wrath" (Romans 4:15) is what to "put away" (Ephesians 4:31); For God has not appointed us to wrath (of law), but to obtain salvation (from law works wrath) by our Lord Jesus Christ (aka by grace and truth came by Jesus Christ): 1Thess 5:9.
What are your opinions & insights about such things?
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus → Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

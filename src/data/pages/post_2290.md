## Content

LIBERTY (free-dom): 7 biblical kinds in NT Greek mode:
alphesus; occurs 17x as remission, forgiveness, deliverance. Eg: Luke 4:18
anesis; occurs 5x as rest, liberty, be eased. Eg: Acts 24:23
apolyo; occurs 69x as release, let go, set at liberty, dismiss. Eg: Acts 26:32 & Hebrews 13:23
epitrepo; occurs 19x as suffer, permit, give leave, let. Eg: Acts 27:3
eleutheria (noun) occurs 19x as free, liberty. Eg: John 8:32; Romans 8:2; Galatians 2:4
eleutheroo (adjective); occurs 23x, like freewoman in Gal 4:22
exousia; occurs 103x as liberty, power, authority. Eg: 1Cor 8:9

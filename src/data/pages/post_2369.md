Ukraine President ZELENSKY ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Interesting to observe this popular comedian, provocative dancer, tv star (played president of Ukraine in Servant of the People), said he got inspired to go into politics by Canadian PM Justin Trudeau. Then in April 2019, he got elected to actually become the president of Ukraine, and by about 70% of voters. Wow...
Yet 
reports say he then closed several tv stations that had been critical of him, and even jailed both a journalist and some political opponents. Other reports say he allowed continuation of neo-nazis to harm and kill pro Russian people in an area called Donbas. 
Hmmm...
Seems, if true, it's opposite the behavior of a democracy leader.
Then in 2020 he's seen not just attending, but addressing the World Economic Forum. That's huge, and makes you go hmmm...
In pandemic 2021, rumors of corruption & of Pentagon BioLabs.
Seems all that inspired Putin to do a Special Military Operation.
Now, in 2022 we see Zelensky being invited to 
address the USA Congress, 
and the UK Parliament, 
and the Canadian Parliament. 
Interesting stuff to observe. 
What are your observations?

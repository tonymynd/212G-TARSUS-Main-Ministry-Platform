# Notes taken why asking God why 2nd temperance. 
> Rom 4:13  For the promise, that he should be the heir of the world, was not to Abraham, or to his seed, through the law, but through the righteousness of faith.

Interesting, while researching re 2nd temperance, notice:

> “And as he reasoned of righteousness(dikaiosune), temperance, and judgment to come, Felix trembled, and answered, Go thy way for this time; when I have a convenient season, I will call for thee.” (Act 24:25, KJV)
Here *righteousness precedes temperance*

## **extract from [[temperance publishing]]**
> 	Temperance (noun) is 1st mentioned in Acts 24:25, following mention of righteousness; And last mentioned in 2Peter 1:5,6 following mention of knowledge(‘gnosis’) leading to patience after temperance. So it reasons it takes knowledge of righteousness to bring about temperance also having patience (steadfast endurance). So in our introduction chapter we noted two lists for Temperance: Gal 5:22,23 & 2Peter 1:5-10, where after attaining Temperance as 9th fruit, we then also add Temperance to our faith as 3rd of 7 upgrades to our faith, since our faith only saves the soul of soul/spirit, noted in 1Peter 1.

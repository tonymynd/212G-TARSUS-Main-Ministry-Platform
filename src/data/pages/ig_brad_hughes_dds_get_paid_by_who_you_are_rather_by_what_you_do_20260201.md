## Transcript

The biggest difference between a doctor who makes a million dollars a year and a doctor
who makes $200,000 a year, the doctor making a million dollars a year is getting paid
for who they are, the doctor making $200,000 a year is getting paid for what they do.
One has built a brand and has built relationships, patients are lining up at the door because
of who that doctor is and the brand that they've built and the relationships that they have
with those patients.
The other one is focused on just drilling holes in teeth and that is a major difference
in making money versus making way less money.
Get paid for who you are, not what you do.

Them or Us: Destruction or Salvation: Law or Grace  

Them all (1Thes 5:3): when they shall say, Peace and safety [Grace and law]; then sudden destruction cometh upon [them](ShewBread2.md#inthem)... they shall not escape.  
Us all (1Thes 5:9): God hath not appointed [us](ShewBread2.md#justus) to wrath ("law worketh wrath": Romans 4:15; So the biblical [allegory](Allegory.md) of 1Thes 5:9 is that God hath not appointed us to law).

that he might have mercy upon [us] all".

Conclusion About Them or Us  

Conclusion (about them or us): if I were one of them, I'd be converting to one of us, and asap, by taking the [grace walk](GraceWalk.md) [from Sinai to Sion](Sevens2.md), not even look back at such Horeb-bull law, the "great terror" to all Israel (all them) via terror-ist law giver Moses (Deut 34:12; Jn 1:17).  
  
From the same lump the Potter has created two sorts of vessels, one to dishonour and one to honour, mirrorly the reverse for the sake of a this/that shew (to "shew the only Potentate", of twain: "the only wise God" (of foolish/wise Gods), "the only true God" (of false/true Gods), is "the God of all grace"). And in such an allegoric shew, notably written as a mystery aforetime for our learning (Rom 15:4), we find them/us, ye/you, the former being "created" imperfect, the latter being "made" perfect. Since there are two alls, two one man scenarios, the objective is to convert from law to grace, as from the dead end of no escape for them to the living end of escape for us, via abolition of law which was "added" to "my grace is sufficient" (no law req'd).  
  
In a them or us God shew both sides are played out in heavens and earth created for such purpose; As if the two options given for let us make man: (i) in our image (ii) after our likeness; Allegorically the two one man scenarios of Romans 5, and the two Adams of 1Cor 15; Which things are an [allegory](Allegory.md) being law and grace, them and us: Beginning them-ward and Ending us-ward; [ye and you](YeYou.md), ye (do err) is made a perfected you by knowing [the truth (about law)](Law.md), as noted in places like Jn 8:32 and 1Pet 5:10 which notes conversion is via [the God of all grace](GodShew2.md#Allness). Hence we find the biblical term [you-ward](You-ward.md) denoting not only conversion from ye->you, but also denoting the fwd direction of <-bwd\[fwd-> go on unto perfection](Hebrews7) (endure to [the end](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md)). For it's neither pleasing to God nor Paul to draw back, turn back to law, as [John did in Rev](Revelation5.md).  
  
However in every good mystery there are twists, many [twain things](TwainShew.md) to sort out, rightly divide; And then clarify even further as does Pauline clarity, which first compares and clarifies twain things as good/better, such as [two sorts of men](TwainShew.md#Adams) in Romans 5, [two sorts of servants](TwainShew6.md#Servants) in Rom 6, [two sorts of laws](TwainShew4.md#Laws) in Rom 8, and [two sorts of children](TwainShew2.md#Childrens) in Rom 9. Then in other [Pauline Epistles](PaulineEpistles.md) we find even further clarity about three things: good better best, and what's best for all is neither what's good/better: like be not a servant (neither in Rom 6) but a son: Gal 4; and in Eph 4: be no more children (neither in Rom 9); And what's best for all of [good better best](GodShew.md#GBB) is no law (neither [law law](LawLaw.md) in Rom 8). For such a sides war, blame game, accusing and excusing, is them vs them (oppose themselves), with the us part of them/us being above such (plura) heavens, in heaven (higher than the heavens) where Christ of "[Jesus Christ](GodShew5.md#Peace)" (not Christ Jesus) entered, appeared in the presence of God for "us", and thereafter is long-suffering to [us-ward](Usward.md).  
  
For if Babylon is twice fallen ("Babylon is fallen, is fallen") as are a third of the stars of heaven in Revelation 12: first drawn away from heaven ([higher than the heavens](RevelatorySermons21.md)) to heavens (higher than the earth), then cast down to earth... it then reasons [reconciliation](Reconciliation.md) and a return to heaven also requires two up risings: first to the right side of heavens where them oppose themselves as goats(center) vs sheep(right) in a sides war, yet with purpose: to rightly divide law vs grace things; then also leave such twain "principles" to go on unto perfection, or go higher to heaven where there are no sides to even take, and thereby no war even plausible... ahhhh [peace](PeaceLinks.md) at last.  
  
Of [two sorts of "them"](TwainShew6.md#Thems) to sort out first, we are not the sort of them who draw back (turn back, look back) in our grace walk, but the sort who believe to the saving of the soul (Heb 10:39). Albeit such is better of good/better, but the exhortation is to also leave such "principles" and go on unto perfection (no mention of law), which speaks of be no more them, but us of them/us. For when they shall say Peace and safety (Grace and law; safety in law), then sudden destruction cometh upon them, the "no escape" sort mentioned in 1Thes 5:3. However there is an escape for us, but not via [rapture theories](RevelatorySermons8.md), rather via [give earnest heed to what's been said](Hebrews3.md). What's been said? The will of God is "I will have mercy(grace), and not sacrifice(law)". God was never pleased with offerings and sacrifices which were "of the law".  
  
  

 How(?) shall we escape? Perhaps "the end" is already written:  

  

The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ with you all. Amen.

THE ONLY WISE GOD ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
According to the Bible:
OT 
LORD [Yehova] who saves & destroys is not the only wise God.
NT "God our Saviour", who saves & awares is "the only wise God".
It takes both grace & truth to both save & aware all.
Paul makes 5 of the 6 mentions of "God our Saviour", from 1Timothy 1:1 - Jude 1:25. 
He awares in 1Tim 2:3,4 that "God our Saviour" will have ALL men [anthropos] to be saved AND to come unto the (higher spiritual) knowledge [epignosis] of the truth.
So, after Paul's 5 of 6 mentions of "God our Saviour" 
(1Tim 1:1; 1Tim 2:3,4; Tit 1:3; Tit 2:10; Tit 3:4) 
we find Jude 1:5 firstly reminding OT LORD [Yehovah] both law saved & destroyed after, then Jude 1:25 concludes "God our Saviour" (who doesn't save & destroy, but save & aware) is "the only wise God:
Jude 1:24,25
24 Now unto him that is able to keep you from falling, and to present you faultless before the presence of his glory with exceeding joy,
25 To the only wise God our Saviour, be glory and majesty, dominion and power, both now and ever. Amen.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

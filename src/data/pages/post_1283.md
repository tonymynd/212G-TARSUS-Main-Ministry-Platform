## Content

Some believers still talk of receive 'favour' from God, as if God has partiality to believers. Nope. True God has No partiality. 
Here's what I later learned, after first believe I had favour:
Both "favour" & "grace" are used for 'charis' (greek), But...
- 
"favour" only occurs 7 times, only from Luke to Acts, and is lastly requested "against" someone; OMG...
- "grace" occurs 100s of times from Luke to Revelation 22:21, never "against" any, but "with" you all, when it's JC grace.
Thank you.

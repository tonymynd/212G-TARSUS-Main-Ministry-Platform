December Questions:
(not to humbug, but for discussion)
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
was 
Jesus born at Christmas, or perhaps 9 months later?
is Christmas nicest time of year, or not according to police stats?
did shepherds abibe flocks in the fields, by night, in winter, in Israel, or could this be allegory?
is Christmas tree Godly, or a Jeremiah 10 vain Babylonian custom of fools?
did the magi visit the babe in a manger, or a child at his house years later?
was there 3 magi, or enough Babylon Jews to stir up all Jerusalem?
was the heralding of "on earth peace" about Jesus, or "Christ" in Luke 2:11-14? If Christ, what's the difference?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

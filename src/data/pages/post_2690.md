## Content

New Testament things noted about God:
- He is not the God of the dead, but the God of the living: Mt 22:32; Mk 12:27; Lk 20:38).
- God [is] a Spirit: and they that worship him must worship [him] in spirit and in truth: John 4:24.
- 
the only true God, who sent Jesus Christ: John 17:3
- Now we have received, not the spirit of the world, but the spirit which is of God; that we might know the things that are FREELY given to us of God: 1Corinthians 2:12.
- God is not [the author] of confusion, but of peace: 1Cor 14:33.
- the God of all comfort: 2Corinthians 1:3.
- To wit, that God was in Christ, reconciling the world unto himself, not imputing their trespasses unto them: 2Corinthians 5:19.
- a mediator is not of one, but God is one: Galatians 3:20.
- One God and Father of all, who [is] above all, and through all, and in you all: Ephesians 4:6.
- God our Saviour: 1Tim 1:1; 2:3; Titus 1:3; 2:10; 2:4.
- the only wise God: 1Timothy 1:17; Jude 1:25.
- he hath said, I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee: Heb 13:5.
- the God who upbraideth not: James 1:5.
- the God of all grace: 1Peter 5:10.
- that God is light, and in him is no darkness at all: 1John 1:5.

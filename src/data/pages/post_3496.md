## Content

JUDE'S CONTRAST CONCLUSION
of OT "LORD" vs NT "God our Saviour":
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Let's 
notice Jude 1:24,25 go together, and as the contrasting conclusion of Jude that "the only wise God" is "God our Saviour"(from 1Timothy 2:3,4).
The contrast:
- Jude 1:5 reminds OT "LORD" [Yehova] both saved and destroyed afterward. Such did not end well.
Jude 1:5 KJV
I will therefore put you in remembrance, though ye once knew this, how that the Lord, having saved the people out of the land of Egypt, afterward destroyed them that believed not.
- Jude 1:24,25 notes "God our Saviour" is "the only wise God" who in 1Tim 2:3,4 will save & aware all. Such does end well, for all.
1Timothy 2:3,4 KJV
For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour; Who will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.
Jude 1:24,25 KJV
Now unto him that is able to keep you from falling, and to present you faultless before the presence of his glory with exceeding joy, To the only wise God our Saviour, be glory and majesty, dominion and power, both now and ever. Amen.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all.
Amen.

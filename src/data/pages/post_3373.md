## Content

Wisdom:
not from above vs from above,
perhaps as if contrasting 
earthly law vs heavenly grace.

WISDOM (not from above) vs WISDOM (from above): James 3
Wisdom not from above is earthly, sensual, devilish: 3:15
Wisdom that is from above: 3:17 is first pure (as if the 8th above 7 things), then (seven things):
peaceable
gentle
easy to be intreated
full of mercy (midst of 7)
full of good fruits
without partiality
without hypocrisy

# Above the above

**Date Received:** Wednesday, July 13, 2016 5:35 PM

What are your thoughts about this 8th aspect of perfection in Heb 6:1-2 being the 8th that’s above(epi) the above(ano) in Col 3:1-14, and Father in Eph 4:4-6, also occuring in 2Pet 1:5-10

The "perfection" ('teleiotes') to go on unto in Hebrews 6:1-2 KJV is the 8th thing above & beyond these seven:

8\. perfection (teleiotes)
7\. eternal judgment
6\. resurrection of the dead
5\. laying on of hands
4\. doctrine of baptisms
3\. faith toward God
2\. foundation of repentance from dead works
1\. principles\* of the doctrine of Christ
\*different than the principles (elements, rudiments) in Heb 5:12
This "perfection" ('teleiotes') is only used twice, first as "perfectness" ('teleiotes) in Col 3:12-14 KJV, where "charity" is also the 8th above & beyond 7 things; and such "above" ('epi') is the second kind of TWO "above"(s) in Col 3:1-14: 'ano' & 'epi'.

8\. charity: bond of perfectness (teleiotes) = 'epi' above (not 'ano')
7\. forgiving one another = still 'ano' = on high
6\. forbearing one another
5\. longsuffering
4\. meekness
3\. humbleness of mind
2\. kindness
1\. bowels of mercies

The 8th being above(epi) the 7(ano) also occurs in Eph 4:4-6 KJV:
8\. and Father of all = above(epi) all
7\. one God = still 'ano' = on high
6\. one baptism
5\. one faith
4\. one Lord
3\. one hope
2\. one Spirit
1\. one body

Same thing occurs in 2Peter 1:5-10 KJV with charity = 8th
8\. charity = the 'epi-gnosis' knowledge of our Lord JC
7\. brotherly kindness
6\. gosliness
5\. patience
4\. temperance (from Gal 5:23)
3\. knowledge (still 'gnosis'; not yet 'epignosis')
2\. virtue ('arete', not 'dunamis')
1\. your faith
SO THEN, going on unto perfection is as leaving 7 things to go on unto the 8th, which is leaving 'ano' above to go on to 'epi' above, which is above the above, aka higher than the heavens on high

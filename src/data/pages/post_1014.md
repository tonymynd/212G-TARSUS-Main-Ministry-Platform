## Content

My God is THE God of the living (Mark 12:27), THE only true God (John 17:3), THE God of all comfort (2Cor 1:3), THE only wise God (1Tim 1:17), THE God who does not upbraid (James 1:5), THE God of all grace (1Peter 5:10), THAT God = light void of darkness (1John 1:5), God OUR Saviour (Jude 1:25) -- many other verses note what God is not, such as God is not the author of confusion, but of peace (1Cor 14:33). So I wish Grace, mercy, and peace to you all.

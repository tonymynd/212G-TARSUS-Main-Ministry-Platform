Son of man?
Grace, mercy, [and] peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
"Son of man" occurs over 70x in Matthew to Acts. But beyond Acts there is NO "Son of man" mention in Romans → forward for the "gospel of Christ" of through Jesus → Christ = end of the law.
Beyond Acts there is only mention of Son of God, as if having gone through 2 Sons Jesus to 1 Son Christ. 
So, then the greeting is:
Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.
So, by NO mention of Son of man, ONLY Son of God beyond Acts, it seems to thereby essentially cancel all the previous THREATS of Son of man to come with clouds and bring:
- mourning (not rejoicing): Mt 24:30,
- everlasting punishment (not mercy): Mt 25:46,
- woe (not safety): Mt 26:24,
- destruction (not salvation): Lk 17:27,29.
Sadly, many are still looking for the coming of Son of man, and using such threat mentions in Matthew to Acts to fear monger others with partiality; When NO mention of Son of man even occurs beyond Acts, and then it's called the gospel of "Christ".
Let's edify and exhort unto comfort (not comfort & discomfort).
The grace of our Lord Jesus → Christ be with you all. Amen.

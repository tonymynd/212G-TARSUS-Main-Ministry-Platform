CHRISTMAS? 
What may we learn by investigate about Christ-mas? 
(Not to speak against nor humbug, but to increase awareness)
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
BIRTH TIME OF CHRIST? Not True. Seems study of biblical & historical data can reveal birth of Jesus was NOT December 25, but either in Spring (March) or at Sukkot (9 months later than Dec 25). So, Christmas time could be a conception time, but not a birth time. 
note: Luke 2:8's shepherds abiding flocks in fields by night would not be in winter, but spring to summer: in season of lambs). 
note: for 1st 300 years Christians did not celebrate birth of Jesus, but the death, burial, resurrection of Christ as the gospel of Christ. Seems some say Christmas was started by mixture of what some call pagan festival of Saturnalia.  
DECORATED TREE? Some note Jeremiah 10 calls it a vain, brutish, foolish Babylonian custom: a (2400 year old) doctrine of vanities. 
THREE WISE MEN (magi) VISIT BABE IN MANGER? Not True. Pageants portray this; Yet Bible notes only shepherds visited a "babe" in a manger, but there was enough magi (perhaps Babylonian Jews) to stir up "all of Jerusalem", also Herod who dwelt in another Royal city; And it's noted magi visited the "child" (toddler) at his "house" in Nazareth, not "babe" in manger.
NICEST TIME OF YEAR? Not True. It may be for some, but not all. Police & Newspaper records reveal Christmas season has more shop lifting, domestic feuds, drunk drivers, bad weather, deadly accidents, depressions, suicides, debt incursion, etc, than any other time of year.
SHEPHERDS ABIDING FLOCKS IN FIELD BY NIGHT (Luke 2:8)? Seems it's too cold at Christmas in Israel; flocks would be in sheep holds. Shepherds would only do such during lamb season. Also, Bethlehem region was noted for raising lambs for temple sacrifice. So, it would be appropriate for these shepherds to visit Lamb of God at Bethlehem.
MAKE MERRY & SEND GIFTS? Seems Revelation 11:10 mentions such, but in an apocalypse scenario, after Rev 6:4 takes away peace.
ON EARTH PEACE (Luke 2:14)? Seems "Jesus" (made under the law: Galatians 4:4) firstly said "Nay" to give or send "peace on earth"; Rather a "sword" & "division": Mt 10:34 & Lk 12:51. 
So, 
John 7:43; 9:16; 10:19 say "division" (not peace), and "because of him" (not because of "Christ" = our peace: Ephesians 2:15).
SANTA CLAUSE, ELVES, FLYING REINDEER? Seems more like adult fables, myths, lies to children than like grace & truth or true reality. 
Question: Is it ok to lie to children, or even to one another?
REASON FOR SEASON? 
What are your thoughts & insights about such things?
Clarity: Again, not shared to speak against Christmas nor humbug; But only to mention things we may investigate & ponder & discuss. 
If you do Christmas, fine, or if you don't, fine.  
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

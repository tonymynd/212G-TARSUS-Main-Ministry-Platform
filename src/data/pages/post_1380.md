## Content

Christ Jesus? 2Timothy 3:12 KJV notes "all" who will live "godly" in "Christ Jesus" shall "suffer" persecution...
"Christ Jesus" only occurs from Acts 19 to 1Peter 5; But "after", when made (more) perfect (than before) by the "God of all grace": 1Pet 5:10 KJV, as if to end "suffer" in "Christ Jesus", then no more mention of "Christ Jesus" past 1Pet 5...
Seems to portray before/after scenarios, as if before it's both "suffer" & "rejoice" 
(1Cor 12:26), but after only "rejoice"; As if before it's both law & grace (duality), but after it's all grace (no law, by no more duality); Allegory...
As if UpGrade from plural God on high (has plural "heavens") to higher God ("higher than the heavens") has neither duality nor partiality; For in higher heaven there's no more leaven. 
Interesting stuff.

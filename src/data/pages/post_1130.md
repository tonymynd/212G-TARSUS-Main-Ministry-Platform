## Content

WOW: 2nd "reconciled" = 
#MuchMore
 in Romans 5:10 KJV.
Eg: Romans 5:10 KJV:
For if, when we were enemies, we were RECONCILED to God by the death of his Son, MUCH MORE, being RECONCILED, we shall be saved by his(God's eternal) life. 
Be (fully) reconciled (through Jesus→Christ)→to God: 2Cor 5;
as 
if ALSO a 2nd reconciled of 2 mentioned in Romans 5:10;
- as if 1st THROUGH our Lord Jesus→Christ to be "in Christ",
- then ALSO (in that Christ) THROUGH Christ→God-ward.
For the only true God & only wise God cannot lie nor die.
So being reconciled to a death is not [yet] unto "that God",
later on called "the God of all grace" in 1Peter 5:10 KJV;
Which seems higher than a plural law/grace God on high.
Seems to explain why the conclusion of Romans 5 KJV's five mentions of "much more" also lastly concludes this:
grace reigns through→righteousness→unto eternal life
by Jesus Christ our Lord (as if more than Christ Jesus; 
For Christ Jesus only high calling of high/higher callings).
Seems Apostle Paul told Bishop Timothy, in 1Tim 6:12b KJV: "lay hold on eternal life, whereunto thou art ALSO called": Allegory as if there's ALSO a higher calling than firstly fight the good fight of faith (is still in FightClub mode on high). Seems why 1Tim 6:17 then mentioned "be not highminded".
Seems to explain the phrase "higher than the heavens" on high mentioned in Hebrews 7:26, to be-come "harmless" us; Allegory as if a 2nd & higher "us-ward" of 2 mentioned, and to then not only do no harm, but ALSO receive no harm. 
Seems why Colossians 3:1-15 KJV has 2 kinds of "above", as if hidden mystery in greek mode, revealed by the Spirit:
- 'epi' above: Col 3:14 as if higher than
- 'ano' above: Col 3:1,2 
Seems time now for people to also "go up higher":
(not as a rapture, but as if higher learning in our mind), 
as if above 'gnosis' knowledge to 'epignosis' knowledge.
Seems 1Timothy 2:4 notes: God our Saviour will have all men saved, AND come to the knowledge('epignosis') of the truth.
WOW, it's like the Spirit of truth is guiding us into "all truth" (John 16:13), as if taking us above & beyond law/grace duality to "all grace" (1Peter 5:10); As if unto a 3rd way called a "more excellent way" in 1Cor 12:31, called "charity" (more than love), which "thinketh no evil" in 1Cor 13:5 KJV.

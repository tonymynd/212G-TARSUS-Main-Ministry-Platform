## Content

Two Double Mind-Blowing biblical things to notice & ponder: For me, I didn't yet notice, until prompted by the Spirit to re-read Genesis 1:26a, it didn't say let us create man, as I'd thought; but of "create/make" (bara/asah) asif of before/after, said let us "make" man; That was mind-blowing for me to see; And..
. 
Then, as if yet even more mind-blowing for my double-mind:
also in Gen 1:26b gave 2 "image/likeness" modes to play out, 
asif also before/after, for an allegoric compare-i-son of twain:
- "in our image" (asif mirrorly reverse image mode, before)
- "after our likeness" (asif "likeness" = "after", isn't "image")
So...
now, when I look in mirror, at a reverse "image", I spiritually ponder it may only be an "image", or only an "imagination", to let go of (as 2Cor 10:5 says cast down every "imagination").
For Then, after blow my mind, TWICE (both left/right sides),
Spirit directed me to see in 1Peter 5:10 "The God of all grace" will "make" you more "perfect" (asif yet more than before when 1st "created": Eph 2:10 in "image" mode: Gen 1:27, asif to descend); But "make' mode, of create/make, is not until "after" suffered a while (as noted "all" do in CJ: 2Tim 3:12); Why?
So that then, "after" made perfect, by "all grace" of a higher "God of all grace" our flawed awareness (perhaps only imagined in error) can then also re-ascend, "higher than the heavens" on high=high-minded, akin to double-minded,  by being further e-stablished, strengthened, settled, WOW.
Asif thereby (some sort of simulated descent event) we learn law (flaw imagined) isn't "grace"=really what our "likeness" is.
For me, it took led of the Spirit of grace & truth, to even notice, let alone fathom. I now better understand why bad things I see on news & weather only 'seem' an ill-u-sion, not real-ity. 
Hope my share not too absurd to ponder, and only FYI, not to suggest flaw to any, nor offend any, only awareness sake...
of Grace, mercy, and peace to you all, from God our Father...

kingdom of heaven -vs- kingdom of God
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. For your information:
The term "kingdom of heaven" occurs only in book of Matthew, wherein Jesus also said in Matthew 6:33:
But 
seek ye first the "kingdom of God" and his righteousness: and all these things (needed) shall be added unto you (of ye/you).
So "kingdom of heaven" & "kingdom of God" somehow differ.
Both terms occur in Mt 19:23,24, yet alleogry as if in contrast;
Perhaps allegory as if how law differs from grace and truth;
For the kingdom of heaven is like unto leaven: Mt 13:33.
Hmmm...
Seems why is because there is no mention of grace yet in Mt or Mk, no NT grace until Luke 2:40 (then allegory as if LukeWarm mode instead of still cOLD mode of cOLD → LukeWarm → Hot).
So then, 
it kinda makes the 9 "Blessed"s mentioned in Mt 5 to be law blessed,
which can still be both blessed & cursed by law is both good & evil.
It also reasons why Jesus did not begin to preach the "kingdom of God" until John the Baptist (aka violent law prophet Elijah who didn't yet die) got jailed and beheaded; Allegory as if then the end of law and prophets mode, then the begin of grace and truth mode (Mk 1:1-15; Lk 16:16).
Also reasons why only when grace is mentioned in Luke, then it says the kingdom of God is within you (of ye/you); Lk 17:21 KJV.
interesting "mystery" details to notice and ponder.
What are your insights about such things?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

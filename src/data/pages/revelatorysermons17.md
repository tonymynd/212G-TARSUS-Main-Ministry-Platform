# Perfect Prototype: The Author of Eternal Salvation

## Perfect Prototype: Christ is the end of the law: Christ is our [peace](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Peace.md).  

Jesus isn't the perfect prototype, as he brought division, not peace.  
Jesus is [not yet perfected](GodsheworgExplanation.md#ThirdWay) while casting out devils and doing cures.  
Jesus: was "made" of created/made; "made under the law": Gal 4.  
But: We are not under the law, but under grace: Romans 6: 15.  
  
Christ: "made perfect" of "made [one proselyte](Allegory3.md)" / "made [perfect](Perfection.md)".  
Christ being "made [higher than the heavens](RevelatorySermons21.md)" became "harmless".  
Harmless: "upbraideth not": Neither bites Nor smites: no law.  
  
Hebrews 5:9  
And being made perfect, he became the author of [eternal salvation](TwainShew6.md#Salvations)  
  
Hebrews 7:26  
For such an high priest became  
us, [who is] holy, harmless, undefiled, separate from sinners, and made higher than the heavens  
  

Two types of Life: Temporal Life + Death & [Eternal Life](EternalLife.md) void of Death.  
Two types of Salvation: Temporal: saved + destroyed & Eternal Salvation.  
Christ: as the end of the law: is the author of eternal life of eternal salvation.  
Therefore reconciling the world to God is done "in Christ", not in Two Sons Jesus.  

 It dawned on me that "Christ" is only the "author" of "eternal salvation" in [Hebrews Chp 5](Hebrews6.md); And not both "author and finisher", as "Jesus" is of "faith", in Hebrews 12:2. It takes the whole body of Christ to [finish](HumanRace.md) eternal salvation Christ (head of the body of Christ) first authored. Nobody gets zapped with a magic 'he did it for me' wand, lest many be [ignorant](ShewBread7.md#Bliss) of [what(?) they're saved from](RevelatorySermons6.md). So [all are grounded till all get it](GodShew4.md#Probate): "[understanding](GodsheworgExplanation.md)". Only by faith are we seated with Christ.  
  

If Christ is only "the author of eternal salvation",  
then perhaps it's up to the body of Christ to finish it.  

  
For of created/made, many deceived by many are "made one propselyte": twofold: more the child of hell: Matthew 23:15; But the Christ of "through Jesus > Christ" is notably "made perfect", and thereby became the author of eternal salvation of temporal/eternal salvations. So it reasons many get bewitched by a 'Jesus did it for me' magic wand of sacrifice (bloodshed) waved over them.  
  
Worse ("more the child of hell" folk) than those ("child of hell" folk: Mt 23) saying ya gotta earn yer salvation by being [law](Law.md) abiding, are those now saying 'Jesus did for me', so I don't need to study nor be perfect (mercifu) to get "eternal" salvation. Newspapers abound with 'obituaries' of 'many' such like who die ignorant, thinking death is a part of life, when death/life are as "contrary" as <BC/AD>. God did NOT send his Son with life + death, rather with "life", the "eternal" sort. The obvious reason: all were already dying from a bad case of life + death (grace + law). [Evidence](CurrentEvents.md).  
  
It's worse: "more [child-ish](GodShew.md#nomorechildren)", and hence more hell-ish (devilish, earthy, sensua) than those who previously sat in Moses' seat: law, in Matthew 23. For those "Pharisees and scribes" called "hypocrites", "fools", "vipers", and "blind guides" were "under the law" of law/grace; But one made proselyte by such like compassing land and sea are not under the law. Example: foolish Galatians. Many new-agers spouting their grace & peace plus sacrifice aren't better, but worse than those who 'diligently' attempted to attain righteousness of law, but failed. At least they were 'exemplary' of diligent study & learning.  
  
Jesus is both "author and finisher" of "faith". Faith is only great of three things [great greater greatest](GodShew.md#Greatest) (faith, hope, charity: 1Cor 13:13). Christ is notably only the "author" of "eternal" salvation, rather than the temporal salvation all those "saved" out of Egypt had: saved + destroyed... bad ending; the BAD ending of the oxyMORON grace + law = life + death = a dead end.  
  
Salvation, the "eternal salvation" of [two salvations](TwainShew6.md#Salvations), seems to be only authored by the "greater" ("better") of "some" good/better (great/greater) things God hath provided for them/us in such things are an "allegory", also a "mystery" with lots of twists. Of great/greater the [greater](TwainShew4.md#Lights) one of twain "is here" (Matthew 12:41,42), just as that of this/that "[is come](GodShew3.md#Second)", was sent by the greatest of all (John 3:17). What "came" (past tense: John 1:17) by the greater one of twain is grace, and truth thereof; both the truth about the law and the truth about God. Hence such word of truth still has to be [rightly divided](RightlyDividing.md).  
  
Of  "some" good/better things God hath provided for them/us, some "better" thing God hath provided for "us" is still only a "some" thing, not an all thing.  What's for "[all](GodShew2.md#Allness)" is not better, but best of [good better best](GodShew.md#GBB).

## Description

The visual explanation of integration in calculus

## Transcript

Leibniz incorporated the idea of calculating area by imagining a picket fence of little rectangles.
Imagine them diminishing in size and add their areas.
Eventually, their sum approaches a limit, which is equal to the area of the region under the graph of a function.
This is the process of integration.
Leibniz summarized it with the integral sign and elongated S from the Latin word for song.

## Content

Many don't know this, not even KJV ONLY-ists
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
KJV Bible uses both "love" & "charity" for Greek 'agape'. No doubt love is excellent, yet when Paul mentions a "more excellent way" in 1Cor 12:31, then he describes "charity" in 1Corinthians 13.
Why?
Because of love [agape], Jesus said in Mt 24:12, because iniquity shall abound the love [agape] of many shall wax cold (aka fail).
So of love & charity, 1Cor 13:8 KJV says charity never faileth.
But the most important thing about charity is this:
1Cor 13:5 notes charity "thinketh no evil" (no evil, none, as if neither to punish nor even to forgive)...
So in Colossians 3:12-14 Paul lists 7 things new man can put on (after put off old man things), seventh thing being forgiving one another; And then says above these put on charity, even above forgiving, then as 8th above 7 things; Like Father is in Eph 4:6. 
Matthew 24:12 (love [agape1] shall wax cold)
And because iniquity shall abound, the love of many shall wax cold.
1Cor 12:31 (a more excellent way: above broad/narrow ways)
But covet earnestly the best gifts: and yet shew I unto you a more excellent way.
1Cor 13:4-8 (charity thinketh no evil)
Charity suffereth long, and is kind; charity envieth not; charity vaunteth not itself, is not puffed up, Doth not behave itself unseemly, seeketh not her own, is not easily provoked, thinketh no evil; Rejoiceth not in iniquity, but rejoiceth in the truth; Beareth all things, believeth all things, hopeth all things, endureth all things. Charity never faileth: but 
whether there be prophecies, they shall fail; 
whether there be tongues, they shall cease; 
whether there be knowledge [gnosis: science], it shall vanish away.
Ephesians 4:4-6 (Father is 8th above 7)
8 - and Father of all, above all & through all & in you all 
7 - one God 
6 - one baptism 
5 - one faith 
4 - one Lord 
3 - one hope 
2 - one Spirit 
1 - one body
Colossians 3:12-14 (charity is put on 8th above 7)
8 - charity: above all these things, as bond of perfectness 
7 - forgiving one another 
6 - forbearing one another 
5 - longsuffering 
4 - meekness 
3 - humbleness of mind 
2 - kindness 
1 - bowels of mercies
What is your insight about such?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

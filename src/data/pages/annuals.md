Observing non eternal things like annual holydays around the world (rooted in laws and traditions) is as if being bewitched. After we're saved (delivered from law) our focus should be on [eternal](EternalLife.md) things. So Apostle Paul admonishes churches for observing [plural](shew.md) days, and months, and times, and years he calls weak and beggarly elements. For if we compromise on little things, like observing natural birthdays, counting our age in years, observing annual holydays, it's as if we've compromised on returning to law bondage, like a dog turned to his [vomit](Law.md), a washed sow to her [mire](RevelatorySermons23.md), a fool to his [folly](RevelatorySermons24.md).

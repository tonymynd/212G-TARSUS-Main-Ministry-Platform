BIBLICAL "LORD" [Yehova] -vs- "God" CONTRASTS
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
In Noah's Ark Story: Genesis 6 - 9: God [Elohim] vs LORD [Yehova] give Noah different & contrary commands:
- 
God[Elohim] said two of each, male & female: Gen 6:19,
- LORD[Yehova] said clean by sevens, unclean by two: Gen 7:2.
note:
Story says Noah did both: all God [Elohim] commanded: Gen 6:22,
and all LORD [Yehova] commanded: Gen 7:5. 
Gen 8:20 notes Noah offered burnt offerings (sacrifice) of every clean beast & fowl to the LORD [Yehova]. 
In contrast, 
God did not see any unclean, nor desire sacrifice: Hosea 6:6.
So, based on that info, that God [Elohim] & LORD [Yehova] DIFFER, it reasons the plural LORD God [Yehova Elohim] commands to man in Genesis 2:16 & 17 are contrary commands: an oxymoron:
- Gen 2:16 says "every tree" (as "God" said in Gen 1:29),
- Gen 2:17 says not every tree (is contrary to every tree).
note:
Seems why the serpent asks woman: hath "God" said Gen 2:17? For if "God" said both every tree & not every tree it would make "God" an oxymoron: two contrary things (such as grace & law) mixed, which can be as contrary as live & die, by truth & lie mixed. 
The day of the "LORD" [Yehova] is darkness & not light: Amos 5:18.
(note: seems it may be allegory for it's law, and not grace)
In contrast: 
"God" is light, and in him is no darkness: 1Tim 6:16; 1John 1:5.
So,
Jesus Christ said in Luke 11:35
Take heed therefore that the light which is in thee be not darkness.
The day of the "LORD" [Yehova] shall come as "destruction" [sod: violence, devastation, ruin]: Isaiah 13:6 (aka not as salvation), 
In contrast,
"God" has not appointed us to wrath, but to obtain "salvation" by our Lord Jesus Christ (aka by grace & truth): 1Thessalonians 5:9.
The "LORD" both saved & destroyed people afterward: Jude 1:5.
(note: this seems to be by law is both good and evil afterward).
In contrast,
"God our Saviour" will have both all men to be saved and to come to the knowledge [epignosis] of the truth: 1Timothy 2:3,4.
So,
Jude 1:25 concludes "the only wise God" is "God our Saviour".
(note: God our Saviour is not also destroyer, but only Saviour).
LORD [Yehova] will be a "lying" spirit, speak "evil": 1Kings 22:22,23.
In contrast,
"God" cannot lie: Numbers 23:19; Titus 1:2; Hebrews 6:18.
LORD told Saul to kill every man, woman, child: 1Samuel 1:15.
In contrast,
death is the enemy of God that Jesus will destroy: 1Cor 15:26. 
Based on these, and many other biblical LORD vs God contrasts, it reasons we should put away the notion OT LORD is only good or only God; Put away such good & evil law (as if both truth & lie) to have "the only true God" which sent only "grace and truth" came by "Jesus Christ", as our Lord & Saviour: John 1:17; John 17:2.
What are your insights about LORD & God contrasts?
Jude seems to contrast OT LORD, who both saved and destroyed afterward, and God our Saviour (both saves & truths: 1Tim 2:3,4) 
to conclude 
"the only wise God" is "God our Saviour" in Jude 1:24,25.
Jude 1:24,25 KJV
Now unto him that is able to keep you from falling, and to present you faultless before the presence of his glory with exceeding joy, To the only wise God our Saviour, be glory and majesty, dominion and power, both now and ever. Amen.
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus Christ (be/is) with you all.
Amen.

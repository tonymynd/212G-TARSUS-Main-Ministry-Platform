## Content

Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Interesting that Paul often mentions be not children; As if not even better children of God (of children of wicked vs children of God):
1Corinthians 13:11 KJV
When 
I was a child, I spake as a child, I understood as a child, I thought as a child: but when I became a man [aner], I put away childish things.
1Corinthians 14:20 KJV
Brethren, be not children in understanding: howbeit in malice be ye children, but in understanding be men [teleios: full age].
Galatians 4:1 KJV
Now I say, That the heir, as long as he is a child, differeth nothing from a servant, though he be lord of all;
Ephesians 4:14 KJV
That we henceforth be no more children, tossed to and fro, and carried about with every wind of doctrine, by the sleight of men, and cunning craftiness, whereby they lie in wait to deceive;
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all.
Amen.

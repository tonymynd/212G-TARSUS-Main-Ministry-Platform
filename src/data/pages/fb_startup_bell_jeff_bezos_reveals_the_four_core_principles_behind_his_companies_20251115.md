## Four Core Principles (Jeff Bezos)

1. **Customer Obsession** - Focus on customers rather than competitors
2. **Long-Term Thinking** - Willingness to operate with longer investment horizons than peers
3. **Eagerness to Invent** - Embrace innovation and accept that failure is often part of the invention process
4. **Operational Excellence** - Take professional pride in high standards, especially for the 90% of work that nobody sees, driven by internal accountability and pride

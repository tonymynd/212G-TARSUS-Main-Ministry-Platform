Noah's Ark Story ?
Grace, mercy, peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
What's notable about Noah's Ark story of Genesis 6 - 9 is that "God" [Elohim] and "LORD" [Yehova] DIFFER, not only in Hebrew name, but in contrary commands to Noah:
God 
[Elohim] said two will do (to keep seed alive): Genesis 6:19.
LORD [Yehova] said clean by sevens & unclean by two: Gen 7:3.
Story says
Noah did all God [Elohim] commanded: Gen 6:22.
and
Noah did all LORD [Yehova] commanded: Gen 7:5.
So that would be 9 instead of only 2.
Can the ark dimensions hold 7+2=9?
But Gen 7:15 says they entered two & two,
then Gen 7:16 says only two "as God commanded".
Hmmm...
Seems "God" did not see unclean/clean,
but "LORD" did, wanting to sacrifice the clean after flood.
(but God never desired sacrifice: Ps 40:6; 51:16; Hos 6:6)
interesting "mystery" details to notice, ponder, discuss.
What are your insights about such God & LORD difference?
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus → Christ be with you all. Amen.

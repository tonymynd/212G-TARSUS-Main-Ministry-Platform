## Content

The Word of God?? Many still argue that the entire Bible is the Word of God; BUT the Bible seems to allegory contrast 2 words of 2 different Gods: Law & Grace... Until we thereby learn which one is "the only true God" & "the only wise God": "that God" is "the God of all grace" (no law at all): "the God of all comfort" (no dis-comfort at all). Allegory: that God is light (grace), and in him there is no darkness (law) at all. That God is not the God of the dead (law), but God 
of the living (grace): Mt 22:32; Mk 12:27; Lk 20:38.

## Content

SAY NO TO WAR
Grace and grace: no war. 
Law and grace: one-half war. 
Law and law: full scale war. 
If nobody takes side, small war. 
If all takes sides, world war. 
If nobody gives up, non-stop war. 
If all give up, no more war. 
If eye for eye, all eyeless. 
If tooth for tooth, all toothless. 
If life for life, all lifeless. 
In peace, children bury fathers. 
In war, fathers bury children. 
In olden days, rulers fought for the people. 
Now, people fight for the rulers.
In ancient times, rulers went out to fight. 
Now, rulers stay at home. 
At one time, ruler died for the country. 
Now, country dies because of the ruler. 
One that wins wins without war. 
None that war wins. 
War conquers none. 
Love conquers all.
War does not determine who is right but who is left. 
If there is WW3, WW4 will be fought with sticks and stones.
If we don’t end war, war will end us.
Ceasefire. Stop the war. 
Say NO to war. Say YES to peace.
World suffers because of violence. 
World suffers because of silence. 
For the sake of nations. 
For the sake of generations - to come. 
For the sake of your children. 
For the sake of our future. 
SHARE TO ALL.

COMFORT MYSTERY
God of all COMFORT [paraklesis] isn't COMFORT [paramythia]
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
I had assumed all "comfort" [paraklesis] of the "God of all comfort" in 2Cor 1:3 was no discomfort at all; Aka the best comfort possible (of good, better, best); Allegory like "all grace" is no law at all and all truth is no lie at all, and all light is no darkness at all... 
But then the Spirit prompted me to research "comfort" in Greek mode, and helped be see this:
the "comfort" [paramythia] noted in 1Cor 14: 3 is the 3rd of 3:
edification and exhortation [paraklesis] and comfort [paramythia]
but
all "comfort" in 2Cor 1:3 is [paraklesis],
which is only 2/3 of 3, and called "exhortation" in 1Cor 14:3
So then
all "comfort" [paraklesis] of God of all comfort in 2Cor 1:3 is only  like  "exhortation" [paraklesis] in 1Cor 14:3, which is only 2/3 of 3. So the best comfort is not paraklesis, but paramythia (only used once).
1Cor 14:3
But he that prophesieth speaketh unto men to edification, and exhortation [paraklesis: 2/3] and comfort [paramythia: 3/3].
2Cor 1:3
Blessed be God, even the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of mercies, and the God of all comfort [paraklesis];
What are your insights about such ?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

Bible Phrases: "most high" (48x) and "most high God" (11x) ??
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Of 11 mentions of "most high God" it seems Genesis 14:22 defines such as being the OT "LORD" [Yehova, aka JEHOVAH]. 
The 
problem with this is things said about this "LORD" [Yehova] are NOT NICE in many places like:
- Psalms 47:2 (LORD = terrible); Is 13:6 (day of LORD comes as destruction, not salvation); 
- Isaiah 45:7 (LORD creates darkness and evil); 
- Jeremiah 23:39 (LORD will forget & forsake you); 
- Ezekiel 7:24,25 (LORD brings worst, and then no peace to seek);
- Amos 5:18 (day of LORD is darkness, and not light); 
- Jude 1:5 (LORD both saved and destroyed afterward).
So, based on this info, I had not perceived a "most high God" as being like NT mentions of a NICE God, such as 
- "God of peace", 
- "God of all comfort", 
- God that "upbraideth not", 
- "God of all grace", 
- "God is love", and 
- "that God" is light having no darkness in him at all. 
Hmmm
It's also interesting to notice a phrase "most high" occurring 48x can mean 4 things:
elyon (Hebrew: also means higher, highest, above, uppermost)
marom (Hebrew: Ps 56:2 & 92:8 used for LORD=Yehova)
illay (Hebrew: in Daniel, where it says most high "God"= ela)
hypsistos (Greek: 3x in NT, also meaning highest for God=Theos).
I did not know this, perceiving "most high" was only "high" mode of high, higher, highest; 
As why Paul said mind not "high" things. 
Hmmm
What are your insights about "most high" & "most high God" ??
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

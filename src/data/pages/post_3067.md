## Content

WHAT ARE "THINGS ABOVE" (not on earth) 
to set your affection on in Colossians 3:1,2?
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Colossians 3:1,2 KJV
If ye then be risen with Christ, seek those things which are above [ano], where Christ sitteth on the right hand of God. Set your affection on THINGS ABOVE, not on things on the earth.
My first thought of things "above" [ano] was born again(from above) of water and Spirit, to be spirit (of soul/spirit), and thereby see & enter the kingdom of God within you (John 3; Luke 17:21).
For in John 8:23 Jesus said (to the Jews):
Ye are from beneath; I am from above: 
ye are of this world; I am not of this world.
Then my thoughts, re "above" went to James 3:15-17 says:
3:15 This wisdom descendeth not from above [anothen], but is earthly, sensual, devilish.
16 For where envying and strife is, there is confusion and every evil work.
17 But the wisdom that is from above [anothen] is FIRST PURE, 
then 
peaceable, 
gentle, and 
easy to be intreated, 
full of mercy and 
(full of) good fruits, 
without partiality, and 
without hypocrisy (no such Pharisee leaven in higher heaven).
then I began thinking of dual things in Greek as beneath/above:
epignosis (spiritual) knowledge: above
gnosis (science) knowledge: on earth
epistrepho converted (from above),
strepho converted (from on earth)
then the Spirit of truth prompted me to consider Genesis 2:16,17 as if comparing 2:16 above 2:17, allegory like grace above law:
Genesis 2:16,17
16 every tree (as God Gen 1:29 said) freely - Grace of God: ABOVE,
17 not every tree, surely die lie - as if law given by Moses: UNDER.
Hmmm,
I would have never noticed this unless prompted by the Spirit.
then my thought went to 2Tim 2:15 which says study to shew thyself approved unto God, as a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, RIGHTLY DIVIDING the word of truth (aka grace). 
So it allegory portrays correctly dividing the word of truth (aka grace) is not just right of left/right below, but 
above of under/above, so it's all truth, no lie.
hmmm
What are your insights about THINGS ABOVE to focus on?
The GRACE (only) of our Lord Jesus → Christ BE WITH YOU ALL. AMEN.

## Content

TEMPERANCE [egkrateia: SELF CONTROL]:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Interesting temperance [egkrateia: self control] is 9th of 9 fruit of the Spirit in Galatians 5:22,23 KJV,
and 
then
only 3rd of 7 things to add to your faith in 2Peter 1:5-8.
Also interesting in KJV is "love" [agape 1] is used for 1st fruit of Spirit, but "charity" [agape 2] is used for 7th thing to add to your faith, even above brotherly kindness seems to still have blindness.
Galatians 5:22,23 KJV
But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, goodness, faith, Meekness, temperance: against such there is no law.
2Peter 1:5-8 KJV
And beside this, giving all diligence, add to your faith virtue; and to virtue knowledge; And to knowledge temperance; and to temperance patience; and to patience godliness; And to godliness brotherly kindness; and to brotherly kindness charity.
For if these things be in you, and abound, they make you that ye shall neither be barren nor unfruitful in the knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.
Note:
that makes "charity" [agape 2] the 8th above 7:
8 - charity 
7 - brotherly kindness 
6 - godliness 
5 - patience 
4 - temperance 
3 - knowledge 
2 - virtue 
1 - your faith
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

# What is The Abomination of Desolation?  

Hint: "The abomination of desolation spoken of by Daniel the [prophet (servant)](AboveAServant.md)".  
Daniel the prophet had "night visions" (allegory: night mares) from reading a book.  
The book he was reading was the five books of Moses collectively called [The Law](Law.md).(Jesus allegorically called the five books of Moses: The Law "five in one house divided")  
God called the [darkness](ShewBread7.md#Bliss) "night"; So then Daniel had [darkness](RevelatorySermons19.md) visions = [law](Hebrews11.md) visions.  
What is "the transgression of desolation": spoken of in Daniel 8:13? It's the [Law](Law.md#Was)!  
"For [where no law is](RevelatorySermons17.md), [there is no transgression](Reconciliation.md)": [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/06-Romans/Chapter-04#^34f583|Rom 4:15]] (Paul [The Apostle](Paul.md)).  
Solomon said, 7 times, that law was "[all vanity and vexation of Spirit (Grace)](RevelatorySermons24.md)".  
So, the abomination of desolation spoken of by Daniel the prophet is the [Law](RevelatorySermons23.md).  
Which things are an allegory and a mystery; [allegoric mystery](Allegory.md) to solve in time.  
The mystery is solved by being [led of the Spirit](HGvsHS.md) and [connecting biblical dots](BiblicalDots.md).

To understand what the abomination of desolation spoken of by Daniel is, we need to connect the [biblical dots](BiblicalDots.md) concerning "the transgression of desolation" spoken of by Daniel in Daniel 8:13 and "where no law, there no transgression" spoken of by Paul in Romans 4:15 and sin being transgression of the law spoken of by John in 1John 3:4. Paul also notes in Romans 5:13 that when there is no law, no sin (transgression) is imputed, which is also Pauline noted about [reconciling the world](Reconciliation.md) in 2Corithians 5:19. So wherever and whenever no law, there is neither any transgression of desolation nor any abomination of desolation. Where and when no law is in the [conclusions](RevelatorySermons13.md) of [Pauline Epistles](PaulineEpistles.md), including [Hebrews](Hebrews.md) and [Revelation](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Revelation.md) which are also [Pauline authored](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Revelation.md#Author); And it's confirm able by [the salutary "token" of grace](Paul.md#Format) written by his own hand in each and every one of the Pauline Epistles to let us know that Paul is the author, and [his angel](Revelation2.md) (star, church leader, messenger) who [plays](RevelatorySermons13.md) the [last trump](TwainShew6.md#Trumps): [grace](GraceUntoYou.md), as the writer of more NT writings than all six other NT writers combined. Thereby where law imputed sin abounds, grace does "much more" abound, so that in the end already written, there is no mention of law at all, only of grace, will have mercy and not sacrifice, which thereby multiplies peace world wide.

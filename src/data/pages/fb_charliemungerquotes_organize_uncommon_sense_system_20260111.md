## Description

How do you borrow Charlie Munger’s system? Take conventional wisdom, then identify something that doesn’t make sense to you, and strip it out. Charlie did this by looking at how Modern Financial Theory defined risk as beta, which means volatility. Volatility isn’t risk. Risk is losing your money.…

I have a very simple system.
I call it organized, uncommon sense.
All I do is take conventional sense
and strip the acidity out of it.
And then I have uncommon sense,

instead of uncommon sense.
So my whole method of operation is just to avoid acidity.

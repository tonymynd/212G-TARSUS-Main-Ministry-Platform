## Content

When does Saul of Tarsus become Paul? Many assume Acts 9 when, after blinded by Jesus, he regains sight via Ananias at Damascus. But it's not then. It's not even Paul yet in Acts 11 where, at Antioch, they were first called Christians. It's not until Acts 13. Another thing many don't notice is Paul is still in "Christ Jesus" mode from Acts 19-28, until in Acts 28:31 that also changes to "Jesus Christ" mode. So in Romans-forward he 
always first clarifies he's now in "Jesus Christ" mode.

## Content

God contrasts & clarifiers in biblical mystery made known:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
God in the "highest" (of high, higher, highest): Lk 2:14.
"he 
is not a God of the dead, but of the living; for all live unto him": Lk 20:38 (allegory: God is not law, but grace)
"the only true God" to kinow sent Jesus Christ (aka grace & truth): John 17:3
Gifter of "eternal life", through Jesus→Christ: Rom 6:23
"God only wise": Romans 16:27
God is not (author) of confusion, but of peace: 1Cor 14:33
"God of all comfort": 2Cor 1:3
God which "cannot lie": Titus 1:2
The God who giveth "liberally" (aka solely to bless) to all and who "upbraideth not": James 1:5
The "God of all grace" (no law at all): 1Peter 5:10
"That God is light, and in him there is no darkness at all": 1Jn 1:5
The "only wise God our Saviour": Jude 1:25 
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

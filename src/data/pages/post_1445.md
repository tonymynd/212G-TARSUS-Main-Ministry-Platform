dead unto sin = dead to the law = alive unto God (aka Grace).
So,
As apostle Paul clarifies, there's only one church, called the body of Christ, who's head is Christ = the end of the law, as if through our Lord 
Jesus Christ, in his Christ mode, it's then the end of our flaw.
So,
fatherly Paul's 3 best wishes to sonly Timothy are: 
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God "our" Father, and our Lord "Jesus Christ"; 
As if yet higher Grace, mercy, and peace, than firstly from God "the" Father and our Lord "Christ Jesus" (on high); 
Like 2 different Grace, mercy, peace's in 1Tim 1:2 and 2Tim 1:2 also note a difference as to who it's "from", allegory as if from high/higher modes.

## Content

Galatians 4
WHICH THINGS (WRITTEN) ARE AN ALLEGORY.
WHAT SAITH THE (ALLEGORIC) SCRIPT-U-ARE? 
Grace to you, and peace from God the Father and our Lord Jesus Christ. 
UNTO 
THE CHURCHES of Galatia, that got bewitched, apostle Paul Galatians 3:16 reveals the "seed", not seeds, promise to Abraham IS "Christ" (is the end of the law for righteousness: Rom 10:4)...
... then in Galatians 4:22-31 Paul, as a mystery revealer, notes:
Things written (aforetime for our learning) ARE an ALLEGORY:
Eg: two sons of Abraham by two mothers ARE 2 covenants.
bondmaid Agar (mother of Ishmael) IS mount Sinai (aka law).
Jerusalem which is above is free, IS mother of us all (aka grace)...
Now we, brethren, as Isaac was, are the children of promise; But as then, he born after the flesh persecuted him born after the Spirit, even so it is now. So...
WHAT SAITH THE (ALLEGORIC) SCRIPT-U-ARE?
Cast out the bond-woman and her son...
Allegory: cast out the law and result of law: sin and death.
SO THEN, brethren, we are not children of the bondwoman, but of the free (allegory: not under the law, but under grace: Rom 6:14).
Gal 5 goes on to encourage being "led of the Spirit" (Spirit of grace & of truth: to then not be under the law 5:18); And by being Spirit Led through all 9 "fruit of the Spirit" unto temperance (self control), "against such there is no law".  
Note: 
seems fruit aspects 1 to 5 of 9 still as if law mode: for 5th of 9, gentleness, is still 'chrestotes' (aka still conditional law "goodness": still has "severity" in Rom 11:22), but 6th fruit is 'agathosyne' (aka grace "goodness": is not conditional nor has severity attached)...
Gal 6:18 salutation concludes (to "brethren", even if bewitched):
Brethren, the grace (not the law) of our Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit. Amen.

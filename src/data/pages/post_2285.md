## Content

What does God will have mercy, NOT SACRIFICE (allegory) MEAN?
Mt 9:13 But GO ye and LEARN what that meaneth, I will have mercy, and NOT SACRIFICE (from Ps 40:6; Ps 51:16; Hosea 6:6).
Mt 12:7 But if ye had known what this meaneth, I will have mercy, and NOT SACRIFICE, ye would not have condemned the guiltless.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Seems Jesus was saying there is an allegory meaning to "I will have mercy, and NOT SACRIFICE" to GO LEARN.
Seems it requires go to Hebrews 10:8 which clarifies God never desired, nor ever took pleasure in any sacrifice offerings for sin which were offered BY THE LAW. Galatians 3:11 said it's evident that  no man is justified BY THE LAW, and because the law is not of faith.
Connecting these verses it becomes more evident:
Whatsoever is NOT OF FAITH is sin: Romans 14:23b.
And the law is NOT OF FAITH: Galatians 3:12a
So it reasons I will have mercy, and NOT SACRIFICE,
may allegory mean
God will have grace, and NOT LAW.
What are your insights about the will of God Jesus Christ came (with grace and truth) to do?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

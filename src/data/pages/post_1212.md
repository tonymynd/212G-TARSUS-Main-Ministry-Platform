In biblical allegory there's 3 ppl types, not just better of 2:
them1... them2... us (of them2, but now neither of 2thems)
servants of sin... servants of righteousness... no more servants
children of wicked... children of God... no more children
OT law of Moses... NT better law of Jesus... NT grace of Christ
So, of great... greater... "greatest" of "three" things (1Cor 13:13), seems Donald Trump's 'great again' is kinda backward regressive, instead of forward progression
Seems why Romans 12:2 first talks of being transformed by renewing of your mind, then also gives this reason: to then be able to prove all three aspects of the will of God:
"good, and acceptable, and perfect". So...
Let's not stop at just being acceptable, but also go on unto perfection

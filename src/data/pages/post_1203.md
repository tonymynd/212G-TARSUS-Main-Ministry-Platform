## Content

CLARITY about the word of God (aka grace) in Hebrews 4:12 KJV gets to the "heart" of the matter: The word(logos) of God is:
- quick, and
- powerful, and
- sharper than any twoedged sword,
- piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit,…

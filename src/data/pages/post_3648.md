## Content

What the Bible reveals about LAW:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
The law was given by Moses, 
but 
grace and truth came by Jesus Christ: John 1:17,
sent from the only true God to kNOW: John 17:3.
No law = No enmity = No enemy = No jeopardy = Peace.
Law worketh wrath: Romans 4:15,
so put away wrath in Ephesians 4:31
requires put away law.
Our Father....... deliver us from evil: Luke 11:4.
Now we are delivered from the law: Romans 7:6.
Where no law, there no transgression: Romans 4:15.
When no law, then no sin is imputed: Romans 5:13.
Christ is the end of the law: Romans 10:4.
Christ abolished the enmity, the law, to make peace:
Ephesians 2:15.
The law can never make comers perfect: Hebrews 10:1.
The God of all grace (aka no law at all) makes you perfect:
1Peter 5:10.
So, 
no mention of law, sin, death, destruction in the end,
rather only grace (will have mercy, multiplies peace):
Revelation 22:21 KJV
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

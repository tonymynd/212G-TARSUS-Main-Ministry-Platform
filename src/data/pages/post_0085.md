## Content

A constant Bible theme in both OT & NT many seem UNaware of:
There is no respect of persons (partiality, dissimulation) with God.
Eg: Proverbs 24:23 KJV 
[It is] not good to have RESPECT OF PERSONS in judgment. 
Eg: 
Romans 12:9 KJV
[Let] love be without DISSIMULATION. 
So religious people saying you too can have their 'personal Saviour',
instead of "the Saviour of the world", should be made aware of this.
Eg: Proverbs 28:21 KJV
To have RESPECT OF PERSONS [is] not good
Eg: Romans 2:11 KJV
For there is no RESPECT OF PERSONS with God. 
Even those demanding 'respect' for their personal beliefs
should be withstood to the face, and dressed down for it;
As it's not good (not God) to have divisions and schisms.
Eg: 2Chronicles 19:7 KJV
Wherefore now let the fear of the LORD be upon you; take heed and do [it]: for [there is] no iniquity with the LORD our God, nor RESPECT OF PERSONS, nor taking of gifts. 
Eg: James 3:17 KJV
the wisdom that is from above is first pure, then peaceable, gentle, [and] easy to be intreated, full of mercy and good fruits, without PARTIALITY, and without hypocrisy. 
ALL is neither MANY nor FEW.
Of MANY disciples who became the chosen FEW,
a little leaven made the whole twelve a dirty dozen;
And it was not Judas Iscariot who'd got called "Satan",
and foretold he'd get 'epistrepho' converted. It was Peter.
So Paul withstood St Peter, at Antioch, about his dissimulation,
which caused Barnabas to get carried away: Galatians 2:11-14.
Paul also told childish Corinthians having divisions and schisms:
Your glorying [is] not good. 
Know ye not that a little leaven leaveneth the whole lump? 
If a jot or tiddle of f-law be applied to anyone, it does this:
makes entire world sinners: "none righteous, no, not one".
So the Lamb of God had to take away the sins of the world
by abolishing law, every jot & tiddle of such poop & piddle.
For if all law not abolished & ww sins thereby not taken away,
we get this kind of poop & piddle resulting from a jot & tiddle:
G.W. Bush's 'if you're not with us, then you're against us'
resulting in a decade of war, and loss of millions of lives.
Worse yet, we get leaders of kingdoms and nations,
(Eg: Queen, Obama, Putin, Harper, United Nations)
all saying 'rule of law' shall be the New World Order;
And if rod or iron law worketh wrath, it's ww OUCH.
For then the weather turns from Nice to NASTY ww.

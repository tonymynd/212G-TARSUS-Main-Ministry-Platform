Re the errorneous meme picture:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Re the erroneous meme picture, 
notice:
There were NOT 3 wise men [magos], but enough to trouble [tarasso] "all Jerusalem" and Herod the king who lived in another royal city: Mt 2:3; 
And 
the wise men did NOT visit the babe in the manger in Bethlehem (as the shepherds did in Luke 2); But visited the "young child" at his "house" in Nazareth to present him with 3 mentioned gifts: Matthew 2. 
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

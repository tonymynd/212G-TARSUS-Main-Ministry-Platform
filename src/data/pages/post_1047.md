YA, BUT
- YA, the law is holy, just, good, and spiritual in Romans 7:12-14,
- BUT the law is "both good and evil", so it's both just and unjust, also both holy and unholy. So then, it's lastly "evil concupiscence" and "unjust judge" and "spiritual wickedness". What's good about the law, noted in 7:13, is it makes sin become "exceedingly sinful", a crISIS, thereby a need to put away law (source & strength of sin), to a-void "law worketh 
wrath" of 'rule of law' being IMPlemented.

Insights ABOUT Revelation:
Revelation's LAMB [arnion] is NOT the LAMB [amnos] of God.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
A "Lamb" [arnion] which occurs 29x in Revelation's prophecy is NOT the "Lamb [amnos] of God" noted about Jesus in John 1:29,36. The "Lamb of God taketh away the sin of the world". In contrast, after a  Lamb slain having 7 horns & 7 eyes in Rev 5 opens a book by loose seals in Rev 5, it 
notes in Rev 6:4 peace gets taken from the earth. 
It's common to ASSUME a "Lamb" in Revelation is Jesus Christ; But it never says such. For the Lamb [arnion] has "wrath" (Rev 6:16), which Christ (is the end of the law worketh wrath) does not have. 
It's not law, but grace and truth that came by Jesus Christ (Jn 1:17), sent from the only true God (Rev 17) which cannot lie (Tit 1:2). It's like allegory saying the God of all grace cannot law.
What are your insights about Lamb [arnion] is NOT Lamb [amnos]? 
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

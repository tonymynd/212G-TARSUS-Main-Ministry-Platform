 Expired Food ("the law and the prophets") isn't safe to eat:

Law & prophets←time past isn't last days→Grace & truth.
Law & prophets had an expiry date ("fulness of the time").
Law & prophets endead at the beheading of John the Baptist.
Then the "kingdom of God"* began to get preached by "Son".
(Mt 11:13; Mk 1:14,15; Lk 16:16; Jn 1:17; Gal 4:4; Heb 1:1,2)

*"kingdom of God" doesn't occur at all in the Old Testament.

So then, law & prophets is as expired food;
And it's no longer safe to eat expired food.
That's why stores take it off of the shelves.

So the Lamb of God taketh away the sins of the world,
by the abolition of law, for law = "the strength of sin",
and sin, when "it is finished", brings "death" (not life).
All law written in stone & ink is to be done away: 2Cor 3,
because law was a ministration of condemnation & death.
God didn't send his Son to minister condemnation or death.

The best way to avoid breaching a jot or tiddle of law,
and thereby become guilty of breaching all of the law,
is to have no law to break; By flush law as dung, daily.

Survey says: everyone feels better after a good flush.

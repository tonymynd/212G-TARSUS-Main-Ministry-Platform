## Content

Generations ('genea') vs Generation ('genesis'): Plural vs Singular:
I found it a mind blowing eye opener, when prompted of the Spirit, to re-read & notice (in greek), in Matthew 1, the SINGULAR NT "generation" ('genesis') of "Jesus Christ" is not like the PLURAL OT "generations" ('genea') of Adam... 
The "generation" of "Jesus Christ" (aka grace & truth) neither begins with Adam nor even ends with Jesus; But begins with Abraham (to 
whom the "seed" promise given), and then goes through 40 "generations", even through Jesus,  (still 'genea' mode),  "UNTO Christ" (the "seed", not seeds: Gal 3:16). WOW...
That helped me ponder & reason why apostle Paul, beginning in Romans, said he was now in "Jesus Christ" mode (no longer in "Christ Jesus" mode like in Acts), and noted, in Rom 1:16,17 his gospel was now "the gospel of Christ", by which the righteousness of God is revealed; And... 
then began oft using the phrase "THROUGH our Lord Jesus Christ", as if thereby taking us THROUGH Jesus mode (still 2Sons in Mt-Acts) TO Christ mode (only Son of God mentioned beyond Acts), noting it's THRU J-C we C how we have peace with God & God given victory...
It then began to make sense why Paul told Timothy & Titus to stop giving heed to Jewish fables, and endless "genealogies" ('genea'), and contentions about the law. For...  
Hebrews 1:1,2 notes "God" has not spoken to us in time past nor by prophets, but in AD time of BC/AD by "Son" (not Sons)... 
For me it was an AHA, to notice even Jesus was still 2Sons in Mt-Acts, but then beyond Acts there's no more mention of Son of man, only Son of God. So... 
Paul said the life I now live is by the faith(grace) of the "Son of God".

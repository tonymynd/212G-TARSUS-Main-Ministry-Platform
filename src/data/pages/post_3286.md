## Content

WHAT ARE YOUR INSIGHTS ABOUT 2Thessalonians 2:11,12 ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ out Lord.
What sort of "God" would send "delusion" to "damn" people?
Would such a God accusation apply to:
the only true God cannot lie sent grace & truth by Jesus Christ?
that God in Christ reconciling world doesn't impute trespasses?
God of peace?
God our Saviour will have all saved and aware?
God who upbraideth not?
God of all grace?
God is light having no darkness in him at all?
God is love?
the only wise God?
OR
is 2Thess 2:11,12 Paul's allegoric satire about Law some think is God?
as why there seems to be a lot of delusion going to & fro on earth.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

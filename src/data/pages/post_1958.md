## Content

Grace, Mercy, Peace. Interesting in John 14 - 16 KJV it contrasts two (1st/2nd; temporal/permanent) Comforters to be sent: "Holy Ghost" & "Spirit of truth":
Eg: - Comforter 1: "Holy Ghost": John 14:26 as (law) teacher & reminder of all things Jesus had said, including both nice & nasty things. This law teacher, in OT, says "walk before me", as if thereby "driven", like 1st of 2 multitudes in Mt 21:9. Such came at Pentecost, but result 
was great fear, due to both nice & Nasty: Acts 2 - 6. Note: In Mt 12:32 & Mk 3:29 blaspheme the Holy Ghost had never forgiveness.  
Eg - Comforter 2: "Spirit of truth": John 14:16,17, as "another Comforter" is 2nd, and "will abide with you for ever", even also "be in you". Also noted in John 15:26, and in 16:13 will grace "guide" you "into all truth". So as a "guide" it's then "follow me" like Jesus said & "led of the Spirit" like Paul said. Seems why John 4:24 clarified God is a "Spirit", and Paul clarifies God has forgiven you all trespasses.  
Interesting "mystery" details to notice & discuss. The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

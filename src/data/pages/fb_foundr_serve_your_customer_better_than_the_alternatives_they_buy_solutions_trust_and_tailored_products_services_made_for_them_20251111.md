## Description

You don’t need something new, just a better way to do it.

## Transcript

So in business, you don't need to invent something to succeed.
In fact, most businesses aren't built on brand new ideas.
They're built on better execution.
You don't need to reinvent the wheel.
You just need to serve your customers better than the alternatives.
That could mean better branding, faster shipping,
or solving one specific pain point that competitors overlook.
Because customers don't buy things that are new and unfamiliar.
They buy solutions, trust, and products that feel tailor-made for them.
So focus less on being original and focus more on being better than the rest.

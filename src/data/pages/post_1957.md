## Content

ETERNAL LIFE: biblically another 3rd of three: much more than OT "everlasting life" & more than NT "life everlasting".
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
OT "everlasting life" mention ends in Rom 6:22, when 6:23 contrasted with "eternal life" is the (free) "gift of God"  through Jesus→Christ our Lord.
and
NT "life everlasting" mention also ends in 1Tim 1:16. 
So then, "eternal life" is a third of three:
OT everlasting life→NT life everlasting→NT eternal life.
Titus 1:2 clarifies only "eternal life" is what God that "cannot lie" promised before the world began. 
Yet
last mention of eternal life in Jude 1:21 says keep looking for the mercy of our Lord Jesus Christ unto eternal life,
But
no mention of mercy nor of eternal life in Revelation.
Interesting mystery details to notice, ponder, discuss. 
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

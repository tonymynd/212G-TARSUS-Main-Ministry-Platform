## Content

ETERNAL LIFE
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
Interesting how Jude 1:21 has last mention of "mercy" and of "eternal life" (neither occur in Revelation).
Jude 
1:21 KJV
Keep yourselves in the love of God, looking for the mercy of our Lord Jesus Christ unto eternal life.
Also interesting how in 1Timothy 6:12 Paul tells Timothy to fight the good [kalos] fight, then lay hold on eternal life whereunto he is ALSO called (as if eternal life is a higher calling than fight mode).
1Timothy 6:12 KJV
Fight the good fight of faith, lay hold on eternal life, whereunto thou art also called, and hast professed a good profession before many witnesses.
Interesting mystery details.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

Two Kinds of IMMORTALITY to put on
Grace, mercy, peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ. 
1Corinthians 15:54 KJV
So 
when this corruptible shall have put on incorruption [aphtharsia: also "immortality" in Rom 2:7 & in 2Tim 1:10], 
and 
this mortal shall have put on immortality [athanasia: also "immortality" in 1Tim 6:16], 
then shall be brought to pass the saying that is written, Death is swallowed up in victory.
interesting details in 1Cor 15:51-57 for 
behold I shew you a mystery
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

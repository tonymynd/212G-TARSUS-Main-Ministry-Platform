## Content

GRACE unto you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ. ~1Thessalonians 1:1
QUESTION: 
So then, what causes destruction to manifest (as if wrath)?
Romans 4:15 noted "law worketh wrath" [orge: anger, vengeance].
Isaiah 13:6 noted the day of the LORD [Yehova} shall come as  "destruction" [sod: violence, ruin; Not as salvation].
Seems 
the day of the LORD allegory portrays the day of LAW (aka wrath).
1Thess 5:2,3 notes the day of the LORD comes as a "thief" in the night, such that when they shall say Peace and safety (in the law, of the OT LORD); then sudden destruction cometh upon them.
Note: John 10:10 noted the "thief" only comes to steal, kill, destroy; But the Lord Jesus Christ (aka grace & truth) comes to bring life. 
God has NOT appointed us to wrath, BUT to obtain salvation by our Lord Jesus Christ. ~1Thessalonians 5:9
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen. ~1Thessalonians 5:28

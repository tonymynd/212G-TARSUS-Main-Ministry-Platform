When looking at biblical things, say "two covenants" (testaments), and noting such "things" (as two covenants) are an "[allegory](Allegory.md)". And when looking at such 'allegorical' things in the "greater" light (of [lessor/greater lights](TwainShew4.md#Lights)) noting that the greater light rules the day (only) of such night/day; It becomes clearer there are "three things" mentioned in 1Cor 13:13, namely: "faith hope [charity](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Charity.md)". But it's also told charity is "greatest" of "three things", "which things are an allegory" (in case you forgot). So, it should lead one who believes thinking allowed to think of three things one greatest of 'as if' [great greater greatest](GodShew.md#Greatest). Of [prophesy](Prophesy.md): edification exhortation comfort, the end to endure unto of three things is comfort in knowing of faith-ing hope-ing know-ing, as to know is safest. On the other hand, prophecy doesn't even [edify](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Charity.md#Pauline) (build up), rather puffeth up.  
  
Nobody gets from great (tribulation) to greatest (no tribulation at all) without going [through](through.md) greater of such great => greater => greatest. For it is written nobody gets to God without going through [Jesus => Christ](GodShew5.md). It is also written a "greater is here", "[that Jesus Christ is come](GodShew3.md#Second)" (that of this/that is come, "is here", "is come" to [shew](shew.md) [who is] the blessed and [only](GodShew3.md#Only) Potentate; allegorically the [King of Kings and Lord of Lords](Allegory1.md) in 1Tim 6:15, who is the "greatest" of great => greater => greatest things such as Moses => Jesus Christ => God, in such an allegorical shew about what's all right of  [law](Law.md) => [law/grace](TwainShew.md) => [grace](GraceUntoYou.md)).  
  
Moses was great, but his law was a "great terror", making Moses a great terror-ist (Deut 34:12). Jesus Christ is greater, both a "greater" prophet and king than Jonas and Solomon (Mt 12: 41,42). And JC spoke of an even greater than himself, hence greater than greater, allegorically making his one true God of [false/true](TwainShew6.md#Spirits) [Gods](TwainShew3.md#Gods) ([Holys](TwainShew3.md#Holys)) the "greatest" of such great greater greatest things; just as charity greatest of three things and charity out of a [pure](RevelatorySermons7.md) (singular) heart [the end](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) of the commandment: Love(God) one another, "as I have loved [you](YeYou.md)"; in accordance with ye/you scenarios of ye=>you in Jn 8:32 & 1Pet 5:10 since all the KofG is located within "you", so God hath forgiven "you" applies to all.  
  
It's also written ([also told](CurrentEvents.md) ww): [To wit](ShewBread7.md#Bliss), "[that God](ShewBread5.md#IAMTHAT)" was in "Christ" ([the end of the law](ShewBread9.md#Expired)) [reconciling the world](Reconciliation.md) unto [himself](GodShew2.md#Allness) (2Cor 5:19). And when it comes to the greatest and his (seventh) day, namely [the day of God](RevelatorySermons19.md), well, there's no night/day (darkness/light, ignorance/understanding) in such an eternal day, only light (understanding). Hence: "[with all thy getting, get understanding](GodsheworgExplanation.md)", y(our) understanding being the only goal of [Godshew.org](GodShew-Home.md)), a [third day](ThirdDay.md) ministry.  
  
For there is no mention of both evening and morning on the seventh day, and "that God is light, and in him there is no darkness at all"; Which is what(?) we have heard, and declare unto [you](YeYou.md), from [the beginning where the end declared](TheShew4.md) (Is 46:10; 1Jn 1:1-5): "let there be light"(understanding); Which is perhaps allegoric for no ignorance in [that God](ShewBread5.md#IAMTHAT) of [this/that Gods](ShewBread7.md#Worlds) [opposed about "them"](ShewBread6.md#Gods), no foolishness in "the [only wise God](GodShew3.md#Only)" of foolish/wise [Gods](TwainShew3.md#Gods), no lie in "the only true God" of false/true [Spirits](TwainShew6.md#Spirits)(Gods), and [no law](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) in "[the God of all grace](GodShew2.md#Allness)".  
  
Such also leads one, who believes thinking allowed, to also ponder three things 'as if' they're [good better best](GodShew.md#GBB), smart smarter smartest, and safe safer safest. Now consider three things as if they're progressively safe => safer => safest, with safe/safer only for some, them/us, whereas safest is for all, not some only. But such all in the latter part of the two part (old/new) shew is [just us](ShewBread2.md#justus).  
  
Hence what(?) the Lord (now that Spirit, of such then/now and this/that) is longsuffering unto is [us-ward](Usward.md), and the reason given is so none should perish. So all [them](ShewBread2.md#inthem) convert to [us](OneLiners.md#J), in the manner [ye (do err) become you (made perfect)](YeYou.md), by a [changE](biblicalemblems.md) of this=>[that](GodShew3.md#Second) [minds](TwainShew4.md#Minds), [faiths](TwainShew2.md#Faiths), [glorys](TwainShew3.md#Glorys), [mtns](TwainShew4.md#Mountains), one of [twain](TwainShew.md) by taking the [grace walk](GraceWalk.md) [from Sinai to Sion](Sevens2.md) [through](through.md) the valley of the shadow of death, which is notably a cHange from "not come" to "come" in Heb 12's two mtns.  
  
Such victory us Sion peace with God is allegorically through [Jesus=>Christ](GodShew5.md). Else none can escape such all [grounded](GodShew4.md) till all get it: [understanding](Topics.md), since there is "no respect of persons with God", neither with law God (in Adam all die) nor with grace God (in Christ all live). It's never some only with God, nor is it ever grace + law (good + evil, life + death) with "the God of all grace", rather all grace and no law at all, as "that God" is all light and no darkness at all. For as Solomon clarified oft, OT grace + law is "all vanity and vexation of Spirit"; the [fault](Topics.md) thereof being law (worketh wrath) added to "grace is sufficient".  
  
Perhaps to get a grasp on such, let's look at three things as (i) earth (ii) heavens (iii) heaven; earth being lowest, plural [heavens](TwainShew3.md#Heavens) are "higher than the earth", and singular heaven is "[higher than the heavens](RevelatorySermons21.md)".  
  
the "earth" - where 1/3 of stars of heaven cast down to in Revelation 12  
the (plura) "heavens" - "higher than the earth", but still kinda [law law](TwainShew4.md#Laws)  
the (singular) "heaven" - "higher than the heavens" and grace us only  
  
Note: of earth => heavens => heaven, it seems heavens is in the midst of such, kinda like "firmament" in Gen 1 is in the midst of waters under and above to divide such, then plural lights are allowed to be in the firmament called Heaven, to divide darkness/light, night/day, the greater light ruling the day of such night/day, evening/morning. Yet God did not say let there be lights in the beginning where God declared the end. Rather God said: "let there be light". So it reasons of plural lights (understandings), there is one false and one true; the objective being to try such twain spirits, see for yourself [which is witch](TwainShew8.md).  
  
Now when it comes to safe => safer => safest, obviously some feel safe on earth, others feel safer in heavens, but the safest place of all and for all is a singular heaven. And therein also a singular name above earth and heavens, the [hallowed name](ShewBread6.md#Hallowed) "grace", the "name" of our Father, and the "name" of Jesus Christ, also the "name" by which ye are called, to "come", come to repentance ([change](biblicalemblems.md) of [mind](ShewBread3.md#dblem), [change of faith](TwainShew2.md#Faiths), [change of glory](TwainShew3.md#Glorys), [change of mtn](Sevens2.md) which is notably a change from "not come" to "come"), in order to come home to the place that's "higher than the heavens", the safest place of all and for all.

The only true God sent Jesus Christ.
Grace and Truth came BY Jesus Christ, yes, but
Peace and Victory are THROUGH Jesus→Christ;
For Jesus was only mend of the law: Mt 5:43,44,
But "Christ is the end of the law": Romans 10:4.

So "_through him_" (John 3:17)
is "_through his name_" (John 20:31)
is "_through Jesus→Christ_" (Romans x7)
Because
Two Sons Jesus was both salvation and damnation, but
One Son Christ is global salvation and no condemnation.

So we are not called Jesus-ian, but called _Christ-ian_.
We are not the body of Jesus, but _the body of Christ_.

So then, the gospel of Paul is not the gospel of Jesus;
The gospel of Paul is "_the gospel of Christ_": Rom 1:16.

2Corinthians 5:19,20 KJV
_To→wit, that God was in Christ,
reconciling the world unto himself,
not imputing their trespasses unto them; and
hath committed unto us the word of reconciliation.
Now then we are ambassadors for Christ_ (not Jesus).

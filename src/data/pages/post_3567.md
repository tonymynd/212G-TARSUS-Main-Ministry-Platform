## Content

God is harmless, by being perfect and also merciful; not also merciless. God is always harmless, never harmful. So let us not think God changes, but rather it is we all who get changed x2; first outwardly from child to man, then inwardly from law to grace. For born again is not what happens to a child, but to a man, and the biblical order is natural first, spiritual afterward; But if law be spiritual afterward such spiritual abuse is harmful instead of harmless. By 
Daniel Miles

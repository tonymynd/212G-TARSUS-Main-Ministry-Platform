Spiritual Ears:  Hearing God Speak
AS I read this Story, All I kept thinking about was the Ears will ALL need to Truly Hear the Voice of God speak in Our spirits. This article is about Carnal Ears, Physical, and the more I listened to it, the More I Understood why so many People do not Listen to their Spirits within. A Deaf person uses their Eyes  to Hear as Well. In a universal sign of LOVE, they Listen In a Unique Way Using A Double-Shot of the Spirits Ears.   The same goes 
for the Blind, as They Do See, Read and Hear just as Well as WE Do that are Blessed with ALL Functions. The next time you see your fellow Brother's and Sister's who lack Our Vision, Do not Look the Other Way, or Pity Their Lives. These special Children of God are very in Tune with God, and WE can Learn Many things from Them.  It would do US All Good, to Deafen our Carnal Ears, and Blind Ur Collective Eyes, and See the Beauty of Christ, within Us All.  Blessings on the World as We Are the Children. Let's Make a Brighter Day, and Keep On Living--In Love I Am MicHEAL Our World Jesus, I AM A LIGHT.

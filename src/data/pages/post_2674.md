## Content

"rightly dividing" [orthotomeo] the word of truth ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
We ass-u-me "rightly dividing" is right of left/right, but perhaps rightly dividing the word [logos] of truth (aka the grace of God) is to above of under/above.
For 
when grace is only right of law/grace it's still division and partiality, but when grace is above of under/above it's the pure grace wisdom that is without partiality and without hypocrisy. 
It's why Jesus Christ said:
in John 3 Ye must be born again [anothen: from above] to see and enter the kingdom of God. 
I am from above [ano] of beneath/above in John 8:23.
It's why Paul said:
seek those things which are above [ano] in Colossians 3:1,2.
It's why James 3:17 said:
wisdom that is from above [anothen] is first pure (then 7 things).
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

Lent season... leading to Passover-Easter?
Season Peter, called Satan, bound & loosed.
Another stabbing. More senseless violence.
Another boat sinking. More troubled water.
A blood moon, sign-ifying more blood shed.
Stupid tv shows like marvel's agents of steel.
More winter, 3 weeks after officially spring.
More media reporting of 'worst's & 'death' tolls,
things that should only occur in Old Testament.
Makes you wanna ask: who let the dogs out?
Apostle Paul names three top dogs in Galatians 2,
says beware, beware, beware, thrice in Philippians 3,
Beware of dogs, as if evil workers, and as if concision;
"concision" being defined as cut up, as if by mutilation.

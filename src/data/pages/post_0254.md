## Content

Now Available on Netflix: 2011 sci-fi movie: In Time.
It seems to have three different biblical themes in it:
- Nobody should immortality if even one person has to die
- If your time is running out, You Better Run: 1Cor 9:24-27
- Another one ran out of time, running to obtain corruptible
Seems to bring out these three in 1Corinthians 15:52
- Immortality: 3rdOf3 (an UpGrade from BornAgain)
- Incorruption: 2ndOf3 (BornAgain, UpGrade from Born)
- Corruptible: 1stOf3
Interesting stuff to ponder, when noticing this in 2Cor 5:14:
the love(God) of Christ had to constrain us prior to reconcile us
because we thus judged if one died, then the result was all dead

## Content

For those who believe (assume) "God" is "LORD" in OT, as I did, here's what I finally learned: God (elohyim) & LORD (Yahovah) are DIFFERENT commanders of Noah in Genesis 6 & 7. God said 2 will do, but LORD said clean x7 & unclean x2. So not the same.
Then... 
I noticed this, re LORD (Yahovah) in Amos 5:18 KJV:
"Woe unto you that desire the day of the LORD! to what end [is] it for you? the day of the LORD [is] darkness, and not light".
Seems why...
JC said this in Luke 11:35 KJV:
"Take heed therefore that the light which is in thee be not darkness".
So...
I finally put away LORD, to have Grace, mercy, peace from God

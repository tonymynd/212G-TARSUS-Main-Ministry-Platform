Puny scenario. For me, this morning, as if to allegory-ize about this:

We (finally) have peace with God (that lasts) THRU our Lord J→C: Rom 5:1...We are delivered from the law [as if Our Father's response to deliver us from evil: Mt 6:13; Lk 11:4]: Rom 7:6...For Christ is the end of the law [dung: Phil 3]: Rom 10:4... Eg:

Got up, had a pee to relieve unrest, Felt Better (thanks Jesus); But went back to bed, to soon (one hour) find out this peace (feel better) didn't last; So ALSO had a poop, to then Feel Best (thanks Christ)...

I laughed about it, and thought I could share it as if a puny allegory story, about how ppl can & do feel better (for a while) in Jesus mode, of CJ; Yet still suffer many afflictions, like Paul of Saul/Paul did, while still in CJ mode... Until he later got in JC mode, then ALSO went THROUGH J→C to be in that Christ is the end of the law, not just the mend of the law.

Eg:

in Philippians, Paul (a JC writing to CJs)

- greets CJs with GraceToU & peace from God our Father & JC;

- then prays for them, that 'epignosis' knowledge may increase (b/c in CJ mode they only have 'gnosis' knowledge: Phil 1:9; 3:8);

- then turns CJ around to JC, encourages CJs to, so their tongue can also confess JC is Lord to the glory of the Father (Phil 2:5-11);

- then notes he flushed all his legalism (self-righteousness of law), as if flushing "dung", to WIN Christ (Phil 3:8);

- then says, in 4:8, FINALLY, brethren,

whatsoever things are true (not both false/true),

whatsoever things [are] honest (not both lie/truth),

whatsoever things [are] just (not both unjust/just),

whatsoever things [are] pure (not both evil/good),

whatsoever things [are] lovely,

whatsoever things [are] of good report (not evi);

if [there be] any virtue ('arete', not 'dunamis'), and

if [there be] any praise (commendation, not condemnation),

think on these things.

- then concludes, in 4:23, The grace of our Lord JC (not just CJ) be with you all. Amen.

POINT: only when I flushed my dung (legalism) did I feel best, at total rest, and for the rest of the day, which began with feel better.

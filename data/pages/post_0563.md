## Content

PTSD? Root cause of it seems to be evil concupiscence*; And
the Root cause of such concupiscence is Law: Romans 7 KJV,
Where Apostle Paul tried a NT Law he'd thought was unto life,
but found it brought him into captivity to OT Law, which did all manner of concupiscence on him til he was wretched & suicidal.
*concupiscence of law is an overwhelming evil desire to do what law forbids, or not do what law demands; Which then also hounds the victim with guilt until the victim feels wretched and suicidal.

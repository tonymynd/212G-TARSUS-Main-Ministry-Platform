One of twain is obviously the false God of [false/true Gods](TwainShew6.md#Spirits), the foolish God of foolish/wise [Gods](ShewBread7.md#Worlds). And such a [Phantom Menace](Law.md), mirrorly imagined as God by [children](GodShew.md#nomorechildren), [servants](TwainShew6.md#Servants), and fools (hypocrites, blind guides) who sit in [Moses' seat](Allegory4.md), is obviously not "the blessed and only Potentate"... in such things are an "[allegory](Allegory.md)", as a "mystery" to solve "[in time](Hebrews5.md)" (there's no mystery, nor time for such, in "eternity"), as a "[shew](shew.md)" (to shew "in his [times](TwainShew6.md#Times)" who is the blessed and only Potentate); And such scriptures (plura) were notably written "aforetime" for our "learning": Romans 15:4. In the Holy Bible containing Old/New Testaments both law/grace (the law was given by Moses, but grace came by Jesus Christ) are [allegorically](Allegory.md) compared as old/new, first/second, false/true, this/that, lie/truth, foolish/wise. Two Gods, or one (plura) God (Elohyim) divided for the sake of a God shew (plural of show), are portrayed in the form of a mystery to solve in time, the purpose being to [shew](shew.md) who is the only Potentate of twain as if two slices. But when it comes to [God is one slice](ShewBread2.md#OneSlice), we are to rightly divide the Word of truth about Grace or Law ("either make the tree good or evil") to one only: "the only true God", "the only wise God", the blessed and only Potentate". Yet "the God of [all grace](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md)" is more than just grace of law/grace, as is the "[fulness](GodShew2.md#Allness) of Christ" is more than just Christ: the end of the law for righteousness. For "much more" abounding grace in "where [sin](http://pub30.bravenet.com/forum/2526810710/fetch/344590/) abounded, grace did much more abound" is grace which does not impute sin: [global (universa) reconciliation](Reconciliation.md).

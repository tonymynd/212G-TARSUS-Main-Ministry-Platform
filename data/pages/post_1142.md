## Content

Clarity By Contrast. Eg: two different biblical Comforters are best seen & contrasted in KJV (not in modern bibles):
- Spirit of truth: John 14:17; 15:26; 16:13 KJV = "guide" = "follow me" = Grace led to "God of all comfort" = comfort only.
- Holy Ghost: John 14:26; 16:8-12 KJV = "teacher" = walk before me = Law driven to God of goodness+severity = both comfort+discomfort=Ouch, as portrayed in Romans 11:22.
Recommend using KJV for Clarity By Contrast; Because:
at 
Pentecost, when Holy Ghost came upon eleaven apostles, it got NASTY: KILL (not forgive) Ananias & Saphirra, resulted in "great fear" (not perfect love void of fear) coming upon whole church (still assembling in a Law Temple); Acts 1-5.

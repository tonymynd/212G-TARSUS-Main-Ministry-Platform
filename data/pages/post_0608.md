## Content

Susan McCook QUESTion, worthy of share: Which me, of 1000's of ever changing me's, changing even moment by moment, should I be judged as?

The fact is that I am not the same person I was 10 years ago or 20 years ago - and people who do not like me now might have liked one of the older "me"s and people who 
like me a lot now might not have liked the older "me" that much. I might even have been a thousand versions of me when its all said and done. So which "me" should be judged as "me"? Who I was or am or will be at any given point in this existence in this body on planet earth - or the "me" that exists beyond and through it all?

Well as for my 2015 Year in Review--It's been a year of Awakenings for me. 
Life altering changes. Bountiful Blessings , A Wonderful World of Friends that shines LOVE so Bright, I can See It, Feel It, and Even touch you with a Smile.
 
Health struggles and triumphant Victories of Healing. Holding my Boozie Bear for the last time was a huge impact on my life in October. I've seen friendships flourish, 
families bond, and many separate, no speak, no words.
My Facebook pals, My Family, My Church, Helping Hand Agency have All Been my Miracles this year. Through combined efforts of Unity, I am doing alright Everyone. I want to say Thank You to God, The Almighty Father, of All Creations for sending me the presence of Angels in August that surrounded me. That to me is All the Confirmation I needed Lord to serve you as a Proud Soldier in your Army of God, as we Spread Truth, and Love Across All Nations.
I Am your vessel God, I Am Yours. 
 Michael 
I welcome 2016 as the year of Complete restoration, and doing mightier works for God, and My Savior, Jesus Christ.  Amen 
  My Prayer is a simple one: For ALL togo deep within and find the Kingdom, Heaven, and LOVE EVERYBODY Everywhere you go: Love is All there is.

re LOVE [agape1] & CHARITY [agape2] in Holy Bible
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ. Interesting 'agape' in Greek means both love & charity in English; In this manner: 
Matthew 24:12 (re love: agape1)
And because iniquity shall abound, the LOVE [agape1] of many shall wax cold. 
(allegory as if shall go bwd to cOLD←>LukeWarm→Hot)
1Corinthians 13:7,8 (re charity: agape2)
Beareth all things, believeth all things, hopeth all things, endureth all things.
CHARITY [agape2] never faileth: but 
whether there be prophecies, they shall fail; 
whether there be tongues, they shall cease; 
whether there be knowledge [gnosis], it shall vanish away.
Seems by modern bibles change charity back to love for 1Cor 13, just bc both mean 'agape' in Greek, and 1Jn 4 says God is love [agape1], modern bibles thereby contradict themselves by say two contrary things: both love "shall" wax cold & love "never" fails.
Seems divorce has proven love of many shall wax cold.
Seems why Holy Bible (KJV) uses both love & charity.
Could love & charity "allegory" be law & grace agape's?
(allegory like favour & grace both mean 'charis' in Greek)

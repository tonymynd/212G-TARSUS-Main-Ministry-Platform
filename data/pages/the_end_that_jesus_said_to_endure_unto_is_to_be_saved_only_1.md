THE END that Jesus said to ENDure→unto, is to be saved only:
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
It's interesting to notice: on the way unto the end, to be saved only, we see in [Jude 1:5](https://eng.global.bible/bible/de4e12af7f28f599-02/JUD.1?passageId=JUD.1.5) a reminder the OT "LORD" [Yehova: Jehovah] both saved and afterward destroyed (is a bad end): [Jude 1:5](https://eng.global.bible/bible/de4e12af7f28f599-02/JUD.1?passageId=JUD.1.5),
But for contrast
in [1Timothy 2:4](https://eng.global.bible/bible/de4e12af7f28f599-02/1TI.2?passageId=1TI.2.4) it noted "God [Theos] our Saviour" (doesn't destroy after save) will have ALL men both saved and made aware of higher knowledge [epignosis, higher than gnosis] of the truth after.
So, along the way unto the end, [Jude 1:5](https://eng.global.bible/bible/de4e12af7f28f599-02/JUD.1?passageId=JUD.1.5) & 1:25 contrast concludes (of twain: LORD vs God for contrast) that only "God our Saviour" is "the only wise God".
So the end to endure→unto, to be saved only, is Paul's (not John's) grace salutation, which Paul had thrice noted he always penned his grace salutation with his own hand:
The GRACE of our Lord Jesus Christ BE WITH YOU ALL. AMEN.

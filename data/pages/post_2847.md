Interesting biblical info re "CHRIST JESUS" & "JESUS CHRIST":
Thanks people in "Christ Jesus" for your zealous focus on the GRACE of God. 
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
YET PLEASE ALSO NOTE:
Interesting how NKJV & NIV bibles have changed "Jesus Christ" to "Christ Jesus" in several verses, especially in Romans 6:23, or added "Christ Jesus" to Ephesians 2:8,9. 
Hmmm.
While there is no condemnation to them in "Christ Jesus" (Romans 8:1), and in "Christ Jesus" there is wisdom, and righteousness, and sanctification, and redemption (1Cor 1:30), 
there are these biblical matters to ALSO consider:
- The term "Christ Jesus" used 57x ONLY occurs from Acts 19:4 to 1Peter 5:14 KJV, and is ONLY used by Paul 55x & 1Peter 2x. 
(as if in contrast, "Jesus Christ" occurs 196x from Mt 1:1 to Rev 22:21 KJV, and is used by all NT writers) 
- In Acts, even after name change of Saul to Paul (Acts 13, not 9 nor 11), THEN there is ALSO another change for "Paul": from "Christ Jesus" mode in Acts 19:4 to "Jesus Christ" mode in Acts 28:31. 
So,
THEN, in Romans → forward, Paul always 1st clarifies he is now in "Jesus Christ" mode, and always turns his mention of "Christ Jesus" around to "Jesus Christ" in every epistle, including in Hebrews 3:1 KJV says "Christ Jesus" to Hebrews 10 - 13 KJV says "Jesus Christ".
- ONLY "Jesus Christ" is "the same yesterday, and to day, and for ever", as noted by Paul in Hebrews 13:8 KJV. Such is never said about "Christ Jesus", called the new High Priest (of NT law). 
- 1Timothy 1:15 KJV does note "Christ Jesus" came into the world to "save sinners"; BUT "Christ Jesus" is never called "Saviour", a term used ONLY for and through "Jesus Christ". 
So 
the NT Title says: The New Testament (not new covenant) of our Lord and Saviour "Jesus Christ"; 
And 
the KJV NT both opens and closes with "Jesus Christ", not removing the word "Christ" from the end as the NIV translation does.
Considering these things are not trivia, please consider who has published the NIV bible translation, and how it changes "charity" to "love" for Greek 'agape', and also "Ghost" to "Spirit" for Greek 'pneuma", so NIV contradicts itself by saying both:
love of man shall wax cold (Mt 24:12) & love never fails (1Cor 13:8),
also contradicts itself by saying both,
blasphemy the Spirit has never forgiveness (Mt 12:32; Mk 3:29) & God is Spirit (John 4:24) has forgiven you all (Eph 4:32; Col 2:13).
Romans 6:23 AKJV (does NOT say "in Christ Jesus" as NIV does):
For the wages of sin is death; but the gift [charisma] of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord.
Why?
Notice the 'charisma' (free) gift of God: eternal life, 
is Rom 6:23 KJV noted as THROUGH Jesus → Christ our Lord.
(for in that Christ it is then all things new, noted in 2Cor 5:17) 
Notice "reconciled" to God (as if yet more than redemption in "Christ Jesus") is ONLY by "Jesus Christ" in 2Cor 5:18 KJV.
(note: there is no mention of reconciliation about "Christ Jesus").
Notice THE END: Revelation 22:21 KJV, 
which Jesus Mt 24:12 said to ENDure unto to be saved (aka saved only, not both saved & destroyed after like reminded in Jude 1:5 about OT nice & nasty LORD Yehova = Jehovah) is: 
ONLY the grace salutation of Paul, NEVER of John:
(Rom 16:23 ... Phil 4:23 ... 2Thess 3:17,18 ... Revelation 22:21 KJV)
always written by Paul's "own hand", as noted in 2Thess 3:17,18 KJV,

**GRACE IS SO EXCEEDINGLY MUCH MORE giving, abounding, glorious;**

**Law finally fades to ZERO by compare-i-son in the end of the God shew.**

Eg: Romans 5:17 KJV (one man vs one man compare-i-son)

_For if by one man's offence death reigned by one;_

_MUCH MORE they which receive abundance of grace_

Eg: Romans 5:20 KJV (law abounds, but grace much more abounds)

_Moreover the law entered, that the offence might abound._

_But where sin abounded, grace did MUCH MORE abound_

Eg: Revelation 22:21 KJV (Law = Zero: not even mentioned in the end)

## Content

Interesting what we can discern & learn when we get prompted by the Spirit to connect some Bible verses. Eg:
- Our Father...deliver us from EVIL: Mt 6:13 (Lk 11:4)...
- We are delivered from the LAW: Romans 7:6. AHA...
Allegory meaning: "evil" = "law" = reverse of "live". So then, 
charity(grace) thinketh "no evil" = no law: 1Cor 13:5 KJV; As how "charity" (grace void of law) "never faileth": 1Cor 13:8.
- whatsoever is NOT OF FAITH is sin: Romans 14:23
- And the law is NOT OF FAITH: Galatians 3:12. AHA...
Reasons law was what produced sin (flaw) consciousness; So take away the sin of the world = take away the law (flaw), to then see & grow in grace, truth thereof, unto higher righteousness of "God of all grace" = neither law, nor even law/grace duality, like "the only true God" = neither false nor false/true duality. 
Seeing such things helped me to put away all my legalism.

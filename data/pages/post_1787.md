## Content

Let God be true, but every man[anthropos] a liar (Romans 3:4). It's not said for accusation but awareness 'anthropos' man needs mind renewal to be transformed into 'aner' man.
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Spirit helped me see that hidden in NT Greek mode there's 2 old← & →new kinds of "man": 'anthropos' ← & → 'aner'; And when the law← & →grace difference is studied out, I would  gladly 
decide to stop being an 'anthropos' sort of man by put away childish things to become an 'aner' man: 1Cor 13:11. 
- 'anthropos' man is a judge-mental self-condeming liar: Romans 2:1; 3:4.
- blessed is the 'aner' man to whom the Lord will not impute sin: Romans 4:8
- the head of every 'aner' man is Christ (the end of the law): 1Cor 11:3
- God our Saviour will have all men[anthropos] saved, and come to the knowledge[epignosis] of the truth: 1Tim 2:4.
To learn more about anthropos & aner, use Blue Letter Bible to bring up verses mentioned, then use Interlinear Tool to give Greek, then follow link to get all mentions of such.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

**TRULY** **Y(OUR)S: God our Father

And "very good" is [allegoric](Allegory.md) for 'truly good'(only).  
  
POINT: [True love](TwainShew4.md#Loves) never sees good + evil, only good.  
For grace + law an oxy[MORON](DahQuiz.md) with a [BAD](lawphoto.md) ending.  
[His](GodShew2.md#Allness) [grace](ShewBread6.md#Hallowed): [firstly](TwainShew7.md#Wisdoms) [pure](RevelatorySermons7.md)**(undefiled), **[remains](ShewBread.md#contraryto) [pure](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md)**(unspotted)!

[Grace unto you](GraceUntoYou.md), and peace,  
[from God our Father](GodShew2.md#Allness) and the Lord Jesus Christ  
  

[Genesis 1](Bibles.md): [seven times](Sevens.md) mention is made of what(?) "God saw". And six times God(Love) saw (only) "good" (not good + evi). The seventh time looking Love(God) saw "very good", which is truly good. So Love(God) is not blind as many ass u me, rather God(Love) sees "good" for all six millennial days, and "very good" the seventh ([non millennial](http://pub30.bravenet.com/forum/2526810710/show/281273)) day, which is also the third day. It is now the [prophetic third day](ThirdDay.md), so [it's time](ShewBread8.md#Time) for [us](Usward.md) [all](Reconciliation.md) to see very (truly) good.  
  
The POINT of God seeing only good and very good, in the two part Godshew about a third part getting drawn away by the tail of a dragon and cast down to earth... then [reconciled unto God](Reconciliation.md), is: that perfect love sees only good, not good + evil, for good + evil is an oxyMORON with a BAD ending. God, who is only good, firstly good ([firstly pure](TwainShew7.md#Wisdoms)), always good, never begins anything with a bad end such as good + evil, for beginning such would end [bad](lawphoto.md) for all.  
  
God sees sow good he even sees [the end](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) from the beginning is (only) good, else God would never begin such a [shew](shew.md) if the ending of it was uncertain. Both [Isaiah 46:10 and 1John 1: 1-5](Bibles.md) reveal the end is God declared from the beginning as being only good (not good + evi). For in saying "let there be light([understanding](GodsheworgExplanation.md))", not [lights](TwainShew4.md#Lights) nor light + darkness (not two understandings nor the misunderstanding of understanding + [miss](Law.md) "added", witch is as truth plus lie added, witch is as merciful grace plus "law worketh wrath" added).  
  
For "[that God](ShewBread5.md#IAMTHAT)" of [this/that](ShewBread7.md#Worlds) [Gods](TwainShew3.md#Gods) is "light" only (light only, not two lights nor light + darkness; good only, not good + evil; grace only, not grace + law). 1John 1:5 "[that God](Reconciliation.md) is light, and in him there is [no darkness at all](GodShew2.md#Allness)". Such is what(?) we have heard, and declare unto [you](YeYou.md), "from the beginning". Selah: read it again, thinking about it (thinking is allowed), and let it sink in. The only true God of [false/true Gods](TwainShew6.md#Spirits), [the only wise God](GodShew3.md#Only) of foolish/wise Gods, is only good of evil/good, is only light of darkness/light, is only [understanding](GodsheworgExplanation.md) of [ignorance](ShewBread7.md#Bliss)/[understanding](Topics.md), is only grace of law/grace.  
  
What(?) God saw: good (not good + evi). [What(?) God will have](RevelatorySermons6.md): only [mercy](ShewBread5.md#willx2) (not sacrifice nor mercy + sacrifice, witch was notably "all vanity and vexation of spirit", and 'never pleased God': Ps 40; Heb 10). What(?) God will not have (not then, not now, not ever): [law](Law.md) worketh wrath, [infirmities](Hebrews8.md), even death to all, the dead end thereof such grace + law, [is as](Allegory.md) life + death = a dead end.  
  
[No dead end](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) in the Holy Bible where the end is God declared from the beginning as only good six times and very good the seventh time looking at what was "made" of created/made (plura) things, "which things are an allegory". Hence it is "you" of ye/you who is "made" perfect in 1Peter 5:10, and notably by "the God of all grace". Similarly JC being ["made" perfect](Hebrews6.md) became the ["author"(only)](RevelatorySermons17.md) of "eternal salvation" of [temporal/eternal salvations](TwainShew6.md#Salvations).  
  
So of things created/made, such as them/us, in such a before/after God shew, first Adam was (created) a living soul, last Adam was (made) a quickening spirit. What(?) God saw as good only, is what was "made" of created/made. I say again, for clarity: what God saw as good (only) is what God "made" of such created/made things, [which things are an allegory](Allegory.md). To understand the Holy Bible, moral thereof: "grace is sufficient" (no law req'd), it is imperative to understand such things are an "allegory", and are not to be taken literally.  
  
Of them/us, [wrath](RevelatorySermons18.md)([law](Law.md)) appointed to "them": 1Thes 5:3, not "us": 1Thes 5:9. Who are "them" in such an allegory? Genesis 5 tells us "them" are Adam, both male and female; whereas God is one, of us, with us, for us, and hath given us the victory "[through](through.md)" [Jesus => Christ](GodShew5.md) (not Christ Jesus, witch is [mirrorly](SAQs.md) the reverse of Jesus Christ; for [peace](PeaceLinks.md) with God is notably through "Jesus Christ").  
  
[Them](ShewBread2.md#inthem), the host of them (prepared for war) and their plural heavens get "finished" (Gen 2:1) first in the allegoric shew of Old/New (first/last) Testaments.  Such is after the sixth day and before the seventh day, which is really no place; Yet for the sake of the shew the place of a last days [shewdown](Revelations2.md#Rev4) of [two sabbaths](TwainShew6.md#Sabbaths): ([thief-y](Law.md)) day of the LORD -vs- day of God (2Peter 3). The real Sabbath, seventh day, day(light) of God, is an eternal day(light); So seventh day in Gen 2 has no mention of both evening + morning as six other days in Genesis 1 do. Pls remember: it's an "allegory": Gal 4:24, and such things were written "aforetime" for our "learning": Rom 15:4.  
  
[Us](OneLiners.md#J), and there is now an [ever increasing cry for just us](ShewBread2.md#justus), are to make "man". God said: let "[us](Usward.md)" make "man", before giving two allegoric options which seem to get played out in [all the worlds a stage](OneLiners.md#Shakespeare) for all to see better [which is witch](TwainShew8.md) of such [twain](TwainShew.md) things allegorically portrayed as law/grace. The two before/after options for making (one) man are: (i) in our image (ii) after our likness. So the "likeness" part, the "express image" of God's person, is the "after" part of such a before/after shew, kinda like the priesthood of JC is "after" the priesthood of Melchisedec, just as the eternal part of temporal/eternal salvations is "after" we all get it: understanding. For in the eternal realm of [His Grace](GodShew2.md#Allness), understanding is what prevails among all, lest any add law worketh wrath to grace is sufficient.  
  
Hence it is written: "with all thy getting, get understanding" (Solomon: Prov 4); It is also clarified ([also told](CurrentEvents.md)) what sort of understanding to get: the sort which when embraced results in thy head (Christ: "[the end of the law](ShewBread9.md#Expired)", is the head of every "[man](GodShew.md#nomorechildren)" kind) being crowned with [one crown](biblicalemblems.md), [one glory](TwainShew3.md#Glorys): "grace" glory.  
  
Hence we all  are (being) [changed](biblicalemblems.md), [from (blind) faith to (seeing) faith](TwainShew2.md#Faiths): Romans, [from (law) glory to (grace) glory](TwainShew3.md#Glorys): 2Cor 3, and [from Sinai to Sion](GraceWalk.md): Heb 12, as by the Spirit, "that Spirit"(of Spirits), who is "now"(of then/now) "the Lord" (of Lords). Notably the Lord (that Spirit) has been l-o-n-gsuffering to [us-ward](Usward.md) (not to them-ward nor to us-ward + them-ward). So it reasons there is now an [ever increasing cry for just us](ShewBread2.md#justus). It's all quite [allegorical](Allegory.md), and perhaps to teach us all the [allegorical duality of (being) Up-Right](ShewBread10.md).

## Content

Good question: Why did incorruption create corruption?
Note, in Genesis 1, "create"(bara) is not "make"(asah).
God said let us make(asah) man = NOT create man. 
Perhaps the allegory POINT is 
Creator (both creates + destroys) is not Maker (makes perfect);
AsIf to allegorize Law (good + evil) is not Grace (is good only).
Meet Your Maker:  The God of all grace: 1Peter 5:10.
Interesting how CJ* = "suffer" mode, VANISHES afterward.
*all who will live godly in Christ Jesus shall suffer: 2Tim 3:12

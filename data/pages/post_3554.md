## Content

Many online ministries and churches seem to be preoccupied with alienation of the world God so loved rather than on reconciliation of the world unto God. Many are preoccupied with sacrifice, when the will of God is flat out stated as "I will have mercy, and not sacrifice". For God never desired sacrifice for sin (which is of the law and to the law); Nor did God ever take pleasure in such law law (Ps 40:6; Ps 51:16; Hos 6:6; Mt 9:13; 
Mt 12:7; Heb 10: all). As such many tend to focus on alienation of others rather than on global reconciliation unto God. By 
Daniel Miles

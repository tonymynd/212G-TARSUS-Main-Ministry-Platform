## Content

Colossians 3 says focus on things above

The Tree of Life isn't good + evil, because that's double minded = unstable both ways: James 1:8; Even if it's divided under/above instead of left/right division. So Matthew 12:33 (KJV) says: "Either make the tree good, and his fruit good; or else make the tree corrupt, and his fruit corrupt: for the tree is known by [his] fruit".

## Content

GRACE is More than Love, in this allegoric manner:
- Love is still a new "commandment" (rule, ordinance, law); But Grace does NOT command; Rather Grace awares with trUTH to help u become a graceful, merciful, peaceful U.
- Love is only first (not last) fruit of the Spirit: Galatians 5, wherein when led of the Spirit (not driven by a Ghost), it's as led FROM under the law...TO..."not under the law" (Gal 5:18); Which takes us beyond 'chrestotes' goodness of love, in midst of 9 
fruit, still having condition & severity attached (IF & OtherWise of Rom 11:22) to grace-us 'agathosune' goodness (6th fruit) having neither any condition nor severity attached.
- Love(LawGod command) of "many" not only can, but "shall wax cOLD": Mt 24:12; Eg: many divorces prove if love only makes it to LukeWarm (not Hot), love can still grow cOLD.
- Heck, even love of a "few" good men (few apostles chosen from many disciples) turned nasty in Acts 5, where Ananias & Saphirra get KILLED (not forgiven), bringing "great fear" (not love nor grace) upon the whole church, and due to such-like still assembling in "Solomon's porch" of the Law temple. 
- Love is WONderful, but Grace is ONEderful, as if then the  MoreExcellentWay that's neither Broadway of Many nor NarrowWay of Few; For ALL is neither MANY nor FEW; And God our Saviour will have ALL men both saved, AND come to the knowledge(epignosis) of the trUth, as if making u a U.
- So in KJV, which uses both "love" & "charity" for 'agape', it does this to help us distinguish BetterLaw & Grace. For Love one another is Better NT Law than OT Law, but still Law; Not [yet] pure Grace having "no law", like "the God of all grace". So when get thru all 9 fruit of the Spirit, from Love... to... Temperance, it then says: against such there is "no law": Allegory as if when Temperance=SelfControl is learned, then no more need to be commanded, like know not children & servants still need to be commanded (Gal 4:1-7).

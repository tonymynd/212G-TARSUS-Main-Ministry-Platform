## Content

Grace, Mercy, Peace 
New Testament things which begin & end mention before the end:
"conscience": 
begins: John 8:9... then even evil conscience: Colossians 3:5... lastly ends as good [agathos] conscience toward God: 1Peter 3:10.
"Christ Jesus": 
doesn't begin until Acts 19:4, but ends in 1Peter 5:10-14. Thereby ends both mediation: 1Tim 2:5 & suffer: 2Tim 3:12. 
In contrast 
"Jesus Christ" mention occurs from Mt 1:1 to Rev 22:21.
"favour"('charis'): 
begins in Luke (not Mt & Mk), then used only 7 times, and ends in Acts 25:3 after being requested against Paul to kill him. 
But "grace"(higher 'charis') 
begins in Luke (not Mt nor Mk), and continues, over 100 times, unto the end: Revelation 22:21.
new "commandment" to "love"('agape') one another: 
begins in John 13:34, but then 1Cor 12:31;13:1-13 KJV note that a "more excellent way" than excellent love (can wax cold: Mt 24:12) is "charity"(as if a higher 'agape' than love); So 1Timothy 1:5 KJV notes Now the end of the "commandment" (to love) is "charity", which in Col 3:14 KJV can even be new man put on above forgiving, and in 2Pet 1:7 KJV is put on above brotherly kindness (still had some blindness).
interesting mystery details to notice, ponder, discuss
So the end to endure unto (Rev 22:21 KJV) says:
The "grace" of our Lord "Jesus → Christ" be with you all. Amen.

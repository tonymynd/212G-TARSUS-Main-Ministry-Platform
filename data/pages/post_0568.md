WHAT is A MORE EXCELLENT WAY? Is it Love or Charity?
MANY say "Love" is the MoreExcellentWay that NeverFails;
But Mt 24:12 KJV says "love" of "many" shall wax cOLD = fail,
And 1Cor 13:8 KJV says "charity"(not love) is what NeverFaileth.
KJV uses "love" & "charity" to contrast LawAgape & GraceAgape.
Matthew 24:12 KJV (notes Law 'agape' can still wax cOLD):
"And because iniquity shall abound, the love ('agape') of many shall wax cold".
1Corinthians 13:8 KJV (notes Grace 'agape' shall NeverFail):
"Charity ('agape') never faileth: but 
-whether [there be] prophecies, they shall fail; 
-whether [there be] tongues, they shall cease; 
-whether [there be] knowledge*, it shall vanish away.
*the 'gnosis' knowledge (aka law knowledge) that puffeth up in 1Cor 8:1 KJV, shall eventually vanish away, when replaced with 'epignosis' knowledge (aka grace knowledge) = MuchMore, aka MoreExcellentWay than either BroadWay or NarrowWay on high.
Erroneously, MANY modern bibles changed "charity" to 'love', and then say 'love never ends' or 'love never fails'; But the LawAgape called love does end, fail, as often as people in love get divorced. Yet this does not make them bad, per se, just more to learn yet.
So it's time to say B.S. (Bible Stupid) to this MANY still deceived, and by many shall come, and shall say such erroneous things; To which Matthew 24:23,26 TWICE says: "believe [it] not". It's a lie. 
Sure, Bible says "God is love", but also clarifies f-law love still fails; So yOUR Father which art in higher heaven having no such leaven, is "perfect": Matthew 5:48 & "merciful": Luke 6:36 KJV, 
which speaks of God's ↑Grace↑, being neither of these:
OT Law of Moses ←vs→ NT BetterLaw of Jesus on high.
For neither OT Law nor NT BetterLaw are perfect or merciful.

TITLE of Book of prophecy called Revelation ?
It's NOT The Revelation of Jesus Christ, as many ass-u-me; For that phrase is verse 1, allegory as if a 2nd apokalypsis (uncovering) to compare to the revelation (uncovering) of a saint named John:
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
The 
original TITLE in Oxford Authorized KJV for the book of prophecy called Revelation is NOT The Revelation of Jesus Christ, NOR The Revelation of Jesus Christ to John, NOR The Revelation; BUT is:
"The Revelation [apokalypsis] of Saint John The Divine".
(found in pre 1900 Oxford Authorized King James Version)
So, it's about the revelatory uncovering of a saint named John, whom many thought to be the divine [theios]. 
PERHAPS 
allegory written by Paul (who in Revelation 22:21 KJV signs it with his grace salutation, which he had thrice noted he always hand signed every epistle with his salutation, to reveal it's his epistle.
(noted in 2Thessalonians 3:17,18 about Revelation 22:21 KJV) 
Then, as if for contrast and compare-i-son, Revelation 1:1 notes the phrase "The Revelation of Jesus Christ" (found in gospels of Paul):
Revelation 1:1 AKJV
The Revelation [apokalypsis] of Jesus Christ, which God gave unto him, to shew unto his servants things which must shortly come to pass; and he sent and SIGNified it by his angel [angelos: messenger] unto his servant [doulos] John:
Note:
John would not call himself a "servant", for in John 15:15 he wrote that Jesus said (in red ink): 
"Henceforth I call you not servants [doulos]; for the servant knoweth not what his lord doeth: but I have called you friends; for all things that I have heard of my Father I have made known unto you".
So, it would seem the book of Revelation (law) prophecy x7, was about revealing John got "turned" (Rev 1:10-12) to what was "behind" him, which 2Peter 2:22 notes happened, as if a dog "turned" back to his own vomit (allegory the law).
The things SHORTLY to come to pass seem to be before AD70 when the law temple got destroyed (not one stone upon another). 
So,
it's plausible, from research, and evidence from 26 previous books, that the angel (messenger) of Jesus Christ is Paul, who writes the last book as if a spoof on John (who law bewitched churches Paul  established in grace) getting "turned" to what's "behind" him: aka "one like Son of man" (not Son of God), dressed as an OT law priest, with a "twoedged sword" (vengeance & punishment) coming out his mouth (Ps 149:6,7; Heb 12:4; Rev 1: 10-16). 
interesting allegoric & symbollic "mystery' details to notice.

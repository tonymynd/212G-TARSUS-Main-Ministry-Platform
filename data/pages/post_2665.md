## Content

Some OT LORD [Yehova] & NT God [Theos] Bible Contrasts:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
In Noah's Ark story: Genesis 6 - 8 it notes LORD [Yehova] and God [Elohim] not only DIFFER in name, but in commands to Noah:
God 
[Elohim] said two: Gen 6:19
in CONTRAST
LORD [Yehova] said clean by sevens & unclean by two: Gen 7:2.
Jeremiah 23:39 notes this about LORD [Yehova]:
I will utterly forget you and I will forsake you.
in CONTRAST
Hebrews 13:5 notes this about God [Theos]:
I will never leave thee nor forsake thee.
Amos 5:18 notes this about the LORD [Yehova]:
the day of the LORD is darkness, and not light 
(perhaps allegory meaning it's law, not grace).
in CONTRAST
1John 1:5 notes this about God [Theos]:
That God is light, and in him there is no darkness at all.
Isaian 13:6 notes this about LORD:
The day of the LORD [Yehova] comes as destruction (not salvation).
in Contrast
NT portrays this about God:
the day of true God comes as salvation (not also destruction)
Isaiah 45:7 notes OT LORD creates darkness and evil.
1Peter 5:10 - 12 notes all grace of God of all grace is true grace.
Jude 1:5 reminds this about OT LORD:
OT LORD both saved and destroyed people afterward.
1Timothy 2:3,4 clarifies this about God our Saviour:
God our Saviour will have all saved and aware of truth.
So
Jude 1:25 concludes 
the only wise God is God our Saviour (doesn't also destroy).
Seems the biblical narrative (via allegoric CONTRAST) is:
The OT LORD [Yehova] was both nice and nasty: lastly nasty.
The only true God is always and only nice, never also nasty.
Perhaps the contrasting message is about Law & Grace:
Law was both good and evil, lastly evil (reverse of live).
Grace is only good[agathos], not good[kalos] & evil[kakos].
What are your insights about how LORD & God Differ?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

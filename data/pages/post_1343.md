## Content

Under grace? Although Romans 6:14 KJV is no doubt the better of two "UNDER" modes (CAPS mine for effect): 
"YE are not UNDER the law, but UNDER grace", 
it's still as "ye" mode of ye/you in KJV, and 
it's still as "under" mode  of under/above. 
So, 
let's also consider 3rd of 3 things in this allegoric manner:
"under" law / "under" grace ... "with" grace (Heb 13:9 KJV)
servant of sin/servant of righteousness ...no servant(Gal 4:7)
death / everlasting life ... eternal life (through Jesus-Christ)
When we begin to look at three things, beyond twain mode, then even "under grace" becomes allegory as if:
- still LukeWarm of 3: cOLD/LukeWarm... Hot
- still acceptable of 3: good/acceptable... perfect (Rom 12:2b)
- still greater of 3: great/greater... greatest of 3 (1Cor 13:13)
So Col 3:1,2,3 KJV says if ye be risen with Christ (of through our Lord Jesus-Christ), seek those things which are "above", where Christ (is the end of the law) sitteth at the right hand of God ... above, not things on the earth (is still under mode).
Clarity: not said to speak against any still under grace, but to exhort all to consider what's best of 3 things is like rest of the story.

AUTHOR OF HEBREWS?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
Re author of Hebrews, let's notice these things that reason Paul is author of Hebrews:
- 
Pre-1900 AKJV has this title for Hebrews:
The epistle of Paul the apostle to the Hebrews.
- Hebrews is canonized together with Paul's epistles, prior to the seven general epistles of James x1, Peter x2, John x3, Jude x1.
- Hebrews has an end note in Oxford AKJV, and only Paul's epistles have such end notes. Hebrews end note: 
'written to the Hebrews from Italy by Timothy'.
(note: Timothy often penned Paul's epistles)
- Hebrews turns mention of "Christ Jesus" (Heb 3 - 10) around to "Jesus Christ" (Heb 10 -13), as Paul does in all his epistles. 
Note: mention of "Christ Jesus" 57x only occurs by Luke 1x, Paul 54x, Peter 2x.
- Hebrew's conclusion is the GRACE salutation of Paul (only). Paul thrice noted he always penned his grace salutation by his own hand.
Hebrews 13:25
Grace be with you all. Amen.

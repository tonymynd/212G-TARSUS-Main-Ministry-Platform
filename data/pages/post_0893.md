Affect (action) can have Effect (result). Eg: churches of Galatia  Affected by a f-law gospel, had an Effect: soon removed from him that called you into the grace of Christ unto another gospel". So Paul tells such "bewitched" churches it's "Grace [be] to you and peace from God the Father, and [from] our Lord Jesus Christ", Then notes what Effect it can have to even fall for the law of Christ: "Christ is become of no EFFECT unto you, 
whosoever of you are justified by the law; ye are fallen from grace".

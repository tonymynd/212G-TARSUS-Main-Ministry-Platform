LAST TRUMP & TRUMP OF GOD ??
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
I found it interesting to notice KJV uses both last "trump" & "trumpet" for 'salpigx' in 1Cor 15:52
In a moment, in the twinkling of any eye, at the last trump [sarpigx]; for the trumpet [sarpigx] shall sound, and the dead shall be raised incorruptible [aphthartos: also means immortal], and we shall be changed [allaso: also means 
transformed].
Then
also 2 kinds of immortal in 1Cor 15:53,54:
this corruptible must put on incorruption [aphtharsia: also "immortal"]
and
this mortal must put on immortality [athanasia: only "immortality"]
Seems only when both are done, is death swallowed up in victory, which
15:57 notes God hath given us "through" our Lord Jesus → Christ.
Then 
1Thess 4:16 speaks of the "trump of God" as 3rd of 3 things:
For the Lord himself shall descend from heaven 
with a shout, 
with the voice of the archangel, and 
with the trump [sarpigx] of God: 
and the dead in Christ shall rise first.
Hmmm
Seems in Hebrews 12:19 sound of a "trumpet" was at Mt Sinai
Seems in a card game the last trump can take the whole pot.
What is your insight about the "last trump" & trump of God"??
interesting mystery details to notice, ponder about, discuss.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ (be/is) with you all. Amen.

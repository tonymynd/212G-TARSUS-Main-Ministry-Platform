Word of God: two kinds in greek mode: 'rhema' & 'logos'??
Seems 
- 'rhema' word of God is still for fight mode (Eph 6:13-17), but
- 'logos' word of God much more: Heb 4:12 (sharper than any twoedged sword; As if Grace smarter than both such OTlaw/NTlaw).
For
when 
look at Revelation 1:16 KJV, a "sharp twoedged sword" comes out mouth of one like Son of man, dressed as OT high priest of law (will both law flatter & law fault-find to flaw u); 
And 
when look at Psalms 149:6,7 KJV it notes a "twoedged sword" executes both vengeance & punishments  (as if law & law): vengeance upon heathen & punishments upon people. 
Seems why, 
in 1Timothy 6:12a&b KJV, 
- after Paul 1st told childish Timmy fight the good fight of faith, 
- then said "lay hold on eternal life", to which you are ALSO called = a higher calling than FightClub mode in plural L/R heavens on high, can still be like 2houses of Israel vs Juda,
where even if on right side, still have enemies (jeopardy).
So it get's interesting to look in greek mode, then study out how there's a lot of twain things hidden in greek, like:
- 'strepho' child converted & 'epistrepho' man converted,
- "favour" & "grace" used for 'charis' to distinguish twain,
- "love" & "charity" used for 'agape' to distinguish twain,
- "Ghost" & "Spirit" used for 'pneuma' as if 2Comforters
- 'rhema' word of God & sharper 'logos' word of God also
- 'gnosis' knowledge & higher 'epignosis' knowledge of JC
- 'ano' above (as if high) & 'epi' above (as if higher) in Col 3
Seems why, in 1Timothy 2:4, it notes God our Saviour will have TWO things, as if like before/after of high/higher:
- all men('anthropos'=liars) saved (by grace, thru faith), AND
- come to the knowledge ('epignosis') of (higher) truth

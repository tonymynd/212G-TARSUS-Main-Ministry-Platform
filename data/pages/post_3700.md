2nd TEMPERANCE
Three Best Wishes unto ALL: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
“And to knowledge TEMPERANCE; …” ~ 2Pe 1:5 AKJV  
Love → Joy → Peace → Longsuffering → Gentleness → Goodness → Meekness → Faith → Temperance (Galatians 5:22-23) → All Diligence → Virtue → Knowledge → Temperance(2nd) → Patience(in the midst of 7 things to add to “your faith”) 
(Attribution and Spiritual Map on the comments)
<quick summary re knowledge>
- knowledge is the 2nd thing to add to your faith and 6th before charity in our project list(see map on comments)
- There are two types of knowledge: gnosis and epignosis.
-- lower: knowledge(gnosis) puffs up 1 Cor 8:1 leading to contention and divisions 
-- higher: knowledge(epignosis) a higher knowledge aka charity edifies.
- Paul The Apostle uses his PEP rhetoric to first edify corinthians and applauded their use of knowledge(gnosis), but not approved. 
- knowledge(gnosis) called science, led them to fall into a snare of the devil, because this is the knowledge of good and evil, told not to eat in Gen 2:17, also the reason why knowledge(gnosis) “it shall vanish away” 1Co 13:8
- Charity is perfecting us by also having knowledge(epignosis) “For the perfecting of the saints, for the work of the ministry, for the edifying of the body of Christ: Till we all come in the unity of the faith, and of the KNOWLDEGE(epignosis) of the Son of God, unto a perfect man, unto the measure of the stature of the fulness of Christ:” ~ Eph 4:12-13 AKJV
<end of quick summary>
Is the turn for 2nd TEMPERANCE on our list.
Intro
There are only three instances of temperance(ekrateia) and they connect as follows:
- Act 24:25’s child temperance: having knowledge of righteousness but still in child mode, green fruit: still awful lawful judgment, not yet applied knowledge(epignosis), which is wisdom from above, is also “full of mercy” Jam 3:17 AKJV
- Gal 5:23’s stronger temperance:  Fruit of the (Walk in the) Spirit matures in temperance(against such, no law), not yet a fully grown Son of God, perfect man(aner) Eph 4:13, so, no yet able to “So run” but matured enough to add 7 things to “your faith” in 2Pe 1:5, which end, only saves the soul of soul/spirit, 1Pe 1:9.
- 2Pe 1:6’s STRONGEST temperance: having righteousness, no law against(free and ready to run, but still in standby); able to control and stop knowledge(gnosis) dead on its tracks* from puffing up (snare of the devil), and gets ready to relay the baton to “patience”. 
* Used "dead on its tracks" on gnosis, as an allusion to 1Cor 15:56 death = law, conveying several layers of innerstanding in one phrase; As knowledge(gnosis) also called “science” 1Ti 6:20, can cause mass death as carnal scientist developing smart AI weapons to seek, kill and destroy, not having self-governance, but still governed by the law.
Now, let us enter the heart of the matter. 
The Progression: The nine aspects of the fruit of the Spirit (Gal 5:22;23) reach maturity when "against such there is no law"; meaning there is no fulfilling of the lusts of the flesh(no more law worketh wrath Rm 4:15 work of the flesh), then "led" to the full and mature "Walk in the Spirit" Gal 5:16;18.
After attaining Temperance as 9th fruit, we then also add Temperance to our faith as 3rd of 7 upgrades to y(our) faith
“With all diligence” 
Longsuffering (patience) fosters resilience, enabling us to endure trials as we work with diligence to add 7 things to your faith, starting with the awareness of virtue, think on these things…if there be any virtue, and obviously there are many virtues in our path to charity, the greatest virtue.
"your faith"
Still only saving of the soul of soul/spirit 1Pe 1:9, 1st temperance is not yet "my grace is sufficient" to save also spirit.
“and to virtue knowledge;”
Notice how this knowledge is still gnosis(not yet epignosis); the reason thereby is that knowledge without self-control (temperance, our next aspect in the list, 2Pe 1:6) ensures that knowledge is used humbly and constructively, aligning with God's will rather than fueling arrogance or selfish desires:
temperance<<knowledge(gnosis)>> temperance
"as he reasoned of righteousness" Act 24:25
It reasons that it takes knowledge of righteousness to bring about temperance also having patience (steadfast endurance)
“And to knowledge TEMPERANCE; …” (2nd)
Fully strengthened and complete temperance—also known as "My grace is sufficient" (no law required)—precedes patience. This is because patience is necessary to bear the growth of others rather than placing them under a puffed-up law. Grace reigns through righteousness (Rom 5:21), and there is no condemnation in Christ (Rom 8:1), who is the end of the law for righteousness (Rom 10:4).
I’m starting to have “exceeding joy” from our father, knowing we are getting closer to charity; This temperance adventure filled me with gladness, as our Father gave me what I asked for in due time:
“Let us therefore come boldly unto the throne of grace, that we may obtain mercy, and find grace to help in time of need.” ~ Heb 4:16 AKJV.
This study has no parts, as having no more partiality, interesting that next aspect “patience” is the midst of 7 things to add to your faith, a twist in the middle perhaps, right before the end:
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [is/be] with you all. Amen.

## Content

Immortality? Are there TWO (1st/2nd) kinds of "immortality" to put on in the biblical mystery? Let's investigate:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
1Cor 15:53,54 says put on 2 things for death to be swallowed up in victory: this corruptible must (1stly) put on incorruption [aptharsia = "immortality" in Rom 2:7 & 2Tim 1:10], and this mortal must (2ndly) put on immortality [athanasia]. Hmmm, seems TWO…

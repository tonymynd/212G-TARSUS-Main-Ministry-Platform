GLORY: Law Glory vs Grace Glory
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Seems 2Corinthians 3:7-11 contrasts 2 kinds of glory:
law 
glory was 2 ministrations of death & condemnation.
grace glory is 2 ministrations of spirit & righteousness.
So 
it took "done away" x2 to make law glory = no glory at all.
Then in 2Cor 3:18 it concludes
we all, with open face beholding as in a glass (aka mirror) the glory of the Lord, are changed into the same image, from glory to glory (aka from law to grace), as by the Spirit of the Lord
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

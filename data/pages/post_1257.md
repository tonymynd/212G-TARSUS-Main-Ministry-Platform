## Content

Biblical Pattern: first define what it is not, then what it is:
Eg: God is not God of the dead, but [is God] of the living. (allegory for God is not merciless Law, but merciful Grace).
Eg: we (of they/we) are not under the law, but under grace. 
Eg: God is not [the author] of confusion, but of peace.
Eg: God has not appointed us to wrath, but to obtain salvation (from law worketh wrath) by our Lord JC (by grace & truth).…

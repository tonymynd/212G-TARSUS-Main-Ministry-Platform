## Content

Does 2Timothy 3:1-5 KJV's mention of "from such turn away" suggest we be not be ALL INCLUSIVE? I don't think so. Rather, it seems there comes a time, in our journey to spiritual maturity, we have to leave those un-kind of people listed, and move on, as if evolving, as we "grow in grace", as if, from the previous highminded 'gnosis' knowledge (which is not all inclusive), unto the (higher) level of 'epignosis' knowledge (which is all inclusive).

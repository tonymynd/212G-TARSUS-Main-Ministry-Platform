that he may establish the second (new covenant);

So 
then, it's as if the "third" of 3: great, greater, greatest;
(b/c greater mode can get seduced bwd to 'great again')

(Mt5:48=be perfect... 1Pet5:10=all grace make you perfect)

(of these 3: charity thinketh no "evil" = no reverse of "live")

## Content

Law and Grace should not be MINGLED:
Because,
Contrary things mingled make an oxymoron.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Genesis 2:16,17 are CONTRARY "LORD God" commands:
Every tree & not every tree MINGLED make an oxymoron.
Note: "God" had previously said "every tree": Genesis 1:29.
So, the serpent asked the woman: did "God" say Gen 2:17?
(note: Because if God said both every tree & not every tree, it would make God an oxymoron: contrary things mingled)... 
So,
Christ refused "mingled" drink: Mt 27:34; Mk 15:23.
Matthew 27:34
They gave him vinegar to drink MINGLED with gall: and when he had tasted thereof, he would not drink.
Mark 15:23
And they gave him to drink wine MINGLED with myrrh: but he received it not.
Galatians 4 "allegory" says:
two sons of Abraham by two mothers ARE the two covenants, and this Agar (mother of Ishmael: bondmaid) IS Mt Sinai = law;
So
the script-u-are saith
cast out the bondwoman and her son
(allegory: cast out law & result of law: sin, death)
Ephesians 2:15 notes
he abolished the enmity, the law of commandments
(perhaps he abolished Gen 2:17 to have only grace)
Hebrews 10:9
he taketh away the first that he may establish the second
(allegory: he taketh away law to establish grace)
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

"on earth peace" via Saviour "Christ": Luke 2:11-14 - ponder how that contrasts with Saviour "Jesus", said in Mt 10:34 & Lk 12:51 he did not come to "send" nor "give" peace on earth. So Jn 7:43 says there was division (not peace) because of him, and Jn 16:7 says Jesus had to go away. --- Seems why Apostle Paul says we only have peace THROUGH our Lord Jesus→Christ, in Christ is the end of the law (for Jesus mode was only mend of the law). Interesting.

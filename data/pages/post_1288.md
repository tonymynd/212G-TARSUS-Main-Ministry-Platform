## Content

THRICE "Jesus Christ" (aka "grace and truth"), sent from "the only true God", said both what God is not, and what God is, for our learning: 
"He is not God of the dead, but ]is] God of the living".
Seems to allegory(contrast) say God is not Law, but is Grace.
For Law is a ministration of DEATH, but Grace is eternal LIFE.

As long as both "law of Christ" & "grace of Christ" believed in the body of Chirst there'll still be both "suffer" & "rejoice" for "all" therein: 1Cor 12:26. It's why Romans 10:4 clarified "Christ is the end of the law"; And Galatians explains how churches of Galatia got removed from the "grace of Christ" was by still try to be justified by fulfill the "law of Christ". So after noting both suffer & rejoice in 1Cor 12:26, Paul notes 
there's a "more excellent way" in 1Cor 12:31, which he describes in 1Cor 13 KJV as "charity" (grace 'agape') .

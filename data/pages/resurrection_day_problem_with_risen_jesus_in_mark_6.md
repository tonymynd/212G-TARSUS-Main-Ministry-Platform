# RESURRECTION DAY PROBLEM WITH RISEN JESUS IN MARK !6:


Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.

RESURRECTION DAY PROBLEM WITH RISEN JESUS IN MARK 16:
Risen Jesus upbraided (Mk 16:14) vs God upbraideth not (Jam 1:5)

Jesus of Nazareth is risen: Mk 16:6. Then in 16:9-13 he appeared to Mary & in another form to 2 (same day: Lk 24:13). Yet in 16: 14-16 when Jesus appeared to the eleven he still "UPBRAIDED" and still "DAMNED" [katkrino: condemned] non-believers. Hmmm. Seems CONTRARY to the "God" that "upbraideth not", in James 1:5, and the "God of all grace" in 1Peter 5:10.

Good news is in Mk 16:19 it noted after risen Jesus of Nazareth had still upbraided & said non-believers would be damned, even after the cross; He then got received up into heaven and sat on the right hand of God. So then he returned to say "Peace" in Lk 24 & Jn 20.

Seems it took being re-ascended & sat on right hand of God even for a risen Jesus to stop upbraiding & damning (condemning), to then say "Peace".

Seems a reason Mark noted a risen Jesus still upbraided & damned, even after the cross, is there's no mention of grace yet in Mt or Mk, none until Lk 2:40 fwd. So it allegory portrays both Mt & Mk are as if still cOLD mode of cOLD←>LukeWarm→Hot re seek & find. Seems LukeWarm mode is Lk to Acts, Hot mode is Romans forward.

Seems why we don't see "Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ" until Romans forward, as then it's called "the gospel of God" & "the gospel of Christ", then noted peace with God is "through our Lord Jesus → Christ" (Rom 5:1),; Perhaps allegory as if having gone through his 2 Sons Jesus mode to his 1 Son Christ mode. Christ is our peace.

Seems another thing to notice is Jesus is still 2 Sons: Son of God & Son of man with 2 Voices (John 5:25-29) saying 2 things:
Son of God voice says "live"
Son of man voice voice says "judgment [krisis] also".

It portrays Jesus as both Son of God & Son of man in Mt -Acts; But in Romans forward there is NO more mention of Son of man, ONLY Son of God.

So Paul says he now lives by the faith of the "Son of God" (not Son of man nor both Son of God & Son of man).

Seems it's because the only true God has only one true Son, who is not just risen from the dead, but is also re-ascended to the Father, from where he could send the Spirit of truth to guide you into all truth (John 15:26; 16:7-13).

What are your insights about these things?

The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

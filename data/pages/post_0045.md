## Content

"The God of all comfort" isn't both comfort + dis-comfort.
"The God of all grace" isn't both graceful + awful lawful.
"That God" which "upbraideth not" doesn't bite nor smite. 
The higher "God is one" isn't the plural God on high.  
"Be not highminded" involves "leaving" plural God.

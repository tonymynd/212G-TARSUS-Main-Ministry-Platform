## Content

"life eternal" is reverse of "eternal life" (perhaps like "evil" is reverse of "live"). Hmmm...
Interesting to notice "life eternal" occurs only 4 times in Mt 25:46, John 4:36, John 12:25, John 17:3. 
In contrast the only true God sent Jesus Christ to give "eternal life". So the "gift of God" is "eternal life", which occurs so much more (27 times). Grace reigns through righteousness unto "eternal life" by Jesus Christ our Lord: Rom 
5:21. So Jude 1:21 says (before entering Revelation) look for the mercy of our Lord Jesus Christ unto "eternal life".

## Content

Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Jesus said 
Matthew 24:12 KJV 
And because iniquity shall abound, 
the 
love [agape] of many shall wax cold.
(aka be cold of cOLD ←> LukeWarm → Hot)
Jesus also said
Matthew 24:13 KJV 
But he that shall endure unto the end, the same shall be saved.
(aka be saved only by grace only in Revelation 22:21 KJV)
Interesting thing about the end: Revelation 22:21 KJV is it is the (pure) grace salutation of Paul (not John), and always written by Paul's "own hand", as clarified in 2Thessalonians 3:17,18 KJV.
2Thessalonians 3:17,18 KJV
The salutation of Paul with mine own hand, which is the token in every epistle: so I write.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.
Revelation 22:21 KJV (The End)
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

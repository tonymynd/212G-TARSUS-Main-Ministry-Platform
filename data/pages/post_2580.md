## Content

Grace, mercy, and peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
Romans 5:20,21 KJV
Moreover the law entered, that the offence [paraptoma] might abound. But where sin abounded [pleonazo], grace did (ALREADY) much more abound [hyper-perisseou]:
That as sin hath reigned unto death, even so might grace reign through righteousness unto eternal life by Jesus Christ our Lord.
note: seems Rom 5:21's through righteousness "unto eternal life" is referred to in Jude 1:21 KJV says:
Keep yourselves in the love of God, looking for the "mercy" of our Lord Jesus Christ "unto eternal life".
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus → Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

Seems even know TheTruth, if only 2/3, NotYet AllTruth. Eg:
Jn 8:30→32's believe→know TheTruth = only 1st kind of free*.
Jn 14:6's I am = TheWay(1/3)→TheTruth(2/3)→TheLife(3/3).
Jn 16:7→13 takes you beyond TheTruth(2/3)→AllTruth(3/3).
*two kinds of liberty (free): 1st = 'eleutheria' & 2nd = 'apoluo'.
Seems, in Greek mode, there's not only 2kinds of liberty, but  2kinds of converted, thereby 2kinds of knowledge, even 2kinds of above; AsIf to contrast NT BetterLaw of CJ with NT Grace of JC.
Eg: 2kinds of Converted:
- 'epistrepho' converted: Luke 22:32
- 'strepho' converted: Matthew 18:3
Eg: 2kinds of Knowledge:
- 'epignosis' knowledge: 1Timothy 2:4
- 'gnosis' knowledge: 1Corinthians 8:1 & 13:8
Eg: 2kinds of Above:
- 'epi' above: Colossians 3:14
- 'ano' above: Colossians 3:1,2

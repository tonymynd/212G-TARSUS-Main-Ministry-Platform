## Content

What if we look at the possibility of TWO kinds of salvation: 
1: salvation of your soul, by your faith: 1Pet 1:9, AND 
2. salvation of your spirit, from spirit of error to spirit of truth
Seems WHY (?) 1Timothy 2:4 clarifies God our Saviour will have TWO things:
1. 
all men saved, AND
2. all men come unto the ('epignosis') knowledge of the truth
Seems WHY (?) Hebrews 4:12 notes the word of God 1stly divides asunder soul/spirit, AND 2ndly also joints/marrow, to then get to the heart of the matter.
Seems WHY (?) when spiritual of natural first, spiritual afterward (1Cor 15), there's still this to do: "comparing spiritual things with spiritual"; Since law is both natural & spiritual, but law being "both good and evil" is also natural disaster & spiritual wickedness. So we go on from natural & spiritual law(s) to spiritual grace, which is neither disaster nor wickedness.
Seems WHY (?) ManBlessed comes after DiaperBlessed in Romans 4:7,8; For DiaperBlessed is only a temporal CoverUp of sins that can also get UnCovered by Mt 10:26; Lk 8:17; Lik 12:2.
Seems WHY (?) after mention of Jesus being author & finisher of our faith, there's mention of Christ being the author of "eternal salvation" (beyond temporal salvation); Also mention of God not being the author of confusion, but of peace. But not of RIP of life+death, Rather eternal peace of eternal life having no death.

## Content

Human RACE they -vs- we RUN: Allegory noted by Apostle Paul?
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
- 1Corinthians 9:24,25 
all 
RUN in a RACE, but one receives the prize: THEY (run) to obtain a corruptible crown (aka law), but WE an incorruptible (aka grace).
- Romans 5:20 
law entered that the offence might abound, but 
where sin abounded, grace did much more (hyper) abound.
- Hebrews 12:1 
wherefore seeing we are compassed about (surrounded) with so great a cloud of witnesses, let us lay aside every weight, and the sin which doth so easily beset us, and let us RUN with patience the RACE set before us.
Notes:
- In a race, 1st finished = win & 2nd finished = place; 
So, 
Paul talked of "win" Christ (Philippians 3:8),
And told Ephesians neither give "place" to the devil (Eph 4:27). 
What are your insights about they & we RUN a RACE?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

Two biblical things can be conclusive:
- By Grace: it's all righteous, yes, every one
- By Law: it's none are righteous, no, not one
But what if only one of such things is the truth.
[[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/04-John/John-Chapter-01.md#^Jn1-17|Joh 1:17]] contrasts Law & Grace as if Lie & Truth:
For the law was given by Moses, [but] 
grace and truth came by Jesus Christ.
[[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/10-Ephesians/Ephesians-Chapter-04.md#^Ep4-25|Eph 4:25]] tells childish saints which to put away:
Wherefore putting away lying, speak every man truth

## Content

New Testament Grace is Amazing: it not only differs from Law, but from Old Testament grace found in eyes of OT LORD, as being only for some. In contrast NT grace, truth thereof, came by "Jesus Christ": John 1:17, who's sent by "the only true God": John 17:2. NT grace has no partiality, for that God has no partiality. NT grace not only saves all, but awares all with truth, until all put away all old things, even OT grace. That involves 
going THROUGH our Lord Jesus Christ unto & into "Christ" wherein "all things new" for a "man" (of child/man).

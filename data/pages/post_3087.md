## Content

1Timothy 2:3-6
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
1Timothy 2:3-6 KJV
For 
this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour; 
Who will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.
For there is one God, and one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus;
Who gave himself a ransom for all, to be testified in due time.
Some notes about 1Timothy 2:3-5 KJV:
- the term "God our Saviour" begins being mentioned in 1Tim, and is mentioned 6x, 5x by Paul: (1Tim 1:1, 2:3; Titus 1:3, 2:10, 3:4), then lastly by Jude in Jude 1:25 as his conclusion, that "the only wise God" is "God our Saviour":
"To the only wise God our Saviour, be glory and majesty, dominion and power, both now and ever. Amen."
note:
"God[Theos] our Saviour" being Jude concluded as "the only wise God" is as if in allegory contrast to Jude's reminder in Jude 1:5 that the OT LORD[Yehova] both saved & destroyed afterward;
But
God our Saviour will have ALL men both saved & truthed afterward.
(so it seems as if an allegory contrast between law and grace)
Jude 1:5 KJV
I will therefore put you in remembrance, though ye once knew this, how that the Lord, having saved the people out of the land of Egypt, afterward destroyed them that believed not.
- God our Saviour will have ALL men[anthropos] to be saved AND to come unto the (higher spiritual) knowledge [epignosis, not gnosis] of the truth (so it's also contrasting 'gnosis' knowledge, which in 1Cor 13:8 shall vanish away, with higher spiritual 'epignosis' knowledge of the Son of God in Eph 4:13.
- SHOCKER: God our Saviour will have all men[anthropos: liars in Rom 3:4] to be both saved and to be higher spiritual truthed: would INCLUDE the mediator man[anthropos] Christ Jesus (reverse of Jesus Christ) between God and men[anthropos] in 1Tim 2:5.
note: 
"Christ Jesus" only occurs from Acts 19:4 to 1Peter 5:14).
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

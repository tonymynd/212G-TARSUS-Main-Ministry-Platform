Not "[this](CurrentEvents.md)" (Jn 3:19); But rather "that": [the world](Reconciliation.md) (all men) [through](through.md) him might be saved (Jn 3:15). That's all saved [AND](RevelatorySermons6.md) all aware what they're saved from... lest any ever again get carried away, draw back, or turn back, and get bewitched. For if any do, it add-verse-ly affects brethren all one, and all of one (Heb 2); namely brethren all of "Christ" of "Jesus Christ", who is one with God is one.  
  
And it seems "many" (Mt 7:13; 24:5) priests, pastors, internet and political "ministers", even many Prime "Ministers", even many grandparents, parents, and [teachers](OneLiners.md#BibleTranslators), are sadly not yet [aware](GodsheworgExplanation.md) of what(?) they're all saved from; or that all's well that ends well when and where [his angel](Paul.md) plays the [last trump](RevelatorySermons13.md) it:

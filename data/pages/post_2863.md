## Content

FORGIVE, TO BE FORGIVEN -vs- FORGIVEN ALL, SO FORGIVE ALL.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Please notice & post the Law -VS- Grace contrast:
Forgive, to be forgiven -VS- Forgiven, so forgive.
You must first forgive -VS- God has first forgiven you.
Mt 6:14,15; Mk 11:25,26 -VS- Eph 4:32; Col 2:13.
Law (no grace in Mt & Mk) -VS- Grace (Luke → fwd)
Never forgiveness: Mt 12:32; Mk 3:29 -VS- Forgiven all trespasses.
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

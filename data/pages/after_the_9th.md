# After the 9th

**After the 9th:** Clarity re Galatians 5's nine "_fruit of the Spirit_".

It's not a McDs fast food drive thru, but a "walk in the Spirit".

So learn to Spirit walk before take off running, else stumble.

Fruit #1: Love. Many things to learn about love, before #2: joy.

Eg: there's 10 kinds NT love to first learn about, and sort out.

Yet...

Even if 10th kind: 'agape', there's still two such to learn about.

- wrong kind: law agape: can also wax "cold": Matthew 24:12.

- right kind: grace agape: endures to the end: Matthew 24:13.

Grace agape called "charity" in KJV, to distinguish from love.

Dangerous help: most modern Bibles change charity to love,

And then say love never fails, or love never ends. I say B.S.

There's plenty of evidence love fails, as often as ppl divorce.

So then, it was the law agape that waxes "cold" in Mt 24:12.

It was not the grace agape that "never faileth" in 1Cor 13:8.

Grace says this: Hebrews 13:5

I will never leave you, nor ever forsake you.

Law says this: Jeremiah 23:39

I will utterly forget you, and I will forsake you.

So let's learn to walk in grace love before running for joy.

Fruit #2: Joy. Things to learn about joy, before #3: peace.

You can have this Romans 14:17 "_joy in the Holy Ghost_";

But even though 'chara', it's not a joyful "_walk in the Spirit_".

There is no fruit of the Ghost. It's called "_fruit of the Spirit_".

For John 4:24 clarified "God is a Spirit" (aka not a Ghost).

So what happens to joy when law love = divorce? Gone.

Fruit #3: Peace. Also two kinds; One can get removed.

So unless 1st learn about love & joy, peace won't last.

Revelation 6:4 notes peace removed by red horse rider.

God is not [the author] of confusion, but of peace; But

peace God authors is eternal peace can't get removed.

So Rom 1:7 says "_Grace to you, and peace, from God_".

**POINT:** there's things to learn about all 9 fruit of Spirit.

When done all 9, last being _temperance_ = self-control;

Then it mentions "_no law_"; But not until AFTER the 9th.

After the 9th, then it is safe to "_run_", and never stumble.

**The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ** [be] **with you all. Amen.**

## Content

"God our Saviour": saves & awares, by grace & truth; 
NOT saves & destroys as Jude 1:5 reminds re OT LORD.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Interesting the biblical term "God our Saviour" occurs 6x, beginning in 1Timothy (the epistle from Laodicea: Col 4:16; 1Tim 6:21 KJV), and is clarified in 
1Tim 2:3,4 
"God our Saviour" will have: 
ALL men saved (by grace) & truthed (by epignosis knowledge),
NOT both saved & destroyed as reminded in Jude 1:5 re OT LORD[Yehova]...
1Timothy 2:3,4 KJV (God our Saviour saves & awares all):
For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour; Who will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge [epignosis, not gnosis] of the truth.
Jude 1:5 KJV (OT LORD[Yehova] saved & destroyed after):
I will therefore put you in remembrance, though ye once knew this, how that the Lord, having saved the people out of the land of Egypt, afterward destroyed them that believed not.
And lastly, in Jude 1:25,
clarified "the only wise God" is "God our Saviour" (who does not save & destroy, but save and aware all):
Jude 1:25 KJV
To the only wise God our Saviour, be glory and majesty, dominion and power, both now and ever. Amen.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

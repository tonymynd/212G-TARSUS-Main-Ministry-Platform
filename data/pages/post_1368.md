## Content

When contrast let's always do it so it ends well. For example here's a REVERSE order contrast witch always ends BAD:
Grace is opposite of law, like truth is opposite of lie, like mercy is opposite of wrath, like freedom is opposite of bondage, like peace is opposite of confusion, like good news is opposite of bad news, like justified is opposite of condemned, like spirit of truth is opposite of spirit of error, and most importantly 
like life is opposite of death = DEAD end. 
NOW a Proper order of biblical contrast always ends GOOD:
law is opposite of Grace, like lie is opposite of truth, like wrath is opposite of mercy, like bondage is opposite of freedom, like confusion is opposite of peace, like bad news is opposite of good news, like condemned is opposite of justified, like spirit of error is opposite of spirit of truth, and most importantly like death is opposite of LIFE end.  
Eg: Proper order contrast of Jesus Christ re God:
He is not God of the dead, but God of the living=GOOD end.
Eg: Proper order contrast of apostle Paul re God:
God is not the author of confusion, but of peace=GOOD end.

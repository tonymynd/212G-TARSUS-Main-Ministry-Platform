## Content

Old man / New man (Born / Born Again) allegory:
Seems born again(from above) allegory, 1Peter 1:23 clarifies is not of corruptible seed (law), but of incorruptible (grace), is allegory like how Paul contrasts two different kinds of "man" in NT greek:
- 'anthropos' man = law judgmental & self-condemning, so called "inexcusable" in Romans 2:1 & "liar" in Romans 3:4.
- 
'aner" man = grace blessed (no sin even imputed) in Romans 4:8, as having put away childish things (laws) in 1Cor 13:11
So, in Ephesians 4, "put off" the "old man", to "put on" the "new man" is allegory like put off 'anthropos' man (law liar) to be 'aner' man (grace honest);
For the fulness of Christ is the perfect 'aner' man-kind.

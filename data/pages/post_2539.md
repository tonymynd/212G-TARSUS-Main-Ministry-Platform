Now 80 Young Canadian Doctors DEAD
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Dr. William Makis has tracked, documented, and reported thrice to the Canadian Medical Association that firstly 10, then soon 30, and now 80 young Canadian doctors have unexpectedly suddenly died in the past 2 years. The common thing among all these was a 2nd, 3rd, or 4th injection that was reportedly 'safe and effective'. 
What's shocking about this is the documented data, now available, has not moved the CMA nor the media to investigate such. What the media does now report is that there's a critical shortage of paramedics, nurses, and doctors, causing long waits for ambulance and emergency room care, due to SAD (Sudden Adult Death) also occurring among young doctors, nurses, and paramedics. 
What are your insights about such ?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

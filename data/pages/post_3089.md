## Content

LUKE 2:14-18?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Let's notice Luke 2:8-14's mention of "shepherds abiding in the field, keeping watch over their flock by night" DID NOT happen in December (winter), but in the spring at lamb time. For in winter the sheep would be down in the sheepfold, especially at night.
The 
grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
1Corinthians 15:21,22 KJV
For since by man[anthropos] came death, by man[anthropos] came also resurrection of the dead.
For as in Adam all die, even so in Christ shall all be made alive.
BUT 
note:
as in "Adam" all die would include "last Adam" in 1Cor 15:45, which is called second man[anthropos] in 1Cor 15:47; Yet in Rom 3:4 it says let EVERY man[anthropos] be a liar, in contrast to let God be true.
Seems it may reveal that "in Christ" is as if 3rd of 3:
First man Adam → Last Adam → Christ = aner man.
anthropos = liar → anthropos2 = liar → Christ = truth.
(allegory: in Law1 all die → in Law2 all die → by Grace: all live).
So, reading on in 1Cor 15:54 reveals it takes two things:
WHEN
- this corruptible shall have put on incoruption,
AND
- this mortal shall have put on immortality,
THEN
shall be brought to pass the saying that is written: Death is swallowed up in victory.
1Cor 15:55-57 KJV
O death, where is thy sting?
O grave, where is thy victory?
The sting of death is sin; and
the strength of sin is the law.
But thanks [charis: grace thanks] be to God, which giveth us the victory through our Lord Jesus → Christ.
interesting "mystery" details to notice, ponder, discuss 
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

The God of all grace (aka no law, nor law/grace duality). 
Why do so few know about or ever speak of that God?
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
JC 
noted God isn't God of the dead, but "God of the living" (allegory: not law, but grace). 
Then noted he was sent by "the only true God" to give "eternal life", before the cross: John 17. 
Then Paul noted "the God of all comfort" (no discomfort), and the God which "cannot lie". 
Then James noted the God who "upbraideth not". 
Then Peter noted the "God of all grace".
1Peter 5:10 KJV:
But the God of all grace, who hath called us unto his eternal glory by Christ Jesus, after that ye have suffered a while, make you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you.
I hear much about God from believers, yet little of that God.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

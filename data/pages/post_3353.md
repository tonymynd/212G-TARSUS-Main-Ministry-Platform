## Content

True God is (grace) good [agathos],
Not (law) good [kalos] and evil [kakos].
Romans 7:21 KJV notes 
when do good [kalos], then evil [kakos] is present.
Hebrews 5:14 KJV says 
discern (aka avoid) both good [kalos] and evil [kakos].

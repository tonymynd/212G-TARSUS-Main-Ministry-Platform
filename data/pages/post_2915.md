## Content

"fulness of God" is 4/4, not 3/4
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Interesting Ephesians 3:17-19 notes FOUR aspects for the fulness of God: breadth, length, depth, height;
But
in Revelation 21:16 there is still only THREE aspects mentioned: length, breadth, height are equal,
Still no mention of depth, yet, so nigh the end.
Ephesians 3:17-19 KJV
That Christ may dwell in your hearts by faith; that ye, being rooted and grounded in love, May be able to comprehend with all saints what is the breadth, and length, and depth, and height; And to know the love of Christ, which passeth knowledge, that ye might be filled with all the fulness of God.
Revelatioin 21:16 KJV
And the city lieth foursquare, and the length is as large as the breadth: and he measured the city with the reed, twelve thousand furlongs. The length and the breadth and the height of it are equal.
Thank God the end to endure unto (to be saved only, not both saved and afterward destroyed like in Jude 1:5 reminds re OT LORD) is the pure grace salutation of Paul (not John), written with his own hand (as noted in 2Thessalonians 3:17,18): 
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

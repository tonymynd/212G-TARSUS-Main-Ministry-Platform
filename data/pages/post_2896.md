ALLEGORY CONTRAST: word[rhema] of God vs word[logos] of God
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Ephesians 6:17 defines the "sword" of the Spirit (used for battle mode) as the word [rhema] of God. 
note: This is the same "sword" Jesus Mt 10:34 said he brought instead of peace (when first made under the law: Gal 4:4).
Hebrews 4:12 defines the word [logos] of God as seven things:
For the word [logos] of God is 
quick, and 
powerful, and 
sharper than any twoedged sword, 
piercing even to the dividing asunder of soul and spirit, and 
of the joints and marrow, and 
is a discerner of the thoughts and 
intents of the heart.
note: 
Revelation 1:16 notes what comes out the mouth of a character dressed as an OT law priest is a sharp "twoedged sword". 
Such is not the logos of God, which is "sharper than any twoedged sword" in Hebrews 4:12.
note:
Psalm 149:6,7 notes a "twoedged sword" executes vengeance and punishments (not grace & truth).
INTERESTING mystery details to notice.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

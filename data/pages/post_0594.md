REVELATION = UnCover & Reveal what's CoveredUp & Veiled.
Seems UnCover of CoverUps is happening More & Faster now;
Almost as if Mt 10:26; Lk 8:17; Lk 12:2 shifted into a higher gear,
as if to help ppl navigate thru what seems like the best of spam.
Yet UnCover is for awareness; Not judge, accuse, nor condemn.
Matthew 10:26
there is nothing covered, that shall not be revealed; 
and hid, that shall not be known.
Luke 8:17
nothing is secret, that shall not be made manifest; neither [any thing] hid, that shall not be known and come abroad.
Luke 12:2
there is nothing covered, that shall not be revealed; 
neither hid, that shall not be known.

## Content

MEEKNESS: just finished Bible study on meekness = 8th of 9 fruit of the Spirit in Gal 5:22,23; and found there's 2 NT kinds of meekness, perhaps as different as if strength & weakness:
- meekness (praotes): "spirit" of "meekness and gentleness" of Christ, found only in epistles of Paul. It's inner man strength.
- meekness (prautes): a soul-ish "meekness and fear" used by James & 1Peter; still has a weakness aspect of fear attached. 
Seems the spirit of meekness (praotes) leads to temperance.

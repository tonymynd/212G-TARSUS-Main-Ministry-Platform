## Content

Woke up today with this in mind: Merciful does not perceive apocalypse, nor economic collapse, nor punish any. So, let's not leave out "mercy" of "Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord" ~1Timothy 1:2. 
Why? 
Colossians 1st got greeted with Grace & peace: Col 1:2 but ALSO read the epistle from Laodicea (aka 1Tim): Col 4:16. 
Why?
- 1Tim Begins 6 mentions of "God our Saviour"(1Tim...Jude)
- 
1Tim UpGrades Paul's Holy Kiss greet to INCLUDE "mercy"
- 1Tim 2:4 notes "God our Saviour" will have much more than Jesus = Saviour of Israel, and even more than Christ = Saviour of the world: aka:
- all men('anthropos') saved, AND
- come to knowledge('epignosis') of the truth (after saved)
Be Merciful (Luke 6:36) requires IN-CLUDE mercy in Grace, mercy, peace; To have peace that lasts; For the peace of Grace & peace offered in Rev 1:4 does not last, but gets taken away in Rev 6:4.
Interesting stuff to notice, ponder, discuss.

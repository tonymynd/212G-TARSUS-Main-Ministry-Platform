Like other NT words, there’s six different kinds of “peace” in Greek mode:  
  
- eirene (noun) = peace, one, rest, quietness  
  
- eireneuo (verb): to make peace, live peaceably  
  
- siapao (verb): be silent, hold your peace  
  
- phimoo (verb): put to silence, muzzle  
  
- hesychazo (verb): keep quiet, rest, cease from labour, be quiet  
  
- siago (verb): keep silent, keep secret  
  
The Greek word used most often in NT, especially in Paul’s epistles, is ‘eirene’. Eg:  
  
Grace, mercy, and peace(‘eirenne’) from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.

## Content

Enjoyed hearing this confession of a news photojournalist: "They look at us with hope, but we can only document their despair...  I feel sickened, disgusted by the scene, by the idea people can feel so detached from suffering before them that they choose to just photo record it rather than offer suffering people comfort... I feel sick because surely this is what I, as one of hundreds of journalists (assigned to photograph atrocities), 
have been doing... for television rating points". Seems like progress, even if only by one of many such.

- Grace(charis2) occurs over 100 times, frm Lk to Rev 22:21. 
it's like go on from JC's "gnosis' knowledge: 1st, also unto JC's higher 'epignosis' knowledge (who knew both kinds, not me till revealed by Spirit to also investigate in greek mode); 
For 
1Cor 13:8 KJV notes when it comes to what never fails, then (lower) 'gnosis' knowledge shall "vanish away"; As if to be replaced with (higher) 'epignosis' knowledge of Son of God.
So 
1Cor 13:11 allegorizes such as put away childish things(plural) to be man (notably 'aner' of 'anthropos/aner' man kinds).
Yet...
Eph 4:13 also notes perfect 'aner' man as then "fulness" of Christ; Allegory like then graceful, merciful, peaceful man.

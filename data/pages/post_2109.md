## Content

re GRACE & TRUTH CAME BY JESUS CHRIST FROM THE ONLY TRUE GOD
3 Best Wishes: Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
The New Testament opens & closes with "Jesus Christ", who's also  mentioned 196x. John 1:17's contrast says "The law was given by Moses, but GRACE AND TRUTH CAME BY JESUS CHRIST". Seems  we may use that info as allegory meaning for some Jesus Christ mentions:
John 
17 notes the only true God to know sent "Jesus Christ" (aka sent grace & truth) to do a work: give eternal life
1Cor 3:11 notes there is no other FOUNDATION to lay (and build upon) than "Jesus Christ" (aka than grace and truth)
2Cor 5:18 notes God hath reconciled us to himself by "Jesus Christ" (aka by grace & truth)
Hebrews 13:8 notes "Jesus Christ" (aka grace & truth) is the same yesterday, and to day, and for ever.
Seem why, in 1Cor 1:10 Paul told saints in Christ Jesus in the church of God at Corinth who were being divisional:
Now I beseech you, brethren, by the name of our Lord "Jesus Christ", that ye all speak the SAME THING (aka the Jesus Christ thing: grace and truth), and that there be no divisions among you;
What are your thoughts about such allegory things?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

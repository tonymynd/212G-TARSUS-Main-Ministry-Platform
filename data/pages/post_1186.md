SO-CALLED TRUTH about someone or something, like uncover a cover up, whistle blow, etc, can be either POSITIVE or NEGATIVE: 
- POSITIVE if FYI: to reduce ignorance, increase awareness. 
- NEGATIVE if to accuse, condemn, punish, or force a belief.
If to fault, accuse, condemn, control, it's GOSSIP & MALICE.
If reveal a problem, without a solution, a NEGATIVE 1/2 truth.…

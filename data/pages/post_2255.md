## Content

About God 
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Jesus clarified in Luke 20:38 that God is not a God of the dead, but of the living; for all live unto him. (seems the allegory meaning could be God is not law, but grace)
"The 
only true God" (John 17:3) isn't false/true, but "only true".
"God only wise (Rom 16:27) isn't foolish/wise, but "only wise".
"God of all comfort" (2Cor 1:3) isn't both discomfort/comfort.
God our Saviour (1Tim 2:3) isn't a Destroyer, but a Saviour.
God that "cannot lie" (Titus 1:2) isn't lie/truth, but "all truth".
God that "upbraideth not" (Jam 1:5) doesn't fault-find any.
"God of all grace" is not law/grace duality, but "all grace".
"That God is light" has no darkness in him at all (1Jn 1:5).
"God is love" isn't fear/love, but perfect love (1Jn 4:16-18).
The only wise God will have mercy, and not sacrifice (Mt 9:13).
(seems it allegory means God will have grace, and not law).
What are your insights about the only true God?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

## Content

Bible Allegory - script-u-are isn't plural & contrary scriptures. 
Seems 2Timothy 3:15,16 KJV uses both "scriptures" and "scripture", as if to clarify God only inspired all "scripture". For if God inspired all "scriptures" it would make God an oxymoron.
Eg: 2Timothy 3:15,16,17 KJV:
3:15 
And that from a child thou hast known the holy scriptures, which are able to make thee wise unto salvation through faith which is in Christ Jesus.
3:16 All scripture [is] given by inspiration of God, and [is] profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness:
3:17 That the man of God may be perfect, throughly furnished unto all good works.

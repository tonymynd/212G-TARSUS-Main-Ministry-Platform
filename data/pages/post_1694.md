## Content

Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Plural Observations Re The Biblical Story, Mystery, Allegory:
1. Hot FB topic being discussed is biblical story isn't our story. Agree, our reality IS "eternal life", has no beginning, nor end.
2. Yet also if pinch myself i still feel i got simulated into flesh. 
How can both be true, or simultaneous? Let's in-vestigate...
For 
me, when i humbled myself & asked God "upbraideth not" for His wisdom about my lie/truth duality, as being in conflict with His all truth, then i got a revelatory Glimpse of myself in heaven watching a biblical story that simulated me to earth; Because i beLIEved, bit a book hook of "In the beginning"... 
Seems if i bit a hook about our eternal life having a beginning, even if written for learning sake, a mystery twist therein is: if u began such Horeb-bull it requires u to "endure unto the end" .
Seems why Spirit prompted me investigate the end, notice of versions, KJV ends best, by grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] "with" you "all" (you & all the kingdom of God within you); But modern bibles remove "Christ" (end of f-law) from their end & even infer the grace of Jesus is partial to beLIEvers. 
So, after also notice in an Epistle Dedicatory KJV translators revealed they were neither Roman Catholic nor Protestant; For my learning journey through all allegoric mystery twists of biblical story, from old/new begins to the end, I felt KJV was a more exact translation & better end version for my learning.  
Seems duality contrast is how we learn both what we are not and what we are. As such I liked how KJV uses & contrasts both Holy Ghost(pneuma) as Comforter1 & Spirit(pneuma) of truth as Comforter2 that event-u-ally guides u into all truth.
All truth of "God of all comfort" & "God that cannot lie" & "God of all grace" is we cannot die because we are eternal LIFE.
The "grace" of our Lord "Jesus→Christ" [be/is] "with" you "all". Amen.

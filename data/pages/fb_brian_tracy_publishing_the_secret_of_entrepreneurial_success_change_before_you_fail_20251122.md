The Secret of Entrepreneurial Success: Change Before You Fail

## Transcript

Businesses started by successful entrepreneurs
succeed at a rate of 80 or 90 percent.
Businesses started by people with no business experience fail at a rate of 90 percent.
So if you start a business, a new business, even a simple business with services,

large competitors, large armies, were able to change continually in the middle of a battle.

## Content

WHY does (only) John have a double (Big, little) "Verily, verily" ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
Matthew, Mark, Luke say "Verily". John says double "Verily, verily" for Jesus quotes. In Greek "Verily, verily" means 'Amen, amen'. What's interesting about this is the 1st bible mention of Amen in Number 5:22 was a double (Big, little) "Amen, amen" of woman to being Blessed, cursed by a law priest…

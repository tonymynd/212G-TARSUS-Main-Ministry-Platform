## Content

Many Christian Bible readers ignore book of James. But I like James 1:5 in KJV, for it introduces the "God"  who "upbraideth not" we can "ask" for His wisdom directly, without going through any mediator man. I found That God (the God of all grace) is so gracious He will not force His Grace on anyone (like Law does), but he patiently waits to be asked. Asking that God for His wisdom will help you to then receive spiritual revelations from the Spirit of grace and truth.

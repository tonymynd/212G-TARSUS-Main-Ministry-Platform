Bible Mystery, about ETERNAL LIFE
Loving Grace, Mercy, Peace (to everyone everywhere) from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Here's what I learned from KJV Bible study:
Although Jesus mentioned these four terms:
- OT "everlasting life" (Daniel 12:2; Mt 19:29; Jn 3:16; Rom 6:22)
- NT "life eternal" (about Son of man: Mt 25:46),
- NT "life everlasting" (Lk 18:30; Jn 12:50; Ga; 6:8; 1Tim 1:16),
- NT "eternal life" (only term God gave: Jn 17:2; Rom 6:23; Tit 1:2)
NOTICE THIS:
"life eternal" is REVERSE of "eternal life",
"life everlasting" is REVERSE of "everlasting life",
AND NOTICE THIS:
- OT "everlasting life", also noted in NT, ends being mentioned in Rom 6:22 when contrasted with "eternal life" thru J→C in 6:23.
- NT "life eternal" ends being mentioned in John 17:3.
- NT "life everlasting" also ends being mentioned in 1Tim 1:16.
SO,
ONLY NT "eternal life" is clarified as what is God given,
noted in John 17:2; Romans 6:23; Titus 1:2.
Yet, 
even "eternal life" ends being mentioned in Jude 1:21,
and is not mentioned in Revelation
Jude 1:21 KJV
Keep yourselves in the love of God, looking for the mercy of our Lord Jesus Christ unto eternal life.
interesting mystery details to notice, ponder, discuss
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

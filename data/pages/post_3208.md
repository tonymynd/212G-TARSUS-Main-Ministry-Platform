## Content

Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Let's notice:
"as in Adam all die": 1Cor 15:22,
includes
first man "Adam" and last "Adam" in 1Cor 15:45.
For,
both are 'anthropos' in 1Cor 15:47
(let every anthropos be a liar: Rom 3:4).
So then,
Christ is third of three:
first Adam → last Adam → Christ.
all die → all still die → all made alive.
1Corinthians 15:22 KJV
For as in Adam all die, even so in Christ shall all be made alive.
1Corinthians 15:45 KJV
And so it is written, 
The first man Adam was made a living soul [psyche]; 
the last Adam was made a quickening spirit [pneuma].
1Corinthians 15:47 KJV
The first man [anthropos] is of the earth, earthy: 
the second man [anthropos] is the Lord from heaven.
Romans 3:4 KJV
let God be true, but every man [anthropos] a liar.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

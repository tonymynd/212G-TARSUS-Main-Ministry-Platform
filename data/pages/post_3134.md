## Content

"YE" & "YOU" in KJV Bible. 
Some ye/you examples, to help reveal if both ye & you occur in same verses, it's allegory contrast, and not just obsolete English as many ass-u-me.
Grace to "you", and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ (Romans-forward)
"ye" 
do err, because "ye" know not (Mt 22:29; Mk 12:24,27) 
Note: said to law folk, unaware of the lie in their beLIEve.
the kingdom of God is within "you" (Lk 17:21)
Note: if within ye, it would be a flaw kingdom of know not err.
"Ye" must be born again[from above] (John 3:7).
And "ye" shall know the truth, and the truth shall make "you" free (John 8:32).
Note: seems ye to you upgrade requires ye to know the truth.
A new commandment I give unto "you", That "ye" love one another; as I have loved "you" (John 13:34).
Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide "you" into all truth (John 16:13).
For sin shall not have dominion over "you" for "ye" are not under the law, but under grace.
Christ is become of no effect unto "you", whosoever of "you" are justified by the law; "ye" are fallen from grace (Gal 5:4).
One God, and Father of all, who is above all, and through all, and in "you" all (Eph 4:6).
Grace [be] with "you" all. Amen (Heb 13:25).
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with "you" all. Amen (Rev 22:21)

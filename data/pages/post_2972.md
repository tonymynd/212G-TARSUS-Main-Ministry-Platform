## Content

2Corinthians 5:17
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Interesting it says "in Christ", as if through Jesus → Christ our Lord to be "in Christ", which is only then all things new, and because in Christ = the end of the law = all grace = all new, for the old has passed away. For in Jesus it was still both old and new, but in Christ it's all new, a new creature.
The 
(pure) grace of our Lord Jesus → Christ be with you all. Amen.

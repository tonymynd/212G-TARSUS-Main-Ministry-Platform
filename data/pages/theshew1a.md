About the Author  
The Man Who Couldn't Be Killed  
  

Daniel Miles was born when Israel became a nation again: May 1948. His religious parents named him Daniel, after the Old Testament prophet of doom and gloom prophecies (shall fai) which many such like then associated with the book of Revelation as if such "like unto the Son of man" Horeb-bull would only be the fate of others whenever it (such vengeful law <= law) came to pass.  
  
They obviously didn't read the end a Son of man has for "them all", which is the opposite of the end the Son of God has for "us all". Two ends via two Sons for two alls: "them all" & "us all". Go figure.  
  
At the time (1948), many legalistic-Christians thought the end of the world might come within forty years (a biblical generation according to the Holy Ghost) due to "Israel" being perceived as the "fig tree" sign in the fig tree parable of Matthew 24, Mark 13, and Luke 21:  
  
"Now learn a parable of the fig tree; when his branch is yet tender, and putteth forth leaves, ye know that summer [is] nigh. So likewise when ye shall see all these things (deceit, wars, rumors of wars, famines, pestilences, earthquakes in diverse places), know that it (false end of false Christs: abomination of desolation spoken of by Daniel in the holy place, great tribulation, followed by the sign of the Son of man appearing in heaven) is near, [even] at the (pluralized) [doors](Allegory5.md). Verily I say unto you, This generation shall not pass, till all these things be fulfilled. (Matt 24; Mk 13)."  
  
"And he spake to them a parable; Behold the fig tree, and all the trees; When they now shoot forth ye see and know of your own selves that summer is now nigh at hand. So likewise ye, when ye see these things come to pass, know ye that the kingdom of God is nigh at hand. Verily I say unto you, This generation shall not pass away, till all be fulfilled. (Lk 21)"  
  
Funny thing about "them": a wicked and adulterous (Adamic) generation seeketh after a sign, is they easily get convinced Israel is the fig tree sign, but will neither see nor admit the [Bible is allegoric](Allegory.md); Even though it defines itself (both covenants) as being "allegory" (Gal 4:24) from Genesis to Revelation, also states scriptures were written "aforetime" (before time) for our "learning" (Rom 15:4).  
  
Forty years (1988) came and went with as little doom and gloom as Y2K (false of false/true ends of last millennium). And Daniel went on through what may very well be the last decade, century, and millennium... on into the prophetic "third day" (beyond 2,000 AD), to become an impure religion Ghost Buster.  
  
Daniel Miles was a happily married with children Real Estate Broker with positive foresight, and prospered for the first quarter century. That was followed by this: Murphy's Law, and disaster for another l-o-n-g quarter. From such half century of evidence, he finally realized he was simply experiencing a comparative biblical teaching.  
  
During the latter end worse of two periods of time, and barely functioning as a grieving widowed, bi-polar, and impoverished Mr. Mom, he got re-invovled again with many churches (Protestant, Roman Catholic, Independent, Messianic); And began intensive Bible study (all day, every day), to the point he's perhaps now the most 'Bible' studied person on the planet, including the likes of evangelastic Billy (goat) Graham and Jack of the 'Impe' family (who rattles off scriptures out of context like a serpent about to strike), perhaps also the world's leading expert on biblical allegory.  
  
Daniel couldn't be killed. Earlier in his biblical seek and find (looking for reason) the Spirit reminded him of many times he miraculously survived while having foolish fun with friends. And among many miraculous tales he could tell, it seems thrice people "taken captive" deliberately tried, and failed, to kill him with their vehicles (high speed collisions: both head on and rear end, followed by another failed attempt to run him over whilst walking on the sidewalk.  
  
Each time the offending vehicles became undriveable, their drivers and passengers taken to hospital; But Daniel both drove and walked away without a scratch, even though not wearing a seat belt. Whatever(?) they all hit (just short of hitting Daniel or his vehicle), and brought them to a dead 'stop it' (Woe!), was not visible by any. In the rear end collision, dirt on Daniel's bumper wasn't scratched.  
  
Daniel also has some miraculous weather stories. The last three places he lived, previous l-o-n-g and severe droughts became lakes full in short time. Once a group of native witch doctors gathered from two countries, sent him a man-made tornado the size of suburb. When he saw it forming from all four winds and the descending funnel appeared, he sent his children to the basement and spoke to it, saying return to sender. The next day it was on the national news, declared a national disaster. Interestingly, an entire Indian reserve had been demolished by it; Yet there was no loss of life, nor a blade of grass harmed on land neighboring the reserve.  
  
At age 50 Daniel began internet ministry, knowing the "third day" was nigh and the world wide web was rapidly becoming globe-all. He began with emails to existing religious web sites, challenged all comers on religious discussion forums. On one particular discussion forum (Gospelrevolution.com) he impressed the non-disclosed host, a southern Baptist PK (Preacher's Kid), and to the point this awakened man offered to also host Daniel with an unlimited web site and unmoderated discussion forum (for Danie) on his web server.  
  
So, Daniel began Godshew.org the 2nd year of the 3rd day (2003) from Atlanta; Where he visited an internet gal friend who became his first sponsor, to both give and receive assistance of mutual benefit.  
  
Daniel now resides in Canada again, and the web site has abounded accordingly with "wheresover sin abounded, grace did much  more abound". With a hundred web pages made in the first two years, and as many keywords in the #1 position on the #1 internet search engine (Google), Godshew.org was thereby established, and by it's third year (2005) it boasted over 500 keywords in top ten positions.  
  
Daniel will not compromise; For God does not compromise. He's refused many invitations to link to other ministries and commercial web sites; Also returned a ministry license from Gospel Ministries International, since they wouldn't stop imputing sin for the hell of it.

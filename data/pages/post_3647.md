## Content

WHY do these Bible terms cease to be mentioned at certain points?
Grace, mercy, peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
OT→NT "everlasting life":
ceases being mentioned in Romans 6:22 when contrasted with "eternal life" 6:23 is clarified as the gift [charisma] of "God" through our Lord Jesus → Christ (is the end of the law: Rom 10:4).
OT→NT "Son of man":
ceases being mentioned in Acts 7:56, never again mentioned, for beyond Acts it's the gospel of "Christ", who is only Son of God.
"favour" [charis]:
ceases being mentioned in Acts 25:3, where it is requested "against" Paul to "kill" him. Grace [charis] continues to the end.
"conscience" [syneidesis], which began in John 8:9
ceases to be mentioned in 1Peter 3:21 as "good conscience".
"Christ Jesus", which began in Acts 19:4, and mentioned only by Paul 55x and 1Peter 2x, ceases to be mentioned in 1Peter 5:14, 
but "Jesus Christ" = only Son of God continues unto the end.
"mercy":
ceases to be mentioned in Jude 1:21, and notably does not occur in John, nor in 1John, nor in Revelation.
"eternal life":
ceases to be mentioned in Jude 1:21, 
so not mentioned in Revelation.
What are your insights about such?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

It is preposterous order to teach first and learn afterward. 
~KJV Translators (learned men, in Notes To KJB Readers)
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
Seems why
Jesus Mt 9:13 told Pharisees GO and LEARN what that (Hosea 6:6) "I will have mercy, and not sacrifice" (allegory) meaneth, 
and
Mt 12:7 noted if ye had known what this (allegory) meaneth: I will mercy, and not sacrifice, ye would not have condemned the guiltless (perhaps allegory for ye would not have lawed the grace). 
Paul Romans 15:4 noted whatsoever things were written aforetime were written for our LEARNING (as if to learn first, teach after).
What are your insights about such things?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

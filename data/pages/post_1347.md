## Content

Re "Blessed": 
Seems Romans 4:7,8 Contrasts 2 law/grace (they/us) kinds:
- Rom 4:7 = Diaper Blessed: sin imputed, forgiven, covered*.
- Rom 4:8 = Man** Blessed: no sin imputed to forgive nor cover up.
*JC said all cover ups also get uncovered & made known 
**this 'aner' man differs from 'anthropos' man = liar in Rom 3:4.
Romans 4:7,8 KJV (from Psalms 32:1,2 KJV): CAPS mine:
4:7 Blessed are THEY whose iniquities are forgiven, and whose sins are covered. 
(note: seems like diaper blessed, sins only "covered" up)
4:8 Blessed is the MAN to whom the Lord will not impute sin.
(note: seems when 'aner' man, then sin is not even imputed, neither to forgive nor to cover up) 
Seems point of this "Blessed" contrast is to help us realize:
- Where no law, There no transgression: Romans 4:15
- When no law, Then sin is not imputed: Romans 5:13. 
- So, Reckon yourself dead to sin = dead to law = alive to God: Rom 6:7 - 7:4
Seems if we connect these verses it says evil = law:
- Our Father...deliever us from EVIL: Mt 6:13 (Lk 11:4)
- We are delivered from the LAW: Romans 7:6.

## Content

Are there TWO OF EVERYTHING in Bible to sort out?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Genesis 1 has a small h heaven & Big H Heaven (firmament)...
in 
Greek there's TWO kinds of:
"knowledge": gnosis & epignosis 
"converted": strepho & epistrepho 
"word" of God: rhema & logos
TWO covenants: old covenant & new covenant (of OT LORD).
TWO testaments: old testament & new testament.
TWO kinds of "Adam": 1Corinthians 15:45 KJV
first man Adam = soul & last Adam = spirit
TWO kinds of law: Romans 8:2
law of sin and death
law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus.
Two kinds of wrath: thymos & orge; 
Both to be put away in Eph 4:31.
TWO before/after modes: 1Cor 15
Natural [psychikos] first, 
Spiritual [pneumatikos] afterward.
TWO kinds of immortality: 1Cor 15:53,54
incorruption [aphtharsia: also called "immortality"]
immortality [athanasia] 
(note: takes both for death to be swallowed up in victory)
TWO kinds of wisdom: James 3
wisdom not from above: earthly, sensual [psychikos], devilish.
wisdom from above: first pure, then seven things
TWO kinds of Comforter to be sent: John 14 - 16 KJV
Holy "Ghost": law teacher & reminder: John 14:26
"Spirit" of truth: grace "guide" into "all truth": John 15:26; 16:13.
Perhaps even TWO kinds of God to sort out:
OT LORD [Yehova]: both saved and afterward destroyed.
NT God [Theos]: will have all men saved and made aware. 
(only one is called the "only true God" & "only wise God").
Perhaps even TWO kinds/modes of Jesus to sort out:
Jesus as "Son of man": both nice & nasty (healed & threatened).
Jesus as "Son of God": only nice as "the only begotten".
(Note: no mention of Son of man, only Son of God, beyond Acts).
TWO kinds of "Lamb" in Greek to contrast:
Lamb [ammos] of God: John 1:29
Lamb [arnion]: Revelation 20x
What are your insights about such biblical things?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

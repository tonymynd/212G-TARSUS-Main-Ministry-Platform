"peace on earth" or "on earth peace": What does Bible say? Seems wording difference is the difference between "Jesus" & "Christ":
- Re "peace on earth": "JESUS" said, "think not" nor "suppose" I am come to either "send" or "give" peace on earth: Mt 10:34; Lk 12:51.
- Re "on earth peace": Lk 2:11-15 heralding of "on earth peace, good will toward men" is about "CHRIST" (not 2SonsJesus). 
SO, what's the DIFFERENCE between "Jesus" & 
"Christ"? Perhaps, when we learn Law isn't Grace, we can then have "on earth peace".

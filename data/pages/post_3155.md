## Content

LOVE [agape 1] & CHARITY [agape 2]
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and our Lord Jesus Christ. 
Of 3 biblical seek & find modes: cOLD←>LukeWarm→Hot,
Jesus first noted in Matthew 24:12 KJV, about "love" [agape 1], 
"Because iniquity shall abound, the love [agape 1] of many shall
wax ← cold [psycho]", aka go backward to cold of 3:
Cold←>LukeWarm→Hot.
So 
a "more excellent way" in 1Corinthians 12:31 described in 1Corinthians 13 KJV is "charity" [agape 2].
1Cor 13:5 notes:
Charity "thinketh no evil" (no reverse of live, not even to forgive).
(note: modern versions say love keeps no record of wrong).
So 1Cor 13:8 KJV clarifies (of love & charity):
Charity "never faileth": But 
whether prophecies, they shall fail,
whether tongues, they shall cease,
whether knowledge [gnosis], it shall vanish away.
So, 1Cor 13:13 KJV notes:
Charity is "greatest" of "three": faith, hope, charity. 
(allegory as if greatest of great ←> greater → greatest).
Then in Colossians 3:12-15 KJV it notes:
Charity: bond of perfectness: is also 8th above 7.
Charity can be new man put on above forgiving: Col 3:12-15 KJV.
(note: that's 8th above 7 things, like Father is 8th in Eph 4:4-6).
Then,
Charity should be added above brotherly kindness in 2Pet 1:5-11;
As if to allegory say even brotherly kindness has some blindness.
So of "love" [agape 1] & "charity" [agape 2] both used in KJV for Greek word 'agape', there's something about "charity" which makes apostle Paul call it a "more excellent [hyperbole] way" in 1Cor 12:31 which then is described as "charity" in 1Cor 13 KJV.
Such is not posted to diminish love [agape 1], but to notice what Jesus said about love, in Mt 24:12, that of 7 kinds of NT love, even 'agape' can wax cOLD [psycho], of three seek & find modes: 
cOLD ←> LukeWarm → Hot
as if 
go BACKward to great again of great ←> greater → greatest. 
So when it comes to three things (not just twain)
good ←> better → best = rest of the story.
Do the allegoric math:
1/3=0.333 ←> 2/3=0.666 → 3/3=1.000
nothing ←> nothing → everything
Also, for Revelation 22:21 KJV,
Consider KJV end to endure unto is the grace salutation of Paul (not John), written by Paul's "own hand", as Paul clarified in Colossians 4:18 and 2Thessalonians 3:17,18;
The GRACE of our Lord Jesus → Christ (BE) WITH YOU ALL. AMEN.

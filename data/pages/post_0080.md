## Content

Bible Mystery: "from such turn away": 2Timothy 3:5. Such?
"such" includes "last days" of plural God on high in Hebrews;
Because such "last days" of this God are also "perilous times".
It's as "turn away" from a mirror to let an image "vanish away";
For 
until we do that, an image vs likeness war is never ending.
From a dark side image reverses both left/right & under/above.
As Solomon said, then it's "all vanity and vexation of Spirit" x7.
Yet when it comes to let such "vanish away", there's two kinds:
- "vanish away" in 1Cor 13:8 KJV means just rendered idle
- "vanish away" in Heb 8:13 KJV means to disappear: Poof.
So it's the difference between Puff or Poof goes the dragon.
If Satan is only bound + loosed, then it's Puff goes the dragon.
But if Satan is flushed as dung, then it's Poof goes the dragon.
If sin is only diaper covered up, rendered idle, it can get uncovered.
But if sin is taken away, by abolition of law, it cannot get uncovered.
Behold the 'amnos' Lamb of God TAKETH AWAY the sin of the world,
is different than an 'arnion' Lamb covers up + uncovers sin afterward. 
Movie Example of "from such turn away":
In a 1998 Merlin movie, they finally turn away from evil Queen Mame.
Then her evil is no longer given "place", but does this: "vanish away".

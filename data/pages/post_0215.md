## Content

WHEN... THEN
Perhaps WHEN we put away law, which is "both good and evil",
THEN we'll stop having the duality of both friends and enemies.
I found WHEN I put away law to be graceful, merciful, peaceful,
THEN 
due to 
#NoEnmity
, even former enemies became friends. 
THEN I had a lasting peace of true love that doesn't wax cOLD;
The peace of grace→mercy→peace that cannot be removed.
So I really like Bible message of 1Timothy 1:2 KJV, which says:
Grace, mercy, [and] peace from
God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
It has mercy in midst of grace and peace.
Wishing grace→mercy→peace to you all.

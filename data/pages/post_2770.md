## Content

Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Interesting things about Romans 4:7,8 KJV:
4:7 Blessed are THEY whose iniquities are forgiven, and whose sins are covered.
(note: allegory as if diaper cover up, about which Jesus also said cover ups get uncovered & revealed: Lk 12:2)
4:8 Blessed the MAN [aner] to whom the Lord will not impute sin.
(note: no sin imputed to forgive for the 'aner' man like Christ)
Seems Paul allegory contrasts two Blessed's as if
4:7 Law Blessed
4:8 Grace Blessed 
Interesting allegoric mystery contrast to notice, ponder, discuss.
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus → Christ be with you all. Amen.

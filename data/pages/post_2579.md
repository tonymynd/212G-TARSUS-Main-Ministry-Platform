## Content

The NEW TESTAMENT of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ 
Reveals 
The "Only True God" is not both good [kalos] and evil [kakos], but one: only good [agathos]; as if not law, but grace (Mark 10:18)  
That 
God to know sent Jesus Christ with grace and truth (John 1:17) in contrast to Old Testament Law and Prophets.
That God of Abraham (not Abram) and of Isaac (not Ishmael) and of Jacob (not Israel) is NOT God of the dead, but of the living; For ALL live unto him: Luke 20:38.
To "God only wise" be glory through Jesus → Christ for ever. Amen. (Romans 16:27 conclusion).
That God is NOT the author of confusion, but of peace (1Cor 14:33).
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
(1Timothy 1:2)
That "God our Saviour" will have:
ALL men saved (by grace),
AND 
to come unto the (higher) knowledge [epignosis] of the truth. (1Timothy 2:3,4)
That God "upbraideth not" (James 1:5).
That God is "the God of all grace" (no law at all): 1Peter 5:10.
To "the only wise God" = "God our Saviour" be glory and majesty, dominion and power, both now and ever. Amen. (Jude 1:25).
The End to endure unto to be saved only, by grace only, not both saved and afterward destroyed by OT LORD [Yehova] did: Jude 1:5, is notably not the salutation of John, but the salutation of Paul:
Revelation 22:21 KJV 
The GRACE (only) of our Lord Jesus Christ BE WITH YOU ALL. AMEN.

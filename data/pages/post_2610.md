## Content

ALLness
ALL TRUTH ... ALL NEW ... ALL GRACE ... ALL LIGHT 
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
John 
16:13 
(ALL TRUTH, as if no more lie to beLIEve): 
Howbeit when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into ALL TRUTH: 
2Corinthians 5:17 
(ALL NEW, as if no more old nor old/new twain):
Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, ALL things are become NEW.
2Corinthians 9:8 
(ALL GRACE, as if no more law):
And God is able to make ALL GRACE abound toward you; that ye, always having all sufficiency in all things, may abound to every good work:
1Peter 5:10 
(God of ALL GRACE, as if having no law to flaw):
But the God of ALL GRACE, who hath called us unto his eternal glory by Christ Jesus, after that ye have suffered a while, make you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you.
1John 1:5
(ALL LIGHT, as if "that God": ALL GRACE: has no darkness at all)
This then is the message which we have heard of him, and declare unto you, that God is light, and in him is no darkness at all.
What are your insights about ALLness?
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus → Christ be/is with you all. Amen.

## Content

END GOAL? As I see it, pure grace (higher grace than grace on high) is our end goal and source of unconditional love to all without partiality. 
Eg: Paul's epistles all end with grace, even Hebrews.
Eg: heart established "WITH grace" (Heb 13:9) then neither "under law" nor "under grace" = still under of under/above.
Eg: "grace of God" is more than "grace of Christ" as churches still got removed from "grace of Christ": Gal 1:6. So 2Cor 
5:20 beseeches be reconciled to "God" (not Jesus, nor Christ).
Eg: the God of "all grace" = neither law nor law/grace duality.

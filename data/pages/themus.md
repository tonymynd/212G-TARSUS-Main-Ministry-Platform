### God giveth us the victory [through](through.md) our Lord Jesus>[Christ](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md)

While not wanting to dwell on divisions and sides wars (them vs them), but on unity and peace (us), it seems necessary to address the allegorical distinction between them and us; And how it's mirrorly reversed in the Old Testament and New Testament. For Hebrew is read backward, from right to center, and Greek is read forward, from center to right. Yet the distinction between them and us is not a center vs right scenario, but a beneath(law) and above(grace) scenario. So change, or conversion from them to us, is not a political change from center wing politics to right wing politics nor a religious conversion from law to law (Adam1 to Adam2) as many (deceived by many shall come [to deceive](Heart.md)) suppose; But rather it's a transformation from them beneath (law) to us above (grace), as if spiritually growing in grace, from child to man.  
  
For God said: let us make man; And David said: blessed the man to whom the Lord will not impute sin; and Paul said: when I became a man, I put away childish things (laws). So there's an ever increasing cry for [just us](ShewBread2.md#justus) (no more them at all) world wide, and for [true peace](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Peace.md) thereof [unity of the Spirit](GodShew2.md) (Grace). Mean while, Christ(is the end of the law) is our peace who abolished the law([enmity](Law.md#Was)); And [them vs them](Romans8.md) still continue to try and continue to fail to attain peace via their [childish](GodShew.md#nomorechildren) sides wars, which have side effects to both sides of law vs law and law vs grace [blame games](RevelatorySermons15.md), which induct all players thereof into a [hall of shame](Hebrews12.md), not a hall of fame. For even when playing "your part" of [their part vs your part](TheirPartYourPart.md), it's still [part](Partiality.md)iality, and not yet [perfect](GraceUntoYou.md)ion. When perfect-ion comes, then that which is in part is done away; As childish things (laws) are done away when becoming [man](TwainShew.md#Blesseds) kind (grace us): 1Cor 13:10,11.

Not wanting to dwell on division rather unity, it seems necessary to address the biblical fact the allegorical difference between ye and you, or them and us, is as if comparing division and unity, war and peace, confusion and understanding. God is not the author of confusion. Rather God is the author of peace. For from the beginning, God did not see both good and evil (law), but rather God saw good x7: only "good" x6, then "very good" the 7th time, in Genesis 1. So those who see both good and evil are not seeing it as God saw it, from above.

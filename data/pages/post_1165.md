## Content

LOVE was indeed Jesus "commandment" (rule, ordinance, law) to them, as unto "little [law] children" (John 13:33,34) not yet full grown in grace, mercy, peace; But as we go further in New Testament, beyond LukeWarm Mt-Acts to them, into epistles of Apostle Paul to us, we not only learn God hath given us the victory, but in 1Timothy 1:5 KJV there's even an "end" to the "commandment", "NOW", and the end of the commandment is "charity" (more than "love") out of a pure heart 
(established with grace). 
Point: Grace is more than Love. Love was a commandment. Love was first (not last) fruit of the Spirit. Yet Grace does not command, but awares until become graceful, merciful, peaceful to point of no longer need commanding.

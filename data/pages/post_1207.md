Hurricane Harvey: Texas Event? While not suggesting any judgmental condemnation, nor finger pointing at Texas, it does seem this hurricane + lingering flooding + tornadoes allegory portrays that "wrath" of "law worketh wrath" has somehow manifested and aimed itself at Texas people. It kinda resembles what's (allegory) written in 
1Thessalonians 5:3 KJV: (re THEM)
When THEY shall say Peace and safety (in law), then sudden destruction (of 
law worketh wrath) cometh upon THEM...
In allegory them/us contrast 1Thess 5:9 says (re US):
God hath not appointed US to wrath, but to obtain salvation (from wrath) by our Lord Jesus Christ (by whom came grace and truth in contrast to law: John 1:17)
While I wish only grace, mercy, and peace (that lasts) unto all, and applaud acts of compassion also noted in this event; I suggest there may be something we can all learn from it: that if we give place to 'rule of law', then "law worketh wrath" can manifest, and bring destructive loss, even loss of life...
What are your thoughts about it?

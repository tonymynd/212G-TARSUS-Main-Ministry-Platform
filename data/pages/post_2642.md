## Content

FAVOUR[charis] & GRACE[charis]??
Grace, mercy, peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ. 
God's grace[charis] is much more than unmerited favour[charis].
Favour mention 6x ends in Acts 25:3, where it's requested "against", to "kill" Paul. 
Grace mention then still occurs over 100x more, never against nor ever to kill. 
Paul's Holy Kiss Greeting:
Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.
Paul's Salutation, written by his own hand:
The GRACE of our Lord Jesus Christ BE WITH YOU ALL. AMEN.

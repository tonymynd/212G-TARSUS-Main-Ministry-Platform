## Content

Some Jesus & Christ differences of through our Lord Jesus Christ to be in Christ we have God given victory:
- Jesus was only called Saviour of Israel (not all)
- Christ is called Saviour of the world (all)
- Jesus, made under the law, was still only change of the law
- 
Christ is the end of the law & by abolish the law is all grace 
- Jesus, as 2Sons with 2voices, was allegory like 2seeds
- Christ, as 1Son, with 1voice is called "the seed, not seeds"
- Jesus sat down on right hand of Majesty on high
- Christ sitteth on the right hand of God

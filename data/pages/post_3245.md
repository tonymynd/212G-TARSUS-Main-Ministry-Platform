PALM SUNDAY TWAINS
Matthew 21; Mark 11: Luke 19; John 12
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Coming up from Jericho to Jerusalem Jesus approaches TWO villages: Bethpage & Bethany near Mount of Olives. 
There he sends TWO disciples to the village over against to fetch TWO animals: an ass & colt of an ass whom no man has sat on.
The place where TWO disciples find the TWO animals to fetch is called the place where TWO ways met: Mark 11:4.
Then, after a meal with Lazarus, 
that many came to the feast at Jerusalem also went to Bethany to see Lazarus whom Jesus had previously raised from the dead, 
Jesus made a triumphal entry to Jerusalem as TWO things: king (of the Jews) & the prophet of Nazareth.
On the way from Bethany to Jerusalem, there were TWO multitudes who accompanied Jesus:
- the multitude that went before (as if law driven),
- the multitude that followed after (as if grace led).
Interesting twains to notice and discuss.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

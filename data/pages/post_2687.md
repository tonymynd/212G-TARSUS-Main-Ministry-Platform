OHIO (East Palestine, Ohio, USA) TRAIN DERAILMENT 
(chemical disaster).
some interesting things being reported:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
- One of train cars had wheels on fire (shown on camera in another town) about 40 minutes before the train derailment. Seems a safety thing called a hot box indicator did not catch it in time.
- They made a movie about it called White Noise, about a year ago, filmed in Ohio, using some East Palestine extras. Then it happened.
- Seems a railroad strike was planned earlier to address things like safety concerns, but Biden stopped it by declaring railroad was an essential service, so could not go on strike.
- Seems this train had about 150 cars (only 100 car maximum is considered safe), of which about 50 derailed, of which about 20 contained toxic chemicals, such as 'vinyl-chloride'.
- Seems this toxic chemical caused dead fish to appear in water systems, and chickens to die in nearby farms.
- After the primary fire from the derailment was put out, officials reportedly determined that a "controlled-burn" of other cars, carrying the chemical "vinyl-chloride" was the safest way to prevent an uncontrolled release of such chemicals or uncontrolled explosion.  So officials reportedly engaged in a controlled burn.
The problem is that when vinyl chloride burns, it gives off other, deadly, chemicals; primarily hydrogen chloride and . . .  phosgene gas. Phosgene gas is a chemical weapon used in World War one, choking troops to death on battlefields in Europe. 
This is a HUGE problem that won't go away by msm avoiding it.

## Content

Here's my rant, and quandry, and apology to you all:
I find, in Paul's epistles, formatted as PEP talks (Positive, Evaluate, Positive), he first begins with a Positive "holy kiss" greet to all brethren (has NO mention of law, sin, unrest):
"Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ".
Paul 
even first mentions Positive affirmation prior to Evaluate mode, later sharing positive upgrades to "put on", after first "put off" negative things; And so, by also Evaluate & apply upgrades, the last Positive be even much more Positive: 
"The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be/is] WITH you ALL. Amen".
Seems, in Evaluate mode, Paul mentions, in Galatians 5, "fruit" of Spirit having 9 aspects to walk through, first "love", 9th being "temperance", as if then self-control, and then mention of "no law"; 
Perhap as if then, when temperance, 
no more need for laws to command or govern a person previously in child=servant mode noted in Gal 4:1-7's no more a servant(child, still serving law, sin, etc), but a son.
My quandry is: 
Why don't we see a holy kiss greeting among Believers? 
I share this especially as note to myself, first, and not to point a finger at anyone, with three then pointed back at me, no, but as to suggest, and challenge (yes,, myself included), all brethren claiming to be grace believers, to greet all brethren with Grace & peace from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ, prior to Evaluate any flaw to anyone else. 
I hereby apologize to all, for not firstly holy kiss greet Grace & peace to all brethren, in my posts and comments.

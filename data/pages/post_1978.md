## Content

Voice from within: we are not really here. Made me smile.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
After a month of hearing Covid-19 pandemic Death Tolls had me going to bed with a frown, on my way to bed I heard a voice from within say: we are not really here: Made me smile. It helped my spirit recall this flesh mode world of life + death mortality (by grace + law duality) is not our eternal life reality.
No 
wonder why last year, when frustrated by hear of "worst" scenarios & "death" tolls in news & weather, the Spirit of truth guide had prompted me to study about & focus on eternal life. Then it was still diverse places; Now it's ww: 184 countries.
Seems we're manifested here to learn by way of contrast, both what our reality is not (not grace + law), and IS (ALL GRACE).
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

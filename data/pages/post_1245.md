## Content

Fruit of the Spirit: I found it interesting to notice, when doing a study of fruit of the Spirit in Galatians 5:22,23, grace "goodness" (has no conditions nor severity attached) seems to occur as 6th fruit, beyond the 5th in the midst of 9 fruit. So... 
although it begins with Love, it also Spirit leads us (as guide = follow me) forward, through all 9 fruit, unto 9th = Temperance, allegory as if His Grace does not want to control 
people, but rather wants all people to have Temperance = Self Control, such that we no longer need to be commanded, allegory like child=servant in Gal 4:1-7, becomes "no more a servant".

## Content

If using duality contrasts, always let such twain end Positive. 
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Jesus Christ contrasted what God is not vs God is; But his contrast ended Positive: "He is not the God of the dead, but the God of the living": Mt 22:32; Mk 12:27; Lk 20:38. (As if to thereby allegory say God is not law = lie, but grace = truth).…

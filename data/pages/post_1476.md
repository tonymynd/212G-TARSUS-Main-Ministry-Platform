## Content

from Colossians 3: re ALSO focus UPward beyond FORward:
- IF risen with "Christ", sitteth on right hand of "God" (allegory not still on right hand of "Majesty" on "high", where "Jesus" 1st sat "down" & there still had "enemies": Heb 1:3,13); 
AS IF risen with "Christ" is like yet higher than high: like "far above all heavens": Eph 4:10; and like "high priest" also got made "higher than heavens" on high: Heb 7:26.
- THEN focus on things 
above, not lower things on the earth.
Why?
So the ride is not from side to side, but focused above sides. 
For...
As apostle Paul allegory notes, there's 2 kinds of "through":
- FORward "through" our Lord Jesus Christ, to be "in Christ".
- UPward "through" Christ to God-ward, as ALSO then reconciled to "God" (as if higher than "Majesty" on high).

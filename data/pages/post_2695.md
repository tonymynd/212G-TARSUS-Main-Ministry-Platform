## Content

Philippians 2:11

The Tongue?
Every tongue should confess that Jesus Christ (grace & truth) is Lord to the glory of God the Father. Why? 
Salvation of all is by grace & awareness for all is by truth, until "all grace" (no law) & "all truth" (no lie) is eternal life for all.

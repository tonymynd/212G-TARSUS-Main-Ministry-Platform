"Grace unto you" ... "from God our Father" ("the God of all grace") is [pure grace](GodShew2.md#Allness), allegorically [pure religion](RevelatorySermons7.md) in James 1:27 and the [pure wisdom](TwainShew7.md#Wisdoms) in James 3:17. Noahic grace is not the same as the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ  

The grace [Noah](ShewBread8.md#Noah) found in the eyes of the LORD was partial: only saved some; and only eight "souls". The grace which came by Jesus Christ is spiritual grace and impartial: saves all. Save some only scuttles all perish till we get to the end where none perish, which speaks of the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ with you all, against none of you. Hence there are [two sorts of grace](TwainShew3.md#Graces) to sort out.  
  

That God is light and in him there is no darkness at all  

Just as "[that God](GodShew2.md) is light and in him there is no darkness at all"; So also "the [God of all grace](GodShew2.md#Allness)" is all grace and no law at all. In the same manner "God is love", the perfect (mercifu) sort, is also all love and [no fear at all](ShewBread4.md#freedom). "God [is] a Spirit" is also all Spirit and [no Ghost at all](HGvsHS.md). And in such pure wisdom, such pure light(day), such perfect love, such a Spirit of grace and [truth](YeYou.md) thereof, we find grace is the root source of all God (good) stuff which is not only a given, but "freely given", to all, [without partiality](Partiality.md). Grace comes from a "throne of grace", where mercy is mercy full and forgiven is a given, unto "you"; and by taking away [the law](Law.md) from grace + law, which is by "[the operation of God](Allegory5.md)".

# What is Grace? Of Enmity/Friend, it is Friend.

What is Grace?  
First let's see what grace is not:  
Of Law/Grace, it is not [Law](RevelatorySermons24.md); And  
Of Sacrifice/Mercy, it is not [Sacrifice](Sacrifice.md);  
And Of Division/Peace, it is not [Division](Division.md).  
  
Of 4 mentions of "grace" in 4 gospels, [2 Sons Jesus](Revelations10.md) never mentions it.  
"Jesus" brought division (unrest), not peace (rest): Lk 12:51: Heb 4:8.  
"[Christ](RevelatorySermons17.md)" is the end of the law, and is not divided, is notably our peace.  
Therefore reconciling [the world](Partiality.md) to [God](GodShew2.md) is done [in Christ](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md), not in Jesus.  
Yet [reconciled](Reconciliation.md) in 2Cor 5 is neither to Jesus, nor to Christ, but to God.  
For the 2nd of [reconciled x2 in Romans 5](TwainShew6.md#Reconcileds) can be to life of life + death.  
God didn't send his Son to reconcile us unto life + death, but life [only](Potentate.md).

## Content

Grace Inspiration: GOODNESS?? Revelatory look at 2 kinds.  New Testament, in greek mode, allegory contrasts Law "goodness" ('chrestotes') & Grace "goodness" ('agathosune'): 
- LAW "goodness", also called "gentleness" ('chrestotes') is conditional(if) & if u not always good, has severity attached. eg: a Law "God" Apostle Paul allegory portrays in Romans 11:22 has both both "goodness and severity"; And because Law is "both good and evil". So, IF not always good: OUCH.
- GRACE 
"goodness" is not conditional nor has punishment  attached. The God of all Grace is only & always good, never both goodness & severity, nor both good & evil. YEAH.
Another revelatory thing about NT "goodness", which occurs 9 times, is it occurs in a 5/4 ratio: firstly 5 times as 'chrestotes', then 4 times as 'agathosune'. What's revelatory about such is "goodness" in Galatians 5:22,23 KJV's 9 fruit of the Spirit is 6th, after 5, and 5th "gentleness" (chrestotes) in MIDST of 9 is still like unto law "goodness" (chrestotes) having severity.
So then, first 5 fruit of Spirit are still Law, last 4 are Grace:
- First 5: love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness = Law.
- Last 4: goodness, faith, meekness, temperance = Grace.
So,
Gal 5:18 IF led of Spirit (not Ghost driven), it's as if LED: 
FROM under the law x5 ...TO... "not under the law" x4. 
This FYI can be quite shocking for CJ law lovers to learn.
Learning the difference of 2 kinds of "goodness", by revelation & study, helped me to say Nay to Law & Yay to Grace. For it explained why I'd experienced goodness & severity. It was because I'd put trust in law. not yet knowing law is both good & evil, lastly evil with a vengeance. So I put away legalism.
For there's no song called Amazing Law, only Amazing Grace.

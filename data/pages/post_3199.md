TWO KINDS OF IMMORTALITY
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Interesting in 1Corinthians 15:53,54 KJV there is TWO kinds of immortality in Greek; And both must be put on for death to be swallowed up in victory.
1Cor 
15:53,54 KJV
53 For this corruptible must put on incorruption [APTHARSIA: immortality in Rom 2:7 & 1Tim 1:10], and this mortal must put on immortality [ATHANASIA].
54 So when this corruptible shall have put on incorruption, and this mortal shall have put on immortality, then shall be brought to pass the saying that is written, Death is swallowed up in victory.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

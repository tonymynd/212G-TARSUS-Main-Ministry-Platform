## Content

LAMB vs LAMB
The Lamb [arnion] in Revelation is NOT the Lamb [amnos] of God.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Many 
Bible readers assume "Lamb" in Revelation is the same as the "Lamb" of God. It is not. "Lamb" of God which taketh away the sin of the world in John 1:29; Jn 1: 36; Acts 8:32; 1Peter 1:19 is 'amnos' in Greek. 
But "Lamb" in Revelation is always 'arnion', never 'amnos'. 
Lamb [arnion] in Revelation 5:6 has 7 horns & 7 eyes. 
Lamb [arnion] in Rev 6 opens seals of a 7 sealed book written within and on the backside. Then when a second seal is opened a rider of red horse takes away peace from the earth, causing people to kill one another. 
This "Lamb" has wrath in Rev 6:16.  Law worketh wrath in Rom 4:15, and wrath is what to put away in Eph 4:31.
Lamb in Rev 13:11 has 2 horns, but speaks as a dragon. 
In Rev 12 a great red dragon has 7 heads, 10 horns, 7 crowns upon his heads. His tail casts stars of heaven to earth. 
In Rev 20:2 the dragon is that old serpent, which is the Devil, and Satan.
So let's not ass-u-me this Lamb [arnion] in Revelation is Christ. For Christ is Saviour of the world, by Christ being the end of the law. Thereby Christ is end of wrath, and thereby our peace.
What are your thoughts & insights about such Lamb vs Lamb?
The grace (only) of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

"Son of man" coming in the clouds?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
NO "Son of man" mentioned beyond Acts,
then 
ONLY "Son of God" is mentioned for 
the gospel of "Christ" through Jesus → Christ.
NO mention of Son of man in Romans → forward for Paul's gospel of "Christ" (is the end of the law for righteousness: Romans 10:4) SEEMS to Nullify about a dozen threat mentions in Matthew, Mark, Luke, of "Son of man" (not "Son of God") coming in the clouds of (law) heaven with:
- division (not unity): Mt 25:32.
- punishment (not mercy): Mt 25:46.
- causing mourn (not rejoice): Mt 24:30.
- flood & fire destruction (not salvation) as days of Noah & Lot:
"destroyed (not saved) them all": Luke 17:27,29.
In contrast, the voice of "Son of God" said only "live": John 5:25, and said I (JC = grace & truth) will not accuse you: John 5:45.
In contrast God our Saviour (not also destroyer) will have all men to be saved and to come unto the knowledge of the truth: 1Tim 2:4.
So, if preaching, let it be the gospel (good news) of "Christ" in the 14 epistles of Paul The Apostle, which is:
- peace with God through Jesus → Christ: Romans 5:1.
- eternal life gift of God through Jesus → Christ: Rom 6:23.
- God given victory through Jesus → Christ: 1Cor 15:57. 
The end to endure unto 
is not the coming of Son of man to divide, threat, condemn, punish,
is the pure grace salutation of Paul (not John) by his "own hand", as clarified in 2Thessalonians 3:17,18 about Revelation 22:21 KJV:
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

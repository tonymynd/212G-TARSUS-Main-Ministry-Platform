Why?

(Jn 8:9 = con-victed) & end (1Pet 3:21 = good conscience, no longer a con-vict), like love has both begin (Jn 13:23) & end (1Tim 1:5), so then 
the end of the new commandment to Love, in 1Timothy 1:5 KJV, is "charity" (more than "love", allegory like grace more than law);  
- out of a  pure heart (e-stablished with grace), and
- [of] a good con-science, and
- [of] faith unfeigned (no more hypocrisy leaven)

almost forgot the important word "Now" (contained in kNOW):

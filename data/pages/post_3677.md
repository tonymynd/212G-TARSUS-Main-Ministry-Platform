KNOWLEDGE (part2)
To All: Grace, Mercy and Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
“…and to virtue KNOWLEDGE; …” ~ 2Pe 1:5 AKJV
(attribution and spiritual map on the comments)
Quick summary of part1
- In part1 we learned that knowledge(gnosis) without “temperance” led to arrogance (puffing up) and division among them (of them/us), so gnosis needs to be put in check.
- There are two temperance(s) laid out: 1st Gal 5:22:23’s end; then 2nd 1Pe 1:6 perhaps second time where knowledge(gnosis) stays encapsulated:
 1st temperance << knowledge(gnosis) >> 2nd temperance 
Otherwise, ‘gnosis’ can become a stumbling block (snare) if it’s not properly tempered, then gradually and ultimately replaced by ‘epignosis’.
- We also observed how Apostle Paul masterfully uses a Positive-Evaluate-Positive (PEP) rhetorical approach, first affirming their strengths (1Co 1:5-7), then correcting their misuse of gnosis (1Co 8:1) and finally redirecting them toward edification through charity (1Co 13:2).
- Corithians tried to justify eating law (1Co 8:1, 10:19-22); things sacrificed to idols.  Nicolaitans did the same, who probably spawned from Nicolas, (a proselyte Act 6:5: twofold child Mat 23:15)
* Nicolaitans: Strong's meaning: destruction of people: a sect mentioned in Revelation 2:6,15 charged with holding the error of Balaam, casting a stumbling block before the church of God by upholding the liberty of eating things sacrificed to idols as well as committing fornication.
The accusation of lawlessness against those in grace is ironic—because law itself is what truly leads to Satan’s seat (Rev 2:13). Just as the Pharisees sat in Moses’ seat (Matt 23:2) and were called hypocrites, fools, blind guides, and vipers (Matt 23:13-33), John’s reference to Satan’s seat in Pergamos mirrors this same legalistic system.
Instead of walking in grace, the Corinthians, like the Nicolaitans, clung to gnosis, which deceived them into justification by law. This led them back into the snare of the devil (2 Tim 2:26), reinforcing division, judgment, guilt-tripping, and spiritual death.
With this, we conclude: puffeth up (‘physio’ in geek mode) is being proud or arrogant, So the Corinthians, just like the Nicolaitans, who ate idol food, FELL into the 'snare of the devil' (2 Tim 2:26) cause of death, division, arrogance, pride, judgment, guilt-tripping others, is a looong-suffering list. 
End of quick summary
Gnosis→Transformation→Epignosis
Knowledge(gnosis) puffs up, but charity edifies; This contrast of gnosis vs charity is as if contrasting knowledge(gnosis) vs knowledge(epignosis) which is the renewing of mind of “be ye transformed”, from a doubleminded “ye”  believe lies to a “you” know the truth John 8:32 a “Walk” then be guided into “all truth” Jhn 16:13 by Spirit of truth, then finally equipped to “So run” 1Co 9:24.
This transformation occurs when knowledge(epignosis) of our Lord Jesus Christ 2Pe 1:8 is completed with charity, the last and greatest to add in the list. So this transformation from gnosis to epignosis is through edification, not by being puffed or highminded, a trait found among things of the man 'anthropos' (the liar Rom 3:4) of last days and perilo-us times shall come 2Ti 3:1-7 but hopefully shall not come because God our saviour would have us all saved and come to the knowledge(epignosis) of the truth and guided “into all truth”, so:
“And be not conformed to this world: but be ye transformed by the renewing of y(OUR) mind, that ye may prove what is that good, and acceptable, and perfect, will of God.” ~ Rom 12:2 AKJV
Connect the dots
The mind has memory, which contains knowledge, which is the collective WORD formed of connected dots by innerstanding word is an “allegory”, which then gets processed and applied through wisdom. Yet, we ought to define which of the two words, which of the two knowledge and which of the two wisdoms:
Two Word of God
- Rhema (word of God): Ephesians 6:17 notes the "sword of the Spirit" is the "word" (rhema) of God (when still in fight mode, needs armour: Eph 6:11-17). This is the same "sword" [machaira] Jesus said he first brought instead of peace in Mt 10:34 (when first made under the law in Gal 4:4).
- Logos (word of God): Hebrews 4:12 notes the "word" (logos) of God is 7 things, 3rd of 7 being "sharper than any two-edged sword". So not the sharp twoedged sword coming out the mouth of the character in Revelation 1:16, or that such is not the word (logos) of God.
Two Knowledge
- Knowledge(gnosis): so, called science 1Ti 6:20, puff up, genders division, gets all HIGH by edibles (food offered to plural “idols” from tree of knowledge of good and evil).
- Knowledge(epignosis): HIGHER, “freely” given nutritious fruit Gen 2:16, where charity edifies, from the bottom (1st fruit aspect: love ‘agape’ Gal 5:22) to all the way up: “wherein dwelleth righteousness” and “grace reigns through righteousness” → the greatest of these is charity ‘agape’, 1Cor 13:13 (thinketh no evil, is not devilish1Co 13:5).
Two Wisdom
- Earthy Wisdom Jam 3:15;16 “is earthly, sensual, devilish. For where envying and strife is, there is confusion and every evil work.” (works of the flesh “under the law” Gal 5:19-21)
- “But the wisdom that is from above is first pure, then peaceable, gentle, and easy to be intreated, full of mercy and good fruits, without partiality, and without hypocrisy.” ~ James 3:17
Gnosis snare to destruction
Now, knowledge(gnosis) has mingled or “contrary” plural scriptures (which sets us for destruction (2Pe 3:16), but knowledge(epignosis) comes from singular "scripture" which is inspired by God, 2Ti 3:16.
The solution to contrary scriptures and obtain singular scripture, is to exercise senses to be able to avoid(to discern) contrary scriptures Heb 5:14
Eventually knowledge(gnosis) is replaced through charity:
“Charity never faileth: …whether [there be] knowledge(gnosis), it shall VANISH away.” ~ 1Co 13:8 AKJV
Gnosis is temporary, but charity is eternal—because knowledge without charity is useless (1Co 13:2), Gnosis is a tool, but charity is the above all; gnosis must vanish because it only serves the perfect charity is kind…is not puffed up.
A friend pointed out that if I had knowledge(gnosis) mingled in mind and was not building myself up to epignosis, then I would be “vanish away” ‘katargeo’—to render idle, unemployed, inactivate, inoperative—and I did on my journey through gnosis, I was paralyzed for a while, felt like everything was law around me, got depressed like little Timothy who needed to be “set at liberty” (apoluo)* by “know” the truth to be made “free” of believe any lie(law), and thereby  free of being any sort of servant(slave) to become a “a son; and if a son, then an heir of God through Christ.” Gal 4. 
* Two kinds of liberty(free): John 8:30→32’s believe→know TheTruth (not yet all truth) = only 1st kind of free(eleutheria) 
Epignosis: complete knowledge
There's both 'gnosis' knowledge of our Lord JC & 'epignosis' knowledge of our Lord JC. So, in 1Tim 2:1-4, after noting good and acceptable things, it says God our Saviour will have all men saved, AND come to the knowledge ('epignosis') of the truth (after saved). Then switcheroo:
But grow in grace, and [in] the knowledge(gnosis) of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.”
Peter switches back to gnosis, perhaps to convey that there is still a need to grow in grace. Once grown in grace, then we can assert we can fully innerstand epignosis knowledge.
Relying only on scientific knowledge (gnosis) is unaware that we even have a higher knowledge (epignosis) of the law, is the knowledge of sin Rom 3:20, and sin, when it is finished, bringeth forth death(Jas 1:15), OMG, we even have two epignosis, as if law trying to be higher than God. This is like a scientist working for a law-driven institution, creating weapons that ultimately serve sacrifice rather than life, as law offers sacrifice (Hebrews 10:8). 
But when Spirit led (Gal 5:18), Science aka 'gnosis' knowledge (as if still f-law knowledge), it shall vanish away (like the Dead Sea is vanishing). When it comes to grace, it is 'epignosis' knowledge, which God our Saviour will have all men also come unto, after all are saved (1 Timothy 2:4). For even CJ law will save people but still threaten destruction; but only the "grace & truth" of JC (not CJ) will both save and fully aware all.
Epignosis knowledge lead us to the will of God:  Will of God: "I will have mercy, and not sacrifice". The "not" part is: Not then, Not now, Not ever: Ps 40:6; Ps 51:16; Hos 6:6; Mt 9:13; Mt 12:7; Heb 10: Do the will of God PRECEDES "receive the promise".
This is the end of part2, time for part3 will come, meanwhile let's take a rest in:
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] is with you all. Amen

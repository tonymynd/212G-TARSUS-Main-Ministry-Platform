KNOWLEDGE (part3: END)
To All: Grace, Mercy and Peace, from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
“…and to virtue KNOWLEDGE; …” ~ 2Pe 1:5 AKJV
<quick summary of part2>
- knowledge(gnosis) transformation to knowledge(epignosis): it's allegory as if 2 kinds of "knowledge" of JC: - 1st we learn the lower 'gnosis' knowledge of our Lord JC, - then also learn the 'epignosis' knowledge of our Lord JC. Seems why 1Cor 13:8 KJV said when it comes to "charity* never faileth", then 'gnosis' knowledge** shall "vanish away "; And why, in 1Timothy 2:4, God our Saviour will have all men saved, AND come to the 'epignosis' knowledge of the truth. 
*KJV uses "love" & "charity" for 'agape', charity for never fail; To allegory say charity=grace agape is more than love=law agape. 
**gnosis "knowledge" is also rendered as "science" in English.
-Re “be ye transformed”: transformation is completed(perfected) in charity never faileth, the last and greatest to add in the list. So this transformation from gnosis to epignosis is through edification, not by being puffed or highminded
-We discussed briefly what knowledge is in general: Is contained in mind, which has memory and the collective word formed of connected dots by innerstanding then applied through wisdom. This requires identifying which of the two words, which of the two knowledge and which of the two wisdoms in part2.
- We observed how knowledge(gnosis) can become a snare due to its contrary nature "good and evil"
<end of quick summary>
Part 3: What else can we learn about knowledge?
Is interesting how in 1 Cor 13:9 and forward, there is a recognition of partial knowledge: We know(ginosko) in part, a we prophesy in part (still part-iality mode) 
THEN, 
1Cor 13:9;10; When that which was in part(divided, twined) due to "good and evil" knowledge(gnosis) shall be done away(vanish), 'poof' goes the dragon. Now we have fully matured knowledge(epignosis), this is why Paul said, “When I was a child” did childish things (laws). 
Interestingly, 1Co 13:21reveals that while being on the image side of the mirror: we see through a glass, darkly; but then translated, “from face to face” asif from law-face to grace-face: now I know(ginosko) in part; but then shall I know(epiginosko) even as also I am known(epiginosko) to fully realize “charity” the greatest 1 Co13:13.
Knowledge(epignosis): “if these things be in you, and abound”
No more ye making mistakes, no more a know-not’s (the know not what they do Luc 23:34). No more judge-mental child, two fold; no more “ye do err” stumbling into the snare of the devil: the lousy law tree of knowledge of good and evil: “thou shall surely die”, Gen 2:17; where subtil serpent at enmity with God, got completely and effectively abolished by Christ in Eph 2:15 via end of the law Rom 10:4:
“For if these things be in you, and abound, they make you that ye shall neither be barren nor unfruitful in the knowledge(epignosis) of our Lord Jesus Christ.” ~ 2Pe 1:8 AKJV, So, be ye therefore PERFECT (merciful Mat 5:48 → Luc 6:36). Not barren nor unfruitful by adding things to your faith(2Pe 1:5-11):
charity 'agape' (never fails, last to add)
↑ brotherly kindness 
↑ godliness
↑ temperance 
↑ patience
↑ knowledge 'gnosis'
↑ virtue ‘arete’
↑ add to your faith
↑ with all diligence
Also, “For these things be IN YOU” since Kingdom of God with you & in you Luke 17:21 AKJV. As the Spirit of Wisdom: “That the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give unto you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge (epignosis) of him.” ~ Ephesians 1:17
Such wisdom is obtainable by “ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him.” Jam 3:17,  
"ye" gets FILLED with the KNOWLEDGE 'epignosis' to have fullness of God.
“That ye might be filled with the knowledge (epignosis) of his will in all wisdom and spiritual understanding... unto all pleasing, being fruitful in every good work.” ~ Colossians 1:9-10
Exhortation is not to prohibit science(gnosis) knowledge, but to become aware how this gnosis science, although is good(and evil) has driven many to destruction, the evidence is irrefutable. We have uncountable instances of scientific institutions that have resulted in widespread deception, destruction and war between nations, not yet grace, mercy and peace from God our father and Jesus Christ our Lord.

The exhortation is to seek first the kingdom of God to alleviate the sub-conscious anger and urge for conquest, of raise and demise of earthy kingdoms with a huge death tolls and mass PTSD, like today, they said would be an exodus of Ukrainian soldiers into other countries with severe war traumas that would last for decades.

**Two Gains: Law Gain (Dung) -vs- Grace Gain (Profit Witha)  
**

  

Law Gain (worldly gain) is later on counted as Dung by Paul  

[Paul The Apostle](Paul.md) talks about two different sorts of "gain" he experienced: law gain and grace gain. Law: the first sort of gain to him later became counted as all loss, even as if "dung", in order to gain Christ: ([the end of the law](ShewBread9.md#Expired)), and be found in him. For in Christ there is no law, therefore no sin, and none of the first sort of worldly gain, especially  no righteousness of his own, no righteousness by law, no justification by law. For when Paul of Saul/Paul tried another law it made him feel wretched, because it hindered him from doing things he would and made him do things he would not, and then hounded him with guilt for either doing what he would not do or not doing what he would do. So all law is dung, and dung should flushed.  
  

Matthew 16:26 (Mark 8:36)  
For what shall it profit a man, if he shall gain the whole world, and lose his own soul?  

  
  

Grace Gain Paul expirienced is as Profit Withal  

It it became "evident" to Paul, from all the [evidence](CurrentEvents.md), "that no man is justified by the law in the sight of God": Galatians 3. So the second and better sort of grace gain Paul experinced he calls "profit withal", which is to die to the law and thereby become alive unto God (Grace). For if we do not die the first death to the law, we die the second death by the law, a deadly "sting" (1Cor 15:56), a dead end by law, from a bad case of grace + law = life + death = dead end.


Two Gardens: Eastward In Eden and Out of Eden

But he is ousted from the garden in Eden lest he also partake of the tree of life (grace) after eating the tree of death (law); For such grace vs law are contrary things that can't co-exist in peace, and God is [the author] of peace.

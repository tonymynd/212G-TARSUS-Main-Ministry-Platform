## Content

God said: Let us MAKE (asah) MAN (adam). 
His Christ (end of law) = head of every man;
But of every 'aner' man, not 'anthropos' man:
so of spiritual (Adam2), not natural (Adam1):
so of man up (grace), not man down (law). 
Romans 4:8 KJV
Blessed [is] the man (`aner`) 
to whom the Lord will not impute sin.
Romans 7:6 KJV
But now we are delivered from the law
Romans 10:4 KJV
Christ [is] the end of the law for righteousness 
1Corinthians 11:3 KJV
 the head of every man (`aner`) is Christ
1Corinthians 13:11 KJV
when I became a man (`aner`), 
I put away childish things (laws & prophets)
The GRACE of our Lord JC be with you all. Amen.

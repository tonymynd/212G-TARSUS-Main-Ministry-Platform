Paul's Epistle Format: PEP
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
Paul's epistles have a notable Epistle Format:
holy 
kiss Greet: GRACE to you & peace, from God our Father & JC.
Discussion: law vs grace allegory analysis & evaluation.
Salutation: The GRACE of our Lord Jesus Christ BE WITH YOU ALL. AMEN.
Such Epistle Format is also known as PEP talk:
Positive
Evaluate
Positive
note: in Paul's Epistle Format he allegory quarantines his own mention of law things between opening & closing GRACE.
The GRACE of our Lord Jesus Christ BE WITH YOU ALL. AMEN.

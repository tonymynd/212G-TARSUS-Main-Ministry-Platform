## Content

Romans 11:22? Seems Paul therein sarcastic allegorized "God" as if Law: has "goodness and severity" (not Grace & peace); Noting Law "goodness" is conditional ("if") "and" has "severity" after. So Rom 11:21 warned if still have Law as  God (Law being both good and evil), take heed Law as God will not spare you. So keep Rom 1:7 in mind when later read Rom 11:22. Rom 1:7 said "Grace (not Law) to you, and peace (not severity), from God 
our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ (grace & truth)". Interesting allegory stuff to notice.

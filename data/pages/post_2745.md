## Content

WAR: what is it good for? Nothing!
It's friend only to the under-taker.
Grace, Mercy, Peace from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord.
As 
the graphic image (from Ukraine) reveals: 
War is harmful, even deadly.
As the Bible reveals:
Law is the ministration of death, aka deadly.
God is not the author of confusion, but of peace.
Let's put away war by put away law, to have grace, mercy, peace.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

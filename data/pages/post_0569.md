## Content

1Cor 12:31→13:1-13 KJV*: "A MORE EXCELLENT WAY":
-than Love Thy Neighbor (is still law contained in flaw)
-than Love Your Enemies (still has jeopardy every hour)
-than Love One Another (is still one & another = duality)
-than NarrowWay of Broad/Narrow (only leadeth unto life) 
-than Forgiving One Another (still thinks evil to forgive)
-than Brotherly Kindness (still has some blindness)
*note: it's NOT "love" that never fails in KJV, but "charity".
"ALSO" LAY HOLD ON ETERNAL LIFE = a ↑higher↑ calling 
than either L/R sides of a PluralGod on high = high-minded:
"HIGHMINDED" is what we are NOT to be in the KJV:
-be not highminded: Romans 11:20 KJV
-mind not high things*: Romans 12:16 KJV
-spiritual wickedness occurs in high places: Eph 6:12
-be not highminded: 1Timothy 6:17
-highminded listed among unholy things: 2Timothy 3:1-5
*high things include: Majesty on high, old/new high priests

## Content

"God our Saviour" 
is "the only wise God", 
because...
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
God 
our Saviour does NOT both save and destroy after like OT LORD [Yehova} saved out of Egypt and afterward destroyed: 
Jude 1:5 KJV.
I will therefore put you in remembrance, though ye once knew this, how that the Lord (OT Yehova), having saved the people out of the land of Egypt, afterward destroyed them that believed not.
Rather
God our Saviour will have ALL men saved AND truthed: 
1Timothy 2:3,4 KJV. 
For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Saviour; 
Who will have 
all men to be saved, 
and 
to come unto the knowledge [epignosis] of the truth.
The term "God our Saviour" occurs 6x from 1Tim 1:1 to Jude 1:25.
Seems Jude contrasts 
OT LORD [Yehova] -vs- God our Saviour,
saves & destroys -vs- saves & truths.
So,
Jude 1:25 concludes "the only wise God" is "God our Saviour".
Jude 1:25
To the only wise God our Saviour be glory and majesty, dominion and power, both now and ever. Amen.
Then Revelation 22:21 KJV, 
as the saluation of Paul (not John) says:
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

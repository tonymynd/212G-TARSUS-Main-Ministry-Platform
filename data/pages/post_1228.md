## Content

Glory? For me, it's now "all grace" glory to God, and "no glory" to law nor to me. When I dared compare law glory to grace glory, as in 2Corinthians 3:7-11, i realized law's 2 ministrations: death & condemnation had to be "done away" x2, for grace's 2 ministratons: spirit & righteousness to apply x2. Thereby we end with God's righteousness, not our own. Grace glory so exceedingly "much more" excells, until law had "no glory". It's so we may have "all truth" by the God of "all grace".

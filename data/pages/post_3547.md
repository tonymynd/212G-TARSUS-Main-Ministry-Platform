Yet the word of reconciliation (aka grace) is given to us. 
For they without us can’t be made perfect. Yet we cannot force grace upon them, nor decide for them, but persuade them. To do such we have to be fully persuaded ourselves that TheEnd is 100% Positive.

For both of them1/them2 are still ‘anthropos’ man = liar.

to shew, by example of Saul→Paul’s change from Law→Grace, as if to shew them the same Grace, mercy, peace he obtained from God our Father and Jesus Christ our Lord is available to them all.

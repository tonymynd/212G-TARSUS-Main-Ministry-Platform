## Content

The only true God ~John 17:3? Does mention of "the only true God" perhaps allegory suggest there may be this/that (false/true)  God(s) portrayed in Bible we should Dare to Compare? If so, Which God(?) is being portrayed in 
Romans 11:22 KJV:
"Behold therefore the goodness and severity of God: on them which fell, severity; but toward thee, goodness, if thou continue in [his] goodness: otherwise thou also shalt be cut off".
Discussion:
- 
Seems 'this God' portrayed has "goodness AND severity" (not just goodness OR severity); And even goodness (chrestotes) aspect seems CONDITIONAL ("IF") on u be good, else severity=cut off.
- Seems, in Greek, there's 2 kinds of "goodness" to contrast:
-- goodness1 (chrestotes): seems both goodness and severity 
-- goodness2 (agathosune): 6th fruit of Spirit: is goodness only

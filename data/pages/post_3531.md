kNOW [ginosko] & KNOW [epiginosko]: 1Corinthians 13:12 ??
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
1Corinthians 13:12 KJV
For 
now we see through a glass, darkly; but then face to face: 
now I know[ginosko] in part; but 
then shall I know[epiginosko] even as also I am known[epiginosko].
Seems there are TWO Greek words for many English words.
Examples:
Converted = strepho (Mt 18:3) & epistrepho (Lk 22:32).
Knowledge = gnosis (1Cor 8:1; 1Cor 13:8) & epignosis (1Tim 2:4).
Know = ginosko & epiginosko (1Cor 13:12)
In Colossians 3 there is TWO kinds of above: ano & epi.
What are your insights about such ?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

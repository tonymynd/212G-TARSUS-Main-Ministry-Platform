## Content

Bible Mystery: Two of everything: high/higher calling(s).
Many are not even aware there's "also" a higher calling,
calling to leave the good fight & lay hold on eternal life;
for in "fight" mode there's neither peace nor eternal life.
Example: 1Timothy 6:12 KJV
"Fight the good fight of faith, 
lay hold on eternal life, 
whereunto thou art ALSO called"
Example: Hebrews 6:1 KJV
"Therefore LEAVING the principles 
of the doctrine of Christ, 
let us GO ON unto perfection"
Example: Hebrews 7:26 KJV
"For such an high priest became us, 
[who is] holy, harmless, undefiled, 
separate from sinners, and made 
HIGHER than the heavens" (on high)
The "higher" calling is not from on "high";
For such "eternal life" is not "life eternal",
and plural God on high isn't "God is one",
and two Sons Jesus isn't one "Son" Christ,
right of left/right isn't above of under/above.
So the Christ notably sitteth "above" (higher)
where Jesus sat "down" (right of L/R on high).
Which things are an allegory & mystery to solve.
Hence these phrases are said by Apostle Paul:
- "be not highminded": Romans 11:20
- "mind not high things": Romans 12:16
- "wickedness in high places": Ephesians 6:12
- "be not highminded": 1Timothy 6:17
- from such (highminded = unholy) turn away: 2Tim 3:1-5

 # Biblical CONTRASTS

**Grace, mercy, and peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.**

## Old Testament & New Testament
- **Law & prophets** vs **Grace & peace.**
- The law was given by Moses,  
  but grace and truth came by Jesus Christ.
- **OT LORD [Yehova] & NT God [Theos].**
  - (darkness, not light & light, not darkness.  
    Amos 5:18 KJV & 1John 1:5 KJV)  
    - allegory: law, not grace & grace, not law).

## Covenants
- **Old Covenant & New Covenant** (for Israel).
- **Law**: ministration of condemnation.  
  **Grace**: ministration of righteousness.  
- **Law**: ministration of death.  
  **Grace**: ministration of Spirit.  
- The letter killeth, but the Spirit giveth life: **2Cor 3**.

## Son of Man & Son of God
- as if **law threats** & **grace comfort.**
- note: No **Son of man** mentioned beyond Acts.

## Salvation
- The wages of sin is death,  
  but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus→Christ our Lord.

## Knowledge
- **Knowledge [gnosis] puffeth up & shall vanish away:** **1Cor 8:1, 13:8.**
- **Knowledge [epignosis] of the truth** is what God our Saviour will have all men come unto: **1Timothy 2:4.**

## Spirit and Power
- God has not given us the spirit of fear (hath torment),  
  but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind: **2Timothy 1:7.**
- God has not appointed us to (law worketh) wrath,  
  but to obtain salvation by our Lord Jesus Christ.

## Confusion vs Peace
- God is not the author of confusion,  
  but (the author) of peace: **1Cor 14:33.**

## Old Man vs New Man
- Put off the **OLD MAN** things.  
- Put on the **NEW MAN** things: **Colossians 3.**

## Word of God
- **Word [rhema] of God** & **Word [logos] of God.**  
  **Ephesians 6:17 & Hebrews 4:12.**

## Man Types
- **anthropos** man & **aner** man.  
  **Romans 3:4 & Romans 4:8.**

## Conversion
- **strepho** converted: **Matthew 18:3.**
- **epistrepho** converted: **Luke 22:32.**

## Heavens and Christ
- **heavens on high** & **higher than the heavens.**
- **Christ Jesus** & **Jesus Christ.**

**The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.**

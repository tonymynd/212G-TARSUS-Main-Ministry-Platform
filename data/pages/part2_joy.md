# 16th: JOY, part 2
Before the END, joy does not last.

In previous study we learned that joy from Gal 5:22;23 needs to mature by upgrading joy to "exceeding joy" by ENDuring through apocalyptic KillJoy stuff (like hearing Rumours of WWIII) unto TheEnd by having all men(not just some) to be saved, and to come unto the ‘epignosis’ kNOWledge: fully graced & truthed.

twain(duality) is not one(unity)
For the only true God sent Jesus Christ, Jhn 17:3 by whom came grace & truth Jhn 1:17, for salvation and awareness; Yet through Jesus→Christ, in that Christ, is the end of the law, where there is a knowledge upgrade from Jesus BetterLaw.

 Seems, while in PluralGod mode called “double minded”,
There may still be two: “twain”: of everything to sort out.
Eg: If still 2SonsJesus, then both threat/comfort perceived;
And if false/true Christs still arise, then both false/true Y2Ks.

So, to achieve "exceeding joy" we need to first upgrade from 'gnosis' knowledge to 'epignosis' knowledge, the reason by, is because 'gnosis' puffeth up, but "charity" edifies; this puffed up 'gnosis' knowledge is like the little leaven that leaveneth the whole lump 1Co 5:6; Gal 5:9. Also 1Cor 13:8 we learned ‘gnosis’ knowledge shall “vanish away”. 

So as we upgrade from love to charity, our knowledge should begin to upgrade from ‘gnosis’ to ‘epignosis’, in a similar manner ‘strepho’ (child) converted: Mt 18:3 upgrades to ‘epistrepho’ (man) converted: Lk 22:32;  And thereby become a man of God who puts away childish things, noted in 1Cor13:11.

In summary, to have a higher joy we need to integrate our knowledge upgrade:
- Outdated: 'gnosis' knowledge puffs up, get high-minded to look down on others, it gets vanished away in 1 Cor 13:8.
- Upgrade: 'epignosis' knowledge Ep4:13: fully graced & truthed Jn1:17

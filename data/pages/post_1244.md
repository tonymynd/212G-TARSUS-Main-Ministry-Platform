I like sharing, discussing, how when 3 biblical things noted, then it's allegory implied the 3rd of 3 is more than better of 2, like what's best of good, better, best = rest of the story. Eg: 
- in John 8:32, of 2 things: believe/"know the truth" is better, 
- yet in John 14:6, of 3 things, even "the truth" only 2/3 of 3:
I am = TheWay (1/3), theTruth (2/3), and TheLife (3/3). So,
John 16:13 mentions "all truth", as if more than "the 
truth".
The Spirit of truth will guide (follow me) you into "all truth".
Led of Spirit guides above lie/truth duality to NoLie=AllTruth.
It's like guided above law/grace duality to NoLaw=AllGrace.

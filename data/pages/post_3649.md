SCIENCE ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
In Bible "science" [gnosis] used in 1Timothy 6:20 seems as if a lower or lesser of two kinds of "knowledge" in Greek:
knowledge[epignosis] of God (as if grace knowledge: good only)
knowledge[gnosis] of God (as if law knowledge: good and evil)
1Timothy 6:20,21
O Timothy, keep that which is committed to thy trust, avoiding profane and vain babblings, and oppositions of science [gnosis] falsely so called:
Which some professing have erred concerning the faith.
In 1Corinthians 8:1 KJV it says knowledge[gnosis] puffeth up.
In 1Cor 13:8 KJV it says knowledge[gnosis] shall vanish away.
Grace be with thee. Amen.

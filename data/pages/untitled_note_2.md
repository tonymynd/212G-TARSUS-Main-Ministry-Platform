Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
THROUGH Jesus‚ÜíChrist seems to allegorize fwd through Jesus mode (Mt-Acts) to Christ mode (Rom‚Üífwd). Why?
Jesus was only Saviour of Israel. Christ is Saviour of the world.
Jesus mode was twain: old & new. Christ mode is one: all new.
Jesus was twain called: "Master, master". Christ is one: "Master".
Jesus was Son of man & Son of God. Christ is only Son of God.
So, for the gospel of "Christ" in Romans‚Üíforward there is NO more mention of Son of man, ONLY Son of God.

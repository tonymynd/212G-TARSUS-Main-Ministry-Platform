# Compromise? God & Son Do Not Compromise!

His Grace Does Not Compromise With Law.  
(Jesus Christ neither obeyed nor disobeyed law)  
[Contrary things](HGvsHS.md): [Grace vs Law](TwoGods.md): cannot coexist in peace.  
Grace and Law make an oxy-[MORON](DahQuiz.md) with a [BAD](Allegory3.md) ending.  
Rather when [Grace and Truth came](GodShew3.md), then Law and Lie got abolished,  
 put away, done away, taken away, even blotted out, to [establish](RevelatorySermons1.md) grace and truth.  
  

Looking at Law/Grace as Lie/Truth of a Before/After God Shew, we find [law had an expiry date](ShewBread9.md#Expired), and law was a schoolmaster to bring us to [Christ](RevelatorySermons17.md), of peace with God is "[through](through.md) Jesus>Christ", only in Christ. So reconciling the world to God is notably done "in Christ" (not in two Sons Jesus). For "Jesus" was made under the law and brought division (unrest), not peace (rest). But "Christ" is the end of the law, and thereby also the end of sacrifice (which God & Son will not have). Mosaic law was the sort of schoolmaster which failed every student. In Romans 9:31 we find all who diligently tried to attain righteousness of the law failed to attain it, because it's a mission impossible. For there's no righteousness(grace) in such centereousness(law) when comparing spiritual vs spiritual such as spirit of truth vs spirit of error, the latter state worse being antichrist (anti-the end of law).  
  
Mosaic law was the counter part in a two part shew about law vs grace, the imperfect part to be "done away" when [the perfect part is come](GodShew3.md#Second): 1Cor 13:10. Well, the perfect part is come long ago. Grace "came" by Jesus Christ: Jn 1:17, and Mosaic law is [allegorically](Allegory.md) compared to His grace therein as lie vs truth.  
  
Only when the "fulness of the time" (expiry date for law) came did God send His Son: Gal 4:4. God did "not" send His Son to condemn (law: 2Cor 3) the world, but rather that [through](through.md) him (Jesus=>Christ) the world might be saved ([from law](RevelatorySermons6.md)): Jn 3:17; Rom 7:6; etc. Neither did the Son of God accuse anyone: Jn 5:45, for as he noted [Mosaic](Allegory4.md) [law](Law.md) did this, being "[accuser of the brethren](http://pub30.bravenet.com/forum/2526810710/show/319317)". Such is the reason those under law at [Mt Sinai](MtSinai.md) "well said" accusation-all law should not be spoken to them again: Deut 5:25; Deut 18:16; Heb 12:19. Sadly, many Jews, Muslims, and Christians seem to [ignore](ShewBread7.md#Bliss) such truth, even today.  
  
The POINT made throughout Pauline epistles is that grace does not mix with nor compromise with law at all. They are contrary things which cannot coexist in peace. And since "he is our [peace](PeaceLinks.md)", who hath "abolished the law", it clearly denotes all law, everything written in stone and in ink (2Cor 3) is to be "done away" in order to have peace with God & Son. For peace with God is through [Jesus => Christ](GodShew5.md): "the end of the law". Through J=>C law got abolished.  
  
When the end of part one of the shew came, then God sent His Son with grace and truth, to put an end, once and for all, to such law and lie of Moses. And when His grace came, there was [no compromise whatsoever](ShewBread.md#contraryto) with law nor with those mixing law and grace (those who sat in [Moses' seat](Allegory4.md)). For such is an oxymoron with a bad ending, because if grace "is" and law "added", the only plausible mixture is grace(is) + law(added), which is also life + death, a dead end God will not have for himself or anyone else; not then, not now, not ever. As such, we find [what God will not have is sacrifice](ShewBread5.md#willx2), which is "of the law".  
  
When grace and truth came, then law got "abolished": Eph 2:15, to the POINT of "blotting out" all the ordinances against us: Col 2:14, to the POINT that every Mosaic law written in stone and in ink should be "done away": 2Cor 3, as the imperfect part is to be "done away" when the perfect part is come, just as childish things are to be "put away" whenever child=>man occurs: 1Cor 13.  
  
So there is no compromise whatsoever with law and lie when grace and truth come, truth out being on the rise lately, since it is now [the prophetic third day](ThirdDay.md). It is even to the POINT what is old, faulty, and "ready to vanish": Heb 8:13 "shall vanish": 1Cor 13:8. To the POINT there is [no law at all](GodShew2.md#Allness) mentioned at [the end](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) of the shew purposed to shew [the only Potentate](GodShew3.md#Only) is His Grace; nor any mention of law at all in the conclusions of any Pauline epistles, including Hebrews and Revelation; and the reason being so that none perish thereby.  
  
Perhaps the reason our latter state seems to be looking [worse](Allegory3.md) rather than [better](GodShew.md#GBB) is many, perhaps too many, have been compromising with law. Many have been saying [law<=law](TwainShew4.md#Laws) things like Peace & Safety (Grace & Law), thinking there is safety in [law](Law.md) ([worketh wrath](lawphoto.md)). [A big WAVE from LAW](http://pub30.bravenet.com/forum/2526810710/show/377051) worketh [wrath](RevelatorySermons18.md) revealed to the world there is no safety in such [merciless](HGvsHS.md) law. Perhaps imposing 'rule of law' by pre-emptive war produces more sorrows. For peace is not the result of war, but rather the absence of war.  
  
Perhaps Jewish brethren forgot those under law at Sinai well said the law should not be spoken to them again (Deut 5:25; Deut 18:16; Heb 12:19), and that is why(?) the gospel of Christ: "the end of the law" is "to the Jew first" (Acts  13:46; Rom 1:16); Especially since law was only given to the Jews, never to Gentiles (Rom 2:14; 9:30-32). However since Gentiles feared the law, it became global, and Gentiles proselytized by Jews also require deliverance.  
  
Perhaps Muslim and Islamic brethren (peace be upon them all) forgot casting out Agar and her son was an [allegory](Allegory.md) for casting out law (Agar = Sinai = law) and the result of the law: sin & death. Perhaps they also missed the biblical fact that [God never requested](ShewBread8.md#Noah) and was never pleased with sacrifice (Ps 40:7; Hos 6:6) which is "of the law" ([Heb 10](Hebrews11.md)). Hence their devotion to sacrifice is as [marytr-dumb](TwainShew6.md#Sacrifices) as any, all such sacrificial folk being [ignorant](ShewBread7.md#Bliss) of the biblical facts.  
  
Perhaps Christian brethren, both Roman Catholic and Protestant flavors of such [impure religion](RevelatorySermons7.md), forgot even the better sort of two laws in Rom 8:2 is notably in ["Christ Jesus", the reverse of "Jesus Christ"](GodShew5.md#Peace) (the only foundation laid we should build anything on). And that a second beast gives place to a first beast in Revelation 13, which is as law<=law as Israel<=America is today, and it only makes the latter state "worse" (Mt 12:45; Mt 23:15; 2Pet 2:20-22): "[more the child of hell](Allegory3.md)" instead of what's exhorted: "[be no more children](GodShew.md#nomorechildren)". Same concept applies to [servants](TwainShew6.md#Servants); to whom the exhortation is "be no servant, but a son" (Gal 4:7); For children and servants both require being told what to Do-teronomy & Don't-eronomy till they stop being children and servants.  
  
Perhaps "we all are one" have all compromised with law in some fashion. Can it truly be said doctors and so many others working in the field of illness (biggest business on planet) really want wellness to prevail? Or can it be truly said undertakers deriving their livelihood from death really want life to prevail? What of those who make body bags; do they not rejoice at the coming of catastrophies and wars? What of the media who report on such; do they not rejoice at having a horror story? Can it be truly said millions of politicians, judges, prosecutors, lawyers, police, court workers, etc, all deriving their livelihood from law making and law enforcement, really want grace and truth to prevail? Or can it be truly said millions of ministers collecting a [troll toll](RevelatorySermons4.md) from flocks of unaware groupies really want awareness to prevail?  
  
Nobody is excluded from "we all are one", for "there is no respect of persons with God". If any do it, add law to "grace is sufficient", it's as if all did it, and [until we all get it: understanding, it seems we're all grounded](GodShew4.md), many groundead.  
  
Indeed [we all are one, brethren all](Hebrews3.md); Yet the question remains which one of two alls: "them all" or "us all", or two one man scenarios in Romans 5, or two Adams? For [two alls](TwainShew.md#Alls) is one too many alls, twofold rather than onefold, simply a sides war and a [blame game](RevelatorySermons15.md) rather than unity of the Spirit as one God via the awareness game. If the Lord is longsuffering to "us-ward" so that none perish, it reasons them-ward is the reverse: all perish. For as it says, if one died for all, then were all dead; which is extinction, not salvation. So the quest-i-on is as [Shakespeare said](OneLiners.md#Shakespeare): to be "or" not to be, and as [Ghandi said](OneLiners.md#Ghandi): the choice is non violence "or" non existence. Such speaks of grace "or" law since JC said God said: either make the tree good "or" evil (not both); For both is an oxymoronic paradox: grace + law = life + death = a dead end. Not to mention the end of OT law is [fear (hath torment)](ShewBread4.md#freedom) of being 'smitten' by such a "curse".  
  

The Bible has no such dead end, because the end has no mention of law at all.

3 Saviours: Jesus → Christ → God our Saviour

God our Saviour? Allegorical 3rd of 3 biblical Saviours:
1) Jesus: Saviour of Israel...........Good=1/3 of 3
2) Christ: Saviour of the world.....Better=2/3 of 3
3) God our Saviour: awareness...Best=3/3=Rest of story.
2Cor 5:20 KJV: Be ye reconciled to God (thru J→C→G).
FYI:
1Timothy (epistle from Laodicea Colossians told also read) begins 6 mentions of "God our Saviour"... 6th* being Jude's conclusion: the "only wise God" = "God our Saviour; After noting look for the "mercy" of our Lord JC unto "eternal life",  prior to enter Revelation's apocalypse has no mention of mercy. 
*interesting, as 6th fruit of Spirit is different goodness. So...
Let's ALSO ask God our Saviour for His pure wisdom, as if   allegory ASKing for what's Best of Good, Better, Best, as if ask for the AllTruth (no lies) of the God of AllGrace (no laws)...
1Timothy 2:4 (after good and acceptable things, are only 2/3 of 3 in Rom 12:2b = good, and acceptable, and perfect), notes God our Saviour will have MuchMore than Jesus (Israel saved), even More than Christ (world saved). For God our Saviour will have all men('anthropos', includes Son of man='anthropos' & CJ: mediator man='anthropos) saved (by grace), AND come to the knowledge('epignosis') of the trUth; Which is more than 'gnosis' knowledge of JC that u, of u/U, firstly know; Which in 1Cor 13:8 KJV shall "vanish away" when it comes to what never faileth: "charity" (not love), which is 3rd of these THREE: faith, hope, charity. So...
We need to be greatest of great, greater, greatest; Because even greater love of many shall wax cOLD: Mt 24:12, by being on cOLD side of Cold -LukeWarm- Hot in the learning game of seek & find... find grace, in time, to help u become U, as if  higher than the heavens on high, undefiled and harmless: not only to the point of do no harm, but ALSO receive no harm.
For if we only have authored & finished faith in J mode, it's only 1/3 of 3; And if we only have C better of  thru J→C as our hope of eternal life, then it's still only 2/3 of 3, which in Revelation = 0.666. 
So via God our Saviour we progress in our learning journey, firstly thru J→C to C better than when in J mode, then ALSO thru C→God-ward to be fully reconciled, and then, when it's 3rd of 3, it's reconciled to "that God" who can't lie nor die.

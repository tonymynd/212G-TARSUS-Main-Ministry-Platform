## Content

All scriptures are allegorical from Genesis 1:1 to Revelation 22:21
With all thy getting, get it: understanding = grace glory (Proverbs 4 allegory). Understand that "my grace is sufficient" meaneth no law is required; Understand "I will have mercy, and not sacrifice" means I will have grace, and not law. And understand "immutable" means unchangeable by any means. No amount of lying, crying, or dying will ever change the immutable will 
of God. For God(Grace) is not an unjust judge who can be hounded to change his judgment, from righteous judgment (grace) to vengeance (law). God(Grace) neither tempts (laws) nor can be tempted (lawed). God(Grace) neither makes sides nor takes a side to have war. The first of seven aspects of pure wisdom from above (Grace unto you from God) in James 3:17 is peace-able.
John 8:23 Beneath/Above Allegory
I am (grace) from above: not of this world
Ye (do err) are from beneath: of this world (law)

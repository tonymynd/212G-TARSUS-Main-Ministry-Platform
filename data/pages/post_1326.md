## Content

Be perfect: Mt 5:48 ... Let us go on unto perfection, by leaving* six things: Heb 6:1-3 ... God of all grace will make u higher perfect, after suffer awhile in Christ Jesus mode: 1Peter 5:10
*leaving (six elementary) principles of the doctrine of Christ: 
- repentance from dead works, 
- faith toward God, 
- 
doctrine of baptisms, 
- laying on of hands, 
- resurrection of the dead, 
- eternal judgment
For 
in higher perfection (pure grace) mode, these no longer needed; As if no dead to resurrect, nor dead works to repent of, in the God of the living (wherein none die, because none lie, aka none law flaw).

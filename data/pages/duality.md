**Interesting Law & Grace Differences of same Bible words:**

**(hence the need for a Strong’s Concordance as Bible tool)**

**Thank God for computers, KJV search, online Bible Tools;**

**So we can seek and find some of these subtle differences.**

- \`\`_risen_\`\`: \`synegeiro\` (not a grave subject) ... grace\*

- \`\`_risen_\`\`: \`egeiro\` (a \`\`grave\`\` subject)........... law

\*eg: If risen with Christ, focus above: Colossians 3:1

- \`\`_above_\`\`: \`epi\` (higher)................................. grace\*

- \`\`_above_\`\`: \`ano\` (high).................................... law

\*eg: put on charity above forgiving: Colossians 3:14

- \`\`_converted_\`\`: \`epistrepho\` (man of child/man).... grace\*

- \`\`_converted_\`\`: \`strepho\` (childish of child/man).... law

\*eg: when converted, strengthen brethren: Lk 22:32

- \`\`_Lamb_\`\`: \`amnos\` (takes away sin)............... grace\*

- \`\`_Lamb_\`\`: \`arnion\` (covers + uncovers sin)..... law

\*eg: Behold the Lamb \`\`of God\`\`: John 1:29

- \`\`_seed_\`\`: \`sperma\` (incorruptible)................... grace\*

- \`\`_seed_\`\`: \`spora\`(corruptible).......................... law

\*eg: to thy seed, which is Christ: Galatians 3:16

- \`\`_feed_\`\`: \`bosko\` (nourish lambs & sheep)...... grace\*

- \`\`_feed_\`\`: \`poimaino\` (govern sheeple)............. law

\*eg: when Peter is epistrepho, he says \`\`grow in grace\`\`

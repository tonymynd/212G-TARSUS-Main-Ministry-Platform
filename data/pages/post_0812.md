## Content

Bible stuff: Interesting Hebrews 13:9 KJV says it is a good thing the heart be established "with grace", not with meats which have not profit them occupied therein; So not just "under grace" instead of "under the law" like in Romans 6:14, but "with grace", as if then above both such twain of "not under the law, but under grace".
Hebrews 13:9 KJV: (WITH GRACE)
Be not carried about with divers and strange doctrines. For [it is] a good 
thing that the heart be established with grace; not with meats, which have not profited them that have been occupied therein.
Romans 6:14 KJV: (UNDER GRACE)
For sin shall not have dominion over you: for ye are not under the law, but under grace.

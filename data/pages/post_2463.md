REVELATION: THE OPEN BOOK FINAL EXAM OF THE BIBLE
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Revelation, 66th book of Holy Bible, 27th book of New Testament, is canonized last to be as if the Open Book Final Exam of the Bible. As such it takes info from previous books to pass the exam.
For 
example in Hebrews 4:12 it notes the word [logo] of God is sharper than any twoedged sword. Then in Revelation 1:16 it notes a sharp twoedged sword coming out the mouth of qa priestly character.
The end to endure unto is the salutation of Paul (not John):
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

This week I asked God to hear Positive things, as TV news was grieving my spirit to watch suffering portrayed. God's Grace is so Amazing. Already I've had 3 emails from people I've been out of touch with, all Positive, sharing how the Spirit had quickened such people. The latest was from a friend in India who'd grown unto Pure Grace, Pure Mercy, Pure Peace, of God's Pure Wisdom from above. It uplifted me to hear such, as if hearing 
only Positives from the God of all grace.

## Content

Bible Mystery: comparing amnos & arnion Lamb(s),
and clarifying only one of two Lamb(s) is "of God".
John 1:29 = amnos Lamb "of God"
Revelation: all = arnion Lamb (not of God)
Beware the peace offered you by two Sons Jesus,
five times in "Verily, verily" John's gospel account,
since in Revelation 6 he also removes the peace.
In compare-i-son the peace of one Son Christ,
in Ephesians 2:15 doesn't get removed after;
For 'amnos' Lamb "of God" isn't 'arnion' Lamb.
Amnos Lamb taketh away the sins of the world;
But amnos Lamb both covers and uncover sins,
to bite & smite afterward, after peace removed.
Point: two Sons Jesus is not (yet) one Son Christ.
God did not send Sons. God only sent one "Son":
John 3:17 ... Galatians 4:4 ... Hebrews 1:2; And
God sent not his Son to condemn, but to save,
and save is through him... through his name...
through Jesus Christ, which ends with Christ,
who is our peace, author of eternal salvation.

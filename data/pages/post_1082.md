## Content

God forbid???????????? Does Grace forbid, or is it Law which forbids? Is it every tree, freely, or not every tree nor freely?
- Seems Apostle Paul asks questions, and still answers with phrase "God forbid" about a dozen times, which phrase notably ends in Galatians 6, after a big Gal 5 Walk in the Spirit, through 9 fruit of the Spirit, beginning (not ending) with Love; 
- Perhaps allegory as if a Walk from under the law to under grace 
seems beyond such midst of 9, where it's then "goodness" having no severity of Law attached, like temperance having "no law". 
Eg: Gal 5:22,23
5:22 But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness (MIDST of 9), goodness, faith, meekness, temperance: against such there is no law...
6:18 Brethren, the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with your spirit. Amen. <[to [the] galatians written from rome.]>

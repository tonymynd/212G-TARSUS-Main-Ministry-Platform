## Content

Driven/Led?? Seems we're still hearing of some preachers (like Rick Warren) & some churches still calling themselves 'purpose DRIVEN'. Seems as if such are not [yet] LED of the Spirit beyond DRIVEN mode. Seems if we contrast these two things in OT & NT, we find this allegory difference:
- "walk before me" = DRIVEN by the law = "schoolmaster" 
- "follow me" = LED by the Spirit of grace & truth = "guide"
It gets interesting to notice this 
in Matthew 21:9 KJV: when 2SonsJesus, riding on 2animals, made a Palm Sunday entrance to Jerusalem, Jerusalem, he had 2multitudes:
- them that went before = driven (aka "walk before me")
- them that followed after = led (aka "follow me")
Seems both of 2multitudes cried same thing: "Hosanna...", 
But later one such multitude said "Crucify him, crucify him".
Seems schoolmaster law can only bring ppl "UNTO  Christ" (Galatians 3:24), but canNOT also take ppl INTO Christ; For "in Christ" of "through our Lord Jesus Christ", it's then "Christ is the end of the law"; wherein we're LED of the Spirit of truth into all truth, no longer driven by the law...
Because law canNOT make any perfect: Heb 10:1. Seems why apostle Paul told bewitched churches of Galatia, in Gal 5: if LED of the Spirit (of grace and truth), then it's as if LED
from under the law....to "not under the law" = under grace.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

## Content

ASK to RECEIVE. It's interesting how "the God of all grace",  who "upbrideth not", is so gracious He will not force Himself nor His wisdom on anyone; But patiently waits to be asked...
Then, when asked, that God liberally showers upon us His pure wisdom (aka pure grace) from above, as revelations, one by one, along with study hnts to confirm it's from Him, also giving time to digest each one.

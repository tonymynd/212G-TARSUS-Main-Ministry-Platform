Biblical TIME ALLEGORY?
1 day as 1000 years & 1000 years as 1 day
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Psalm 90:4 KJV
For a thousand years in thy sight are but as yesterday when it is past, and as a watch in the night.
2Peter 3:8 KJV
But, beloved, be not ignorant of this one thing, that one day is with the Lord as a thousand years, and a thousand years as one day.
Based on these verses some perceive seven days in Genesis 1 & 2 could (time allegory) be as if seven thousand years: 
four thousand years of Old Testament ← BC, 
two thousand years of AD → New Testament unto Y2Ks, 
then a thousand years called the third day.
Questions:
Could this time allegory aspect apply to these verses?
Hosea 6:2 KJV
After two days (2000 years) will he revive us: 
in the third day (1000 years) he will raise us up, 
and we shall live in his sight.
Revelation 20:7 KJV
And when the thousand years (1 day) are expired, Satan shall be loosed out of his prison,
What are your thoughts about the biblical time allegory?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

LOVE? I learned that it takes more than "love" to never fail.
Sure God is love('agape'), but KJV has two kinds of 'agape':
- Love('agape'): love of many shall wax cOLD: Mt 24:12 KJV.
- Charity('agape') thinks no evil = never fail: 1Cor 13:5-8 KJV.
Seems KJV uses both love & charity to contrast Law & Grace.
Charity ('agape'): thinketh no evil:
neither to punish ←nor→ forgive.  ... allegorically Grace
Love ('agape'): still thinketh to evil
either to punish ←or→ to forgive. ... allegorically Law
Biblical charity doesn't see one & another = duality;
So charity never fails is put on ↑above↑ these: 
Love one another ←&→ Forgive one another.

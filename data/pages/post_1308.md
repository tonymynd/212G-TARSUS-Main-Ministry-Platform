## Content

Let God be true, but every man('anthropos') a liar: Romans 3:4a KJV. Seems Allegory aspect is: Let Grace be true, but every legalistic flaw-ing man still be a liar; As if to say only Grace is our real Truth, but Law-ing is still as if Lying... 
So Rom 3:4b goes on to explain why let God (Grace) be true: so that you may be justified (by Grace) and may overcome when you are flaw judged, both by yourself and by others.
Eg: in Romans 2:1 
Paul noted an 'anthropos' man, who still law judges others, condemns (flaws) himself in the process, therefore is just as "inexcusable" as those he law judged. Ouch.
Seeing this, along with revelations from the Spirit of truth, helped me upgrade from an 'anthropos' kind of man to an 'aner' kind of man, who puts away childish things (laws).
I now wish Grace, mercy, and peace to all, flaw to none.

MERCY: How important is mercy? Perhaps Very Important.
It's interesting to note these NT things about "mercy":
- Only when there's finally mention of "grace" in Luke (none previous in Matthew  nor in Mark), then Jesus said, in Luke 6:36, your Father is "also merciful", so be merciful.
- 
Paul changed his holy kiss greeting to include "mercy" in 1Timothy 1:2 KJV: Grace, mercy, and peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ. (seems then peace is more permanent when 3rd of 3: grace, mercy, peace)
- No mention of grace, nor of mercy, nor of peace in 1John; But mention of Grace, mercy, and peace in 2John 1:3 
- No mention of mercy at all in Revelation.
- Last mention of "mercy" is in Jude 1:21 says: Keep yourselves in the love of God, looking for the "mercy" of our Lord Jesus Christ unto eternal life.

## Content

As AD 2013 becomes AD 2014 remember this:
AD→only goes one way: forward to upward;
1stly forward "through Jesus→Christ", 
as if delivered from law to "grow in grace",
then upward "through Christ→God-ward", 
as 
if going on from high things to "higher".
Yet forward mode is a fast forward race pace of grace. When 
it's law vs grace, race pace of grace "much more" abounding:
Eg: Romans 5:20,21 KJV (race pace of grace is "much more")
"Moreover the law entered, that the offence might abound. 
But where sin abounded, GRACE DID MUCH MORE ABOUND: 
That as sin hath reigned unto death, even so might grace reign 
through righteousness unto eternal life by Jesus Christ our Lord".
So then, grace is so exceedingly much more abounding than law,
when it comes to the end, there's not even any mention of f-law.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

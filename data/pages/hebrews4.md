Theme of Hebrews Chapter 3: Take Heed.  
The deceitfulness of sin is sourced in the deceitfulness of law;  
So take heed, brethren, lest there be in any of you an evil (law) heart.  
Wherefore (the reason or purpose) Christ Jesus is the Apostolic High Priest.  
That generation: they do alway err (law): is the reason they shall not enter my rest.  
There's no rest (peace) in [law (worketh wrath)](lawphoto.md) to be had, so take heed not to go there.  

  
As the first verse tends to set the chapter theme, Hebrews (holy brethren, partakers of the heavenly calling) are asked to "consider" (perceive, remark, observe, understand) [Christ Jesus](ChristJesus.md) (the reverse of Jesus Christ) as an Apostolic High Priest, the [Mosaic](Allegory4.md) house of law, and what(?) the Holy [Ghost](ShewBread3.md#unpardonablesin) saith about such.  
  
Such profession, of [law](Law.md), involved hardened hearts by the [deceitfulness of sin](http://pub30.bravenet.com/forum/2526810710/show/344590) (not of God: [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/08-2-Corinthians/2-Corinthians-Chapter-05.md#^2Co5-19|2Co 5:19]]), temptation (not of God: [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/20-James/James-Chapter-01.md#^Ja1-13|Jam 1:13]]), works (plural: grace + law; not of God: [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/04-John/John-Chapter-17.md#^Jn17-4|Joh 17:4]]), a generation (40 yrs) of grief (not of God: [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/01-Matthew/Matthew-Chapter-23.md#^Mt23-4|Mat 23:4]]), err (not of God: [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/01-Matthew/Matthew-Chapter-05.md#^Mt5-48|Mat 5:48]]), [ways](TwainShew7.md#Ways) (not of God: [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/04-John/John-Chapter-14.md#^Jn14-6|Joh 14:6]]), and wrath ([not of God](RevelatorySermons18.md): [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/03-Luke/Luke-Chapter-06.md#^Lk6-36|Luk 6:36]]); And to the point of not enter for all due to some did provoke. In short, what we're considering is the provocation, of a law God, unto [law worketh wrath](lawphoto.md) (again not of God: [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/13-1-Thessalonians/1-Thessalonians-Chapter-05.md#^1Th5-9|1Th 5:9]])."Wherefore" x 3: 'oyen' & 'dio' & 'oyen' in the greek, is followed by "take heed". But "give (more earnest) heed" was exhorted for "escape" in [Heb 2](Hebrews3.md), escaping the destruction of law worketh wrath in Heb 3, and upon all if only some did provoke it. And perhaps such "give" and "take" in Hebrews is as [tricky](Allegory.md) to navigate as Revelation, both written by [Paul The Apostle](Paul.md): who is notably not only a revelator of "mystery", but also a "faithful steward of the mysteries of God" in such things are an [allegory](Allegory.md).

Wherefore (#1 of 3 wherefores in Hebrews 3)... Wherefore occurs 80 times from Matthew 6 to Hebrews 3. Wherefore holy brethren ... Paul acknowledges all as "brethren", even the bewitched churches of Galatia; but now refers to Hebrews as "holy brethren", perhaps satirically noting they are law(holy) brethren. Yet what remains to be seen is such law brethren makes divisional sides, [two holies](TwainShew3.md#Holys), plural [heavens](RevelatorySermons21.md), [Gods](TwainShew3.md#Gods). Hebrews 3 is about looking at their side, law, considering the deceitfulness of law, the hardheartedness of law, and the dead end of sin consciousness via law: the source of sin and the strength of sin's death sting; And about realizing wrath is not of God, not the "living" God, rather of a [dead God](ShewBread9.md#Expired) of such [dead/living Gods](ShewBread6.md#Gods), false God of [false/true Gods](TwainShew6.md#Spirits). When considering "Wherefore", we should also look into "Wherefore thou art no more a servant, but a son": [[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/09-Galatians/Galatians-Chapter-04.md#^Ga4-7|Gal 4:7]].  

  

Partakers of the heavenly calling ... Both light and darkness are called, but light is called day, darkness is called night, and each have a different ruler. However the heavenly calling (invitation) to embrace salvation of by grace alone is not both grace + law nor both come + depart for both right + center as in Matthew 25. The heavenly calling is [y'all come](Partiality.md), come now let us reason together, come ye blessed. And such blessed isn't the first of twain blesseds in Romans 4:7,8 ([[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/OT/19-Psalms/Psalms-Chapter-32.md#^Ps32-1|Psa 32:1]],2); but the better "[man](TwainShew.md#Blesseds)" kind of blessed: [no sin imputed](Reconciliation.md). The heavenly calling is also a calling (invitation) by the ["heavenly" name](ShewBread6.md#Hallowed): grace: the "name of" our Father, and the "name of" our Lord Jesus Christ, which is also "the name by which ye are called" to come to repentance (to change, from faith to faith and from glory to glory): by His "[grace](GraceUntoYou.md)".  

  

Consider the Apostle and High Priest of our profession, Christ Jesus ... Christ Jesus, as Apostolic High Priest? It's tricky stuff, for victory is not through Christ Jesus; But rather "God hath given us the victory [through](through.md) [Jesus Christ](ShewBread.md#contraryto)". Christ Jesus is mirrorly the reverse of Jesus Christ. Grace and truth came by "Jesus Christ" ([[3-RESOURCES(Pantry)/kjv-bible-blb/NT/04-John/John-Chapter-01.md#^Jn1-17|Joh 1:17]]). Eternal salvation is notably [through](through.md) Jesus > Christ; Only in Christ thereof. Peace with God is also through "Jesus > Christ". The reverse, [Christ Jesus](GodShew5.md), would be war with God, a merciless law God. Song: [I fought the law and the law won](http://pub30.bravenet.com/forum/2526810710/show/344493), should ring a bell. Selah. So we should all consider the Apostle and High Priest of our profession; Consider what we profess to be ours if our profession is law, which imputes sin to all by the curse of the law, which imputes a death sting to all if any go there, to law imputed sin. For if our profession is law, then what we profess to be ours is sin and death.

## Content

"For God sent not his Son into the world to condemn the world; 
but that the world through him might be saved" ~John 3:17 KJV.
"through him" is then further clarified as
"through his name" is further clarified as
"through Jesus Christ" is even further clarified as
"through our Lord Jesus Christ" = peace & victory. 
THROUGH Jesus→Christ leads us unto "Christ" mode;
As if Spirit led unto the peace that cannot be removed,
is not just right of left/right, but above of under/above.
For being right of left/right can be as law2 of law1/law2,
neither of such Mt 22:36-40's law1/law2 being yet grace;
And even if right of laws/grace it's still division, not peace.
So then rightly divided truth should be above under/above.
For then it's no longer them/them, nor them/us, but just us;
As if the higher us of "us-ward" the Lord is longsuffering unto,
as if the "harmless" us who neither do harm nor receive harm,
like the "fulness of Christ" which is graceful, merciful, peaceful.

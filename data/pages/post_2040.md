allegoric bible mystery compare-i-Son: Son of man vs Son of God.
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.

of biblical details to investigate. Here's a few things to notice:
Sometimes Jesus both"answered and said", also"turned", as if from Son of man to Son of God, like in Matthew 16:16-23 KJV Jesus 1st called Peter "blessed", then "turned" to call Peter "Satan". 
In Matthew 25:31-46 KJV OT Son of man threatens to come and divide sheep/goats to right/left (reverse of left/right), then send goats to everlasting punishment, but offers sheep "life eternal" (reverse of "eternal life"). 
In Mark 16:4-16 risen Jesus of "Nazareth" still both upbraided & damned; As why Nazareth also not mentioned in Rom→fwd.
In Luke 8:24 KJV disciples of Jesus called him "Master, master", allegory as if two many (Big, little) masters. Matthew 23:10 clarified "one" is your (Big) "Master": "Christ".
In Luke 17:22-30 it notes if a day of Son of man revealed it would be as days of Noah & Lot wherein it was "destroyed them all".
In John 3:13-17 KJV Son of man calls himself "serpent", then reverses NT→"eternal life" bwd to OT←"everlasting life".
In John 5:25-29 Jesus revealed that when still both 2Sons: Son of God & Son of man, that he had two voices. Son of God voice says only "live" as if only grace; But Son of man voice says "judgment also", as if law also; So 2 resurrections: of life & of damnation.
So,

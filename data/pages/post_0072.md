## Content

Bible Mystery: Diaper blessed isn't the blessedness of the man.
Romans 4:7,8 seems to contrast these two kinds of "blessed",
As if a difference between Law "Blessed" and Grace "Blessed";
Law Blessed being a cover up that also gets uncovered later,
as noted in places like Matthew 10:26; Luke 8:17; Lk 12:2.
Romans 4:7,8 KJV (from Psalms 32:1,2):
- Blessed [are] they whose iniquities are forgiven, 
and whose sins are covered. 
- Blessed [is] the man to whom the Lord will not impute sin.
Luke 12:1,2 KJV (also noted in Mt 10:26 and Lk 8:17)
Beware ye of the leaven of the Pharisees, which is hypocrisy.
For there is nothing covered, that shall not be revealed; 
and hid, that shall not be known. 
So then, cover up blessed (law blessed, diaper blessed)
eventually also gets uncovered to reveal their hypocrisy,
and when uncovered it reveals the pew isn't taken away.
Modern Day Example: The Roman Catholic Church
is now getting many of their cover ups uncovered.

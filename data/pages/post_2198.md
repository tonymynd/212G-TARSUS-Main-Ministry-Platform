## Content

ROMANS 8:11 has 2 mentions of Jesus & Christ raised from dead
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ. 
NOTICE Romans 8:11 has 2 mentions of "raised from the dead": for Jesus & for Christ, but 2nd shall ALSO quicken your mortal bodies:
Romans 8:11 KJV
But if the Spirit of him that raised up Jesus from the dead dwell in you, he that raised up Christ from the dead shall also quicken your mortal bodies by his Spirit that dwelleth in you.
The grace of our Lord Jesus→Christ be with you all. Amen.

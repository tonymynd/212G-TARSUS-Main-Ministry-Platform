## Content

State of Union seems to be confusion, not peace; Oft a state of emergency. Perhaps the reality yet to be learned is grace and law, contrary things, can't co-exist in peace, and don't mix except to make an oxy-moron. So it boils down to one of twain. If it be 'rule of law', instead of grace, mercy, peace, expect more "law worketh wrath".

## Content

fatherly Paul to sonly Timothy: there's even a difference in who the "Grace, mercy, and peace" is from. Eg:
- Grace, mercy, and peace, from God "our" Father, and "our" Lord "Jesus Christ": 1Tim 1:2 KJV (as if higher Gmp in JC), 
- Grace, mercy, and peace, from God "the" Father, and "our" Lord "Christ Jesus": 2Tim 1:2 KJV (as if lower Gmp in CJ) 
Why? Because... 
in 
2Tim 3:12 all who will live godly in "Christ Jesus" shall "suffer" persecution... until [higher] "God of all grace" (as if  Maker of Creator/Maker, will "make" you perfect (more than created), "after" ye "suffered" a while (in CJ mode): 1Pet 5:10. So after 1Pet 5:14, no more mention of CJ, then only of JC.
Paul knew this, for even as Paul of Saul/Paul, he suffered persecution & affliction while still in "Christ Jesus" mode: Acts 19:4... until changed to "Jesus Christ" mode: Acts 28:31. 
So 
in Romans-forward, Paul always clarifies he's now in "Jesus Christ" mode, and always turns "Christ Jesus" around to "Jesus Christ" in all epistles, includiing Hebrews. 
So...
Peter notes, in 2Pet 3:15-18, he'd read all Paul's epistles, which speak of these things, and lastly tells brethren "grow in grace" and in the knowedge of our Lord and Saviour "Jesus Christ". To him be glory, both now and forever. Amen.

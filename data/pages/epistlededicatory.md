# THE

HOLY BIBLE  
CONTAINING THE

**TO THE MOST HIGH AND MIGHTY PRINCE**

**JAMES,**

**BY THE GRACE OF GOD**

**KING OF GREAT BRITAIN, FRANCE, AND IRELAND,  
DEFENDER OF [THE FAITH](Faiths.md), & [c](Partiality.md).**

**The  [Translators](BibleTranslators.md)  of the Bible [wish](TheShew.md#Chapter1)  
[Grace](GraceUntoYou.md)**,  [Mercy](ShewBread5.md#willx2),  and  [Peace](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Peace.md),**  
[through](through.md)  [JESUS CHRIST](ShewBread.md#contraryto)  [our Lord](RevelatorySermons13.md).**

____________

GREAT and manifold were the blessings, most dread Sovereign, which Almighty God, the Father of all mercies, bestowed upon us the people of England, when first he sent Your Majesty's Royal Person to rule and reign over us. For whereas it was the expectation of many, who wished [not well](Law.md) unto our Sion, that upon the setting of that bright Occidental Star, Queen Elizabeth of most happy memory, some thick and palpable [clouds](RevelatorySermons8.md) of [darkness](RevelatorySermons19.md) would so have over[shadow](Hebrews11.md)ed this Land, that men should have been in doubt [which way](Best.md) they were to walk; and that it should hardly be known, who was to direct the [unsettled](ContraryThings.md) State; the appearance of your Majesty, as the Sun in his strength, instantly dispelled those supposed and surmised mists, and gave unto all that were well affected exceeding cause of [comfort](Prophesy.md); especially when we beheld the Government [established](RevelatorySermons1.md) in Your Highness, and Your [hopeful](TwainShew3.md#Hopes) [Seed](BornAgain.md), by an undoubted Title, and this also accompanied with [peace](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Peace.md) and tranquillity at home and abroad.

But among all our joys, there was no one that more filled our hearts, than the blessed continuance of the preaching of [God's sacred Word](GraceUntoYou.md) among us; which is that inestimable treasure, which [excelleth](Best.md) all the riches of the earth; because the fruit thereof extendeth itself, not only to the time spent in this transitory world, but directeth and disposeth men unto that [eternal](EternalLife.md) happiness which is [above](RevelatorySermons21.md) in heaven.

Then not to suffer this to fall to the ground, but rather to take it up, and to continue it in that state, wherein the famous Predecessor of Your Highness did leave it: nay, to go [forward](Usward.md) with the confidence and resolution of a [Man](TwainShew.md#Blesseds) in maintaining [the truth of Christ](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md), and propagating it far and near, is that which hath so bound and firmly knit the hearts of all Your Majesty's loyal and religious people unto [You](YeYou.md), that Your [very name](ShewBread6.md#Hallowed) is precious among them: their eye doth behold You with comfort, and they bless You in their hearts, as that [sanctified](Perfection.md) [Person](Partiality.md) who, under God, is the immediate [Author](RevelatorySermons17.md) of their true happiness. And this their contentment doth not diminish or decay, but every day [increaseth](Hebrews7.md) and taketh strength, when they observe, that the zeal of Your Majesty toward the house of God doth not slack or go [backward](Law.md), but is [more and more](GraceRun.md) kindled, manifesting itself abroad in the farthest parts of Christendom, by writing in defence of [the Truth](RevelatorySermons14.md), (which hath given such a [blow](Vanish.md) unto that [man of sin](Allegory4.md), as [will not be healed](LawLaw.md),) and every day at home, by religious and [learned](OneLiners.md#BibleTranslators) discourse, by frequenting the house of God, by hearing [the Word](TwainShew7.md#Words) preached, by cherishing the Teachers thereof, by caring for [the Church](Revelations7.md#23), as a most tender and loving nursing Father.

There are infinite arguments of this right christian and religious affection in Your Majesty; but none is more forcible to declare it to others than the vehement and perpetuated desire of accomplishing and publishing of this work, which now with all humility we present unto Your Majesty. For when Your Highness had once out of deep judgment apprehended how convenient it was, that out of the Original Sacred Tongues, together with [comparing](ShewBread8.md#Dare) of the labours, both in our own, and other foreign Languages, of many worthy men who went before us, there should be one [more exact](Best.md) Translation of the holy Scriptures into the English Tongue; Your Majesty did never desist to urge and to excite [those to whom it was commended](BibleTranslators.md), that the work might be [hastened](GraceRun.md), and that the business might be expedited in so decent a manner, as a [matter of such importance](Heart.md) might justly require.

And now at last, by the [mercy](ShewBread5.md#willx2) of God, and the continuance of our labours, it being brought unto such a [conclusion](RevelatorySermons13.md), as that we have great hopes that the Church of England shall reap good fruit thereby; we hold it our duty to offer it to Your Majesty, not only as to our King and Sovereign, but as to the [principal Mover and Author of the work](RevelatorySermons17.md): humbly craving of Your most Sacred Majesty, that since things of this quality have ever been subject to the censures of [illmeaning](lawphoto.md) and [discontented](Concupiscence.md) persons, it may receive approbation and patronage from so learned and judicious a Prince as Your Highness is, whose allowance and acceptance of our labours shall more honour and encourage us, than all the [calumniations](http://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/calumniation) and [hard](RevelatorySermons23.md) interpretations of [other](RevelatorySermons12.md) men shall dismay us. So that if, on the one side, we shall be traduced by Popish Persons at home or abroad, who therefore will malign us, because we are poor instruments to make [God's holy Truth](RevelatorySermons14.md) to be yet more and more known unto the people, whom they desire still to keep in [ignorance](ShewBread7.md#Bliss) and [darkness](Law.md#Was); or if, on the other side, we shall be maligned by self-conceited Brethren, who run their own ways, and give liking unto nothing, but what is framed by [them](ShewBread2.md#inthem)selves, and hammered on [their](TheirPartYourPart.md) anvil; we may rest secure, supported within by truth and innocency of a good conscience, having walked the ways of simplicity and integrity, as before the Lord; and sustained without by the [powerful](Hebrews5.md) protection of Your Majesty's [grace](GodShew2.md#Allness) and favour, which will ever give countenance to honest and christian endeavours against [bitter](RevelatorySermons24.md) censures and un[charitable](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Charity.md) [imp](GodShew.md#nomorechildren)utations.

> _The Lord of heaven and earth bless Your Majesty with many and happy days, that, as his heavenly [hand](TwainShew3.md#Hands) hath enriched Your [High](Highminded.md)ness with many singular and extraordinary graces, so You may be the wonder of the world in this [latter age](TwainShew.md#Blesseds) for happiness and true felicity, to the honour of that great [GOD](GodShew2.md), and the good of his Church, [through](through.md) [Jesus Christ](ShewBread.md#contraryto) [our Lord](RevelatorySermons13.md) and [only](Potentate.md) [Saviour](RevelatorySermons6.md)._


The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ with you all. Amen.

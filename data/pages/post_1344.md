## Content

Re "kingdom of heaven" & "kingdom of God" difference in KJV:
it's interesting to notice, in KJV, the "kingdom of heaven" (aka Jewish law heaven in OT) only occurs in book of Matthew, where it ALSO says seek ye first the "kingdom of God",
(perhaps as if to allegory distinguish law and grace) 
and
Mt 
11:13, Mk 1:1-15, Lk 16:16 KJV notes 
Jesus Christ (by whom came grace and truth) doesn't even BEGIN to preach the "kingdom of God" message "UNTIL John" the Baptist (aka 2nd coming of Elijah = violent prophet) gets beheaded (and because John still said "wrath to come" = law to come = contrary to "God has not appointed us to wrath, but to obtain salvation [from wrath, aka from law] by our Lord Jesus Christ": 1Thess 5:9),
and
of ye/you, Luke 17:21 KJV clarifies the "kingdom of God" is within "you" (of ye/you, since Jesus had said "ye" do err) 
So,
John 3 notes a man must be born again(from above, aka of God is a Spirit), to then be able to see and enter the "kingdom of God" (is within "you", allegory as if what's right of ye/you). 
So ya, it's an inside to outside thing, but there's clarity about such to observe, ponder, discuss, especially in KJV allegory.

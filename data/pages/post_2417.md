## Content

WORD [rhema]of God & WORD [logos] of God DIFFER in Greek:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Ephesians 6:17 describes the word [rhema] of God as the "sword"  of the Spirit (as if for battle mode).
Hebrews 4:12 describes the word [logos] of God as being sharper than any twoedged sword.
John 12:48 in Greek uses both rhema words & logos word.
Hebrews 12:19 uses both rhema words & logos word
2Corinthians 5:19's word [logos] of reconciliation is the logos.
Hebrews 6:5 notes have tasted the good [kalos] word [rhema].
(seems if it was good [agathos] it would be the logos word)
Last mention of rhema for word is Hebrews 11:3.
Thereafter only logos is used for word.
1Peter 1:23 notes born again incorruptible is by the logos word, which abideth for ever.
What are your insights about rhema & logos ?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

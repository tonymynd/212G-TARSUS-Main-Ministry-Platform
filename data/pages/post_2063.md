FAVOUR [charis] vs GRACE [charis] Bible Contrast.
Grace to you and peace from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.
In New Testament favour [charis1] & grace [charis2] both occur in Luke to Acts (LukeWarm mode); But beyond Acts ONLY grace occurs, making grace so exceedingly much more than favour, it may not be correct to define grace as undeserved favour.

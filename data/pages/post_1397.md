## Content

Higher God of all grace (neither law, nor double-minded law-grace dual-ity of lower plural God on high) is so grace-us (upbraideth not) he will not force his pure grace on any; But patiently waits to be humbly asked for his pure wisdom (as if hiding, observing). For higher grace is neither an ultimatum nor a even a new commandment like love one an-other. For in pure grace there's no other-wise. Grace doesn't command nor control, but 
Spirit leads unto sell-control & awares with higher truth till not need governing like a child=servant.

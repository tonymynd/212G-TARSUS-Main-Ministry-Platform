## Content

TWOEDGED SWORD ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
First biblical mention of TWOEDGED SWORD in Psalm 149:6,7 notes it executes vengeance and punishment (not grace, mercy, peace).
Proverbs 5:3,4 then portrays a strange woman as having a smooth mouth, but her end is BITTER, sharp as a TWOEDGED SWORD.
Hebrews 4:12 then notes 
the word [logos] of God is:
SHARPER than any TWOEDGED SWORD.
Perhaps 
allegory meaning 
Grace is smarter than vengeance & punishment (law law).
So then, 
a sharp TWOEDGED SWORD portrayed as coming out the mouth of the Revelation 1:10-16 character, noted in Revelation 1:16 is neither a portrayal of Jesus Christ (by whom came grace and truth) nor the word [logos] of God (which is sharper than any twoedged sword). 
note:
a character John saw behind him is described as being "one like Son of man" (mentioned in Daniel 7:13), not Son of God, dressed as an Old Testament law priest, who would both bless and curse with good and evil law.
Revelation 2:12 notes the character described in Rev 1:10-16 claims he has the sharp sword with TWO EDGES, who notes has things "against" and doth "hate".
In contrast, in John 5:45, the voice of Jesus Christ (Son of God) notes he (as grace and truth) would not accuse any to the Father.
Revelation 19:15 notes with the sharp sword out of the mouth, the result would be "smite" nations, to then rule them with a rod of iron, by treading the winepress of the fierceness and wrath of an Almighty God (differs from a graceful, merciful, peaceful God).
What are your insights about a TWOEDGED SWORD out of mouth?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

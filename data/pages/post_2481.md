## Content

Let us see & BE the 3rd of 3 biblical phases:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Some Examples:
3rd: not still children, but men: 1Cor 13:11; 1Cor 14:20; Eph 4:13
2nd: 
children of God: Rom 8:21 (still a child-ish mode)
1st: children of the wicked: Mt 13:38 (children of d-evil)
3rd: no more a servant, but a son: Gal 4:7
2nd: servants of righteousness: Rom 6:18
1st: servants of sin: Rom 6:17
3rd: Christ: neither 1st nor last Adam b/c in "Adam" all die
2nd: last Adam: quickening spirit: second man[anthropos]
1st: first man[anthropos] Adam: living soul: 1Cor 15:22-46.
3rd: Grace: neither of OT Law nor NT Better Law
2nd: Law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus  
1st: Law of sin and death
3rd: Charity: greatest of 3 (as if 3rd of great, greater, greatest)
2nd: Hope
1st: Faith  (1Cor 13:13).
3rd: Perfect will of God: 3rd of 3 in Romans 12:2b
2nd: Acceptable will of God
1st: Good will of God.
What are your insights about BEing 3rd of 3 biblical phases?
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

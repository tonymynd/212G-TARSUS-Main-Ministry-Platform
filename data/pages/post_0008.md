## Content

Holy Bible (KJV: seventh English Bible) Clarity:
The "generation" (genesis) of "Jesus Christ" 
doesn't begin with Adam, but with "Abraham"; 
And it doesn't end with Jesus but with "Christ". 
Matthew 1.
So 
then, the "generation" (genesis) of Jesus Christ
is not like the "generations" (genealogies) of Adam.
And
Although "grace and truth came by Jesus Christ",
peace and victory are "through Jesus → Christ".
For Jesus mode is still division, not peace: Luke 12:51;
But Christ mode is peace, not divided: Ephesians 2:15.
For two Sons Jesus is not (yet) one Son Christ; And
God did not send Sons, but notably sent one "Son",
and sent not his Son to condemn, but to save; Yet
save is thru him... thru his name... aka thru J→C.
Interesting stuff to ponder at AD2013 → AD2014.

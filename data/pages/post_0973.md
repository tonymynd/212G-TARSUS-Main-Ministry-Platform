## Content

Some biblical upgrades are hidden in Greek* mode to discover when prompted by the Spirit. Here's a few examples I discovered:
- "converted" upgrade, from 'strepho' in Mt 18:3 to 'epistrepho' in Mt 13:15; Lk 22:32; Jn 12:40; Acts 3:19; 28:27.
- "knowledge" upgrade, from 'gnosis' to 'epignosis' = the knowledge  God our Saviour will have all men come unto, after saved: 1Tim 2:4.
Seems these upgrades contain the 'epi' prefix; So it's 
interesting to notice there's two kinds of "above" in Col 3:1-14: 'ano' & 'epi'... 
Then it's interesting to notice in Eph 4:4-6 there's 7 one's, lastly "one God", then "AND Father of all" = above(epi) all 7, which is the 8th, yet also through all and in you all.
*yet when I say Greek, I mean Strong's Concordance for KJV, not other Lexi-cons which can give different Greek meanings.

Canada's FREEDOM Convoy(s)... some observations: Feb 9th
Grace, Mercy, Peace to all.
Tow Truck companies reportedly refuse to help remove trucks.
Police reveal about 100 of 400 trucks in Ottawa contain children.
Fuel taken, then returned by police, reportedly has water added.
1 Liberal MP rebuked his political party: don't demonize fellow Canadians nor dismiss their concerns.
PM accuses: Confederate & Nazi flags are signs of terrorists. 
But 
truckers say such were false flag plants & Trudeau's personal photographer was seen & photographed in association with such.
Loud honking has been judge-injunction stopped for 10 days.
Protest has spread to 2 essential border crossings to USA.
Result?

Convoys begin in other countries, encouraged by Canada. 
What are your observations & opinions about such?

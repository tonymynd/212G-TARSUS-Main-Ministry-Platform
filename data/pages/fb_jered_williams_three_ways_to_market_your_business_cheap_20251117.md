I found three stupid, cheap ways to market your business.
Number one, team up with a local place like a bakery and do a giveaway.
Offer $100 gift card to their shop, post it on social media, tag the bakery,
and ask people to comment the word bakery.
Now, lots of people see your business and remember you because you gave them
something away for free.
Number two, go on Facebook and add as many friends as you can.
Start posting about your business.
That's how I got my first six months of work.
Number three, go to a local event and run a giveaway like before.
Ask people to write their name, phone number, and email to enter.
Afterwards, pick a winner, then call and email the rest.
Tell them they didn't win, but give them a coupon for $25 off your service.
We do this every year and get hundreds of jobs from it.

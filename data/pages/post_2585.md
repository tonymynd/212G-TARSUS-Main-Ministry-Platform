Let's also compare these:
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
Our Father ... deliver us FROM EVIL: Luke 11:4.
Now 
we are delivered FROM THE LAW: Romans 7:6 KJV.
Seems to portray what's d-evil as being the law = the curse.
Whatsoever is NOT OF FAITH is sin: Romans 14:23.
And the law is NOT OF FAITH: Galatians 3:12 KJV.
Seems to reveal, by comparison, law = sin.
For WHERE no law, THERE no transgression: Rom 4:15 KJV.
And WHEN no law, THEN no sin is imputed: Rom 5:13 KJV.
Romans 10:4 notes "Christ" is the end of the law.
(note: Jesus was only change of the law: Heb 7:12).

Christ (of thru Jesus→Christ) hath redeemed us from the curse of the law.

## Content

"The only true God" and "the only wise God", is
"That God is light" = no darkness in him at all, is
"The God of all comfort" = no discomfort at all, is
"The God of all grace" = no law at all, is
The God who "upbraideth not" = no smite at all.
To 
→ wit, that God was in Christ, 
reconciling the world unto himself, 
not imputing their trespasses unto them;

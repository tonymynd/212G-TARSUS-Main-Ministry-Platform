## Content

Some Reasons I discovered why biblical narrative talks of THROUGH him: John 3:17... THROUGH his name: John 20:31... THROUGH Jesus→Christ: Romans. Why?
- Jesus said in Mt 10:34 & Lk 12:51, he would neither "give" nor "send" peace on earth. Seems why the heralding of "on earth peace", in Luke 2:10-14, was about "Christ" (not Jesus), and why John 7:43 says, about Jesus: there was division (not peace) because of him. So, seems we have to 
get thru J→C to C better how 2SonsJesus is not [yet] 1SonChrist. 
- Jesus, as noted twice in Acts 5:31 & 13:23, was only Saviour of Israel; But it's also noted twice in John 4:42 & 1John 4:14, that Christ is Saviour of the world.
- Jesus was only change of the law, from OT Law to NT BetterLaw; But Christ is the end of the law (not just mend of the law): Romans 10:4, which then takes us to Grace & Peace via the gospel of Paul, which is then "the gospel of Christ": Romans 1:16.
- It's noted in Romans 5:1 we have peace with God THROUGH our Lord Jesus→Christ; And in 1Corinthians 15:57 God given victory to us is THROUGH our Lord Jesus→Christ. Interesting stuff.
Such is not said to diminish Jesus, but to help us see that in Jesus mode it's still both new and old things; But in Christ mode it's then all things new, nothing old: 2Corinthians 5:17.

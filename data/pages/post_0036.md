## Content

Us-ward, like God-ward, is directional: "above" of under/above.
Man-y still have an erroneous perception of "us" of "us-ward".
It's neither of highminded them1/them2, but "higher": Heb 7:26.
It isn't right of left/right sides wars, but "above" of under/above.
Us-ward is neither of them1/them2 in Heb 10:39, but "of" them2.
Hebrews 7:26 KJV
such an high priest became US, [who is] 
holy, 
harmless*, 
undefiled, 
separate from sinners, and 
made HIGHER than the heavens.
*neither do harm nor receive harm
2Peter 3:9 KJV
The Lord is not slack concerning his promise, 
as some men count slackness; 
but is longsuffering to US-WARD, 
not willing that any should perish, 
but that all should come to repentance.

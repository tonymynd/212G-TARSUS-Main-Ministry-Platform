ABOVE FORGIVING ?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord. 
Of things said about forgive, my favorite is Colossians 3:12-15 KJV, where after put on 7 new man things, then put on "charity" [agape2, as if more than "love"], NOTABLY "above" forgiving one another, makes "charity" 8th above 7:
8. 
charity*: bond of perfectness
7. forgiving one another
6. forbearing one another
5. longsuffering
4. meekness
3. humbleness of mind
2. kindness
1. bowels of mercies
*note: in 1Cor 13:5 KJV it notes the more excellent way: charity "thinketh no evil", none, as if neither to punish nor even forgive. Seems why charity (instead of love) can be put on above forgiving.
prior, in Ephesians 4:4-6 KJV we see "Father" is 8th above 7:
8 And Father of all: above all, and through all, and in you all.
7. one God
6. one baptism
5. one faith
4. one Lord
3. one hope
2. one Spirit
1, one body
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

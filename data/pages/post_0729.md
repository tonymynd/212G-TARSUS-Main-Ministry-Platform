## Content

WHAT shld we do with reverse things, like evil = reverse of live?
What about reverse order of Jesus' twain things in Mt 12:30-37?
Seems they all end bad, such as justified + condemned in 12:37.
What 
about reverse order of 1John 4:6's truth + error. Ending?
What about his "know we" (?) being the reverse of we know (!).
Perhaps POINT of such is not have both good + evil, only good.
eg: Holy Bible containing 2 Holies: Law/Grace ends Grace only.
For if 2holies, and holy of holies, seems Law says it's the holy.
Seems many leaders of nations & UN say Rule of Law = NWO.
Seems say Rule of Law = manifesting Bad Ending: ww crISIS.
For I found this twain thing about the Law:
- Law is good, but both good and evil ends bad: reverse of live.
- Law is just, but both just and unjust ends bad: unjust judge.
- Law is holy, but both holy and unholy ends bad: highminded.
- Law is natural, but also natural disaster of law worketh wrath.
- Law is spiritual, but also spiritual wickedness in high places.
So rather than wrestle against such law things, I flushed Law.
Now I have only Grace, mercy, and peace toward everyone.

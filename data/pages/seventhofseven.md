Such is to allegory say "that God" is grace (light), and in him there is no law (darkness) at all. So John 3:17 notes "God did not send his Son to condemn (law) the world"; No, not this, but that: "[through him](through.md) (through his name: through Jesus>Christ) the world might be saved ([graced and truthed](RevelatorySermons6.md))".

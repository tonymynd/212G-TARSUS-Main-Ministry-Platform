## Content

Bible Mystery: in hope mode (of faith, hope, charity*) 
it can be, get, either hope-less -or- hope- ful. 
*KJV uses love & charity as if law & grace allegory.
Law hope tends to be as if hope-less; So Nay to law. 
Grace hope is much more: hope-ful; So Yea to grace. 
In hope mode there`s also mention of ``better hope`` 
but only in a ``better testament`` 
having a ``better covenant`` 
and thereby ``better promises`` 
such as a ``better resurrection``... 
But better resurrection is still a ``grave`` subject.
So in Heb 6 we leave better hope for perfection;
For that perfection has no f-laws to even mention.
We only faith and hope for what we don`t yet have,
so, until we have charity never faileth, we still hope;
But when we have charity & put it on above forgiving*,
Then we no longer need to faith & hope to never fail.
*Put on charity above forgiving is in Col 3:12-15 KJV.
Interesting stuff to 2Tim 2:15 study, 
to have no more need of shame.
For charity, which thinketh no evil,
no longer plays any blame game.

## Content

1John?
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God, our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Things to notice and consider about 1John:
- 
there is no mention of grace, nor mercy, nor peace in 1John.
- it's to "my little children" allegory as if to John's law children.
- 1Jn 2:1 portrays Father as judge & JC as lawyer in a law court.
- 1Jn 4:6 reverses spirit of truth & spirit of error: ends as error.
- 1Jn 4:17 mentions a day of law judgment[krisis], not of grace 
salvation.
- 1Jn 5 twice says keep the commandments (aka the laws).
- only John mentions antichrist.
Learning these things made me go Hmmm about 1John.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

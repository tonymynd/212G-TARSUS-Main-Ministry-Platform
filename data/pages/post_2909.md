## Content

Jesus received not mingled drink, twice, 
then lastly only received the pure vinegar:
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and Jesus Christ our Lord.
Notice Jesus twice refused "MINGLED" drink (perhaps allegory as if to refuse mingled grace & law, and as if from both sides of cross), then lastly (allegory as if 3rd of 3) received only the pure vinegar:
Matthew 27:34 KJV
They gave him vinegar to drink MINGLED with gall: and when he had tasted thereof, he would not drink.
note:
it says he tasted, perhaps allegory as if to taste death (as if grace & law = life & death: dead end) for every man noted in Hebrews 2:9?
Mark 15:23 KJV
And they gave him to drink wine MINGLED with myrrh: but he received it not.
John 19:30 KJV
When Jesus therefore had received the VINEGAR, he said, It is finished: and he bowed his head, and gave up the ghost.
The (pure) grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

Interesting to notice, with "Son of man", in Matthew 25:31-46, it's sheep/goats = right/left (kinda like OT Hebrew read right←left); And unto sheep it's "life eternal" (also reverse of "eternal life"). Seems more like Back-ward←BC than AD→For-ward. Here's a thought, it may be why Apostle Paul always turns CJ around to JC, to end his epistles with grace, of such law←&→grace. The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

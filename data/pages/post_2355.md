## Content

a MORE EXCELLENT WAY is neither Broad nor Narrow 
and because it's about ALL which is neither Many nor Few
a "more excellent way" isn't greater of twain: great → greater,
but is the "greatest" of "three": great → greater → greatest.
and 
now abideth faith, hope, charity, these three; 
but the greatest of these (three) is charity. ~1Cor 13:13 KJV

Endurance Test ... Test of Endurance  

Let us look at the endurance test of Revelation, at enduring to [the end](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md) through this book of "prophecy" John sent to [seven angels of seven churches of Asia](Sevens7.md) which [writer of Revelation (Paul: his ange)](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Revelation.md#Author) patches into his [allegoric](Allegory.md) satire on John and at the same creates a [final exam](Revelations2.md) to pass which has a comfort ending; as if the end of prophesyings only prophesieth edification exhortation  comfort. Hence we are told prophecies fails, so despise not prophesyings.  
  
[Seven times](Sevens7.md) in [The Revelation of St John The Divine](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Revelation.md) it reveals it's a book of "prophecy", "this" (of [this/that](TwainShew.md)) the [first and last](Revelations6.md#19) time. So it's as if navigating a "[worse](Allegory3.md)" case scenario, kinda like navigating the best of spam; Not to mention such [spiritual perversion afterward](HowbeitAfterward.md) from "seven spirits" oughta ring a bell about a worse scenario JC talked about via: "[seven other spirits more wicked](Revelations9.md)".  
  

(Law: Warfare) Prophecy vs Prophesy (Grace: Peace)  
Prophecy: 'I fought the law and the law won' ... a dead end  
Prophesy: Flush all law as dung and grace wins ... [living end](NoLawNoDeadEnd.md)  

  
Moral: it is far better to flush the law than fight the law; for law is not only the source of sin but also the strength of sin, the strong man we are told to first bind in order to spoil the house of law: sin and death, lest it spoil you instead. Not to mention the end is already written and has no mention of law at all. For losing is not optional with God, nor is knowing the truth in Jn 8:32. Not only salvation is required for all men (so none perish), but also [awareness](RevelatorySermons6.md) for all.  
  

Prophecies fail: whether prophecies, [they](TheirPartYourPart.md) shall fail  

His Grace will have nothing less than [all mercy](Perfection.md) and [no sacrifice at all](ShewBread5.md#willx2). How is that possible you may ask? No law = no sin imputed = [no sacrifice required](RevelatorySermons22.md) = no death sting of law imputed sin. For only law imputes sin: Rom 5:13, and sin brings forth death: James 1:15. So law is not only to be done away, but blotted out to the point of "vanish". As written law's "ready to vanish": [Heb 8:13](Hebrews9.md) and it "shall [vanish](Vanish.md)": 1Cor 13:8. Such is like the boogie man (phantom menace: law) vanishes when a child becomes a man. Blessed the man to whom the Lord will not impute sin.  
  

Gloom vs Groom  

Law is obviously destructive (vs constructive) if "law worketh wrath" and law is both a "ministration of condemnation" and a "ministration of death". Law is oppressive and JC went about healing all "oppressed of the devil(law)". We all need to connect our [biblical dots](BiblicalDots.md) to see law is evil, [evil concupiscence](Concupiscence.md), and God hath not appointed us unto wrath (law worketh wrath) is [allegory](Allegory.md) for God hath not appointed us to law. Not to mention "be ye perfect" and law can never make comers thereunto perfect. Prophecy is about and from law. Prophesy (edification exhortation comfort) is about and from God (His grace). That is why prophecies fail (whether prophecies, they shall fai) and prophesyings pass [the test of endurance: holiness with sobriety](Charity7.md). Despise not prophesyings.  
  

Perversion ([them-ward](ShewBread2.md#inthem)) <Prophecy vs Prophecy→ ([us-ward](Usward.md)) Conversion  
Prophecy: law is spiritual ([afterward](HowbeitAfterward.md)), so [law: love](TwainShew4.md#Laws) thy neighbor as thyself  
Prophesy: grace is spiritual (afterward), so flush [the second](TwainShew4.md#Laws) [(another) law](AnotherLaw.md)  

  
We are told the biblical order is natural first, [spiritual afterward](HowbeitAfterward.md) of such an old/new before/after shew about law/grace. Furthermore the natural must die for the spiritual to be raised immortal (make law [dead testator](RevelatorySermons1.md) to be immorta).  
  

Prophecies vs Prophesyings  
Prophecies draw back to law worketh wrath, as a draw back to [perdition](lawphoto.md)  
Prophesyings [go on](Leave.md) unto the [perfection](Perfection.md) of [my grace is sufficient for thee](RevelatorySermons14.md)  

  

(Begin of Law) Discomfort vs Comfort (End of Law)  

Fear is the "beginning" of knowledge and wisdom: law knowledge and earthy sensual devilish wisdom; And "fear hath torment", which is discomfort. Of two gospels (this/that gospels) this gospel is the "beginning" of sorrows. That gospel: perfect love void of fear (grace void of law) is the end of sorrows. In each allegoric case of such before/after law/grace as begin/end we find the result is discomfort/comfort, as noted in the [ye/you](YeYou.md) scenario of 1Pet 5:10 where "ye" suffer awhile prior to "you" made perfect by [the God of all grace](GodShew2.md#Allness). Not to mention the Lord (that Spirit, liberty thereof) is longsuffering "us-ward" of them/us (before/after) law/grace. Fwd is [you-ward](You-ward.md), [us-ward](Usward.md), [God-ward](GodShew2.md#Allness), as if going on to the perfection of Prophesy: edify → exhort → comfort.  
  

Law ←Prophecy vs Prophesy → Grace  

Prophecy: looks bwd to law worketh wrath as a draw back to perdition  
Midst: which will you believe? will you draw back to perdition or [go on](Hebrews7.md)  
Prophesy: looks fwd to grace is sufficient as if going on unto perfection  

  
No doubt there will be an endurance test if told to endure unto the end. And no doubt there will a test of endurance if told salvation involves holiness with sobriety; Not to mention if told many shall come to deceive, it's to test you to see if you really and truly got it: understanding or perhaps just feigning it, whether or not you can be deceived, seduced, bewitched, subverted, etc. For if "a little leaven leaveneth the whole lump", the pass mark is 100%, and not knowing or knowing the difference between prophecy and prophesy is as to fail or pass the [final exam](GMP/Notes/GodShew/Revelation.md#Intro). Paul gave Corinthians a [CJ vs JC](ChristJesus.md) test they failed, so he warned them not to be [children](GodShew.md#nomorechildren) in understanding if adults. Not to mention Solomon said "with all thy getting, get understanding" clarifying when such is embraced it results in thy head (Christ) crowned with [grace glory](TwainShew3.md#Glorys).  
  

(Prophecy) Destructive vs Constructive (Prophesy)  
Prophecy: law demands perfection, but imperfect law cannot deliver perfection, so all die by law  
Prophesy: grace will have mercy, not sacrifice, makes everybody perfect by the [abolition of law](RevelatorySermons22.md)  

  

Despise not prophesyings. Prophesyings Edify→Exhort→Comfort Only  

The more we flush law as dung, destructive prophecies thereof, the less "terror" we'll have, since the Bible clarifies Moses' law was a "great terror" to all Israel in Deut 34:12. Not to mention the gospel is first to the Jew because they well said at Mt Sinai the law, such accusation and condemnation they could not bare to hear, should not be spoken to them again: Deut & Hebrews. Not to mention God did not send his Son to condemn: Jn 3:17 and the law is a ministration of condemnation: 1Cor 3:7. Not to mention JC flat out reveals Mosaic law was accuser of the brethren: Jn 5:45. Not to mention "law worketh wrath": Rom 4:15 and "God hath not appointed us unto [wrath](RevelatorySermons18.md)": 1Thes 5:9. Not to mention law was the beast thou sawest which "was, and is not". If thou sawest it, then in the bottom "third" of the stars of heaven who got drawn away from heaven, and cast down to earth by such a [dragonic](Vanish.md) tail.

So, reconciled to God, in heaven, is the reverse of twice fallen to "great" ←greater ←greatest, as is charity the great → greater → "greatest". So let us not only get it [up-right](ShewBread10.md) in such divided center/right heavens, but also "[seated](Revelations7.md#21)" with Christ (of [J→C](GodShew5.md)): "[the end of the law](ShewBread9.md#Expired)" thereof, and also realize charity "never faileth" is to be put on "above" forgiving one another (nothing to even forgive whenever there is no law = no sin imputed = no death sting of law imputed sin); For [know](YeYou.md)-ing is above and beyond both faith-ing and hope-ing, just as charity is the greatest of faith hope charity. Not to mention ["the law is not of faith" and "whatsoever is not of faith is sin"](BiblicalDots.md) and "sin, when it is [finished](TwainShew2.md#Finisheds), brings forth death". No such dead end in the Bible:

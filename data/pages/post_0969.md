## Content

Image/Likeness? Created/Made? Perhaps we have to learn the difference between "image" & "likeness" in Genesis 1:26 as being the difference between "created" in Ephesians 2:10 & "made" in 1Peter 5:10. For God NEVER said let us create(bara) man, but said "let us make(asah) man". Yet God gave two before/after scenarios to play out for compare-i-son: image = before, likeness = after. Same thing in Eph 2:10 created = before & 1Pet 5:10 
made = after. So even though God did create man in image mode: Genesis 1:27, such was only to set the stage for the before part of before/after being them of them/us, and law of law/grace contrast. Interesting allegoric biblical mystery to solve by look at all the evidence to see what's evident thereof, aka no man is justified by the law of flaw.

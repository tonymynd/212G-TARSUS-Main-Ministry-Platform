## Content

Learning game of seek to find also includes ask to receive. Ask Who? There is a higher God we may ask for His wisdom, to help us end suffer mode of think evil (reverse of live). Yet...
That God (all grace), who doesn't upbraid, is so grace-us he will not force His pure grace on any, but patiently waits (in hidden mode, where least expected to be found), to be asked for His pure wisdom; But also waits till we stop being double-minded, 
humble ourselves, and ask in faith (aka in grace, for law is not of faith)... 
Then we may receive revelations from the Spirit, as much more than 'gnosis' knowledge (also bible rendered as science), which will then "vanish away" (1Cor 13:8), and be replaced with 'episgnosis' knowledge (Eph 4:13...1Tim 2:4).
Eg: 
1Peter 5:10 notes 3 things, 3rd of 3 being end of suffer: 
The God of all grace
- who hath [first] called us unto his eternal glory by Christ Jesus,
- after ye have suffered a while (2Tim3:12 notes in Christ Jesus),
- make (of create/make) you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you. 
Then
no more mention of Christ Jesus, only Jesus Chist, for the higher 'epignosis' knowledge is only of our Lord Jesus Christ.

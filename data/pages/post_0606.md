## Content

REMEMBRANCE DAY THOUGHTS; Especially about PTSD:
Sad to hear Canada's vet suicides increased from 50's to 60's; Yet glad to hear Canada's military now looking into why(?) of it.
Seems even biblical Paul suffered w/ wretched & suicidal PTSD;
and wrote about it in Romans 7, giving us a clue of root cause.
Seems Paul had assumed another law to obey was unto life; But
found "another law", aka law2 of law1/law2, was also unto death. For when he tried to obey law2, it brought him into CAPTIVITY again to law1: called the law of sin and death, which then did all manner of (evil) "concupiscence" on him: aka made him do things he would not do, also hindered him from doing things he would do, and then afterward hounded him with a guilt trip till he felt as "wretched" and suicidal as war vets currently suffering PTSD.
Seems the point is this: 
law2 of Matthew 22:36-40's law1/law2 is also f-law.  
Seems why JC made a change of the law in Matthew 5:43→44:
from LoveThyNeighbour+HateEnemy←to→LoveYourEnemies.
It's kinda like a change of OT Good+Evil Law into NT BetterLaw.
But it's not TheEnd to ENDure to, for BetterLaw is also still Law;
And LoveYourEnemies still has Enemies = JeopardyEveryHour.
So we go on through Jesus→Christ to Christ=the end of the law.
Then in that Christ, we get reconciled unto grace having no f-law.

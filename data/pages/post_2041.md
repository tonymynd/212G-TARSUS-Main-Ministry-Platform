FAVOUR [charis] vs GRACE [charis] Bible Contrast.
Grace to you and peace from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.
In New Testament favour [charis1] & grace [charis2] both occur in Luke to Acts (LukeWarm mode); But beyond Acts ONLY grace occurs, making grace so exceedingly much more than favour, it may not be correct to define grace as undeserved favour. 
Favour only occurs 6 times (Lk 1:30; 2:52; Acts 2:47; 7:10; 7:46; 25:3), 
lastly Acts 25:3 requested "against" & to "kill" Paul; 
But Grace occurs over 100 times (Lk 2:40 to Rev 22:21) & never against nor ever to harm any, lastly to be "with you all". 
It's interesting to notice in Luke 1:28-35 KJV the angel Gabriel greeted Mary as "highly favoured", which "troubled" her, then also noted she should fear not because she had found "favour" (not grace) with God; And said the Holy "Ghost" [pneuma1], not Holy "Spirit" [pneuma2] would come upon her, and the holy "thing" (aka JESUS) born of her would "also" be called the Son of God. 
Grace does not NT occur until Luke 2:40.
Interesting biblical mystery aspect to notice & ponder about; Especially if modern preachers say you have "favour" with God.
The "grace" of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

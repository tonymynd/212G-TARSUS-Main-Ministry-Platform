So, perhaps this Gentleness being the midst of 9 has some significance we may get enlightened about. Let’s study it; then also discuss it:

There’s only two mentions of “gentleness” in NT. Such are different in Greek:

- Gentleness1: 2Cor 10:1 “Now I Paul myself beseech you by the meekness and gentleness (‘epieikeia’) of Christ, who in presence [am] base among you, but being absent am bold toward you: …”

- Gentleness2: Gal 5:22 “But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness (‘chrestotes’), goodness, faith, …”

So, in English both are gentleness, but in Greek as different as ‘epieikeia’ & ‘chrestotes’. The ‘epieikeia’ gentleness, of Christ, is first rendered as requested “clemency”, by Jewish orator Tertullus, from Roman governor Felix, in Acts 24:4, where Paul is on trial, and the governor Felix is referred to as a Judge who’s hearing both sides of this trial matter. So it’s kinda like Paul is first beseeching Corinthians using this “gentleness” aspect.

Note: first beseeching of Paul reminds of what is first taught is taken away to establish the second as better. Heb 10:9

Now, also looking at ‘chrestotes’ kind of “gentleness”, it’s also rendered as “goodness”, “kindness”(a), “good”.
(a) 'chrestotes' kindness among things where above all, charity is put on Col 3:12-14

Yet it’s notable this “goodness” aspect of this “gentleness” = 5th fruit aspect notably differs from the 6th fruit aspect = “goodness” = ‘agathosyne’ = uprightness of heart and life; Which is yet to be discussed as next chapter. So perhaps Greek renderings, as if inside of and more than English renderings, hereby becomes as if first comparing 2 kinds of “gentleness”, then also as if comparing 2 kinds of “goodness”:

- gentleness1: 2Cor 10: of Christ, was 1st rendered as clemency requested of governor.
- gentleness2: Gal 5: also rendered as goodness, is still only 1st of 2 kinds of “goodness”.
- gentleness2 (midst) = goodness, but ‘chrestotes’ goodness = 5th is not goodness = 6th.
- goodness1: ‘chrestotes’ = 5th aspect, now being looked at, as if the midst
- goodness2: ‘agathosyne’ = 6th aspect, yet to be looked at, as beyond midst

love joy peace longsuffering gentleness2=goodness1 goodness2 faith meekness temperance

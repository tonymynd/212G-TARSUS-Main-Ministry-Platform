## Content

ON EARTH PEACE: HOW? Grace, mercy, & peace, 
from God, our Father, & Jesus → Christ our Lord.
Grace is what we grow in to become graceful, merciful, peaceful;
as the fulness of the stature of Christ of Jesus→Christ=TheSeed.
So a 
singular "generation" ('genesis') of Jesus→Christ described in Matthew 1 neither begins with Adam nor ends with Jesus; But notably begins with Abraham (to whom TheSeed was promised), and goes on, even through Jesus, UNTO Christ (TheSeed)...
...Galatians 3:16 KJV: "Now to Abraham and his seed were the promises made. He saith not, And to seeds, as of many; but as of one, And to thy seed, which is Christ" (of THRU J→C).
So the heralding about "on earth peace, good will toward men" in Luke 2:11-14 is notably about "Christ" (not about Jesus); For in Luke 12:51 Jesus said he was NotYet peace, but division, and in Luke 13:32 Jesus said he was NotYet perfected, but he shall be.
Perfected is THRU 2SonsJesus to 1SonChrist=the end of the law.
So Romans 5:1 clarifies we have PEACE with God THRU J→C. 
So the final words of Peter to the beloved is: "grow in grace, and [in] the knowledge of our Lord and Saviour Jesus → Christ. To him [be] glory both now and for ever. Amen". YET there's more:
For it's NotYet the last Amen nor yet the 'epignosis' knowledge of the truth God our Saviour will have all men come unto, in addition to being saved in 1Timothy 2:3,4; Where "good and acceptable" is still only 2/3 of 3aspects of the will of God in Romans 12:2 KJV:
good (1/3) AND acceptable (2/3=0.666) AND perfect (3/3=1.000).
So the story goes on, as if going on unto perfection in Hebrews 6, by "leaving" plural law/grace "principles" of the doctrine of Christ...
For James 1:8 notes even an 'aner' man (whose head is Christ) is still unstable if double minded. Double is NotYet ONEderful. So...
So the story goes on unto TheEnd to ENDure unto, which is a Positive having no men-tion of any negatives like law: sin: death. For Christ did not stay right of plural heavens on high, but went higher than the heavens on high to be-come the "harmless" us, as if to what's best for all of Good→Better→↑Best↑=Rest of the story. So until we ENDure unto TheEnd, there still remaineth a rest to the people of God. Perhaps all talk of decease should cease to have peace that lasts. Seems why JC, sent by the only true God (who can't lie nor die), finished his work before the cross, as noted in John 17:3,4. Think upon that at Christmas. 
"The ↑grace↑ of our Lord Jesus→Christ [be] WITH you all. Amen".

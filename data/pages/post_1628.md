## Content

Extrapolation (only for contrasting) awareness about God.
Grace, mercy, and peace, from God, our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
Eg:
He is not God of the dead (law), but of the living (grace).
The 
only true God (isn't false God, nor lie/truth duality).
God only wise (isn't foolish God, nor both foolish/wise).
God of all comfort (isn't discomfort, nor discomfort/comfort).
God our Saviour (isn't Destroyer, nor both destroys/saves).
God that cannot lie (isn't lie/truth duality, but only all truth).
God that upbraideth not (doesn't fault-find to upbraid any).
God of all grace (has no law, nor both law/grace duality).
That God is light (grace, has no darkness[law] in him at all).
God is love (isn't fear, nor both fear/love, but perfect love).
The only wise God (will only have mercy, and not sacrifice).
The only wise God (will only have grace, and not law).
The only wise God (will only have truth, and not lie).
The only wise God (will only have peace, not any division).
The only wise God (will only have eternal life, not any death).
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be/is] with you all. Amen.

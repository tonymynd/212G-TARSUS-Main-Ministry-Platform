## Content

Interesting Romans 12:2 KJV has a&b aspects: 
12:2a = 1st renew your mind, as if then to be able to also
12:2b = prove will of God is 3: good & acceptabe & perfect.
Seems then, when it's 3, then if only 2/3 it's not yet 3/3 = 1.
Eg: 
if we notice these allegory 3: "great, greater, greatest"; We'll notice even "greater love" is not yet "greatest" of "three" in 1Cor 13:13 KJV, which is then called "charity" (not love).
So,
Seems, when we investigate, with help of the Spirit, charity is like 3rd of BroadWay, NarrowWay, MoreExcellentWay.
Eg: if notice 3 modes of seek & find: cold, LukeWarm, Hot; We'll notice cOLD mode is like OLD Testament, and even LukeWarm mode (aka Luke & Acts) is not yet Hot mode (aka Romans-forward).
So,
Seems, when we investigate, with help of the Spirit, Hot mode is allegory like 3rd of OT law, NT better law, Grace.
So, in 1Timothy 2:1-4 KJV, we first have mention of things "good and acceptable" (aka 2/3 of 3 in Rom 12:2b), then 1Tim 2:4 is more yet, perhaps allegory as if "God our Saviour" is like 3rd of 3 Saviours (kinda like good, acceptable, perfect):
- God our Saviour = all saved & come to higher truth = perfect.
- Christ: Saviour of the world = all saved = acceptable.
- Jesus: Saviour of Israel = good
Interesting allegory stuff to notice, ponder, and discuss.

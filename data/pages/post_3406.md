## Content

ETERNAL LIFE
Grace, Mercy, Peace, from God our Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ.
"Eternal Life" does NOT occur in Old Testament NOR in Revelation.
Jesus used these 4 life phrases:
- NT "eternal life" 
- NT "life eternal" (reverse) 
- OT "everlasting life" 
- NT "life everlasting" (reverse) 
BUT,
'life eternal", used by Son of man, ends in John 17,
"everlasting life", in John 3:16, ends in Romans 6:22,
"life everlasting", ends in 1Timothy 1:16.
SO,
"eternal life" is NOT life eternal NOR everlasting life NOR life everlasting, which all cease being mentioned, but "eternal life" goes on being mentioned and clarified.
Romans 6:23 KJV notes "eternal life" is the gift [charisma*, not dorea] of God THROUGH Jesus → Christ our Lord.
*charisma is the "free gift" of God without repentance
Titus 1:2 KJV notes the God which CANNOT LIE promised "eternal life" before the world began.
The last mention of "eternal life" (and mercy) is in 
Jude 1:21 KJV
Keep yourselves in the love of God, looking for the mercy of our Lord Jesus Christ unto eternal life.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you all. Amen.

## Content

What is Grace? Dictionary says 'unmerited favour'; But even though both "favour" ('charis') & "grace" ('charis') occur in KJV Bible as 'charis', 
Favour isn't Grace. Favour only occurs from Luke 1:30 to Acts 25:3. Grace occurs from Luke 2:40 to Revelation 22:21 KJV. So perhaps even this guru doesn't yet have the complete picture:

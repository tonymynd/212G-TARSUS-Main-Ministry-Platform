## Content

Bible Mystery: 2 different Comforter(s) in ``Verily, verily`` John 14-16.
- Comforter1: Holy Ghost: not comfort only, but comfort + discomfort. 
- Comforter2: Spirit of truth: another Comforter: world cannot receive.
Tricky stuff, best studied in KJV Bible uses Holy Ghost and Holy Spirit.
Holy 
Ghost: perhaps both comfort + discomfort in law mode
- described as a mighty rushing wind in Acts 2
- appeared to them & sat upon them as cloven tongues of fire
- set a new twelve on fire, but also seemed to make them mean,
for when Annanias & Saphira lied they got killed by this Comforter.
In Ghost mode sin is unpardonable (Mt 12:32; Mk 3:29: Lk 12:10). 
Spirit of truth: perhaps comfort only, no discomfort in grace mode
- will reprove the world of sin, and of righteousness, and of judgment
- when he, the Spirit of truth, is come, he will guide you into all truth
In Spirit of truth mode God, who hath forgiven you all sin, is merciful.
When fully studied out these two Comforters
seem to be as different as Law and Grace.
Interesting stuff.

## Content

gRACE info: John 1:17 KJV:
For the law was given by Moses, [but] 
grace and truth came by Jesus Christ.
gRACE info: Romans 5:20 KJV:
Moreover the law entered, that the offence might abound. 
But where sin abounded, grace did much more abound:
gRACE info: 2Corinthians 3:9 KJV:
For if the ministration of condemnation [be] glory, much more doth the ministration of righteousness exceed in glory.
Seems, when contrasting Law←vs→Grace having a gRACE, 
The GraceGod is so EXCEEDINGLY MuchMore than LawGod, that 
in TheEnd to ENDure unto of a they/we duality RACE being RUN,
there's not even any mention of their LawGod: sin: or death to any.
The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ [be] with you all. Amen.

1John? I notice a lot of 'new' believers in 'unconditional love' of God still quote from 1John, about how "God is love". That's Nice; But Wondering if any of you noticed, yet, that there's NO mention of grace, NOR of mercy, NOR of peace in all of 1John, chps 1-5; And  in 1John 4:6, it seems John therein REVERSES the order of "spirits" to try, to "spirit of truth and spirit of error", which then ends with "error". Seems the proper 
order, when twain spiirts, should be spirit of error first, and spirit of truth last; So we end with "the spirit of truth".

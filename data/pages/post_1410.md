Palm Sunday? Observations re Mt 21, Mk 11, Lk 19 accounts:
There is no grace mentioned in Mt or Mk, none till one in Lk; So there's only peace mentioned by disciples in Lk 19:38. 
Note; seems also different Jesus speak in two 2nd comings to same eleven: Mk 16:16 = damned & Lk 24:36 = Peace. So then, when grace mentioned, things change.
Mt 
21:1-7 notes two animals: ass & colt; But Mk 11:1-7 & Lk 19:29-35 note only one animal: colt.  Hmmm...
Mt 21:9 & Mk 11:9 note two multitudes of 2Sons Jesus:
- they that "went before" him: Perhaps as if law driven by OT Son of man
- they that "followed" him: Perhaps as if grace led by the NT Son of God
Note: seems as different as OT themed "walk before me" = law driven by Holy Ghost & NT themes "follow me" = grace led by Spirit of Son (not Sons). Seems Lk 19:39 noted there were Pharisees among one multitude, perhaps why one mulitude later cried Crucify him, crucify him.
Note: seems in Jn 5:25-29, when Jesus still 2Sons, he revealed he still had 2voices: 
- voice of Son of God = "live"
- voice of Son of man = also "judgment" from "graves"; 
So it reasons WHY, beyond Acts (LukeWarm mix-two-are) there's NO more mention of Son of man, ONLY Son of God. Then it's "gospel of Christ":
Grace to you, and peace, from God our Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.

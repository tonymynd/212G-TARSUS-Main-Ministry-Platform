## Content

ALLness: it helped me to see the process as if two ALLs:
- 1stly By Law: sinners ALL, none righteous, no, not one.
- 2ndly By Grace: sinners none, ALL righteous, yes every one.
- He taketh away (removed) the 1st(law) to establish the 2nd(grace).
It's 
like in 2Cor 3, of two (law/grace) glorys contrasted, it twice mentions to be "done away" for the two ministrations of law glory: death & condemnation; Then concludes law has no glory by reason of how much more grace glory excelleth.
